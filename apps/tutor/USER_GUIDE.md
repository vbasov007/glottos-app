# polyGlottos User Guide

**Turn Every Text Into a Language Lesson**

polyGlottos is an AI-powered language learning tool that helps you study any text in a foreign language. Paste or generate text, select words and phrases, and get instant grammar breakdowns, translations, declension/conjugation tables, and text-to-speech pronunciation.

---

## Getting Started

### Sign In

Open the app and sign in with your Google account. On your first login you'll see a **language setup screen** where you pick:

- **Interface language** — the language of all buttons and labels
- **Text language** — the language you want to study (e.g. German, French, Hebrew)

You can change these later in Settings.

### Your First Explanation

1. **Paste a text** into the main text area (or generate one with AI — see below)
2. **Select a word or phrase** by clicking and dragging (desktop) or tapping (mobile)
3. Click the **Explain** button that appears
4. The right panel shows a full breakdown: translation, part of speech, morphology, declension/conjugation tables, example sentences, and usage notes

---

## Core Features

### Word & Phrase Explanations

Select any word, phrase, or sentence in your text and click **Explain**. The AI analyzes the selection in context and returns:

| Section | What it shows |
|---------|--------------|
| **Meanings** | Translations in your explanation language |
| **Morphology** | Lemma, gender, case, tense, person (as applicable) |
| **Forms** | Noun declension table, verb conjugation, or adjective comparison |
| **Examples** | 3-5 simple sentences using the word with translations |
| **Notes** | Irregular forms, common mistakes, collocations |

For **sentences**, you get a full translation, sentence structure analysis, and key grammar points.

**Quick Lookup** — use the input field below the text area to explain any word without selecting it from the text.

### Text-to-Speech (TTS)

- Click the **speaker icon** next to any word, example, or in the quick lookup field to hear its pronunciation
- Click **Listen to full text** in the toolbar to hear the entire text read aloud sentence by sentence
- The full-text player shows skip back/forward, play/pause, a progress bar, and a sentence counter
- Audio is cached so replaying the same phrase is instant

### Voice Recording

Record yourself reading a word or sentence to compare with the TTS pronunciation:

1. Click the **microphone icon** in the toolbar
2. Speak (max 20 seconds)
3. Click **play** to listen back
4. Re-record or delete as needed

### Generate Text with AI

When the text area is empty, click the **sparkles icon** in the toolbar to generate a practice text. Configure:

- **Language level** — A1 through C2
- **Number of sentences** — 5 to 30
- **Topic** — e.g. "travel", "daily routine", "technology"
- **Additional instructions** — e.g. "use past tense", "include adjectives"

The generated text appears in the text area, ready for study.

### Flashcards

After explaining one or more words, click the **flashcard icon** in the toolbar. The flashcard viewer lets you:

- **Flip cards** — click to reveal the translation
- **Navigate** — previous/next buttons or swipe
- **Listen** — TTS of the current card
- **Hide text** — blur the front side for recall practice
- **Autoplay** — cycle through cards with TTS automatically
- **Shuffle** — randomize the order
- **Reverse** — swap front (word) and back (translation)

### Export to Anki / Quizlet

Click the **download icon** in the toolbar to export all explained words as a CSV file. Import this file into Anki, Quizlet, or any flashcard app. Each row contains the word and its translation.

## Workspaces

Workspaces let you study multiple texts independently. Each workspace keeps its own text, explanation history, TTS history, and recordings.

- **Switch** — click a workspace tab
- **Create** — click the **+** button next to the tabs
- **Rename** — double-click the tab name
- **Delete** — hover over the tab and click the **x** (at least one workspace must remain)

Workspaces sync across devices. If the same workspace is edited on two devices, a conflict dialog lets you choose which version to keep.

---

## Settings

Click the **gear icon** in the header to open settings:

- **Interface language** — language of all UI labels (25+ languages)
- **Text language** — default language for new workspaces (38 languages available)
- **Explanation language** — language used in AI responses and translations
- **Subscription** — view your plan, subscribe to Pro, or manage billing

Each workspace can also have its own text language, changeable via the language dropdown in the toolbar.

## Mobile Usage

The app is fully responsive. On mobile:

- **Read mode** — tap any word to select it instantly (no dragging needed)
- **Edit mode** — tap the pencil icon to type or paste text
- Floating action buttons appear at the bottom when text is selected: **Explain**, **Listen**, **Edit**
- The full-text player docks at the bottom of the screen

---

## Tips

- **Hide your text** with the eye icon in the toolbar for privacy in public spaces or to test your recall
- **History sidebar** shows all words you've listened to — click any to replay
- **Navigate explanation history** with the arrows in the results panel to review past lookups
- **Quick lookup** works even without text in the editor — type any word and press Explain
- The **interactive tutorial** (info icon in the header) walks you through the interface step by step
- **Feedback** — click the chat icon in the header to report bugs or suggest features

---

## Keyboard & Interaction

- **Select text** — click and drag (desktop) or tap a word (mobile read mode)
- **Enter** — submit quick lookup
- **Escape** — close modals
- Changes auto-save 2 seconds after you stop typing

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|---------|
| "Daily limit reached" | Wait until tomorrow or upgrade to Pro |
| Text too long | Shorten your text below the character limit shown at the bottom right |
| Selection too long | Select shorter fragments (max 150 characters) |
| No sound | Check your device volume and browser audio permissions |
| Sync conflict | Choose "Load latest" to get the server version or "Keep local" to keep your edits |
| Login issues | Clear browser cookies and try again, or use a different browser |
