# polyGlottos — Help Guide

Welcome to polyGlottos! This guide walks you through every feature of the app with step-by-step instructions.

## Getting Started

### Signing In

Sign in with your Google account to start using polyGlottos. Your progress, workspaces, and settings are synced across all your devices automatically.

1. Click **Get Started** on the landing page
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. You're in — your first workspace is ready

![Signing in](gifs/sign-in.gif)

### Taking the Guided Tour

When you open the app for the first time, a short tutorial walks you through the interface. You can restart it any time.

1. Click the **ⓘ** (info) button in the top panel
2. Follow the 6-step walkthrough — each step highlights a part of the interface
3. Click **Next** to continue or **Skip** to close

![Guided tour](gifs/tutorial.gif)

## Working with Text

### Pasting or Typing Text

Paste any text you want to study into the editor area. It can be a news article, a book excerpt, song lyrics — anything in your target language.

1. Click the text area
2. Paste or type your text
3. The text is saved automatically

![Pasting text](gifs/paste-text.gif)

### Generating Text with AI

Don't have a text to study? Let AI generate one for you at your level.

1. With an empty workspace, click the **✦ Generate** button in the toolbar
2. Choose a language level (A1–C2)
3. Set the number of sentences (5–30)
4. Enter a topic (optional)
5. Add any special instructions (optional)
6. Click **Generate**

![Generating text](gifs/generate-text.gif)

### Clearing Text

Remove all text from the current workspace to start fresh.

1. Click the **🗑 Clear** button on the left side of the toolbar
2. Confirm the action in the dialog

![Clearing text](gifs/clear-text.gif)

### Hiding / Showing Text

Blur the text to test your memory while listening, then reveal it again.

1. Click the **👁 Eye** button in the toolbar to hide
2. Click again to show

![Hide and show text](gifs/hide-text.gif)

## Explaining Words and Phrases

### Selecting and Explaining on Desktop

Select any word or phrase to get a detailed AI explanation with grammar, forms, and examples.

1. Highlight a word or phrase in the text
2. A floating toolbar appears near your selection
3. Click **Explain**
4. The explanation appears in the right panel

![Explaining a word on desktop](gifs/explain-desktop.gif)

### Selecting and Explaining on Mobile

On mobile devices, tap words directly in read mode.

1. Tap a word in the text — it highlights in yellow
2. Tap more words to extend your selection
3. Tap **Explain** in the bottom bar
4. The explanation appears below

![Explaining a word on mobile](gifs/explain-mobile.gif)

### Browsing Explanation History

Navigate through all words and phrases you've explained in the current workspace.

1. Use **◀ ▶** arrows in the explanation panel header to move between entries
2. The counter shows your position (e.g. "3 / 12")

![Browsing explanation history](gifs/explanation-history.gif)

### Explanation List View

See all your explained words at a glance and jump to any of them.

1. Click the **☰ List** button in the explanation panel header
2. A dropdown shows all cached phrases
3. Click any phrase to jump to its explanation
4. Click the **✕** next to a phrase to delete it

![Explanation list view](gifs/explanation-list.gif)

### Understanding the Explanation Card

Each explanation card contains structured linguistic information.

**For words and phrases:**
- **Meanings** — most common translations
- **Part of speech** — noun, verb, adjective, etc.
- **Morphology** — lemma, gender, case, tense, person
- **Forms table** — full declension or conjugation
- **Examples** — simple sentences with translations
- **Notes** — usage tips, common mistakes, collocations

**For sentences:**
- **Translation** — accurate translation
- **Structure** — word order explanation
- **Highlights** — key grammatical forms with explanations
- **Examples** — similar sentences

![Explanation card details](gifs/explanation-card.gif)

## Listening and Speaking

### Listening to a Word or Phrase

Hear the pronunciation of any selected text.

1. Select a word or phrase
2. Click the **🔊 Listen** button in the floating toolbar (desktop) or bottom bar (mobile)
3. You can also click the speaker icon on any explanation card

![Listening to a word](gifs/listen-word.gif)

### Listening to Full Text

Play the entire text with sentence-by-sentence audio and position tracking.

1. Click the **▶ Listen** button in the toolbar
2. The text is read aloud sentence by sentence
3. Use the playback controls:
   - **⏮ / ⏭** — skip to previous or next sentence
   - **▶ / ⏸** — play or pause
   - **Progress bar** — drag to jump to any sentence
   - **✕** — stop playback

![Full text playback](gifs/listen-full.gif)

### Recording Your Voice

Record yourself reading the text, then compare with the AI pronunciation.

1. Click the **🎤 Mic** button in the toolbar
2. Speak — the recording indicator pulses red
3. Click the mic button again to stop
4. Use **▶** to play back your recording
5. Use **🗑** to delete and re-record

![Recording voice](gifs/record-voice.gif)

## Flashcards

### Starting a Flashcard Session

Study your explained words with interactive flashcards.

1. Click the **Flashcards** button in the toolbar (requires at least one explanation)
2. A card appears with the word on the front
3. Click the card to flip and see the translation

![Starting flashcards](gifs/flashcards-start.gif)

### Flashcard Controls

Customize your study session with various options.

- **🔊 Listen** — hear pronunciation of the current card
- **👁 Hide front** — blur the front for self-testing
- **▶ Autoplay** — automatically flip cards on a timer
- **⏱ Delay** — set autoplay speed (1–5 seconds)
- **🔀 Shuffle** — randomize card order
- **↕ Reverse** — swap front and back sides
- **◀ ▶** — navigate between cards
- **🗑 Delete** — remove current card from session

![Flashcard controls](gifs/flashcards-controls.gif)

### Exporting Cards for Anki

Export your explained words as a file compatible with Anki and other flashcard apps.

1. Click the **⬇ Export** button in the toolbar
2. A TSV file downloads with word–translation pairs
3. Import this file into Anki or any flashcard app

![Exporting cards](gifs/export-cards.gif)

## Workspaces

### Creating a New Workspace

Organize your study materials into separate workspaces — one per article, topic, or lesson.

1. Click the **+** button in the workspace tab bar
2. A new workspace opens with a default name
3. Each workspace has its own text, language, and explanation history

![Creating a workspace](gifs/create-workspace.gif)

### Switching Between Workspaces

Click any tab to switch. Your work is saved automatically.

1. Click a workspace tab to switch to it
2. All your text, explanations, and recordings are preserved

![Switching workspaces](gifs/switch-workspace.gif)

### Renaming a Workspace

Give your workspace a meaningful name.

1. Double-click the workspace tab name
2. Type the new name
3. Press **Enter** or click outside to save

![Renaming a workspace](gifs/rename-workspace.gif)

### Deleting a Workspace

Remove a workspace you no longer need.

1. Hover over the workspace tab
2. Click the **✕** that appears
3. Confirm deletion in the dialog

![Deleting a workspace](gifs/delete-workspace.gif)

## Subscription and Billing

### Free Plan Limits

The free plan includes daily limits on:
- Number of word explanations
- Number of TTS (text-to-speech) requests
- Number of AI-generated texts
- Maximum text length and generated sentences

When you reach a limit, a message appears with an option to upgrade.

### Upgrading to Pro

Unlock unlimited explanations, TTS, and text generation.

1. Click **Upgrade** in the settings modal, or when a limit message appears
2. Choose a plan
3. Complete payment via Stripe
4. Your Pro status activates immediately

![Upgrading to Pro](gifs/upgrade-pro.gif)

### Managing Your Subscription

View your plan status or manage billing.

1. Click the **⚙ Settings** button
2. Your current plan is shown in the billing section
3. Click **Manage Billing** to open the Stripe customer portal
4. From there you can update payment method, cancel, or view invoices

![Managing subscription](gifs/manage-billing.gif)

## Tips and Tricks

- **Study in context**: Paste real articles, books, or dialogues — context makes explanations more accurate
- **Build vocabulary**: Explain words as you read, then review with flashcards
- **Practice pronunciation**: Use full-text listen, then record yourself and compare
- **One workspace per topic**: Keep your study materials organized
- **Export regularly**: Download your flashcards to Anki for long-term spaced repetition
- **Hide text while listening**: Test your comprehension by blurring the text during playback
