# Test for lesson 23: Future tense. The verb werden

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I will fly to the seaside in summer.
2. Will you relax on vacation?
3. He will rent a vacation house.
4. We will travel to an island.
5. I will book the flight tomorrow.
6. She will relax on the beach.
7. Will you (plural) pack the suitcase?
8. They will travel to the sea.
9. I promise — I will help you.
10. Everything will be fine!
11. He's probably already on vacation.
12. One day I will live on an island.
13. I'm flying to Munich tomorrow.
14. Next week we're going to the sea.
15. On Saturday I'm packing the suitcase.
16. Will you rent a vacation house on the island?
17. My brother will fly to Italy.
18. My parents will relax.
19. She is probably on vacation.
20. What will you do on vacation?
21. Where will you travel?
22. Where will you stay?
23. It's getting cold.
24. The days are getting longer.
25. She will become a doctor.
26. Have you already booked accommodation? — Not yet. I'll rent a vacation house next week.
27. What will you do on vacation? — I'll fly to an island and relax on the beach.
28. Will your sister rent a vacation house? — Yes, she will rent a vacation house.
29. Where will your parents travel in summer? — They'll travel to the sea. My mom is really looking forward to the beach.
30. Will you be on vacation in summer? — Yes, I'll fly to the seaside and relax. I'm really looking forward to it!
