# Test for lesson 11: Negation nicht/kein + antonyms

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I don't have a brother.
2. The book is not interesting.
3. He doesn't drink milk.
4. We don't understand.
5. She has no money.
6. The apartment is not big.
7. I'm not buying a bike.
8. He's not coming today.
9. The apartment is small, but not expensive.
10. They have no children.
11. The street is not clean. It's dirty.
12. The coffee is not hot. It's cold.
13. She's not working today.
14. I have no time.
15. The suitcase is not light. It's heavy.
16. My brother doesn't have a car.
17. The music is not loud. It's quiet.
18. This is not expensive. This is cheap.
19. She doesn't buy a bag.
20. We have no problems.
21. The room is not bright. It's dark.
22. The soup is not hot. It's cold.
23. He doesn't speak loudly.
24. The street is not long. It's short.
25. He's not hungry. (Er hat keinen Hunger.)
26. I don't see any difference.
27. My sister doesn't have a dog.
28. Water is not wet — it's dry. (Joke! The towel is not wet. It's dry.)
29. The train is not fast. It's slow. And it's not new — it's old.
30. The apartment is not big and not clean. We have no garage and no car.
