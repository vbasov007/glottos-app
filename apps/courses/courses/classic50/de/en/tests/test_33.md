# Test for Lesson 33: Dass / ob / was. Technology

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I know that the update is already out.
2. I don't know whether the Wi-Fi is working.
3. I don't know what he is doing.
4. He says that the app is very practical.
5. She asks whether I downloaded the file.
6. Do you know what the password is?
7. We hope that the update will come soon.
8. Check whether the smartphone is charged.
9. I believe that the smartphone is too expensive.
10. I don't understand what he wrote.
11. She doesn't know whether the app is free.
12. Show me how to upload a photo.
13. Do you know that the new update is already out?
14. He asks whether we installed the app.
15. I don't know who changed the password.
16. She says that the battery is dead.
17. Can you check whether the Wi-Fi is working?
18. Is it true that you can google everything?
19. Did you see what he uploaded?
20. I know that this app works well.
21. He doesn't know whether there is Wi-Fi here.
22. She doesn't understand what is written in the message.
23. I will show you how this works.
24. Tell her that the password is on the table.
25. Do you know how to download this app?
26. I think that the screen is too small.
27. He asks whether I saved the file.
28. I didn't know that the update is already out. I will check whether it works.
29. She doesn't know what he deleted, and asks whether he deleted important files.
30. Can you show me how to download this app, and explain what it does?
