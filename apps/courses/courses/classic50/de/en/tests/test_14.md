# Test for lesson 14: Days of the week/months/seasons + holidays

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. On Monday I work.
2. In January it's cold.
3. In summer we go on vacation.
4. On Saturday I don't work.
5. My birthday is in May.
6. We celebrate Christmas in December.
7. In spring it's warm.
8. On Wednesday I have an appointment (Termin).
9. When is your birthday? — On the twelfth of June.
10. I'm inviting my friend to the party.
11. He gives his brother a present.
12. We congratulate our mother on her birthday.
13. There are five candles on the cake.
14. In autumn the leaves fall.
15. On Sunday we don't work.
16. Christmas is on the twenty-fifth of December.
17. What are you doing on the weekend?
18. Easter is in spring.
19. I give my mother candles and a card.
20. Are you inviting Mark to the party?
21. The wedding is on Saturday, at seven in the evening.
22. In winter we drink hot tea.
23. He has no present.
24. On the first of January we celebrate New Year.
25. What do you want for your birthday? — I want a new book.
26. She invites thirty people to the wedding.
27. What are you doing at Christmas? — We celebrate at home.
28. I don't have an invitation to the party.
29. The party starts at eight in the evening. Don't be late! Be punctual!
30. When is your brother's birthday? — On the seventh of May. I'm giving him a present and congratulating him.
