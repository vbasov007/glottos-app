# Test for lesson 47: Plusquamperfekt (hatte/war + Partizip II) + biographies

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I had already eaten lunch. (Plusquamperfekt)
2. She had already moved to Berlin. (Plusquamperfekt)
3. We had signed the contract. (Plusquamperfekt)
4. You had arrived late. (Plusquamperfekt)
5. The children had played all day. (Plusquamperfekt)
6. After he had finished school, he began his studies.
7. After she had learned German, she moved to Berlin.
8. After we had had dinner, we went for a walk.
9. Before she moved to Berlin, she had lived in Munich.
10. When we arrived, the concert had already begun.
11. After he had gained experience, he founded his own company.
12. When I called, she had already left.
13. After the children had grown up, the parents retired.
14. He got a position at Siemens after he had earned his degree.
15. After he had failed, he tried again.
16. Before she got married, she had traveled the whole world.
17. Had Mozart already composed music as a child? (Plusquamperfekt)
18. Had the family fled before the war? (Plusquamperfekt)
19. Did he found the company alone? — No, he had founded it with a friend. (Plusquamperfekt)
20. Had the parents lived in a village before? — Yes, they had lived there for twenty years. (Plusquamperfekt)
21. Had you already learned German before? — Yes, I had already studied it. (Plusquamperfekt)
22. Had he already left when you arrived?
23. Anna grew up in a small village. After she had finished school, she studied medicine in Hamburg.
24. First he had lived in Vienna, then he moved to Berlin. (use zunächst / anschließend)
25. Back then she hadn't gotten married yet. (use damals)
26. Finally he had reached his goal. (use schließlich)
27. In the meantime he had already found a new job. (use inzwischen)
28. She had completed her vocational training before she began her career.
29. After she got divorced, she moved to another city.
30. After he retired, he began to travel the world. Before that, he had worked as an engineer for twenty years and had had no time.
