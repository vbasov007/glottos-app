# Test for lesson 27: Perfekt (haben/sein + Partizip II). Household chores

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I cooked soup yesterday.
2. Did you clean the kitchen?
3. She did the laundry.
4. We vacuumed the apartment.
5. He washed the dishes.
6. They ironed the shirts.
7. I tidied up the room.
8. Did you wipe the floor?
9. He went to the cinema.
10. She traveled to Berlin.
11. I have already done everything.
12. Have you cooked today already?
13. The children fell asleep.
14. We dried the dishes.
15. What did you do yesterday evening?
16. I studied German.
17. My brother cooked dinner.
18. Mom cleaned the whole apartment.
19. Did you wash the dishes? — No, I already washed them.
20. Dad vacuumed, and mom ironed.
21. Yesterday I cooked pasta and washed the dishes.
22. She bought groceries and cooked lunch.
23. Who did the laundry? — My brother did it.
24. We tidied up and wiped the floor yesterday.
25. Have you cleaned the kitchen already? — Yes, I already cleaned it.
26. He drove to work and came home late.
27. My sister tidied up the room, and I washed the dishes.
28. Today I cooked, did the laundry, and vacuumed.
29. Did the children help? — Yes, they washed the dishes and dried the table.
30. Yesterday evening I cooked dinner, cleaned the kitchen, and went to bed.
