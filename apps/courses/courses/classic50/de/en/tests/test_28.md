# Test for lesson 28: Perfekt — irregular verbs (geschrieben, getrunken, without ge-). Sport

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I trained today.
2. Did you jog yesterday?
3. She drank a lot of water.
4. We swam in the swimming pool.
5. He rode a bike.
6. I got up at six o'clock.
7. Did you write a training plan?
8. She lost three kilograms.
9. The doctor recommended him a diet.
10. I ran five kilometers.
11. Did you understand the trainer?
12. They did sports.
13. He drove to the gym.
14. She started a new diet.
15. We did the shopping at the supermarket.
16. Did you get up early today?
17. I trained for one hour.
18. She forgot her sports bag.
19. He told me about the training.
20. My brother did sports and swam.
21. Did you drink enough water? — Yes, I drank two liters.
22. She jogged for thirty minutes and then swam.
23. The doctor recommended her more exercise.
24. The team won the competition.
25. After training, he fell asleep right away.
26. I rode my bike to work and ran five kilometers in the evening.
27. Did you do sports? — Yes, I jogged and swam.
28. He got up early, drove to the gym, and trained for two hours.
29. My endurance has improved because I trained a lot.
30. Yesterday I ran ten kilometers, drank a lot of water, and went to bed early.
