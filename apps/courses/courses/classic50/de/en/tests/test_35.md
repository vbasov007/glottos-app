# Block-Test 4 (Lessons 26–35): Graf / Gräfin — past tenses and complex sentences B1

**How do you say this in German?**
Big test on the fourth block: Präteritum of modals and war, Perfekt (regular and irregular verbs, haben/sein), Präteritum, countries and direction prepositions, zu + Infinitiv, um zu / damit, dass / ob, weil / denn, vor/in/seit, wann/wenn/als. Topics: childhood, household chores, sports, news, work, education, technology, opinions, anglicisms. Translate into German.

---

1. As a child I had to walk to school.
2. When I was ten, I went to my grandma's for the holidays.
3. I used to not be able to speak German.
4. I was in Berlin last year.
5. Yesterday I cooked dinner and cleaned the kitchen.
6. I washed the laundry and ironed the shirts.
7. Who vacuumed the apartment? — I did.
8. She did the dishes after dinner.
9. I cycled to the park. (Perfekt)
10. We swam in the lake and ran in the forest.
11. He went on a diet because he wants to lose weight.
12. I jog every morning and go to the gym.
13. The newspaper reported that an accident had happened.
14. What happened yesterday? — There was a scandal.
15. The meeting took place in Berlin.
16. Last year I went to Italy.
17. She is going to France, and her brother is going to Turkey.
18. I'm planning a trip to Switzerland.
19. What's the capital of Poland? — Warsaw.
20. The border between Germany and Austria is open.
21. I forgot to write him an email.
22. I don't have time to take part in this meeting.
23. I start working at eight.
24. I learn German in order to work in Germany. (um zu)
25. I learn German so that my children speak German. (damit)
26. He came to help me. (um zu)
27. I don't know whether he'll come tomorrow. (ob)
28. I hope that you'll pass the exam. (dass)
29. I don't know what he wants. (was)
30. I didn't come because I was sick. (weil)
31. I didn't come, because I was sick. (denn)
32. I'm not working because I'm on vacation. (weil)
33. I've been living here for three years. (seit)
34. In a week I have an exam. (in)
35. I came to Germany two years ago. (vor)
36. When is your birthday? (question)
37. When I come home, I'll rest. (wenn)
38. When I was a child, I lived in Moscow. (als)
39. When I came home, my mom was already asleep. (habitual — wenn)
40. Every day I download files from the internet.
41. I googled it. I downloaded an app.
42. Do you have WiFi at home? — Yes, in the living room.
43. In my opinion, the climate is very important.
44. I think we need to study more.
45. I'm for this idea. He is against.
46. I study at university and take exams.
47. I want to get a scholarship and study abroad.
48. The meeting was cool. Everyone was excited.
49. I chilled all day and didn't work.
50. When I was a student, I hardly traveled because I had no money. But last year I finally went to Italy and saw Rome. I don't know whether I'll go there again, but I hope so.
