# Test for lesson 19: Article-words dieser/jeder/welcher + morning routine

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. This alarm clock is loud.
2. Every morning I get up at six.
3. Which tea do you drink?
4. I brush my teeth every day.
5. This towel is clean.
6. Which muesli do you buy?
7. This coffee is strong.
8. I take a shower every morning.
9. I don't like this kind of weather.
10. This toothpaste is new.
11. Every child brushes their teeth.
12. Which alarm clock do you use?
13. I have breakfast every day.
14. This bathroom is small.
15. I don't use this toothbrush.
16. I set the alarm clock every evening.
17. Which towel is yours? — This towel is mine.
18. Some habits are hard to change.
19. I need such an alarm clock too.
20. Every day I leave the house at eight.
21. I give this child the towel.
22. Which breakfast do you prefer? — Muesli with milk.
23. He uses this old alarm clock.
24. Does everyone in your family have breakfast? — No, some only have coffee.
25. What time do you get up every morning? — Every morning at six. This alarm clock rings very loudly.
26. Without such habits I'm tired all day.
27. Do you drink coffee or tea every morning? — Every morning I drink a strong coffee without sugar.
28. Which mirror is in the bathroom? — This mirror is new.
29. Do you have breakfast every day? — Yes, every day. Without breakfast I have no energy.
30. What's the first thing you do every morning? — Every morning I first turn off the alarm clock, get up, take a shower and have breakfast in the kitchen.
