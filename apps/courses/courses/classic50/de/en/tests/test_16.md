# Test for lesson 16: Ordinal numbers, dates, transport

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. Today is the first of January.
2. The second track.
3. The train leaves from track five.
4. I'm traveling on the third of April.
5. When is the next train?
6. A return ticket to Hamburg, please.
7. What's the date today? — The fifth of March.
8. The tram stop.
9. The bus schedule.
10. The train has a delay.
11. Do I have to change (trains)?
12. I'm getting off at the next stop.
13. My birthday is on the eighth of October.
14. The exam is on the twenty-first of June.
15. From which track does the S-Bahn leave?
16. She arrives on the thirteenth of May.
17. I'm getting on the bus.
18. The train leaves at ten thirty.
19. The third street on the left.
20. How long does the trip take? — About three hours.
21. We're traveling to Berlin on the fourteenth of July.
22. Today is the thirty-first of December.
23. The long-distance bus to Munich.
24. Please get on the train. The train is leaving.
25. He changes (trains) at the main station.
26. Classes start on the second of September.
27. One return ticket — there on the twelfth, back on the fifteenth.
28. The train on track four has a fifteen-minute delay.
29. When are you buying the ticket? — I'm buying it on the first of June.
30. When does the next train to Cologne leave? — At fourteen thirty, from track seven. Change in Hannover.
