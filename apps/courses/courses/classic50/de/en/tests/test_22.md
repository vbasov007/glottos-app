**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

2. The elephant is the biggest animal.
3. My brother is older than me.
4. Which animal do you like the most?
5. The bear is stronger than the fox.
6. The squirrel lives in the forest in the trees.

25. The dog is man's best friend.
