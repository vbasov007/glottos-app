# Test for lesson 21: Reflexive verbs. The particle sich

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I wash myself in the morning.
2. He is looking forward to the weekend.
3. Are you in a hurry?
4. We feel good.
5. She gets dressed quickly.
6. The children go to bed at ten.
7. I'm interested in TV series.
8. Do you remember this film?
9. He apologizes to the (female) neighbor.
10. I brush my teeth.
11. After work I come home and change my clothes.
12. She relaxes on the couch.
13. We're looking forward to the end of the workday.
14. Do you comb your hair in front of the mirror?
15. He takes off his clothes in the hallway.
16. I wash my hands before dinner.
17. Are you (plural) in a hurry? The bus is already here!
18. She sits down on the couch and watches a series.
19. Do you feel tired today?
20. We rest after work.
21. He is interested in history.
22. I go to bed at half past eleven.
23. Are you looking forward to your vacation?
24. She remembers that trip.
25. I tidy up the kitchen and take out the trash.
26. Are you turning off the light and going to bed?
27. She washes her face in the evening.
28. My brother is never in a hurry.
29. What do you do after dinner? — I sit down on the couch and play PlayStation.
30. Are you tired today? — Yes, I feel very tired. I want to lie down early.
