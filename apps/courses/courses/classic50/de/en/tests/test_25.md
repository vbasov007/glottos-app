# Block-Test 3 (Lessons 19–25): Baron / Baronin — extension A2

**How do you say this in German?**
Big test on the third block: article-words (dieser/jeder/welcher), separable verbs, reflexive verbs, comparison, future with werden, wissen/kennen, impersonal sentences (man/es/es gibt). Topics: daily routine, work, animals, weather, plans. Translate into German.

---

1. I've already read this book.
2. I learn new words every day.
3. Which bus goes to the city centre?
4. I get up at seven and take a shower.
5. When does your work start? — At nine.
6. I have breakfast and go to work.
7. He commutes every day from Berlin to Potsdam.
8. We have a lunch break from twelve to one.
9. Where is the canteen here? — On the second floor.
10. After work I tidy up the apartment.
11. I go to bed at eleven.
12. I wash myself and brush my teeth.
13. She is happy about the new job.
14. The kids play PlayStation every evening.
15. I watch a series and go to sleep.
16. Berlin is bigger than Munich.
17. My brother is older than me.
18. This is the most beautiful park in the city.
19. Which animal is the fastest? — The cheetah.
20. The elephant is bigger than the bear.
21. The fox is craftier than the dog.
22. Tomorrow I'll go on vacation to the sea.
23. Next year we'll rent a house on an island.
24. What will you do on the weekend? — I'll relax.
25. Do you know him? — Yes, I've known him for many years. (kennen)
26. Do you know where he lives? — No, I don't know. (wissen)
27. I know Berlin very well. (kennen)
28. I know that it will rain tomorrow. (wissen)
29. He has a good education. She has two children.
30. What's your marital status? — I'm married.
31. They speak German here.
32. In Germany people drink a lot of coffee.
33. Today it's hot and sunny.
34. It's snowing. It's raining.
35. It's cold outside — about minus five.
36. Is there a supermarket here? — Yes, around the corner.
37. You have to buy the ticket in advance.
38. Can you park here? — No, you can't.
39. It's thundering, and there's lightning.
40. This city is more expensive than that one.
41. Tomorrow it will be windy and cloudy.
42. It often snows here in winter.
43. This restaurant is better than that one. (besser)
44. Which student has a question?
45. Every child must learn.
46. I'm interested in this job. (sich interessieren für)
47. When will you come back? — At five.
48. After lunch I rest.
49. Do you know where there is a pharmacy here? — Yes, I know, it's on the right.
50. What's the weather like today? — It's sunny. — And tomorrow? — Tomorrow it will rain, and it will be cold. Which month is the rainiest of the year?
