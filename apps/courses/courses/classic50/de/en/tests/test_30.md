# Test for lesson 30: Countries (nach / in die). Geography

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I'm going to Germany.
2. She's flying to Turkey.
3. We're going to Switzerland.
4. He's flying to the USA.
5. I'm from Russia.
6. She's from Turkey.
7. He lives in Iran.
8. Where are you going in summer?
9. Where are you from?
10. I'm going to Austria.
11. We're flying to Japan.
12. The capital of Austria is Vienna.
13. Have you already booked the flight?
14. She lives in Switzerland.
15. They are going to France.
16. My brother lives abroad.
17. Do you need a visa?
18. My suitcase is heavy.
19. We are planning a trip.
20. He is from Ukraine.
21. Which countries border Germany?
22. Where is your brother flying? — He's flying to Turkey.
23. She's from Switzerland, but she lives in Germany.
24. Have you been to the USA? — No, but I'm planning a trip.
25. I'm going to Switzerland. — I live in Switzerland.
26. His uncle lives in Iran, and his aunt lives in Germany.
27. Where are you going on vacation? — I'm flying to Turkey. I've already booked the flight.
28. My parents are flying to China next month and planning a trip to Japan.
29. Where is your (female) colleague from? — She's from Switzerland, but now she lives in the USA.
30. I want to travel to Spain, book a flight, take my passport, and pack my suitcase.
