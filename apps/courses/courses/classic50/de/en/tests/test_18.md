# Block-Test 2 (Lessons 9–18): Ritter / Ritterin — case foundations A1

**How do you say this in German?**
Big test on the second block: Akkusativ, Dativ, nicht/kein, Imperativ, time and duration, days and months, Wechselpräpositionen, ordinal numbers, personal pronouns in cases. Translate into German.

---

1. I'm buying an apple and bread. (Akk)
2. I don't have a car.
3. That's not my bag.
4. I'm giving the book to my brother. (Dat)
5. I'm helping my mother.
6. Come here! (sg.)
7. Be attentive! (pl.)
8. Take the bag, please.
9. What are you doing now? — I'm shopping at the supermarket.
10. What time is it? — Half past seven.
11. Work starts at nine o'clock.
12. From Monday to Friday I work.
13. I've been living here for three years.
14. Today is Tuesday, and tomorrow is Wednesday.
15. My birthday is in May.
16. It's cold in winter, hot in summer.
17. Christmas is in December.
18. I was at Anna's birthday. It was a nice party.
19. I'm inviting you to the party.
20. The book is lying on the table. (Dat — where?)
21. I'm putting the book on the table. (Akk — where to?)
22. The picture is hanging on the wall. Where shall I hang the picture?
23. The cat is sitting under the table, and the dog is running under the table.
24. We're flying to Berlin. Is there breakfast at the hotel?
25. Which platform is the train on? — On platform three.
26. I take the tram to work.
27. How much does the ticket cost? — Three euros fifty.
28. Excuse me, where is the bus stop?
29. I like this sweater. Can I try it on?
30. I'd like to pay by card.
31. Where is the cash desk? — Straight ahead, then left.
32. I have a headache and a sore throat.
33. Go to the pharmacy and buy pills!
34. I have a fever and a cough. I need a doctor.
35. The jacket is too small. I want to exchange it.
36. What size do you wear? — Size 38.
37. Today is March 15th. (date)
38. My first day in Germany was hard.
39. I'm giving him the book. (ihm)
40. I love her. She loves me. (mich, sie)
41. I'll call you in the evening. (dir)
42. I know them well. (sie)
43. Help me, please! (mir)
44. This bag is heavy. Take a shopping cart.
45. There's a museum on the corner. Go straight to the crossroads.
46. Where is the post office? — On the right, next to the train station.
47. Excuse me, how do I get to the hotel?
48. The old building is dirty, the new one is clean.
49. This apartment is too expensive. Do you have something cheaper?
50. Where are you going? — To the supermarket. What are you buying? — Vegetables and fruit. With whom? — With my sister.
