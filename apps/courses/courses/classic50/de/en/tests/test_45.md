# Test for lesson 45: Advanced Konjunktiv II (als ob, hätte + Part. II) + idioms

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German.

---

1. Could you help me?
2. Do you have a minute? (polite, Konjunktiv II)
3. If I were you, I would go to the doctor.
4. If only he were here!
5. She acts as if she knows everything.
6. If I had known that earlier, I would have called you.
7. Could you please repeat that?
8. If I were you, I would accept the offer.
9. If only I had more time!
10. He looks as if he hadn't slept all night.
11. If you had asked me, I would have helped you.
12. Would you happen to have a pen for me? (polite, Konjunktiv II)
13. If we had left earlier, we wouldn't have missed the train.
14. She acts as if she were an expert. (short form: als + Konj. II, no ob)
15. If only I could speak German better!
16. If I were him, I would not sign the contract.
17. He would have done that differently.
18. Would you have come along? — Yes, I would have come.
19. I don't understand a word. (idiom: "I only understand train station")
20. Can we talk in private? (idiom: "under four eyes")
21. Good luck on the exam! I'm rooting for you. (idiom: "I press the thumbs for you")
22. I'm fed up with this work. (idiom: "I have the nose full")
23. You're on the wrong track. (idiom: "on the wooden way")
24. She got lucky. (idiom: "she had pig")
25. He fails to notice the obvious. (idiom: "he has tomatoes on the eyes")
26. If she had known German, she would have found that job. (past)
27. If we had had time back then, we would have gone to Berlin. (past)
28. Would you have believed him? — No, I would not have believed him.
29. If he had studied more, he would have passed the exam. (past)
30. He acts as if he knew everything, but he's on the wrong track. (als ob + idiom)

9. *Wenn ich doch nur mehr Zeit hätte!*

11. *Wenn du mich gefragt hättest, hätte ich dir geholfen.*
12. *Hätten Sie vielleicht einen Kugelschreiber für mich?*
13. *Wenn wir früher losgefahren wären, hätten wir den Zug nicht verpasst.*
14. *Sie tut so, als wäre sie ein Experte.* (или: *...als wäre sie eine Expertin.*)
