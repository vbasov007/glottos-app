# Test for lesson 9: Akkusativ + supermarket

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German.

---

1. I'm buying bread.
2. He's looking for the checkout.
3. We need a shopping cart.
4. Do you see the discount?
5. She takes a basket.
6. We find the special offer.
7. I have a receipt.
8. I'm buying the cake.
9. He's reading the receipt.
10. Are you taking a cart or a basket?
11. She's looking for the dairy aisle.
12. We see the price. The price is high.
13. He needs a bag.
14. I'd like this product. (polite)
15. Do you see the special offer on fish?
16. Where can I find the scale? — The scale is in the fruit section.
17. Do you need a bag? (formal) — Yes, I need a bag.
18. The shopping cart is at the entrance. I'm taking the cart.
19. The price is high. I don't see a discount.
20. She's buying meat, rice and vegetables.
21. What color is your bag? — My bag is black.
22. Where are you going? — To the supermarket.
23. What are you buying? — I'm buying bread, butter and cheese.
24. How much does the fish cost? — The fish costs five euros.
25. Can we pay here? — Yes, the checkout is over there.
26. She'd like to buy fruit and pasta. (polite)
27. His coat is black. He's looking for his bag.
28. Are you taking a basket? — No, I need a cart. I have a lot of groceries.
29. We're buying the fish, the bread and the milk. We're looking for the checkout.
30. I see the special offer: the sausage costs two euros. I'm buying the sausage and a carton of milk.
