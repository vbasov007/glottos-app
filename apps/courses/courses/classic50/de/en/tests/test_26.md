# Test for lesson 26: Präteritum of modal verbs. The verb war/hatte. Childhood

**How do you say this in German?**
Translate the phrases into German. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I could swim well.
2. She had to get up early.
3. We wanted to travel to Hamburg.
4. Were you allowed to play outside?
5. He had to be quiet.
6. I was at the playground.
7. We had a dog.
8. She couldn't read before.
9. As a child I wanted to become a doctor.
10. Did you have friends at school?
11. Every day we had to do homework.
12. My grandma was very kind.
13. He wasn't allowed to watch TV for long.
14. Could you ride a bike as a child?
15. We were on holiday at grandma's.
16. My parents wanted a big house.
17. She had a neighbor.
18. Back then, children had to obey.
19. My grandpa could play chess well.
20. Did you (plural) want to play hide-and-seek?
21. In summer I was at my grandma's in the countryside.
22. We didn't have a car before.
23. She liked ice cream.
24. In primary school I had to study a lot.
25. His sister could climb trees well.
26. Were you on the schoolyard? — Yes, I was on the schoolyard.
27. We wanted to play football, but it was raining.
28. Did you have a (female) friend? — Yes, I had many (female) friends.
29. I couldn't speak German before, but now I can.
30. As a child I swung on the swings and played at the playground every day.
