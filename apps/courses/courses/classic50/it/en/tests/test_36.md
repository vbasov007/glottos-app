# Test for Lesson 36: Congiuntivo imperfetto and se-clauses

**How do you say it in Italian?**
Translate the phrases into Italian. Use the grammar and vocabulary of the lesson — congiuntivo imperfetto, the hypothetical se-clause (type 2), and sequence of tenses.

---

1. If I had time, I would go to the cinema.
2. If I were you, I would accept.
3. If I knew the answer, I would tell you.
4. If we had money, we would buy a house.
5. In your place, I would talk to him.
6. I wish I had more time!
7. If it's raining, we stay home. *(real)*
8. If I have time tomorrow, I'll call you. *(real, future)*
9. I wanted you to come.
10. I didn't think it was true.
11. I was glad you were here.
12. It was important that you study.
13. I was afraid something would happen.
14. I doubted he knew the answer.
15. I spoke quietly so that you'd understand.
16. What would you do if you won the lottery?
17. If I could, I'd quit my job.
18. If we lived in Italy, we'd speak better.
19. If you told me the truth, I would forgive you.
20. If only it were that simple!
21. I'd like to travel more.
22. I'd love to live near the sea.
23. If I had wings, I'd fly.
24. In your place, I wouldn't do this.
25. If you came tomorrow, we could have dinner together.
26. If she knew the truth, she would be upset.
27. If I had more money, I'd be happier.
28. I asked him to help me.
29. Mum wanted us to come early.
30. If it were possible, I would stay.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *Se avessi tempo, andrei al cinema.*
2. *Se fossi in te, accetterei.*
3. *Se sapessi la risposta, te la direi.*
4. *Se avessimo soldi, compreremmo una casa.*
5. *Se fossi in te, gli parlerei.* (or: *Io, al posto tuo, gli parlerei.*)
6. *Magari avessi più tempo!*
7. *Se piove, restiamo a casa.*
8. *Se avrò tempo domani, ti chiamerò.* (or: *Se ho tempo domani, ti chiamo.*)
9. *Volevo che tu venissi.*
10. *Non credevo che fosse vero.*
11. *Ero contento che tu fossi qui.*
12. *Era importante che (tu) studiassi.*
13. *Avevo paura che succedesse qualcosa.*
14. *Dubitavo che sapesse la risposta.*
15. *Parlavo piano perché tu capissi.*
16. *Cosa faresti se vincessi alla lotteria?*
17. *Se potessi, lascerei il lavoro.*
18. *Se vivessimo in Italia, parleremmo meglio.*
19. *Se tu mi dicessi la verità, ti perdonerei.*
20. *Magari fosse così semplice!*
21. *Vorrei viaggiare di più.*
22. *Mi piacerebbe vivere vicino al mare.*
23. *Se avessi le ali, volerei.*
24. *Se fossi in te, non lo farei.*
25. *Se venissi domani, potremmo cenare insieme.*
26. *Se sapesse la verità, sarebbe triste.*
27. *Se avessi più soldi, sarei più felice.*
28. *Gli ho chiesto che mi aiutasse.* (or: *…di aiutarmi.*)
29. *La mamma voleva che venissimo presto.*
30. *Se fosse possibile, resterei.*
