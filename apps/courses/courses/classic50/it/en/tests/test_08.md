# Test for lesson 8: -ERE and -IRE verbs; key irregulars

**How do you say this in Italian?**
Translate the phrases into Italian. Use the grammar and vocabulary of this lesson.

---

1. I'm having a coffee.
2. Anna is writing a letter.
3. We're reading a book.
4. The children sleep well.
5. I finish at six.
6. Do you understand Italian?
7. Where are you going? — I'm going home.
8. Where are you (pl.) from? — I'm from England.
9. What do you do? — I'm a doctor.
10. How are you? — Fine, thanks.
11. They prefer wine.
12. Marco says (that) it's a good idea.
13. I'm opening the window.
14. Anna closes the door.
15. I give a present to my mother.
16. We can see the sea.
17. They're selling the house.
18. What do you mean?
19. I don't understand.
20. They come on Saturday.
21. I'm going to Italy tomorrow.
22. Anna makes the coffee.
23. What are you writing?
24. We read a lot.
25. The children are leaving tomorrow.
26. I'm answering the question.
27. The train arrives at eight.
28. I live in Italy.
29. Do you (pl.) understand French?
30. Where does this train come from?

<details><summary>Key</summary>

1. *Prendo un caffè.*
2. *Anna scrive una lettera.*
3. *Leggiamo un libro.*
4. *I bambini dormono bene.*
5. *Finisco alle sei.*
6. *Capisci l'italiano?*
7. *Dove vai? — Vado a casa.*
8. *Da dove venite? — Vengo dall'Inghilterra.* (or: *Sono inglese*)
9. *Cosa fai? — Faccio il medico.*
10. *Come stai? — Sto bene, grazie.* (or: *Come va? — Bene, grazie.*)
11. *Preferiscono il vino.*
12. *Marco dice che è una buona idea.*
13. *Apro la finestra.*
14. *Anna chiude la porta.*
15. *Do un regalo alla mamma.* (or: *Faccio un regalo alla mamma.*)
16. *Vediamo il mare.*
17. *Vendono la casa.*
18. *Cosa vuoi dire?* (or: *Che cosa vuoi dire?* — *volere* formally appears in L11; here it's a fixed phrase)
19. *Non capisco.*
20. *Vengono sabato.* (or: *Vengono il sabato.*)
21. *Vado in Italia domani.*
22. *Anna fa il caffè.*
23. *Cosa scrivi?* (or: *Che cosa scrivi?*)
24. *Leggiamo molto.*
25. *I bambini partono domani.*
26. *Rispondo alla domanda.*
27. *Il treno arriva alle otto.*
28. *Vivo in Italia.* (or: *Abito in Italia.*)
29. *Capite il francese?*
30. *Da dove viene questo treno?*
