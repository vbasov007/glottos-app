# Test for lesson 17: Combining pronouns — *me lo, te la, glielo*

**How do you say this in Italian?**
Translate the phrases into Italian. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I'll give it (f.) to you.
2. Will you show it to me?
3. I'm explaining it (f.) to him.
4. We're sending it to you (pl.) by post.
5. The little one — he gives it to me as a gift.
6. She tells us about it every day.
7. You have to tell her this.
8. I want to give it to you as a gift.
9. Can you bring me one?
10. I send it (f.) to them at Christmas.
11. Will you write me a letter? — Yes, I'll write it to you tomorrow.
12. Tell me the truth!
13. I always tell her the truth.
14. They show us their photos.
15. I can lend them to you.
16. He doesn't tell me anything.
17. The students ask the teacher questions — he answers them.
18. I want to read it (f.) to you.
19. I need an explanation — will you give it to me?
20. Mum likes flowers — I often bring her some.
21. I'll lend it to you for a week.
22. We're explaining it to you (pl.) for the last time.
23. How many have you got? — I'll give you five (of them).
24. Have you already told him?
25. I have to tell her this tonight.
26. Show it (f.) to us!
27. I'll buy it for you for your birthday.
28. They don't believe you — you have to prove it to them.
29. The gift? I'll give it to you later.
30. It's a secret. I'm telling it to you, but to nobody else.

<summary>Keys</summary>

1. *Te la do.*
2. *Me lo mostri?* (or: *Me lo fai vedere?*)
3. *Gliela spiego.*
4. *Ve lo mandiamo per posta.*
5. *Me lo regala.*
6. *Ce lo racconta ogni giorno.*
7. *Devi dirglielo.* (or: *Glielo devi dire.*)
8. *Voglio regalartelo.* (or: *Te lo voglio regalare.*)
9. *Me ne puoi portare uno?* (or: *Puoi portarmene uno?*)
10. *Gliela mando a Natale.*
11. *Mi scrivi una lettera? — Sì, te la scrivo domani.*
12. *Dimmi la verità!* (or with both clitics: *Dimmela!*)
13. *Le dico sempre la verità.* (or: *Gliela dico sempre.*)
14. *Ci mostrano le loro foto.* (or: *Ce le mostrano.*)
15. *Posso prestarteli.* (or: *Te li posso prestare.*)
16. *Non mi dice niente.*
17. *Gli studenti fanno domande al professore — lui gliele risponde.* (or, more natural: *...lui risponde loro.*)
18. *Voglio leggertela.* (or: *Te la voglio leggere.*)
19. *Mi serve una spiegazione — me la dai?*
20. *Alla mamma piacciono i fiori — gliene porto spesso.*
21. *Te lo presto per una settimana.*
22. *Ve lo spieghiamo per l'ultima volta.*
23. *Quanti ne hai? — Te ne do cinque.*
24. *Gliel'hai già detto?* (or, for this lesson's tense — present: *Glielo dici?*)
25. *Devo dirglielo stasera.* (or: *Glielo devo dire stasera.*)
26. *Mostracela!* (or, simpler: *Ce la mostri?*)
27. *Te lo compro per il compleanno.* (or: *Te lo regalo per il compleanno.*)
28. *Non ti credono, devi dimostrarglielo.* (or: *...glielo devi dimostrare.*)
29. *Il regalo? Te lo do più tardi.*
30. *È un segreto. Te lo dico, ma a nessun altro.*
