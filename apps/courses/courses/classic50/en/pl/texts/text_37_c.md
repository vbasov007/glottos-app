# Tekst do lekcji 37: A conversation about a job change
**Temat: Dialog o zmianie pracy — meaning shifts, prepositions + -ing**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. So, you decided to leave your old job?
2. Yes, I gave up trying to make it work.
3. I was tired of waiting for a promotion.
4. I kept hoping things would change.
5. But after five years, I stopped believing in promises.
6. What made you finally decide to quit?
7. I remember sitting at my desk one Friday afternoon.
8. I admitted to myself that I hated going to work.
9. I tried to enjoy my projects, but I couldn't.
10. So I started looking for something else.
11. Did you find a new job before leaving?
12. Yes, I refused to leave without having something secure.
13. I'm not used to taking big risks.
14. I considered applying to many places.
15. I avoided telling anyone at work.
16. I'm good at keeping secrets when I need to.
17. Now I look forward to starting next month.
18. I plan to work harder than ever.
19. I want to prove that I made the right choice.
20. Do you regret staying so long at the old place?
21. Sometimes. But I learnt to manage difficult people there.
22. That skill is worth having, even if the rest was painful.
23. I'd never advise anyone to stay in a job they hate.
24. But I'd also never tell them to leave without a plan.
25. By preparing carefully, you avoid making things worse.
26. Are you afraid of failing in the new role?
27. A little. But I refuse to let fear stop me.
28. I prefer trying and failing to never trying at all.
29. I imagine looking back in ten years and feeling proud.
30. I can't wait to see what comes next.
