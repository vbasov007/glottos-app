# Tekst do lekcji 38: A dialogue about a difficult decision
**Temat: Spójniki w żywej dyskusji — dialog dwojga przyjaciół**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. So, have you decided whether you'll take the job in London?
2. Not yet. Although the salary is good, I'm worried about moving.
3. Why are you worried, since you've always wanted to live there?
4. Because of my parents. They're getting older, and they need help.
5. But your sister lives nearby, so they wouldn't be alone.
6. That's true. However, I feel responsible.
7. Even though your sister is there, you want to be near them too.
8. Exactly. While the job is a great opportunity, family comes first.
9. Have you talked to your parents about it?
10. Not yet, because I don't want to worry them before I decide.
11. I think you should talk to them so that they can give you their opinion.
12. You're right. Despite my doubts, they might surprise me.
13. They might tell you to take the job, since it's your future.
14. Yes, although they would never say it directly.
15. Once you talk to them, the decision will be easier.
16. As soon as I see them this weekend, I'll bring it up.
17. Good. By the time you have to give an answer, you'll know what to do.
18. The company wants an answer in two weeks.
19. That's plenty of time, even though it doesn't feel like it.
20. While I'm thinking, I keep imagining myself in London.
21. Do you imagine being happy?
22. Yes, but I also imagine missing everyone.
23. Because of modern technology, you can stay in touch easily.
24. That's true. Despite the distance, we could still see each other often.
25. And you can always visit, as often as you want.
26. The flights are cheap, so that wouldn't be a problem.
27. So the only real issue is your parents.
28. Yes. Until I know what they think, I can't really choose.
29. Whatever you decide, you have my support.
30. Thanks. As soon as I know, I'll call you.
