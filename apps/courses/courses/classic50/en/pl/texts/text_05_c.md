# Tekst do lekcji 5: Our weekend

**Temat: weekend naszej rodziny, różne osoby i przyzwyczajenia**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. On Saturdays we sleep late.
2. My father wakes up first.
3. He makes coffee for everybody.
4. My mother gets up at nine.
5. She reads the newspaper.
6. My brother and I get up at ten.
7. We eat breakfast together.
8. We talk about our plans.
9. After breakfast my father washes the car.
10. My mother goes shopping.
11. She buys food for the week.
12. My brother plays football with friends.
13. He runs in the park.
14. I study English at home.
15. I read books.
16. I write in my notebook.
17. At lunch we eat together.
18. My mother cooks soup.
19. We drink tea.
20. After lunch we watch a film.
21. My father loves old films.
22. My mother prefers new ones.
23. In the evening friends visit us.
24. They bring cake.
25. We eat cake and drink tea.
26. The children play games.
27. The parents talk and laugh.
28. We have a good time.
29. On Sundays we rest.
30. Then a new week starts.
