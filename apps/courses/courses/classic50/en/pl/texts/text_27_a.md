# Tekst do lekcji 27: A day in many tenses
**Temat: wszystkie 12 form w naturalnej narracji**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. My name is Mark and I work in a bookshop.
2. I have worked here for seven years.
3. Right now I am sitting in the back room.
4. I have been sorting new books all morning.
5. Yesterday was very busy.
6. I sold more than a hundred books in one day.
7. While I was helping one customer, three more came in.
8. By six in the evening, I had spoken to about fifty people.
9. I had been standing on my feet for hours.
10. Tomorrow will be quieter, I hope.
11. The shop opens at nine and closes at seven.
12. I am meeting my manager at three tomorrow.
13. We will discuss the holiday schedule.
14. At ten tomorrow I will be working in the children's section.
15. By Friday I will have finished the new display.
16. By the end of next month, I will have been working here for eight years.
17. I never imagined I would stay this long.
18. When I started, I was twenty-five years old.
19. I had just left university and didn't know what to do.
20. The owner gave me the job after a short interview.
21. He is now retired, but he visits us every Friday.
22. He has helped many young people start their careers.
23. The shop has been on this street since 1982.
24. Many things have changed, but the customers still love it.
25. Some of them have been coming here for decades.
26. One old lady has bought a book every week for forty years.
27. She tells me she has read over two thousand books from us.
28. By the time she stops, she will have visited us more than any other customer.
29. I love my job, and I am going to stay for many years.
30. Books are not just my work — they are my life.
