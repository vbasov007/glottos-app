# Tekst do lekcji 1: In the classroom

**Temat: lekcja angielskiego, trening dźwięków i liczb**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. Good morning, students.
2. Good morning, teacher.
3. Today we read the alphabet.
4. A, B, C, D, E, F, G.
5. H, I, J, K, L, M, N.
6. O, P, Q, R, S, T, U.
7. V, W, X, Y, Z.
8. Very good.
9. Now we read numbers.
10. One, two, three, four, five.
11. Six, seven, eight, nine, ten.
12. Eleven, twelve, thirteen.
13. Fourteen, fifteen, sixteen.
14. Seventeen, eighteen.
15. Nineteen, twenty.
16. Excellent.
17. Now read this word.
18. Think.
19. Three.
20. Thank you.
21. This.
22. That.
23. The book.
24. They.
25. Read this word again.
26. Cat.
27. Man.
28. Apple.
29. Thank you, students.
30. See you tomorrow. Goodbye.
