# Tekst do lekcji 4: Body and face

**Temat: opis wyglądu, części ciała**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. This is Anna.
2. She is my sister.
3. Her face is round.
4. Her eyes are blue.
5. Her nose is small.
6. Her mouth is wide.
7. Her hair is long and brown.
8. Her hands are small.
9. Her feet are small too.
10. She is short.
11. This is Tom.
12. He is my brother.
13. His face is long.
14. His eyes are brown.
15. His nose is big.
16. His hair is short and black.
17. His hands are big.
18. His feet are big too.
19. He is tall.
20. He is strong.
21. This is our grandfather.
22. His name is Peter.
23. His hair is grey.
24. His eyes are green.
25. He wears glasses.
26. His glasses are old.
27. His face is kind.
28. His hands are warm.
29. We love him very much.
30. He loves us too.
