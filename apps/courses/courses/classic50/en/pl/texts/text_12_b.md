# Tekst do lekcji 12: Past Simple
**Temat: Wyjazd nad morze zeszłego lata**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Last summer my family and I went on holiday.
2. We travelled to a small town by the sea.
3. The trip took about six hours by car.
4. My father drove, and my mother slept most of the way.
5. We arrived at our hotel in the late afternoon.
6. The hotel was small but very clean.
7. Our room had a beautiful view of the sea.
8. On the first evening, we had dinner in a fish restaurant.
9. The food was delicious and not very expensive.
10. After dinner we walked along the beach.
11. The sky was full of stars.
12. The next morning we got up early.
13. We had breakfast on the hotel terrace.
14. Then we spent the whole day at the beach.
15. The water was warm and very blue.
16. My sister and I swam for hours.
17. Our parents read books under a big umbrella.
18. In the afternoon we ate ice cream by the harbour.
19. On the third day we visited an old castle.
20. The castle stood on a high hill.
21. We climbed many steps to the top.
22. From the top we saw the whole town.
23. We took a lot of photos.
24. In the evening we went to a small market.
25. My mother bought a beautiful blue dress.
26. My father found a hat for the sun.
27. I got a book and my sister got a bracelet.
28. The week passed very quickly.
29. On the last day we said goodbye to the sea.
30. We came home tired but very happy.
