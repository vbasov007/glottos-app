# Tekst do lekcji 33: Looking back
**Temat: Żale dotyczące przeszłości — I wish + Past Perfect**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. I wish I had studied harder at school.
2. I wish I had learned a foreign language as a child.
3. I wish I had travelled more in my twenties.
4. I wish I had said yes to that job offer.
5. I wish I hadn't lost touch with my old friends.
6. I wish I had told her how I felt.
7. I wish we had bought that flat ten years ago.
8. I wish I hadn't wasted so much time on social media.
9. If only I had listened to my parents' advice!
10. If only I had taken better care of my health.
11. I wish I had started saving money earlier.
12. I wish I hadn't argued with him last week.
13. I wish I had read more books in school.
14. I wish we had taken that trip together.
15. I wish I hadn't quit playing music as a teenager.
16. If only I had been braver back then!
17. I wish I had written more often to my grandparents.
18. I wish I had visited my old teacher before he passed away.
19. I wish I had thanked her properly.
20. I wish I had not believed those lies.
21. I wish we had moved to the countryside sooner.
22. I wish I had finished the course.
23. If only I had asked for help earlier!
24. I wish I had been more patient with my children.
25. I wish I had taken photographs of those years.
26. I wish I had told my father I was proud of him.
27. I wish we had not sold the family house.
28. I wish I had eaten less and slept more.
29. I wish I had said goodbye properly.
30. If only I had known then what I know now!
