# Tekst do lekcji 29: What really matters
**Temat: rodzajniki z rzeczownikami abstrakcyjnymi**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. People often ask what truly matters in life.
2. Money is useful, but love is more important.
3. Health is the foundation of everything else.
4. Without health, even the greatest wealth becomes useless.
5. Knowledge is power, as an old English saying tells us.
6. Education changes lives in ways we don't always see.
7. Children who grow up surrounded by books develop strong minds.
8. The love that parents give to their children shapes their entire future.
9. Patience is a quality that very few people are born with.
10. Most of us have to learn it slowly, through difficult experience.
11. Freedom means different things to different people.
12. For some it is freedom to travel and explore the world.
13. For others it is freedom from fear and worry.
14. The freedom we enjoy today was won by previous generations.
15. We should never take it for granted.
16. Happiness is harder to define than people imagine.
17. The happiness that comes from owning things rarely lasts.
18. The happiness that comes from kindness lasts much longer.
19. Time is perhaps our most valuable possession.
20. You cannot buy time, you cannot save it, you cannot return it.
21. The time we spend with the people we love is never wasted.
22. Truth is sometimes painful, but always preferable to comfortable lies.
23. The truth we discover about ourselves shapes who we become.
24. Beauty exists everywhere, but only those who look for it find it.
25. The beauty of a quiet morning is as real as the beauty of art.
26. Kindness costs nothing, yet it is the rarest gift.
27. A simple act of kindness can change someone's whole day.
28. Justice, peace, and respect are not luxuries.
29. They are the basic conditions of a good society.
30. The life we live is the life we choose, one small decision at a time.
