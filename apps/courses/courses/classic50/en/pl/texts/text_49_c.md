# Tekst C do lekcji 49: Życie cyfrowe — email, czat, social media
**Temat: Współczesny angielski w mailach, komunikatorach i social media**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Dear Mr. Johnson, I hope this email finds you well.
2. I am writing to enquire about the marketing position you advertised.
3. Please find my CV and cover letter attached for your review.
4. I have over five years of experience in digital marketing.
5. I look forward to hearing from you at your earliest convenience.
6. Best regards, Anna Volkova.
7. Hey Tom, are you free for a quick video call later?
8. Just wanted to check in about the project deadline.
9. Btw, did you see the email I forwarded yesterday?
10. The attachment was huge, sorry about that.
11. Lol no worries, my inbox can handle it.
12. So tbh I think we need to change our approach.
13. Imo the current plan is too risky and too expensive.
14. Fyi I already talked to the team about it this morning.
15. They agreed we should meet asap to discuss options.
16. Are you available tomorrow or Wednesday morning?
17. Np, Wednesday works better for me actually.
18. Cool, I'll send a calendar invite to everyone.
19. Thanks for being so flexible, really appreciate it.
20. Ty too, see u at the meeting!
21. Just posted a new photo from our trip to Greece.
22. Got over a hundred likes in the first hour, crazy.
23. Some friends commented with their own travel stories.
24. The hashtag I used was trending in our group.
25. I sent her a direct message to follow up on her comment.
26. The whole group chat went wild over the wedding photos.
27. Click the link in my bio to subscribe to my channel.
28. Don't forget to like and share if you found this helpful.
29. Hit the bell icon for notifications about new posts.
30. Thanks for following, you guys are the absolute best.
