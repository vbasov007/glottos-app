# Tekst do lekcji 26: Tomorrow's plans
**Temat: ustalenia, harmonogramy, prognozy — podstawowy zestaw**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Tomorrow is going to be a very busy day for me.
2. The alarm rings at six in the morning.
3. I am meeting my friend Anna for breakfast at seven.
4. The cafe opens at half past six, so we have plenty of time.
5. After breakfast I am going to take the bus to the office.
6. The bus leaves the station every ten minutes.
7. At nine I have a meeting with my boss.
8. We will discuss the new project for about two hours.
9. By eleven I will have finished all my morning tasks.
10. At twelve I am having lunch with two colleagues.
11. We are trying a new restaurant in the city centre.
12. After lunch I will be working on the report until five.
13. I think it will rain in the afternoon.
14. I am taking an umbrella, just in case.
15. The train back home leaves at six fifteen.
16. By seven I will be sitting at home with a cup of tea.
17. My wife is coming home a little later tomorrow.
18. She is meeting her sister after work.
19. They are going to a small concert at eight.
20. The concert starts at half past eight and ends at ten.
21. I am going to cook dinner while she is out.
22. By the time she gets home, the food will be ready.
23. We will probably watch a film together.
24. On Saturday we are visiting my parents.
25. We are taking the early train at eight o'clock.
26. My mother is making her famous apple pie.
27. By the end of the weekend, we will have done all our errands.
28. Next week is going to be much quieter.
29. I am not making any plans for Sunday.
30. I will just rest and read a good book.
