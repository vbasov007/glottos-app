# Tekst do lekcji 42: At the office — business phrasal verbs in action
**Temat: tydzień pracy — spotkania, projekty, negocjacje**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. On Monday we set up a new project team.
2. The CEO put forward an ambitious plan.
3. Some board members brought up serious concerns.
4. They asked us to look into the budget first.
5. We carried out a quick review on Tuesday.
6. We found out that costs were too high.
7. The team had to come up with a new approach.
8. By Wednesday we put off the launch by two months.
9. The marketing director was not happy at all.
10. She turned down our proposal twice.
11. In the end, we backed up the engineering team.
12. We called off the press event for now.
13. On Thursday, the head of sales stepped down.
14. The company announced new leadership the same day.
15. Two senior managers took over her duties.
16. They got along surprisingly well with the team.
17. Some staff were worried about being laid off.
18. The CEO promised that no one would lose their job.
19. He said the company was setting up new branches.
20. He wanted to roll out the plan by spring.
21. We carried on with our work as usual.
22. The IT team backed up all our project files.
23. We logged in to the new system on Friday morning.
24. We had to sort out a few technical issues.
25. The new software booted up much faster.
26. By lunchtime, everything was up and running.
27. We went over the week's progress at four.
28. Most goals were carried out on time.
29. We agreed to meet again next Monday.
30. After work, the team went out for drinks.
