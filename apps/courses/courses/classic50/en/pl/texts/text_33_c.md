# Tekst do lekcji 33: A conversation full of regrets
**Temat: Wish, if only, would rather, it's time — w żywym dialogu**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. I wish I had taken that job in London when they offered it.
2. Really? Why didn't you?
3. I was afraid of moving. If only I had been braver back then.
4. Well, it's not too late. It's high time you tried something new.
5. I'd rather stay where I am. My family is here.
6. I understand. But I wish you would talk to me before deciding.
7. I always do. I wish I had your kind of courage.
8. You have your own kind. If only you could see it!
9. Sometimes I wish life were simpler.
10. So do I. If life were simpler, we wouldn't appreciate the good days.
11. True. I wish I had said that to my brother last year.
12. What happened with your brother?
13. We argued, and I haven't seen him since. I wish I hadn't said those things.
14. Why don't you call him? It's about time you two made up.
15. I'd rather he called me first.
16. Pride. I wish people would just talk instead of waiting.
17. You're right. If only I weren't so stubborn.
18. Hey, none of us is perfect. I wish I had been a better friend to him too.
19. Don't blame yourself. If I were you, I would call him today.
20. Maybe I will. I wish my phone would stop ringing — every call feels like pressure.
21. Take a deep breath. It's time you stopped carrying all this alone.
22. Thank you. I wish all my friends were like you.
23. I'm just a normal friend. If only I could help more.
24. You do help. I wish I had told you that more often.
25. Now you have. So, are you calling him?
26. Yes. If I don't call now, I'll regret it tomorrow.
27. Good. If you had called a year ago, things would already be fine.
28. I know. I wish I had picked up the phone then.
29. The phone is still here. Pick it up now.
30. Okay. I'm calling. If only it weren't so hard to say sorry.
