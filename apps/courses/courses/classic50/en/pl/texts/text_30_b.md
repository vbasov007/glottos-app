# Tekst do lekcji 30: My career so far
**Temat: spójny wieloakapitowy tekst — biografia**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. My career has been long and full of unexpected turns.
2. After I finished school, I went to university to study chemistry.
3. At that time, I wanted to become a researcher.
4. The professor who taught us organic chemistry was the most inspiring teacher I had ever met.
5. He had worked in three different countries before he came to our university.
6. The stories he told us made science seem like a great adventure.
7. However, by the end of my second year, I had started to doubt my choice.
8. I realised that I preferred working with people rather than with substances.
9. Therefore, I decided to change my path.
10. I left the chemistry programme and started studying economics.
11. The transition was difficult, but I never regretted it.
12. While I was studying economics, I worked part-time in a small bookshop.
13. The owner, whose name was Mrs Petersen, became a kind of second mother to me.
14. She had run the shop for over thirty years.
15. From her I learned more about business than from any textbook.
16. After I graduated, I joined a small consulting firm in the city centre.
17. The firm, which had only ten employees at that time, has grown enormously since then.
18. I have worked there for eleven years.
19. In addition, I have travelled to twenty countries on business.
20. Some of the projects I have led were extremely challenging.
21. For example, last year we helped a struggling factory in the north of the country.
22. The factory, which employs over five hundred people, was almost bankrupt when we arrived.
23. By the time we finished our work, the company had returned to profit.
24. Moreover, the staff had developed new skills that will serve them for years.
25. This year I am leading a team of twelve consultants.
26. We are working on a complex project for a national bank.
27. By the end of next month, we will have presented our final report.
28. After that, I am going to take a long holiday.
29. I plan to travel through Asia for six weeks.
30. In short, my career has taught me one thing: never be afraid to change direction.
