# Tekst do lekcji 45: An office chat — neutral register at work
**Temat: roboczy czat między kolegami, rejestr neutralny**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Hi Sara, do you have a minute to chat?
2. Sure, what's up?
3. I wanted to ask about the new client proposal.
4. Did you get a chance to read it?
5. Yes, I went through it this morning.
6. What did you think of the budget section?
7. Honestly, it looks a bit tight to me.
8. We might need to revise the numbers before sending it.
9. I agree, let's go over it together this afternoon.
10. Could we meet around three in the small room?
11. That works for me. I'll bring my laptop and notes.
12. Do you think we should ask Tom to join us?
13. Good idea. He knows the technical side better than anyone.
14. I'll send him a quick message now.
15. Thanks. Anything else you want to cover?
16. Yes, the timeline. The deadline seems too aggressive.
17. We should bring this up at the team meeting tomorrow.
18. Are you presenting the slides yourself?
19. Yes, I prepared a short version with key points only.
20. That sounds great. Less is more in these situations.
21. By the way, did the IT issue from last week get sorted?
22. Mostly. They installed a new tool, and everything's faster now.
23. Good to hear. We were losing a lot of time on small problems.
24. Speaking of time, I should get back to my emails.
25. Same here. See you at three then.
26. Yes, see you. Thanks for taking the time to chat.
27. No problem, anytime. Let me know if you need anything else.
28. Will do. Have a good rest of the morning.
29. Thanks, you too. Talk soon.
30. Bye for now.
