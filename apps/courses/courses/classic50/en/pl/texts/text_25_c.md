# Tekst do lekcji 25: Past Perfect
**Temat: Poranek po włamaniu — sekwencja wydarzeń**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. When Maria came home on Sunday evening, she felt that something was wrong.
2. The lights in the kitchen were on.
3. She had turned them off before she left.
4. The front door was not locked.
5. She had locked it that morning.
6. She walked slowly into the living room.
7. Someone had been there during the day.
8. The drawers were open.
9. Papers were on the floor.
10. Her laptop had disappeared from the table.
11. She had bought it only two months earlier.
12. Her grandmother's ring was missing too.
13. She had kept it in a small box in the bedroom.
14. The box was open and empty.
15. Maria felt cold and shaky.
16. She had never been a victim of a crime before.
17. She sat down on the sofa and called the police.
18. They arrived within twenty minutes.
19. By then she had already checked every room.
20. The thieves had broken a back window.
21. They had climbed in through the garden.
22. The neighbours had not seen anything.
23. They had been at a family party all afternoon.
24. The police took notes and photos.
25. They told Maria that other houses on the street had been robbed too.
26. The thieves had probably planned everything carefully.
27. After the police had left, Maria called her mother.
28. Her mother said she had warned her about that lock.
29. By the end of the week, Maria had installed a new alarm system.
30. She had also bought a small dog who barked at every sound.
