# Tekst do lekcji 7: Rodzajniki — pierwsze podejście
**Temat: Miejsca w mieście**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. I live in a city.
2. The city is big and beautiful.
3. There is a park near my house.
4. The park is green and quiet.
5. I walk in the park every morning.
6. There is also a school on my street.
7. The school is old but nice.
8. My sister studies in this school.
9. Near the school there is a small shop.
10. The shop sells bread, milk and fruit.
11. I buy an apple and a bottle of water there.
12. On the next street there is a bank.
13. The bank opens at nine in the morning.
14. There is a hospital in our city too.
15. The hospital is very big.
16. My mother works in a hospital.
17. She is a doctor.
18. We have a good cafe near the station.
19. The cafe is small but cosy.
20. I love coffee from this cafe.
21. There is a hotel in the centre.
22. The hotel is new and modern.
23. Many tourists stay in this hotel.
24. I like my city.
25. People here are kind.
26. Friends are important.
27. I have a friend, Mark.
28. Mark works in a restaurant.
29. The restaurant is on Green Street.
30. We meet there every Friday evening.
