# Tekst do lekcji 38: A small business in trouble
**Temat: Przyczyna, cel i przyzwolenie w narracji biznesowej**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Maria opened her café three years ago.
2. Although she had no experience, she worked hard.
3. Because of her hard work, the café became popular.
4. In order to keep customers, she added new dishes every month.
5. While business was good, she saved every spare penny.
6. However, last winter everything changed.
7. Due to rising costs, her profit dropped by half.
8. Despite cutting expenses, she still struggled to pay rent.
9. Since the situation was serious, she asked a friend for advice.
10. Her friend suggested raising prices so that she could cover costs.
11. Although Maria didn't want to lose regular customers, she had to try.
12. She raised the prices by ten percent so that the café could survive.
13. Some customers stopped coming because of the new prices.
14. However, most people stayed loyal.
15. By the time spring arrived, the café was stable again.
16. Maria worked even harder than before, in order to keep things going.
17. She offered weekend specials so that families would visit on Sunday.
18. Even though she was exhausted, she felt grateful.
19. As soon as summer came, tourists started arriving.
20. Because tourists order more, profit grew quickly.
21. Maria hired a young woman so that she could finally rest a little.
22. Despite all the worries, the café survived.
23. While many other shops in the street closed, hers stayed open.
24. As the year went on, she gained new confidence.
25. Now she thinks about expanding, although the risks are high.
26. She wants to open a second location so that her business can grow.
27. Even though she's nervous, she's also excited.
28. Whenever she walks into the café, she remembers the hard times.
29. Despite the long hours, she's never regretted starting her own business.
30. By the time she's fifty, she hopes to own three cafés in the city.
