## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Moscow is bigger than Saint Petersburg.
2. London is one of the most expensive cities in Europe.
3. New York is more crowded than Boston.
4. Tokyo is the largest city in Japan.
5. Paris is more romantic than Berlin, in my opinion.
6. Rome is older than London.
7. The summer in Spain is hotter than in Germany.
8. The winter in Norway is colder than in Italy.
9. Russia is the biggest country in the world.
10. Canada is larger than the United States.
11. Australia is smaller than Russia, but it's bigger than India.
12. The Pacific Ocean is the largest ocean on Earth.
13. The Sahara is the biggest desert in the world.
14. Mount Everest is higher than any other mountain.
15. The Amazon is longer than the Mississippi.
16. The weather today is better than yesterday.
17. Winter is the coldest season in this country.
18. Summer is usually the hottest season.
19. Autumn is more beautiful than spring, I think.
20. The mountains are more beautiful in summer.
21. This beach is as nice as the one we visited last year.
22. Greek food is as tasty as Italian food.
23. French wine is more famous than Argentine wine.
24. Chinese is more difficult than Spanish for many people.
25. English is the most popular foreign language in the world.
26. Switzerland is one of the richest countries in Europe.
27. India has more people than the United States.
28. China is the most populated country in the world.
29. The more you travel, the more you learn.
30. The best things in life are free.
