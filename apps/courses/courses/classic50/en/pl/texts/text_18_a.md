# Tekst do lekcji 18: Przyimki czasu i miejsca
**Temat: Rozkład dnia i miejsca w mieście**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. I wake up at seven o'clock every morning.
2. In the morning I drink coffee in the kitchen.
3. I leave home at half past eight.
4. I go to work by bus or on the metro.
5. My office is on the third floor.
6. I work from nine to six on weekdays.
7. At lunchtime I eat in a small cafe near the office.
8. In the afternoon I have many meetings.
9. I come home in the evening, usually at seven.
10. At night I read a book in bed.
11. On Monday I have a class at the university.
12. On Tuesday evening I go to the gym.
13. On Friday we sometimes go to a restaurant.
14. At the weekend I rest at home.
15. In summer we go to the countryside.
16. In winter I stay at home in the evenings.
17. My birthday is on the 15th of March.
18. We celebrate Christmas at home.
19. On New Year's Day we visit friends.
20. The shop is on the corner of my street.
21. The bus stop is in front of the bank.
22. The park is between the river and the school.
23. The bookshop is next to the cafe.
24. My keys are on the table near the door.
25. The cat is sleeping under the bed.
26. The picture is on the wall above the sofa.
27. I live in Poland, in Warsaw, on Marszałkowska Street.
28. My friend lives in a small town in Italy.
29. At the moment I am at home.
30. In two hours I will be at the airport.
