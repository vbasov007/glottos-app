# Tekst do lekcji 28: The old house
**Temat: opowiadanie z aktywnym użyciem where, whose, which**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. My grandmother lives in an old house that her parents built in 1930.
2. The house, which has six rooms, stands on a quiet street.
3. The street, where almost every family has lived for generations, is full of stories.
4. My grandmother, whose memory is still excellent, knows them all.
5. The neighbours who live opposite are her oldest friends.
6. Their son, who became a doctor, was once my best friend.
7. We used to play in the garden that my grandfather planted years ago.
8. The garden, which is full of fruit trees, gives apples every autumn.
9. The apples that grow here have a special taste.
10. My grandmother makes a pie that everyone in the family loves.
11. The kitchen where she cooks is small but full of light.
12. She has a wooden table that she bought at a market in 1965.
13. The table, which is now over fifty years old, is still strong.
14. On the wall there is a clock whose face has lost two numbers.
15. The clock, which my grandfather repaired many times, still works.
16. In the living room there is a piano that nobody plays now.
17. My mother, who learned music as a child, used to play every evening.
18. The book that lies on top of the piano belonged to her teacher.
19. The teacher, whose name was Mrs Petrov, taught three generations of children.
20. The bedroom upstairs is the room where I always sleep when I visit.
21. From the window, which looks east, I can see the river.
22. The river, where I learned to swim as a boy, is the same as ever.
23. The old bridge that crosses the river was built in 1908.
24. People who pass it every day rarely look at it twice.
25. My grandmother, however, remembers the day when it opened.
26. She tells me stories that I have heard many times.
27. But each time she finds details that I never noticed before.
28. The world that she describes seems both very close and very far.
29. One day, this house, which holds so many memories, will be ours.
30. And I will try to keep the spirit that has lived here for almost a century.
