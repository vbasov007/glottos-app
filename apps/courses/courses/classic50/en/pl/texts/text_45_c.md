# Tekst do lekcji 45: Friends catching up — informal register

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Hey! Long time no see! How are you?
2. Doing okay, thanks. You?
3. Pretty good, just super busy these days.
4. Yeah, I bet. How's the new job going?
5. Honestly, it's been a bit of a rollercoaster.
6. The team's nice, but the workload is crazy.
7. Tell me about it. I'm in the same boat.
8. We just kicked off a big project last week.
9. Already running on fumes, to be honest.
10. Want a coffee? My treat this time.
11. Sure, thanks! Just a flat white, please.
12. So, what's new with you outside of work?
13. Well, I finally got a dog last month.
14. No way! What kind?
15. A small mutt from the shelter. He's adorable.
16. Aww, that's awesome. Got pics?
17. Tons. Hold on, let me find some.
18. Look at that face! He's so cute.
19. He keeps me on my toes, that's for sure.
20. Hey, did you hear about Sam and Lisa?
21. No, what happened?
22. They got married last weekend in a tiny ceremony.
23. Wow, that was fast! Good for them.
24. I know, right? Everyone's been over the moon for them.
25. We should all grab a drink soon to celebrate.
26. For sure. Let's pick a date in the next week or two.
27. Sounds good. I'll ping you tomorrow with options.
28. Cool. Hey, I should probably head out — running late.
29. Same. It was so good to catch up. Don't be a stranger!
30. Never! Talk to you soon. Bye!
