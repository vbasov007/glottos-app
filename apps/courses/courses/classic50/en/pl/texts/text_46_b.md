# Tekst B do lekcji 46: Prefiksy w działaniu
**Temat: Idee, opinie i opisy przez un-, dis-, mis-, re-, over-, under-**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Honestly, I disagree with the new approach.
2. The system is unfair and needs to be changed.
3. They completely misunderstood my intentions.
4. The plan is unrealistic and probably impossible.
5. We need to rewrite the entire chapter again.
6. He is irresponsible and unreliable at work.
7. The decision was illegal and quickly overturned.
8. I dislike this style of writing.
9. Many people overestimate their own skills.
10. Others underestimate how hard the work really is.
11. The information was inaccurate and misleading.
12. Their cooperation is essential for the project.
13. Please reconsider your position before tomorrow.
14. He is non-political and prefers not to take sides.
15. The instructions were unclear, so we made mistakes.
16. She decided to rebuild the small house from scratch.
17. We need to prepay the deposit before the meeting.
18. His behaviour was impolite, but everyone forgave him.
19. The new policy is highly unpopular among workers.
20. He is my ex-partner, but we are still friends.
21. The actor gave an unforgettable performance.
22. Their disagreement turned into a serious argument.
23. I had to redo the homework because of a small error.
24. The film is unsuitable for young children.
25. They overslept and missed the early train.
26. This software is incompatible with my old computer.
27. The argument was illogical and easy to disprove.
28. His attitude is increasingly negative and disrespectful.
29. The new manager is preparing a major reorganisation.
30. Our previous results were uncertain and inconsistent.
