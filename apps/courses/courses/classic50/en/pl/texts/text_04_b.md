# Tekst do lekcji 4: Whose is this?

**Temat: czyje rzeczy, w pracy i w domu**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. Whose bag is this?
2. It is my bag.
3. And this one?
4. That is Anna's bag.
5. Anna's bag is brown.
6. My bag is black.
7. Whose phone is on the table?
8. It is Tom's phone.
9. Where is your phone?
10. My phone is in my bag.
11. The phone on the chair is hers.
12. Whose keys are these?
13. Those are my keys.
14. The keys on the desk are Tom's.
15. Tom's keys are old.
16. My keys are new.
17. Whose computer is this?
18. It is our computer.
19. We share it.
20. Whose car is in the street?
21. The red car is my brother's.
22. His car is fast.
23. The blue car is my sister's.
24. Her car is small.
25. Whose dog is this?
26. The dog is mine.
27. Its name is Rex.
28. Rex is friendly.
29. He likes everybody.
30. Everybody likes him.
