# Tekst do lekcji 15: Przyszłość — will i be going to
**Temat: Plany na następne lato**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Next summer is going to be busy for our family.
2. My wife and I are going to take three weeks off work.
3. We are going to travel around Europe by train.
4. First, we are going to fly to Madrid.
5. We are going to spend a few days there with old friends.
6. Then we will take the train to Barcelona.
7. From Barcelona we are going to go to the south of France.
8. We are going to stay in a small village by the sea.
9. I think we will rent a house with a small garden.
10. Our children are going to love the beach.
11. They will probably swim every day.
12. My son is going to take his football with him.
13. He will play in the sand with his sister.
14. After France we are going to take a long train to Italy.
15. In Rome we will visit the old city centre.
16. I'm sure the children will like the ice cream there.
17. We are not going to stay in Rome for long.
18. Next we are going to drive to a small town in Tuscany.
19. My wife and I will cook simple meals together.
20. We will probably drink local wine in the evenings.
21. The children are going to make new friends.
22. On the way home, we are going to stop in Switzerland.
23. We will see the mountains and breathe clean air.
24. I think the trip will be unforgettable.
25. It won't be cheap, but it will be worth it.
26. We will take a lot of photos and videos.
27. When we come back, we are going to print the best photos.
28. We will hang them on the wall in the living room.
29. The summer will end, but the memories will stay.
30. Next year, I'm sure, we'll plan another long trip.
