# Tekst do lekcji 26: Planning the wedding
**Temat: rozbudowany dialog — ustalenia, prognozy, harmonogramy**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. So, the wedding is in June, on the twentieth.
2. We have already booked the church and the restaurant.
3. The ceremony begins at one in the afternoon.
4. My parents are flying in from Boston on the eighteenth.
5. Their flight arrives at three in the afternoon.
6. I am picking them up from the airport.
7. By Friday evening, all the guests will have arrived.
8. On Friday night we are having a small dinner for the family.
9. The dinner starts at seven at my parents' house.
10. On Saturday morning I will be running around like crazy.
11. I am collecting the flowers at nine.
12. The photographer is meeting us at the church at twelve.
13. After the ceremony, the bus will take everyone to the restaurant.
14. The reception starts at three thirty and goes on until late.
15. I think we will probably dance until midnight.
16. My grandmother is going to give a speech.
17. She has been practising it for weeks.
18. The cake arrives at the restaurant at five.
19. By six, all the speeches will have finished.
20. We are going on our honeymoon two days later.
21. The plane to Greece leaves on Monday afternoon.
22. We will be staying on a small island for ten days.
23. By the end of June, we will have visited five different beaches.
24. I am going to take hundreds of photos.
25. When we come back, I will share them with everyone.
26. My sister is taking care of our cat while we are away.
27. She is staying at our flat for the whole period.
28. The cat is going to miss us, but he will be fine.
29. The next big event is in October, my brother's wedding.
30. By that time, we will have already settled into married life.
