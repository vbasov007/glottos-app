# Tekst do lekcji 17: Stopień wyższy i najwyższy
**Temat: Dialog w sklepie i wybór**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Excuse me, which laptop is better?
2. The black one is faster and more reliable.
3. But the silver one is cheaper.
4. Yes, but it's also slower.
5. How much is the most expensive model?
6. The most expensive model costs three thousand dollars.
7. That's too much for me. Do you have a cheaper one?
8. We have a smaller model for nine hundred dollars.
9. Is it as good as the big one?
10. It's not as fast, but it's good for daily work.
11. Which color is more popular?
12. The black one is more popular than the silver one.
13. Can I see your best phone?
14. Of course. This is the newest model.
15. It has a bigger screen than the old version.
16. The camera is much better, too.
17. Is the battery as good as on my old phone?
18. The battery is better. It lasts longer.
19. This phone is more expensive than I expected.
20. We have older models if you want something cheaper.
21. Show me the smallest one, please.
22. This is the lightest phone in the shop.
23. It's also the easiest to use.
24. I will take this one. It's the best for me.
25. Would you like to see our cheapest accessories?
26. Yes, I need a case and a charger.
27. The black case is more elegant than the red one.
28. But the red case is stronger.
29. I will take the stronger one, please.
30. Thank you, this is the best service in the city.
