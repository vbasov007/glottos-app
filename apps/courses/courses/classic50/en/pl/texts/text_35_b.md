# Tekst do lekcji 35: Asking and telling
**Temat: Pytania i polecenia zależne**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. He asked me where I lived.
2. She asked me what time the meeting started.
3. The taxi driver asked me where I wanted to go.
4. He wanted to know if I spoke English.
5. She asked whether I had ever been to Spain.
6. The teacher asked us why we were late.
7. He asked me how long I had been waiting.
8. She wondered if the shop was still open.
9. He asked me whether I could help him.
10. The waiter asked if we were ready to order.
11. My mother told me to clean my room.
12. The teacher told the children to be quiet.
13. He asked me to lend him my book.
14. The doctor told her to take the medicine twice a day.
15. The police officer told us not to park there.
16. She begged him not to leave.
17. He invited me to come to his party.
18. The guide told us to follow him.
19. She advised me to see a specialist.
20. He warned us not to swim in that lake.
21. The boss told everyone to stay late on Friday.
22. My friend asked me to keep a secret.
23. The librarian told us to speak softly.
24. He encouraged her to apply for the job.
25. The instructor told us to fasten our seat belts.
26. She asked her son to do his homework.
27. He reminded me to bring my passport.
28. The receptionist asked us to wait a moment.
29. The pilot told the passengers to remain seated.
30. The judge ordered him to pay a fine.
