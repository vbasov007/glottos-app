# Tekst do lekcji 37: My morning routine
**Temat: Czasowniki z -ing i to + Inf — proste codzienne czynności**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. I enjoy starting my day slowly.
2. I usually wake up at seven o'clock.
3. I try to get out of bed straight away.
4. Sometimes I keep lying there for a few minutes.
5. I like to drink a glass of water first.
6. Then I go to the kitchen and start making coffee.
7. I love drinking coffee in the morning.
8. While the coffee is brewing, I plan to check the news.
9. I avoid looking at my phone too long.
10. After finishing my coffee, I get ready for work.
11. I have to remember to take my keys.
12. I sometimes forget to bring my lunch.
13. I'm trying to eat more vegetables this year.
14. I gave up eating sugar last month.
15. It was hard, but I don't regret making that choice.
16. Before leaving, I make sure all the lights are off.
17. I always look forward to walking to work.
18. I prefer walking to taking the bus.
19. By walking every day, I stay healthy.
20. I sometimes stop to look at the river on the way.
21. I enjoy seeing the city wake up.
22. I admit being a morning person.
23. My friends can't stand getting up early.
24. They prefer staying in bed.
25. At the office, I begin working at nine.
26. I always try to finish my main tasks before noon.
27. After working hard, I deserve a good lunch.
28. I'm thinking about leaving early today.
29. I hope to see my parents tonight.
30. I can't wait to tell them my news.
