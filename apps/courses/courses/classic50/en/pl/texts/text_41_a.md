# Tekst do lekcji 41: A normal morning — phrasal verbs in everyday life
**Temat: zwykły poranek — get up, put on, turn on, take off, go out**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. I get up at seven every morning.
2. I turn on the light and go to the kitchen.
3. I put on my slippers and make coffee.
4. My partner gets up a bit later.
5. We sit down and have breakfast together.
6. After breakfast, I put on my coat.
7. I pick up my keys from the table.
8. I go out and walk to the bus stop.
9. The bus comes at half past seven.
10. I get on the bus and find a seat.
11. I get off at the third stop.
12. I walk into the office at eight.
13. I take off my coat and sit down.
14. I turn on my computer and start work.
15. At noon I go out for lunch.
16. I run into a colleague in the cafe.
17. We chat and catch up on the news.
18. In the afternoon, I go over my notes.
19. I find out that the meeting is delayed.
20. We put off the meeting until tomorrow.
21. At five, I shut down my computer.
22. I put on my coat and leave the office.
23. I get on the bus and head home.
24. At home, I take off my shoes.
25. I put away my bag and keys.
26. I turn on some music and relax.
27. My partner comes back from work too.
28. We sit down for dinner.
29. After dinner, we go through the day.
30. By eleven, we both go to bed.
