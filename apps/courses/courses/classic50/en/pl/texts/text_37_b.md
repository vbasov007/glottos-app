# Tekst do lekcji 37: Learning to play the guitar
**Temat: Decyzje, próby, umiejętności — -ing i to + Inf w działaniu**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Last year I decided to learn to play the guitar.
2. I had always wanted to play, but I kept finding excuses.
3. I told myself I was too busy to start.
4. Then a friend suggested taking lessons together.
5. I agreed to try for three months.
6. We started practising twice a week.
7. At first, I hated holding the guitar.
8. My fingers refused to move the right way.
9. I tried pressing the strings harder.
10. I tried to play simple songs, but they sounded terrible.
11. I thought about giving up after the second week.
12. But my friend kept saying I just needed time.
13. After practising for a month, I began to enjoy it.
14. I learnt to play a few basic chords.
15. I remembered enjoying music more than I thought.
16. I stopped looking at my fingers all the time.
17. I started feeling the music instead.
18. Now I look forward to practising every evening.
19. I can't imagine giving up the guitar now.
20. I'm thinking about joining a small band.
21. They are looking for someone who is good at playing rhythm.
22. I'd love to perform in front of people, even if it's scary.
23. I plan to practise harder before the audition.
24. I expect to make many mistakes, and that's fine.
25. I refuse to be afraid of failing.
26. My teacher advised me to record myself.
27. By listening to the recording, I can hear my own mistakes.
28. I regret not starting earlier.
29. But I don't regret deciding to try at all.
30. Learning something new always makes life richer.
