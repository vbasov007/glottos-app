# Tekst do lekcji 6: Pytania i przeczenia przez do/does
**Temat: Dialog — dwoje przyjaciół planuje tydzień**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. Hi Mark! How are you today?
2. Hi Anna! I am fine, thank you. And you?
3. I am good. What do you do on Monday evening?
4. On Monday I usually study at home. Why do you ask?
5. Do you want to go to the cinema on Tuesday?
6. Yes, I do. What film do you want to watch?
7. I don't know. What do you like?
8. I like comedies. I don't like horror films.
9. Why don't you like horror films?
10. Because I don't sleep well after them.
11. Where do we meet on Tuesday?
12. We meet near the cinema at seven.
13. Does your brother come too?
14. No, he doesn't. He works in the evening.
15. What time does he finish work?
16. He usually finishes at ten.
17. Do you have free time on Wednesday?
18. No, I don't. I have an English lesson.
19. When does your lesson start?
20. It starts at six and ends at eight.
21. Why do you learn English?
22. Because I want to travel. I don't speak it well now.
23. How many lessons do you have every week?
24. I have two lessons, on Wednesday and on Friday.
25. Does your teacher speak Russian?
26. A little, but in class we only speak English.
27. What do you do on the weekend?
28. On Saturday I see my friends, on Sunday I read at home.
29. Do you want to come to my place on Saturday?
30. Yes, I do! See you on Tuesday at the cinema. Bye!
