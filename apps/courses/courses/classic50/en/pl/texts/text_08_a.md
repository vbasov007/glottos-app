# Tekst do lekcji 8: Liczba mnoga i policzalne vs niepoliczalne
**Temat: Na targu**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. I go to the market every Saturday morning.
2. The market is big and full of people.
3. I buy fresh fruit and vegetables there.
4. I usually buy apples, oranges and bananas.
5. Today I want six apples and four oranges.
6. The apples are red, the oranges are sweet.
7. I also buy two kilos of tomatoes.
8. Tomatoes are good for salad.
9. I need bread for the family.
10. I buy a loaf of brown bread.
11. I also need rice and sugar.
12. I buy a kilo of rice and a packet of sugar.
13. My children love milk.
14. So I buy two bottles of milk every week.
15. We drink a lot of water too.
16. I take three big bottles of water home.
17. My husband loves cheese.
18. I buy a piece of yellow cheese for him.
19. The cheese is fresh and tasty.
20. Sometimes I buy fish too.
21. The fish at this market is always fresh.
22. I don't buy meat here, I prefer the shop near my house.
23. There are many people at the market today.
24. Some men sell vegetables, some women sell flowers.
25. Children play near the fruit stalls.
26. They eat apples and laugh.
27. I love these moments.
28. My bag is now full and heavy.
29. I have eggs, bread, rice, fruit and cheese.
30. I go home happy.
