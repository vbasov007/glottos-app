# Tekst do lekcji 44: A startup story — idiomy pracy i pieniędzy
**Temat: opowieść o małym biznesie, jego trudnościach i sukcesie**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Five years ago, my friend Lara started her own business.
2. She had a small idea but a big heart.
3. For months, she had to make ends meet on very little.
4. New equipment cost an arm and a leg.
5. Some days, her account was deep in the red.
6. She felt as busy as a bee, working day and night.
7. Her partner gave her a hand whenever he could.
8. He kept an eye on her health when she forgot to eat.
9. Some friends said she had bitten off more than she could chew.
10. But Lara was determined to go the extra mile.
11. She had a plan and refused to throw in the towel.
12. Lara thought outside the box from the start.
13. She made up her mind to try a new kind of product.
14. Many investors turned her down without much thought.
15. They could not read between the lines of her business plan.
16. Then one investor called her up out of the blue.
17. He had heard about her idea from a mutual friend.
18. They hit it off in their very first meeting.
19. He believed she had hit the nail on the head with her plan.
20. He offered her enough money to keep her going for a year.
21. The team grew, and they were all on the same page.
22. The first product launch was a piece of cake.
23. Customers loved it, and orders rolled in.
24. By year three, Lara was no longer in the red.
25. She could finally afford a holiday once in a blue moon.
26. Her old doubters had to eat their words.
27. Now she goes the extra mile for her staff in return.
28. She knows what it means to be in the same boat.
29. Looking back, Lara feels she was very lucky.
30. But luck favours those who never call it a day too early.
