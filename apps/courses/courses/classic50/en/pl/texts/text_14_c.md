# Tekst do lekcji 14: Przedimki — drugie podejście
**Temat: Kawiarnia na rogu — krótka historia**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. There is a small café on the corner of our street.
2. The café is called «The Blue Cup».
3. The owner is a quiet woman from Italy.
4. Her name is Maria, and she is an excellent cook.
5. Coffee at her café is the best in town.
6. Every morning many people stop there before work.
7. The waiter is a young student from the university.
8. He speaks three languages: Russian, English and Italian.
9. The chairs are old, but the tables are made of new wood.
10. On a wall there is a big black-and-white photo.
11. The photo shows a small village in the south of Italy.
12. Maria says it is her home village.
13. I usually order a cappuccino and a piece of cake.
14. The cake of the day is always different.
15. Sometimes it is a chocolate cake, sometimes an apple pie.
16. I always sit at the same small table near the window.
17. From the window I can see the park across the street.
18. People walk dogs and children ride bikes in the park.
19. Last Saturday I met an old friend at the café.
20. He was a colleague from my first job.
21. We had not seen each other for ten years.
22. He told me about his life in the USA.
23. He works as an engineer in a city near the Pacific Ocean.
24. He came back to Russia for a short visit.
25. Maria brought us a special tea and two small cakes.
26. The tea was hot and the cakes were sweet.
27. We talked about old times, about work, about life and music.
28. Outside, the rain started, and the sky became grey.
29. But inside, the café was warm and full of soft light.
30. That afternoon, the café on the corner felt like a quiet kind of home.
