## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. So you finally finished the fifty lessons. How do you feel about it?
2. Honestly, it's a strange mix of pride and disbelief.
3. I never thought I would actually make it all the way to the end.
4. There were moments when I almost gave up, to be honest.
5. The articles drove me crazy, especially in the first few months.
6. The present perfect was another point where I really struggled.
7. But somehow I kept going, even when nothing seemed to stick.
8. What advice would you give to someone just starting today?
9. First of all, don't aim for perfection at any stage.
10. Aim for being understood, then for being comfortable, then for sounding natural.
11. In that exact order, because trying to skip steps just leads to frustration.
12. Read everything you can get your hands on, even if it feels too hard at first.
13. Watch films and series without subtitles, even when you only catch half of it.
14. Eventually your ear adjusts and you start picking up the rhythm.
15. Speak out loud, even when you're alone in the kitchen.
16. Your tongue needs the workout, and your brain needs the feedback.
17. Don't be afraid of making mistakes — they are the real teachers.
18. Native speakers make mistakes all the time, too.
19. The difference is that they don't stop talking because of it.
20. Connect with the language emotionally, not just intellectually.
21. Find music, films, books, podcasts that you actually enjoy.
22. If learning feels like a chore, you will eventually quit.
23. If it feels like opening a new world, you will never stop.
24. Trust the process, even when progress feels invisible.
25. There are weeks when you'll think you're getting worse, not better.
26. That's actually a sign your brain is rewiring itself for the next level.
27. Be patient, be consistent, and be kind to yourself along the way.
28. Above all, remember that learning a language is a marathon, not a sprint.
29. The course you finish today is just one step on a much longer road.
30. But you have the tools now, and the rest is up to you. Good luck.
