# Tekst B do lekcji 49: Angielski wiadomości
**Temat: Nagłówki i artykuły — formalny rejestr mediów**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. President to address the nation later this evening.
2. Three injured in early morning crash on main highway.
3. Stocks fall sharply on weak economic data from Asia.
4. Minister denies all corruption claims in heated interview.
5. New policy to take effect on the first of next month.
6. Storm causes widespread damage along the southern coast.
7. Talks between the two leaders break down after just two hours.
8. Government announces major investment in renewable energy.
9. Local hospital faces shortage of essential medical supplies.
10. The investigation revealed serious failures in management.
11. According to sources, the decision was made last Friday.
12. A spokesperson said the company would issue a full statement.
13. The controversy has drawn attention from international media.
14. Several witnesses have come forward with new information.
15. The minister claims the allegations are completely false.
16. Critics argue that the response has been too slow and weak.
17. Public opinion remains divided on the new tax proposal.
18. Experts warn that the impact could last for several years.
19. The mayor announced plans for a major urban renewal project.
20. Police have confirmed that two suspects are now in custody.
21. The court found the defendant guilty on all five charges.
22. Sales of electric cars have risen by over thirty percent.
23. The annual report shows record profits for the company.
24. Inflation has reached its highest level in over a decade.
25. Climate scientists warn of dangerous trends in global temperatures.
26. The peace agreement was signed at a ceremony in Geneva.
27. Both sides have agreed to resume negotiations next month.
28. The election results are expected to be announced tomorrow morning.
29. Voters in three regions reported long waits at polling stations.
30. The newspaper has issued a correction regarding the earlier story.
