# Tekst do lekcji 33: I wish things were different
**Temat: Żale w teraźniejszości — I wish + Past**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. I wish I had more time for my hobbies.
2. I wish I could speak three languages.
3. I wish my flat were bigger.
4. I wish I lived closer to the sea.
5. I wish I knew how to play the piano.
6. I wish the weather were warmer today.
7. I wish I had a quieter neighbour.
8. I wish I could travel more often.
9. I wish she were here right now.
10. I wish my job were less stressful.
11. If only I had more energy in the morning!
12. If only the days were longer in winter.
13. If only I could remember everyone's name.
14. I wish my phone weren't so slow.
15. I wish people drove more carefully.
16. I wish I were better at sports.
17. I wish my brother lived in the same city.
18. I wish the prices weren't so high.
19. I wish I could cook like my grandmother.
20. I wish my dog were a little smaller.
21. I wish I had the patience to learn chess.
22. If only I could sleep more on weekends!
23. I wish I were ten years younger.
24. I wish my colleagues were friendlier.
25. I wish the bus came more often.
26. I wish I could play the guitar.
27. If only the world were a kinder place!
28. I wish I had a garden full of flowers.
29. I wish I didn't have to work tomorrow.
30. I wish life were a little simpler.
