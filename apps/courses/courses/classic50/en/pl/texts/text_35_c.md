# Tekst do lekcji 35: A story told twice
**Temat: Przekaz dialogu w mowie zależnej**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Yesterday I met my old friend Mark at the station.
2. He told me he had just come back from Italy.
3. I asked him how long he had been there.
4. He said he had spent three weeks in Rome and Florence.
5. I asked whether he had liked it.
6. He told me it had been the best holiday of his life.
7. He said the food was incredible.
8. He explained that he had walked for hours every day.
9. He admitted that he had spent too much money.
10. I asked if he had visited the Vatican.
11. He said he had, and that it had been amazing.
12. He told me he had also taken a trip to Tuscany.
13. He asked if I had ever been to Italy.
14. I said I had been to Milan once, but it had been a short visit.
15. He suggested going together next summer.
16. I told him I would love that.
17. Then I asked him about his work.
18. He explained that he had changed jobs the month before.
19. He said the new company was much better.
20. He told me the salary was higher and the people were nicer.
21. He mentioned that he was thinking of moving to a bigger flat.
22. I asked where he wanted to live.
23. He said he was looking at the south side of the city.
24. He told me he would let me know once he had decided.
25. We agreed to meet again soon.
26. He promised he would send me photos from Italy.
27. He warned me that the photos were many.
28. I laughed and said I didn't mind.
29. As he was leaving, he reminded me to call his mother.
30. I told him I would call her that very evening.
