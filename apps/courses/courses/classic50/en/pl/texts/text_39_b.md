# Tekst do lekcji 39: A trip across Europe
**Temat: Nazwy własne z rodzajnikiem i bez — podróż**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Last summer, my family travelled across Europe.
2. We started in France, in the city of Paris.
3. We climbed the Eiffel Tower on our first day.
4. The view from the top was unforgettable.
5. We walked along the Seine in the evening.
6. After Paris, we took the train to Italy.
7. In Rome, we visited the Colosseum and the Vatican.
8. The food in Italy was the best part of the trip.
9. We crossed the Mediterranean to reach Greece.
10. In Greece, we saw the Acropolis in Athens.
11. We swam in the Aegean Sea every day.
12. Then we flew to the United Kingdom.
13. London was rainy, but we loved it.
14. We visited the British Museum and the Tower of London.
15. We walked across the Thames at night.
16. After the UK, we went to the Netherlands.
17. In Amsterdam, we travelled by boat through the canals.
18. The Dutch are very friendly people.
19. We rented bicycles and rode along the streets.
20. Next we flew to Spain, to the city of Barcelona.
21. We visited Sagrada Familia and walked on Las Ramblas.
22. The Spanish know how to enjoy life.
23. Our last stop was the Alps.
24. We went hiking in the mountains.
25. The air in the Alps is cool and clean.
26. We saw Mount Blanc from a distance.
27. By the time we returned home, we were tired but happy.
28. We had visited eight countries in three weeks.
29. The young in our group complained about the pace.
30. But all of us agreed: it was a journey of a lifetime.
