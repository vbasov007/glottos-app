# Tekst do lekcji 20: Spójny tekst
**Temat: Weekendowa wycieczka**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Last weekend my family and I went to the countryside.
2. We have a small house near a beautiful lake.
3. We go there every summer, but rarely in winter.
4. On Friday evening we packed our bags.
5. My mother prepared sandwiches for the journey.
6. We left the city at seven in the morning on Saturday.
7. The road was busy, but my father drove carefully.
8. We arrived at the house at about ten o'clock.
9. The weather was perfect — sunny and warm.
10. First we opened all the windows.
11. Then we made coffee and sat on the terrace.
12. After breakfast my brother and I went to the lake.
13. We swam for an hour, although the water was a little cold.
14. After that we walked in the forest.
15. We saw many birds and even a small deer.
16. In the evening my father made a fire in the garden.
17. We cooked sausages and ate them with bread.
18. My mother sang quietly and we all listened.
19. The stars were very bright that night.
20. I went to bed at midnight, but I couldn't sleep.
21. The next morning we got up early.
22. My brother and I rode bikes to the village.
23. We bought fresh bread and milk at a small shop.
24. After lunch the weather changed.
25. Suddenly it started to rain, and we ran into the house.
26. We played card games and drank tea for two hours.
27. At five o'clock we packed our things again.
28. We came home tired but happy.
29. This summer we are going to spend two weeks at the lake.
30. I can't wait to go back.
