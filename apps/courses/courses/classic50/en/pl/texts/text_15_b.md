# Tekst do lekcji 15: Przyszłość — will i be going to
**Temat: Prognoza pogody na ten tydzień**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Good evening, and welcome to the weather report.
2. The week ahead is going to bring many changes.
3. Tomorrow morning it will be cold across most of the country.
4. In Moscow, temperatures will be around five degrees.
5. The sky is going to be grey for most of the day.
6. In the afternoon, light rain will start in the west.
7. By evening, the rain is going to reach the capital.
8. On Tuesday, the weather will improve a little.
9. The sun is going to come out around midday.
10. But the wind will be strong, especially in the north.
11. On Wednesday, a new storm is going to arrive from the Atlantic.
12. It will bring heavy rain to all of Europe.
13. We expect the rain will continue for two days.
14. People on the coast should be careful.
15. The waves will probably be very high.
16. On Thursday, the temperature is going to fall again.
17. In some cities, snow will fall in the evening.
18. Drivers will need to be careful on the roads.
19. The ice will be a real danger early in the morning.
20. On Friday, the weather will finally become more stable.
21. The sun is going to shine in most regions.
22. Temperatures will rise to around ten degrees.
23. It will be a perfect day for a walk in the park.
24. The weekend is going to be even better.
25. On Saturday, we expect blue skies all over the country.
26. The wind will be light and from the south.
27. On Sunday, a few clouds will return in the afternoon.
28. But the rain will not come back until next week.
29. So enjoy the weekend — it is going to be lovely.
30. We will be back tomorrow with the next update.
