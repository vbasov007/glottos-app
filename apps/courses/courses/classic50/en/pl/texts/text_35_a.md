# Tekst do lekcji 35: She said, he said
**Temat: Przekaz cudzej mowy — proste stwierdzenia z backshift**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Anna said she was very busy that week.
2. Tom said he had finished the project the day before.
3. Mary said she would call me the next day.
4. He told me he was working from home.
5. She said she had never been to London.
6. They said they had bought a new house.
7. Jack told us he couldn't come to the meeting.
8. The teacher said the test would be easy.
9. My friend told me she had broken her phone.
10. He said he was tired and wanted to go home.
11. She admitted she had made a mistake.
12. They explained that the train had been cancelled.
13. The doctor said I had to rest for a week.
14. He told me he would help me with the project.
15. She said she had seen that film three times.
16. The manager announced that the office would close at six.
17. He said he didn't know the answer.
18. She told us she had already eaten.
19. They said they were thinking about moving abroad.
20. The driver explained that the road was closed.
21. He claimed he had never met her before.
22. She mentioned that her sister was getting married.
23. The neighbour complained that we were too noisy.
24. He told me he had been waiting for an hour.
25. She said it had been a long day.
26. The receptionist told us that all rooms were taken.
27. He insisted that he had paid the bill.
28. She said she was looking forward to the holiday.
29. The boss told us we needed to work harder.
30. He simply said he had changed his mind.
