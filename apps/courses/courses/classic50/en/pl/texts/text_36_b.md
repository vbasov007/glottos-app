# Tekst do lekcji 36: Strange noises in the night
**Temat: Dedukcja w przeszłości — must / might / can't have V3**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Last night I heard a strange noise downstairs.
2. At first I thought it must be the wind.
3. But the windows were all closed.
4. So it can't have been the wind.
5. Then I thought it might have been the cat.
6. But the cat was sleeping next to me.
7. So it couldn't have been her either.
8. I lay in bed and listened for a long time.
9. The noise must have lasted for ten minutes.
10. It sounded like soft footsteps.
11. There must have been someone in the kitchen.
12. I should have called my brother right away.
13. Instead, I went down alone.
14. That was a bad idea — I shouldn't have done that.
15. In the kitchen, I saw a small grey shape.
16. It must have been a mouse.
17. The mouse must have come in through the window.
18. But the windows were closed, so it couldn't have come that way.
19. It might have come up from the cellar.
20. We should have fixed that old door months ago.
21. I tried to catch the mouse, but it was too quick.
22. It must have known the house better than I did.
23. In the morning, I told my brother the whole story.
24. He said I should have woken him up.
25. He could have helped me catch it.
26. Now we have to buy a trap.
27. The mouse may have brought friends.
28. We can't have a family of mice in the kitchen.
29. We should have been more careful with food on the counter.
30. Tonight I will sleep with one ear open.
