# Tekst do lekcji 23: Past Continuous
**Temat: Wypadek — opowieść z przerwanym działaniem**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. Last Friday I was driving home from work.
2. It was already dark and very cold.
3. The road was wet from the morning rain.
4. I was listening to my favourite music.
5. I was thinking about my weekend plans.
6. Suddenly, a black cat ran across the road.
7. I braked hard but the car was sliding.
8. Another driver was coming the other way.
9. He was honking and flashing his lights.
10. I managed to stop just in time.
11. The other car stopped right behind me.
12. We were both shaking from the shock.
13. The man got out and walked towards me.
14. He was wearing a long coat and a hat.
15. He asked if I was okay.
16. I told him I was fine, but my heart was beating fast.
17. We were standing in the middle of the road.
18. Other cars were passing us slowly.
19. The cat was sitting on the pavement.
20. It was looking at us calmly.
21. A police car arrived a few minutes later.
22. The officer was asking us questions.
23. While he was writing his report, the rain started again.
24. We were all getting wet.
25. The officer let us go after fifteen minutes.
26. I drove home slowly, my hands still shaking.
27. When I got home, my wife was waiting for me.
28. She was making dinner and listening to the radio.
29. I told her the whole story over dinner.
30. We agreed that I was lucky that night.
