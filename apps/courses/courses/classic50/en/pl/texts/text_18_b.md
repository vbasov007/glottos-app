# Tekst do lekcji 18: Przyimki czasu i miejsca
**Temat: W podróży — rozkład jazdy i adresy**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Our plane leaves at six in the morning.
2. We have to be at the airport at four.
3. The flight to London is on Tuesday.
4. In two hours we will be in London.
5. Our hotel is on Oxford Street.
6. The hotel is at number 25.
7. The room is on the fifth floor.
8. There is a small balcony with a view of the park.
9. Breakfast is from seven to ten in the morning.
10. We can eat in the restaurant on the ground floor.
11. The bank is between the hotel and the museum.
12. The metro station is in front of the hotel.
13. We arrive in London at noon.
14. In the afternoon we will visit the British Museum.
15. The museum is on Great Russell Street.
16. It is closed on Mondays.
17. In the evening we have dinner in a Greek restaurant.
18. The restaurant is near the river Thames.
19. On Wednesday we go to Paris by train.
20. The train leaves at eight o'clock from St Pancras station.
21. The journey takes two and a half hours.
22. Our hotel in Paris is on Rue de Rivoli.
23. The Eiffel Tower is in the centre of the city.
24. There is a long queue at the entrance.
25. At the weekend we travel to Rome.
26. In Rome we stay with friends in their apartment.
27. The apartment is on the second floor of an old building.
28. At Christmas we are at home with the family.
29. In summer we always go to the sea.
30. Tomorrow we are flying back home.
