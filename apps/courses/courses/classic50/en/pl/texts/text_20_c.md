# Tekst do lekcji 20: Spójny tekst
**Temat: Ten rok w moim życiu**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. This year is a very important year for me.
2. In January I started a new job.
3. Before that, I worked in a small company for three years.
4. My old job was easy, but I wanted something more interesting.
5. The new office is in the centre of the city.
6. I work with twenty people in a big team.
7. The first weeks were difficult.
8. I didn't know anyone, and the work was new.
9. However, my new colleagues helped me a lot.
10. After two months I felt comfortable.
11. Now I really enjoy my job.
12. I usually arrive at the office at nine.
13. I have meetings almost every day.
14. My English is getting better, because we often work with foreign clients.
15. At the moment I'm working on an important project.
16. We must finish it before the end of the month.
17. It's quite difficult, but very interesting.
18. In April I went to London for the first time.
19. The trip was short — only three days.
20. I visited the British Museum and the Tate Gallery.
21. The weather was rainy, but I loved the city.
22. Next month I'm going to Paris for a conference.
23. I'm a little nervous because my French is not very good.
24. But I'm sure I will manage.
25. After the conference I'm going to stay for the weekend.
26. I want to walk along the Seine and try French food.
27. This summer my family is going to visit me here.
28. We are going to spend two weeks together.
29. I'm really looking forward to it.
30. Life is busy, but it is also very exciting.
