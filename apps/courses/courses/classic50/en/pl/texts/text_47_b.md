# Tekst B do lekcji 47: Prezentacja — rejestr ustny z markerami
**Temat: Dyskurs w mowie — jak brzmi ustrukturyzowane wystąpienie ustne**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. Good morning, everyone, and thanks for coming.
2. Today I want to talk about three main ideas.
3. First of all, let me set the scene briefly.
4. Our team has been working on this project for six months.
5. We have done a lot, but we have also faced real problems.
6. Now, let me move on to my first point.
7. The product is technically ready for launch.
8. In other words, the engineering side has done its job.
9. However, we have a problem with the marketing side.
10. The campaign is not finished, and time is short.
11. Moreover, the budget for advertising is smaller than expected.
12. As a result, we may have to delay the launch by two weeks.
13. Let me give you a quick example to illustrate the issue.
14. Our biggest competitor launched a similar product last month.
15. Their campaign was huge, with TV ads and social media.
16. We simply cannot match that level of spending right now.
17. Now, on the other hand, we have a real advantage in quality.
18. Indeed, our beta testers have praised the product.
19. In particular, they liked the easy setup and the design.
20. So my second point is about positioning.
21. We should focus on the niche of serious users.
22. By the way, this is also where our pricing fits best.
23. Speaking of pricing, let me mention one more thing.
24. We are considering a small introductory discount.
25. Nevertheless, the regular price should stay as planned.
26. Now, finally, what does all this mean for the team?
27. Basically, we have hard work ahead of us.
28. To sum up, the launch will probably move to early June.
29. Anyway, I would like to hear your questions and ideas.
30. Thanks for listening, and the floor is yours.
