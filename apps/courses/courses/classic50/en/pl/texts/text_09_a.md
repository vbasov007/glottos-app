# Tekst do lekcji 9: There is / there are; kwantyfikatory
**Temat: Moje mieszkanie**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. I live in a small but nice flat.
2. There are three rooms in my flat.
3. There is a kitchen, a bedroom and a living room.
4. There is also a small bathroom.
5. In the kitchen there is a table and four chairs.
6. There is a big fridge near the window.
7. There are some cups and plates on the shelf.
8. In the fridge there is milk, cheese and some eggs.
9. There isn't any meat in the fridge today.
10. There is a lot of fruit on the kitchen table.
11. In the living room there is a soft sofa.
12. There is also an armchair near the window.
13. There is a low table in front of the sofa.
14. On the wall there are two pictures.
15. There is a big TV in the corner.
16. There aren't many books in this room.
17. In the bedroom there is a big bed.
18. There is a wardrobe and a small desk.
19. On the desk there is a lamp and some papers.
20. There is a soft carpet on the floor.
21. There aren't any pictures on the bedroom walls.
22. But there is a big mirror near the door.
23. There are two windows in my flat.
24. There is a small balcony in the living room.
25. On the balcony there are a few flowers.
26. There is a lot of light in my flat.
27. There are no problems with my neighbours.
28. There is a quiet park near my house.
29. There are many trees in this park.
30. I love my home very much.
