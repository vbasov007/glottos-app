# Tekst do lekcji 29: My grandmother's living room
**Temat: długie grupy rzeczownikowe — opis rzeczy w pokoju**

---

## Tekst do słuchania i powtarzania

1. My grandmother's living room is full of beautiful old objects.
2. In the middle there is a large round wooden table.
3. On the table stands a tall white china vase.
4. The vase is always full of fresh yellow flowers.
5. Near the window there is a small green leather armchair.
6. My grandfather used to sit in that armchair every evening.
7. On the wall opposite the window hangs a long dark Persian carpet.
8. The carpet has a complicated geometric pattern in red and gold.
9. Above the fireplace there is a small old oil painting.
10. The painting shows a quiet country scene from the nineteenth century.
11. Next to the fireplace stands a tall narrow bookshelf.
12. The bookshelf is full of small leather-bound books.
13. Most of the books are in French and Russian.
14. On the lowest shelf there is a heavy black wooden box.
15. The box contains old letters and faded photographs.
16. By the door there is a tall mahogany clock with a brass face.
17. The clock has been in our family for over a hundred years.
18. In one corner sits a small grey cat called Misha.
19. Misha is an independent and very intelligent creature.
20. Near the window stands a small antique writing desk.
21. The desk has three narrow drawers full of interesting things.
22. In the top drawer my grandmother keeps her silver pen.
23. The middle drawer contains a thin leather diary.
24. The bottom drawer is full of strange little objects from her travels.
25. The curtains are made of heavy dark red velvet.
26. The light in the room is always soft and warm.
27. The whole room smells of wood, old paper and dried flowers.
28. Every object in the room has its own quiet story.
29. I love spending afternoons in this beautiful peaceful place.
30. It is more than a room: it is a small museum of love and memory.
