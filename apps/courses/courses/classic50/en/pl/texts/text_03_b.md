# Tekst do lekcji 3: At the airport

**Temat: na lotnisku, różni ludzie z różnych krajów**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. We are at the airport.
2. The airport is big.
3. There are many people here.
4. A man is from France.
5. He is a doctor.
6. He is tired.
7. A woman is from China.
8. She is a teacher.
9. She is happy.
10. Two boys are from Italy.
11. They are students.
12. They are hungry.
13. A girl is from Germany.
14. She is an artist.
15. She is young.
16. An old man is from Spain.
17. He is a writer.
18. He is calm.
19. The driver is from America.
20. He is busy.
21. He is not tired.
22. My friend is here too.
23. He is from England.
24. He is an engineer.
25. He is thirty years old.
26. We are all here.
27. We are all from different countries.
28. We are all ready.
29. It is a long day.
30. But we are happy.
