# Tekst do lekcji 9: There is / there are; kwantyfikatory
**Temat: Spacer po mieście — co jest, a czego nie ma**

---

## Tekst do słuchania

1. My friend visits my town for the first time.
2. I show him around the centre.
3. There are many old buildings in our town.
4. There is a big church on the main square.
5. Near the church there is a small museum.
6. There are some interesting things in this museum.
7. Is there a park near here, my friend asks.
8. Yes, there is a beautiful park behind the museum.
9. In the park there are tall trees and green grass.
10. There is a small lake too.
11. There are a few birds on the lake.
12. There aren't any cars in the park, only people.
13. There are many children playing here on Sunday.
14. We walk to the centre.
15. There are a lot of shops in the main street.
16. There are some cafes and restaurants too.
17. Is there a good restaurant for dinner, he asks.
18. Yes, there is a nice Italian place near the station.
19. Are there any cinemas in your town?
20. Yes, there is a modern cinema in the new centre.
21. How many hospitals are there in your town?
22. There are two hospitals, one big and one small.
23. There aren't many tourists in our town today.
24. But on the weekend there are a lot of visitors.
25. There is enough to see for two or three days.
26. We sit in a cafe and drink some coffee.
27. There is a little sugar in my coffee, a lot of milk in his.
28. There aren't any free tables outside, so we sit inside.
29. There is good music in this cafe.
30. There is much beauty in a quiet day with a friend.
