# Test do lekcji 25: Past Perfect

**Jak to powiedzieć po angielsku?**
Przetłumacz frazy na angielski. Użyj gramatyki i słownictwa z tej lekcji.

---

1. Kiedy przyszedłem, ona już wyszła.
2. Do momentu, gdy przyjechaliśmy, film już się zaczął.
3. Był zmęczony, bo pracował całą noc.
4. To był pierwszy raz, kiedy widziałem ocean.
5. Nie mogłem wejść, bo zapomniałem kluczy.
6. Złodzieje uciekli, zanim przyjechała policja.
7. Po tym jak przeczytała list, zapłakała.
8. Do południa skończył całą pracę.
9. Nie poznałem jej — nie widzieliśmy się dziesięć lat.
10. Nigdy wcześniej nie była za granicą.
11. To był najlepszy film, jaki kiedykolwiek oglądał.
12. Kiedy zrozumiałem błąd, było już za późno.
13. Pociąg odjechał pięć minut przed tym, jak dobiegłem.
14. Znałem odpowiedź, bo przeczytałem książkę.
15. Dzieci były zmęczone — bawiły się cały dzień.
16. Do tego momentu już podjął decyzję.
17. Zapytała, czy byłem tam wcześniej.
18. Zjadłem w domu, zanim poszedłem na imprezę.
19. Pobrali się w 2015. W 2020 już byli rozwiedzeni.
20. Kiedy przyjechał autobus, czekaliśmy już godzinę.
21. Kiedy otworzyłem drzwi, goście już wyszli.
22. To był najpiękniejszy kraj, jaki odwiedziłem.
23. Pod koniec roku przeczytałem dwadzieścia książek.
24. Zrozumieliśmy, że coś się stało.
25. Powiedział mi, że widział ją dzień wcześniej.
26. Nigdy nie próbowałem tak pysznego jedzenia.
27. Zanim przeprowadziłem się do Londynu, mieszkałem w Warszawie.
28. Pracowała nad tym projektem długo.
29. Kiedy przyszedłem, już ugotowała kolację.
30. Przed tą podróżą nigdy nie leciałem samolotem.

<details><summary>Klucz</summary>

1. *When I came, she had already left.* (lub: *When I arrived, she had already gone.*)
2. *By the time we arrived, the film had already started.* (lub: *By the time we got to the cinema, the movie had begun.*)
3. *He was tired because he had worked all night.* (lub: *He was tired because he had been working all night.*)
4. *It was the first time I had seen the ocean.*
5. *I couldn't enter because I had forgotten my keys.* (lub: *I couldn't get in because I'd forgotten my keys.*)
6. *The thieves had escaped before the police arrived.* (lub: *The robbers had run away before the police came.*)
7. *After she had read the letter, she started crying.* (lub: *After she read the letter, she began to cry.*)
8. *By noon he had finished all his work.* (lub: *By midday he had finished all the work.*)
9. *I didn't recognise her — we hadn't seen each other for ten years.* (lub: *I didn't recognise her — we hadn't met for 10 years.*)
10. *She had never been abroad before.* (lub: *She had never travelled abroad before.*)
11. *It was the best film he had ever watched.* (lub: *It was the best movie he had ever seen.*)
12. *When I realised the mistake, it was already too late.* (lub: *By the time I realised the mistake, it was too late.*)
13. *The train had left five minutes before I ran up.* (lub: *The train had gone five minutes before I arrived.*)
14. *I knew the answer because I had read the book.*
15. *The children were tired — they had played all day.* (lub: *The kids were tired — they had been playing all day.*)
16. *By then he had already made his decision.* (lub: *By that time he had already decided.*)
17. *She asked if I had been there before.* (lub: *She asked whether I had been there before.*)
18. *I had eaten at home before I went to the party.* (lub: *I had had dinner at home before I went to the party.*)
19. *They got married in 2015. By 2020 they had already divorced.* (lub: *They got married in 2015. In 2020 they had already got divorced.*)
20. *When the bus came, we had already been waiting for an hour.* (lub: *When the bus arrived, we had been waiting for an hour.*)
21. *When I opened the door, the visitors had already left.* (lub: *When I opened the door, the guests had already gone.*)
22. *It was the most beautiful country I had visited.* (lub: *It was the most beautiful country I had ever been to.*)
23. *By the end of the year I had read twenty books.*
24. *We realised that something had happened.* (lub: *We understood that something had happened.*)
25. *He told me he had seen her the day before.* (lub: *He told me that he had seen her the previous day.*)
26. *I had never tried such delicious food.* (lub: *I had never eaten such tasty food before.*)
27. *Before I moved to London, I had lived in Moscow.* (lub: *Before moving to London, I had lived in Moscow.*)
28. *She had been working on that project for a long time.* (lub: *She had worked on that project for a long time.*)
29. *When I came, she had already cooked dinner.* (lub: *When I arrived, she had already made dinner.*)
30. *Before that trip, I had never flown on a plane.* (lub: *Before that trip, I had never flown in a plane.*)
