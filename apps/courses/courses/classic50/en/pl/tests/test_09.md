# Test do lekcji 9: There is / there are; kwantyfikatory

**Jak to powiedzieć po angielsku?**
Przetłumacz frazy na angielski. Użyj gramatyki i słownictwa z tej lekcji.

---

1. W pokoju jest stół.
2. Na stole jest lampa i książka.
3. W kuchni są trzy krzesła.
4. W lodówce jest trochę mleka.
5. Na ścianie (są) dwa obrazy.
6. W ogrodzie jest dużo kwiatów.
7. W pokoju nie ma okna.
8. Nie mam czasu.
9. W szklance nie ma wody.
10. Ile pokoi jest w twoim domu?
11. Ile wody pijesz codziennie?
12. Na półce jest dużo książek.
13. W sypialni jest łóżko i szafa.
14. Czy w salonie jest sofa i fotel?
15. Tak, są.
16. W naszym mieście jest dużo sklepów.
17. Mam mało pieniędzy.
18. Czy w lodówce są jajka?
19. Nie, nie ma.
20. Chcesz filiżankę herbaty? (proponujesz)
21. W tej butelce nie ma wody.
22. Na ulicy (jest) kilkoro dzieci.
23. W pokoju nie ma dywanu.
24. Ile ludzi jest w parku?
25. Czy na stole są jakieś owoce?
26. Nie, nie ma żadnych owoców.
27. W moim pokoju jest lustro i dwie lampy.
28. W ogrodzie rośnie (jest) duże drzewo.
29. W kuchni jest trochę chleba i sera.
30. Ile mebli jest w tym pokoju?

1. *There is a table in the room.*
2. *There is a lamp and a book on the table.* (lub: *On the table there is a lamp and a book.*)
3. *There are three chairs in the kitchen.*
4. *There is a little milk in the fridge.* (lub: *There is some milk in the fridge.*)
5. *There are two pictures on the wall.*
6. *There are a lot of flowers in the garden.* (lub: *There are many flowers in the garden.*)
7. *There is no window in the room.* (lub: *There isn't a window in the room.*)
8. *I don't have time.* (lub: *There is no time.* / *I have no time.*)
9. *There is no water in the glass.* (lub: *There isn't any water in the glass.*)
10. *How many rooms are there in your house?*
11. *How much water do you drink every day?*
12. *There are a lot of books on the shelf.* (lub: *There are many books on the shelf.*)
13. *There is a bed and a wardrobe in the bedroom.*
14. *Is there a sofa and an armchair in the living room?*
15. *Yes, there is.* (lub: *Yes, there are.*)
16. *There are a lot of shops in our town.* (lub: *There are many shops in our town.*)
17. *I have little money.* (lub: *I don't have much money.*)
18. *Are there any eggs in the fridge?*
19. *No, there aren't.*
20. *Would you like a cup of tea?* (lub: *Do you want some tea?*)
21. *There is no water in this bottle.* (lub: *There isn't any water in this bottle.*)
22. *There are a few children in the street.* (lub: *There are some children in the street.*)
23. *There is no carpet in the room.* (lub: *There isn't a carpet in the room.*)
24. *How many people are there in the park?*
25. *Is there any fruit on the table?* (lub: *Are there any fruits on the table?*)
26. *No, there isn't any fruit.* (lub: *No, there is no fruit.*)
27. *There is a mirror and two lamps in my room.*
28. *There is a big tree in the garden.*
29. *There is a little bread and cheese in the kitchen.* (lub: *There is some bread and cheese in the kitchen.*)
30. *How much furniture is there in this room?*
