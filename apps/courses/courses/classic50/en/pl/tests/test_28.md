# Test do lekcji 28: Zdania względne

**Jak to powiedzieć po angielsku?**
Przetłumacz frazy na angielski. Użyj gramatyki i słownictwa z tej lekcji.

1. *I know a girl who speaks five languages.*
2. *This is the book that changed my life.* (lub: *…which changed my life.*)
3. *The man who lives next door is a doctor.*

5. *This is the place where we first met.*
6. *I have a friend whose father is a famous writer.*
7. *The man (who/that) I was talking to yesterday is a lawyer.* (lub: *The man I spoke to yesterday is a lawyer.*)
8. *The film (that/which) we watched was boring.*
9. *Moscow, where I was born, is a huge city.* (lub: *…a big city.*)
