1. *I live in Warsaw.*
2. *Tom lives in London.*
3. *We work in a hospital.* (lub: *We work at a hospital.*)
4. *Anna works in a school.* (lub: *Anna works at a school.*)
5. *The children play football.* (lub: *Kids play football.*)
6. *My brother studies English.* (lub: *My brother learns English.*)
7. *My sister studies French.*
8. *I drink coffee every morning.*
9. *She drinks tea in the evening.*
10. *Tom eats pizza on Fridays.*
11. *The cat eats fish.*
12. *I love my family.*
13. *Anna loves music.* (lub: *Anna likes music.*)
14. *They want a new house.*
15. *Tom wants a car.*
16. *I have a brother.* (lub: *I've got a brother.*)
17. *She has a sister.* (lub: *She's got a sister.*)
18. *I walk to work.* (lub: *I go to work on foot.*)
19. *Tom goes to work by car.* (lub: *Tom drives to work.*)
20. *Anna goes to school every day.*
21. *We watch TV in the evening.* (lub: *We watch television in the evening.*)
22. *She watches TV every day.*
23. *I speak Polish.*
24. *He speaks English.*
25. *I always drink coffee in the morning.*
26. *Anna never eats meat.*
27. *We often watch films.* (lub: *We often watch movies.*)
28. *Tom usually works on Saturdays.*
29. *Sometimes they play football.* (lub: *They sometimes play football.*)
30. *The teacher helps the students.*
