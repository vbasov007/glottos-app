# Test do lekcji 24: Present Perfect Continuous

**Jak to powiedzieć po angielsku?**
Przetłumacz frazy na angielski. Użyj gramatyki i słownictwa z tej lekcji.

---

1. Uczę się angielskiego pięć lat.
2. Jak długo czekasz?
3. Pracuje w tej firmie od stycznia.
4. Pada całe rano.
5. Jestem zmęczony, pracowałem cały dzień.
6. Ma czerwone oczy — płakał.
7. Przepraszam za spóźnienie — czekałem na autobus pół godziny.
8. Co robiłeś ostatnio?
9. Myślałem o tobie.
10. Planujemy podróż do Włoch.
11. Trenują do maratonu od lata.
12. Źle sypiam ostatnio.
13. Jak długo ona tam mieszka?
14. Znam ją dziesięć lat. (uwaga: stative!)
15. Szukam kluczy już godzinę.
16. Dzieci bawią się w ogrodzie od szkoły.
17. Czemu masz ręce w farbie? — Malowałem ścianę.
18. Nie pracowałem, tylko czytałem.
19. Pisze pracę magisterską cały rok.
20. Pociąg się spóźnia — czekamy już czterdzieści minut.
21. Przepraszam, nie słyszałem — słuchałem muzyki.
22. Są razem już trzy lata.
23. Budzę się wcześnie ostatnio.
24. Oglądam ten serial dwa tygodnie.
25. Jak długo trwa film?
26. Rozmawiamy o tym cały wieczór.
27. On ostatnio dużo uprawia sportu.
28. Te dzieci hałasują pół godziny!
29. Boli mnie głowa — patrzyłem w ekran cały dzień.
30. Nigdy nie paliłem. (uwaga: faktyczne „nigdy"!)

<details><summary>Klucz</summary>

1. *I have been learning English for five years.* (lub: *I've been studying English for 5 years.*; *I've studied English for 5 years.*)
2. *How long have you been waiting?*
3. *She has been working at this company since January.* (lub: *She's been working for this company since January.*)
4. *It has been raining all morning.* (lub: *It's been raining all morning.*)
5. *I am tired, I've been working all day.* (lub: *I'm tired — I have been working the whole day.*)
6. *He has red eyes — he has been crying.* (lub: *His eyes are red — he's been crying.*)
7. *Sorry I'm late — I've been waiting for the bus for half an hour.* (lub: *Sorry I'm late — I have been waiting for the bus for 30 minutes.*)
8. *What have you been doing lately?* (lub: *What have you been up to recently?*)
9. *I have been thinking about you.* (lub: *I've been thinking of you.*)
10. *We have been planning a trip to Italy.* (lub: *We've been planning a trip to Italy.*)
11. *They have been training for the marathon since summer.* (lub: *They've been training for a marathon since the summer.*)
12. *I have been sleeping badly lately.* (lub: *I haven't been sleeping well lately.*)
13. *How long has she been living there?* (lub: *How long has she lived there?*)
14. *I have known her for ten years.* (uwaga: know — stative, Present Perfect, nie Continuous)
15. *I have been looking for my keys for an hour.* (lub: *I've been searching for my keys for an hour.*)
16. *The children have been playing in the garden since school.* (lub: *The kids have been playing in the garden since school finished.*)
17. *Why are your hands covered in paint? — I have been painting the wall.* (lub: *Why are your hands covered in paint? — I've been painting a wall.*)
18. *I haven't been working, I have just been reading.* (lub: *I wasn't working, I was just reading.*)
19. *She has been writing her dissertation all year.* (lub: *She's been writing her thesis all year.*)
20. *The train is late — we have been waiting for forty minutes.* (lub: *The train is delayed — we've been waiting for 40 minutes.*)
21. *Sorry, I didn't hear — I was listening to music.* (lub: *Sorry, I didn't hear you — I've been listening to music.*)
22. *They have been together for three years.* (lub: *They've been a couple for three years.*; uwaga: *be together* — statycznie, oba warianty OK)
23. *I have been waking up early lately.* (lub: *I've been getting up early these days.*)
24. *I have been watching this series for two weeks.* (lub: *I've been watching this show for two weeks.*)
25. *How long has the film been showing?* (lub: *How long has the movie been on?*; *How long has the film been running?*)
26. *We have been talking about this all evening.* (lub: *We've been discussing this all evening.*)
27. *He has been doing a lot of sport lately.* (lub: *He's been exercising a lot recently.*; *He's been doing a lot of sports lately.*)
28. *These children have been making noise for half an hour!* (lub: *These kids have been noisy for half an hour!*)
29. *I have a headache — I have been staring at the screen all day.* (lub: *I've got a headache — I've been looking at a screen all day.*)
30. *I have never smoked.* (uwaga: „nigdy" jako fakt → Present Perfect, nie Continuous)
