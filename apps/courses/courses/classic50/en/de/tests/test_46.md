# Test zu Lektion 46: Wortbildung — Präfixe, Suffixe, Konversion

**Wie sagt man es auf Englisch?**
Übersetze die Wendungen ins Englische. Nutze die Grammatik und den Wortschatz dieser Lektion.

---

1. Das war eine wichtige Entscheidung.
2. Die Entwicklung der Technologie war unglaublich schnell.
3. Ihre Freundlichkeit hat mich beeindruckt.
4. Ich bin der Arbeitgeber, und sie sind die Arbeitnehmer.

14. Eine unvergessliche Erfahrung.
15. Verantwortungslosigkeit ist seine Hauptschwäche.
16. Was ist deine Verantwortung für dieses Projekt?

18. Vergiss nicht, die Blumen zu gießen.

21. Diese Verbesserung ist erheblich.

<summary>Lösungen</summary>

1. *It was an important decision.*
2. *The development of technology was incredibly fast.* (oder: *...remarkably fast.*)
3. *Her kindness impressed me.* (oder: *I was impressed by her kindness.*)
4. *I am the employer, and they are the employees.*
5. *It is completely impossible.* (oder: *totally impossible*)
6. *He underestimated the complexity of the task.* (oder: *...the difficulty of the task.*)
7. *Please rewrite this paragraph.*
8. *I disagree with you.* (oder: *I don't agree with you.*)
9. *Sorry, I misunderstood you.* (oder: *Sorry, I didn't understand you.*)
10. *Her creativity is remarkable.* (oder: *Her creative ability is remarkable.*)
11. *This plan is completely unrealistic.*
12. *He is my ex-boss.* (oder: *He is my former boss.*)
13. *The cooperation between the colleagues was key.* (oder: *...was crucial.*)
14. *An unforgettable experience.*
15. *Irresponsibility is his main weakness.*
16. *What is your responsibility for this project?*
17. *I booked a table for two.* (oder: *I have booked a table for two.*; *I reserved a table for two.*)
18. *Don't forget to water the flowers.* (oder: *...the plants.*)
19. *It is illogical and unacceptable.*
20. *Her awareness of the problem is impressive.* (oder: *...is remarkable.*)
21. *This improvement is significant.* (oder: *...is considerable.*)
22. *Achieving the goal took five years.* (oder: *The achievement of the goal took five years.*)
23. *The possibilities are endless.* (oder: *...are limitless.*)
24. *Flexibility is her strength.* (oder: *...is her strong point.*)
25. *This recommendation was ignored.*
26. *Unbelievable, but true.* (oder: *It's unbelievable, but it's true.*)
27. *They overestimate their strength.* (oder: *...their abilities.*)
28. *Friendship is more important than money.*
29. *That decision turned out to be wrong.* (oder: *...proved to be a mistake.*)
30. *The implementation of the new system will take months.*
