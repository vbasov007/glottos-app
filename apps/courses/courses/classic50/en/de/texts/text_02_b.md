# Text zu Lektion 2: At the market

**Thema: auf dem Markt, Zahlen und Preise**

---

## Hörtext

1. Tom is at the market.
2. Tom wants bread.
3. Tom wants apples.
4. The man at the table sells bread.
5. The woman sells apples.
6. Tom sees the bread.
7. The bread is good.
8. Tom buys one bread.
9. The bread costs twenty.
10. Tom gives twenty.
11. Tom says thank you.
12. The man says you are welcome.
13. Tom goes to the woman.
14. Tom buys six apples.
15. The apples cost thirty.
16. Tom gives thirty.
17. The woman gives the apples.
18. Tom thanks the woman.
19. The woman smiles.
20. Tom sees milk.
21. Tom wants milk too.
22. The milk costs forty.
23. Tom buys the milk.
24. Tom counts the money.
25. Twenty and thirty is fifty.
26. Fifty and forty is ninety.
27. Tom has ninety in total.
28. Tom goes home.
29. At home Tom eats bread and apples.
30. Tom drinks milk. Tom is happy.
