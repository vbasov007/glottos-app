# Text zu Lektion 11: Present Continuous
**Thema: Im Park am Nachmittag**

---

## Hörtext zum Wiederholen

1. I am walking in the park.
2. It is a warm afternoon.
3. The sun is shining over the trees.
4. A small wind is moving the leaves.
5. Many people are walking with me.
6. A young man is running fast.
7. He is wearing a blue shirt and shorts.
8. Two girls are riding their bikes.
9. They are laughing and talking.
10. An old woman is sitting on a bench.
11. She is feeding the birds.
12. The birds are eating little pieces of bread.
13. A family is having a picnic on the grass.
14. The father is cutting fruit.
15. The mother is opening a bottle of water.
16. Their little boy is playing with a red ball.
17. He is running after the ball and shouting.
18. A dog is watching the boy from the path.
19. The dog is not moving — it is just looking.
20. Near the lake, a man is reading a newspaper.
21. He is sitting on a wooden chair.
22. A musician is playing the guitar by the gate.
23. People are stopping and listening to him.
24. Some are giving him a few coins.
25. I am taking a photo with my phone.
26. The sky is slowly turning orange.
27. The afternoon is ending.
28. People are starting to go home.
29. I am walking towards the exit.
30. I am thinking about a quiet evening at home.
