# Text zur Lektion 31: A rainy day
**Thema: Reale Pläne und Bedingungen — nullter und erster Konditional**

---

## Hörtext zum Wiederholen

1. If it rains tomorrow, I'll stay home.
2. If the weather is good, we'll go to the park.
3. I always take an umbrella if it looks cloudy.
4. If you press this button, the door opens.
5. Plants die if you don't water them.
6. If I drink coffee at night, I don't sleep.
7. If you study hard, you'll pass the exam.
8. I'll call you as soon as I arrive.
9. If she comes, we'll have dinner together.
10. Take a jacket in case it gets cold.
11. If he asks, tell him I'm at work.
12. Unless you hurry, you'll miss the bus.
13. If we leave now, we'll be on time.
14. Water boils if you heat it enough.
15. If the train is late, I'll take a taxi.
16. She gets angry if you call her late.
17. If you mix yellow and blue, you get green.
18. I'll lend you the book if you promise to return it.
19. If it snows, the kids will be very happy.
20. Call me when you get home.
21. If you finish early, you can leave.
22. The shop closes if there are no customers.
23. If I see him, I'll give him your message.
24. If you don't sleep well, you feel tired all day.
25. I'll wait here until she comes back.
26. If the alarm rings, leave the building.
27. If you want some tea, just tell me.
28. We'll go to the beach if the sun comes out.
29. If you don't trust him, don't lend him money.
30. If everything goes well, we'll meet next week.
