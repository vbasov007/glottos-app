# Text C zu Lektion 46: Karrieregeschichte — langer Dialog
**Thema: Überlegungen zu Arbeit und Karriereentwicklung — Wortbildung in der Rede**

---

## Text zum Hören und Wiederholen

1. So tell me about your most recent achievement at work.
2. Well, my biggest success was leading a difficult project.
3. The development of the product took almost two years.
4. We faced a lot of unexpected problems along the way.
5. Some of the team members were inexperienced at first.
6. But their willingness to learn was truly impressive.
7. I had to make many difficult decisions every week.
8. Some decisions were unpopular with the management.
9. Others required real creativity and out-of-the-box thinking.
10. The cooperation between departments was the key factor.
11. Without their flexibility, we would have failed completely.
12. The implementation phase was the most stressful part.
13. We had to rewrite the documentation three times.
14. There were also several misunderstandings along the way.
15. Some of them were caused by poor communication.
16. Others came from cultural differences in the team.
17. In the end, the achievement was a real source of pride.
18. My manager wrote a wonderful recommendation for me.
19. She praised my reliability and my problem-solving skills.
20. After that, I was promoted to a leadership role.
21. The responsibility was much greater, of course.
22. But the satisfaction was also much greater.
23. I learned that kindness is more valuable than talent.
24. And that flexibility beats brilliance in the long run.
25. Honestly, the friendship with my colleagues is what I treasure most.
26. We still meet for coffee even though I changed jobs.
27. My ex-boss became a real friend and mentor.
28. Looking back, every mistake was a hidden improvement.
29. Even the failures led to unexpected discoveries.
30. That is the most important lesson of my career so far.
