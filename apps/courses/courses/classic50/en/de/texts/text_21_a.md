# Hörtext zu Lektion 21: Present Perfect — life experience
**Thema: Lebenserfahrung, Reisen, „jemals" und „nie"**

---

## Hörtext

1. I have travelled a lot in my life.
2. I have been to fifteen countries.
3. I have visited Paris three times.
4. I have never been to Asia.
5. Have you ever been to America?
6. Yes, I have been there twice.
7. My sister has lived in Spain for a year.
8. She has learned Spanish very well.
9. We have tried many different foods.
10. I have eaten sushi only once.
11. My brother has never tried Indian food.
12. He has always eaten European food.
13. Have you ever met a famous person?
14. I have met a writer at a book fair.
15. She has signed my book.
16. I have kept it on my shelf.
17. My friend has climbed a mountain.
18. He has reached the top three times.
19. I have never climbed anything high.
20. I have always been afraid of heights.
21. Have you ever swum in the ocean?
22. I have swum in the Black Sea.
23. I have never seen the Atlantic.
24. My parents have visited Australia.
25. They have taken many photos there.
26. I have looked at all the photos.
27. I have learned a lot from their trip.
28. Have you ever flown business class?
29. No, I have never flown business class.
30. But I have flown many times in my life.
