# Text zu Lektion 13: did in Fragen und Verneinungen
**Thema: Gespräch mit einem Freund über das Wochenende**

---

## Hörtext zum Wiederholen

1. Hi, Tom, how was your weekend?
2. Did you do anything interesting?
3. Yes, on Saturday I went to a concert.
4. Did you go alone?
5. No, I didn't. My sister came with me.
6. Who played at the concert?
7. A young rock band from our city.
8. Did you like them?
9. Yes, I liked them a lot. They were great.
10. Did they play any of their new songs?
11. They played two new songs and many old ones.
12. After the concert, did you go home?
13. No, we didn't go home right away.
14. First we went to a small restaurant near the hall.
15. We ate burgers and drank cold lemonade.
16. Then a friend called us and invited us to a party.
17. Did you go to the party?
18. Yes, we did, but we didn't stay very long.
19. We came home around midnight.
20. Did you sleep well after such a long day?
21. No, I didn't sleep well at all.
22. I felt tired but my head was full of music.
23. On Sunday I didn't get up early.
24. I didn't do any work.
25. I just read a book and drank tea.
26. Did you watch any football on TV?
27. No, I didn't have time for that.
28. My sister and I cooked a big lunch together.
29. We made soup, salad and an apple cake.
30. We didn't go out again — we just talked and laughed at home.
