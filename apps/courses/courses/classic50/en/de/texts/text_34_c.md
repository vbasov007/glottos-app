# Text zur Lektion 34: A short history of the museum
**Thema: Passiv in allen Zeiten — Geschichte eines Gebäudes**

---

## Hörtext zum Wiederholen

1. The building was designed in the eighteenth century.
2. It was originally used as a private residence.
3. The first stone was laid in 1742.
4. The work was completed twelve years later.
5. The house was owned by a wealthy merchant family.
6. Many famous guests were invited there over the years.
7. The gardens were planted by a French designer.
8. The interior was decorated by Italian artists.
9. In 1820, the building was severely damaged by a fire.
10. The family was forced to sell the house.
11. The estate was bought by the city in 1840.
12. It was later converted into a public library.
13. By 1900, the library had been visited by thousands of people.
14. New rooms were added in the early twentieth century.
15. During the war, the books were moved to a safer location.
16. The building was used as a hospital for several years.
17. After the war, the books were brought back.
18. In 1968, the house was officially declared a museum.
19. Important paintings were donated by local collectors.
20. The collection has been expanded ever since.
21. Today, the museum is visited by tourists from many countries.
22. Tickets can be bought online or at the entrance.
23. Children are admitted free of charge.
24. Guided tours are offered in five languages.
25. New exhibitions are organised every three months.
26. The next exhibition will be opened in October.
27. The building is now being renovated.
28. Some rooms have been temporarily closed.
29. The work is expected to be finished by spring.
30. The museum is loved and respected by everyone in the city.
