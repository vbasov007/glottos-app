# Hörtext zu Lektion 22: Present Perfect vs Past Simple
**Thema: Karriere und Erfolge — das Zweischritt-Modell**

---

## Hörtext

1. Have you ever met a famous writer?
2. Yes, I have met one.
3. I met him at a book festival in 2018.
4. He signed my book and we talked for ten minutes.
5. Have you ever won a competition?
6. Yes, I have won two competitions.
7. I won my first prize when I was sixteen.
8. It was a painting competition.
9. I have painted since I was a child.
10. My mother taught me to paint.
11. She has worked as an art teacher for thirty years.
12. She started teaching in 1994.
13. Have you ever travelled to a difficult country?
14. Yes, I went to Mongolia ten years ago.
15. I have not been back since then.
16. But I have wanted to return for years.
17. The journey was hard but amazing.
18. I have seen many photos of the country online.
19. Two of my friends visited it last summer.
20. They have shown me their pictures.
21. Have you ever spoken in front of a big audience?
22. Yes, I gave a speech at a conference last May.
23. There were two hundred people in the room.
24. I had prepared for weeks.
25. I have given three speeches since then.
26. Each one has been easier than the last.
27. Have you ever changed careers completely?
28. I worked as an engineer for ten years.
29. Then I left engineering and became a teacher.
30. I have taught now for five years and I love it.
