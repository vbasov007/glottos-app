# Text zu Lektion 20: Zusammenhängender Text
**Thema: Mein normaler und mein heutiger Tag**

---

## Hörtext

1. My name is Sergey and I live in Berlin.
2. I am thirty years old and I work in an office.
3. Every morning I wake up at seven.
4. I usually take a quick shower and have breakfast.
5. For breakfast I often eat eggs and drink coffee.
6. I usually go to work by metro.
7. The metro station is near my house.
8. My office is in the centre of the city.
9. I work with five colleagues in a small team.
10. We often have meetings in the morning.
11. I always have lunch at one o'clock.
12. There is a small cafe near the office.
13. The food there is cheap and tasty.
14. In the afternoon I have many emails to answer.
15. I usually leave the office at six.
16. But today is a special day.
17. Today I'm working from home because it's a holiday.
18. At the moment I'm sitting in my kitchen.
19. I'm drinking tea and looking at the trees outside.
20. The weather is beautiful and the sun is shining.
21. Yesterday it was cold and grey.
22. Yesterday I worked late and felt very tired.
23. But today I feel relaxed and happy.
24. Tomorrow I'm going to meet my best friend.
25. We are going to visit a new museum.
26. After that we will have dinner in a Greek restaurant.
27. On Sunday I'm going to call my parents.
28. They live in a small town near Hamburg.
29. I miss them, but I see them often enough.
30. Life is sometimes difficult, but I really like my life.
