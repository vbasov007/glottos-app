# Text zu Lektion 8: Plural und zählbar vs unzählbar
**Thema: Frühstück für die ganze Familie**

---

## Hörtext

1. We are a big family.
2. There are six people in our house.
3. Two men, two women and two children.
4. Every morning we have breakfast together.
5. The children eat eggs and bread.
6. They love butter and cheese on their bread.
7. My father drinks coffee with milk.
8. My mother drinks tea with sugar.
9. My brother loves fruit juice.
10. He always has a glass of orange juice.
11. I usually drink water in the morning.
12. Cold water is good before food.
13. We eat a lot of fruit.
14. There are apples, bananas and oranges on the table.
15. My sister loves bananas.
16. She eats two bananas every day.
17. The children don't like vegetables much.
18. But they love tomatoes and carrots.
19. My mother says, "Children must eat vegetables!"
20. So we eat tomato salad with bread.
21. Sometimes we have fish for breakfast.
22. Fish is healthy and tasty.
23. My father doesn't eat meat.
24. He says meat is not good for him.
25. After breakfast, the men go to work.
26. The women and the children stay at home.
27. The children play with their toys.
28. They have many toys: cars, books and a ball.
29. We have a happy family.
30. Family is the most important thing in life.
