# Text zu Lektion 19: Adverbien
**Thema: Bei der Arbeit — Gespräch über Gewohnheiten**

---

## Hörtext

1. How often do you come to the office?
2. I usually come three times a week.
3. I work from home on Mondays and Fridays.
4. Do you always start at nine?
5. No, I sometimes start at eight, sometimes at ten.
6. My boss is very flexible.
7. He never asks where I am.
8. He only cares about results.
9. I work hard, so he is happy.
10. Do you often have meetings?
11. We usually have one meeting a day.
12. The meetings are sometimes quite long.
13. They are rarely shorter than thirty minutes.
14. Our new manager speaks slowly and clearly.
15. Our old manager talked very fast.
16. I always understand the new manager better.
17. How often do you travel for work?
18. I sometimes travel to Berlin or Vienna.
19. I rarely travel to Asia.
20. Last year I went to Tokyo, but it is too far.
21. Do you usually fly business class?
22. No, the company is too small for that.
23. We always fly economy class.
24. The flights are often very tiring.
25. I sleep badly on planes.
26. When I arrive, I am usually really tired.
27. I always need one day to relax.
28. After that, I work normally.
29. I really like my job, even when it is hard.
30. I am always happy to come back home.
