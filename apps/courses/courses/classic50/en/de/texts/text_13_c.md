# Text zu Lektion 13: did in Fragen und Verneinungen
**Thema: Die verlorene Geldbörse — ein kurzer Krimi**

---

## Hörtext zum Wiederholen

1. Last Tuesday I lost my wallet.
2. I didn't know where I left it.
3. First I checked my bag, but it wasn't there.
4. Did I leave it at home? No, I didn't.
5. Did I drop it on the street? Maybe.
6. I tried to remember every place I went that day.
7. In the morning I went to a café.
8. I bought a coffee and a small cake.
9. Did I pay with cash or with my card?
10. I paid with cash, so I took my wallet out.
11. After the café I went to the post office.
12. I sent a parcel to my parents.
13. Did I use my wallet there? Yes, I did.
14. Then I went to a bookshop near the park.
15. I bought a new book about Italy.
16. Did I have my wallet when I left the shop?
17. I didn't remember exactly.
18. I called the café first. They didn't find any wallet.
19. I called the post office. They said no, they didn't see one.
20. Finally I called the bookshop.
21. A kind woman answered the phone.
22. She asked me what colour the wallet was.
23. I said it was dark brown with a small zip.
24. She said yes, she found one near the door.
25. Did I feel happy? Of course I did!
26. I took a bus and went to the shop right away.
27. The woman gave me my wallet.
28. Nothing was missing — not even a single coin.
29. I thanked her many times and bought her a coffee.
30. I didn't lose my wallet again — I now check my bag every hour.
