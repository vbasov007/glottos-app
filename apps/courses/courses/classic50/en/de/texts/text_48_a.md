# Text A zu Lektion 48: Natürlicher Rhythmus und schwache Formen
**Thema: Kurze flüssige Sätze — Training von Rhythmus und Verschmelzungen**

---

## Text zum Hören und Wiederholen

1. I'm going to the shop to buy some bread.
2. She's been working there for nearly five years.
3. We would have come, but the car broke down.
4. He told me he couldn't help me with the project.
5. They've been waiting at the station for an hour.
6. What are you doing at the weekend?
7. I don't know what to say, honestly.
8. Let me think about it for a minute.
9. You should have called me much earlier.
10. I want to learn how to cook properly.
11. Could you turn off the light, please?
12. Don't forget to lock the door when you leave.
13. I'll pick you up at the airport tomorrow.
14. He doesn't really like this kind of music.
15. We could have done it together last week.
16. There isn't much we can do about it now.
17. I would like to order a coffee and a sandwich.
18. She's just popped out for a quick walk.
19. They've been to Italy more times than I can count.
20. I've got to finish this report before five.
21. Have you seen my keys anywhere by any chance?
22. It looks like it's going to rain again tonight.
23. He's been trying to call you all morning.
24. We're thinking about moving to a bigger flat.
25. She didn't want to talk about it at all.
26. I would have helped you if you had asked.
27. There's been a problem with the heating system.
28. Could you pass me the salt and pepper, please?
29. He said he'd be here by half past seven.
30. I'm afraid I have to go in a few minutes.
