# Text zu Lektion 2: A family evening

**Thema: Familienabend, einfache Handlungen und Gegenstände zu Hause**

---

## Hörtext

1. It is evening.
2. The family is at home.
3. Dad opens the door.
4. Dad comes in.
5. Mum is in the kitchen.
6. Mum cooks dinner.
7. The boy reads a book.
8. The girl draws a picture.
9. Dad says hello.
10. The children say hello.
11. Mum says hello too.
12. Dad sits at the table.
13. Mum brings food.
14. The family eats dinner.
15. Dinner is bread, cheese and apples.
16. The children drink milk.
17. Dad drinks tea.
18. Mum drinks coffee.
19. After dinner, the girl helps Mum.
20. The girl carries the plates.
21. The boy reads the book again.
22. Dad opens the window.
23. The room is fresh.
24. The boy asks a question.
25. Dad answers the question.
26. The girl asks a question too.
27. Mum answers the question.
28. The family is happy.
29. The children go to bed.
30. Mum and Dad say good night.
