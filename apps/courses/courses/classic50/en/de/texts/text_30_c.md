# Hörtext zu Lektion 30: A letter from a long journey
**Thema: zusammenhängender mehrteiliger Text — Reiseeindrücke**

---

## Text zum Hören und Nachsprechen

1. Dear Anna, I am finally writing to you from a small town in northern Spain.
2. I have been travelling for almost three weeks.
3. By the time I get back home, I will have visited eleven cities.
4. The trip has already changed me more than I expected.
5. I left Berlin on the fifth of June.
6. The plane that took me to Madrid was almost empty.
7. I remember sitting by the window as we were flying over the mountains.
8. By the time I arrived in Madrid, I had not slept for almost twenty hours.
9. However, the energy of the city woke me up immediately.
10. I spent four days in Madrid, where I walked from morning until late at night.
11. The museum that impressed me most was the Prado.
12. I had read about its paintings for years, but seeing them was something else entirely.
13. After Madrid, I took a slow train to the south.
14. The train, which crossed dry yellow plains for hours, gave me time to think.
15. In Seville, I met a young French woman whose name was Camille.
16. She had been travelling for two months and knew Spain better than most Spaniards.
17. We spent three days exploring narrow streets that smelled of orange blossom.
18. Although we have known each other for only ten days, we are now real friends.
19. We will probably meet again in Paris next year.
20. Now I am in a small fishing village whose name I cannot even pronounce.
21. The houses are white, the sea is wild, and the food is simple.
22. The family who runs my small hotel makes me feel like a long-lost relative.
23. Their grandmother, who is ninety years old, has lived in this village all her life.
24. She tells me stories about a Spain that does not exist anymore.
25. In a few days, I am taking a ferry to a small island where I have never been.
26. By the end of the trip, I will have travelled by plane, train, bus, ferry and on foot.
27. I am going to write a long article about everything I have seen.
28. The article, which I am already planning in my head, will probably take months to finish.
29. I hope you are well, and I hope we will see each other very soon after I return.
30. With love and many photos to share, your old friend, Mark.
