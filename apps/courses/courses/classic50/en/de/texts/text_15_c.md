# Text zu Lektion 15: Zukunft — will und be going to
**Thema: Die neue Wohnung — Umzugsplan**

---

## Hörtext zum Wiederholen

1. We are going to move to a new flat next month.
2. The flat is in a quiet part of the city.
3. It will be a big change for our family.
4. The new place is bigger and brighter than the old one.
5. The children will have their own bedrooms.
6. My daughter is going to paint her walls light blue.
7. My son will probably choose a darker colour.
8. The kitchen is going to be the heart of the flat.
9. We are going to buy a new table and six chairs.
10. I will cook our first dinner there next Saturday.
11. My wife is going to invite both grandmothers.
12. They will see the flat for the first time.
13. I think they will love it.
14. Next week we are going to start packing our things.
15. We will pack the books first.
16. Then we are going to pack the kitchen.
17. The clothes will be the last thing in the boxes.
18. I am going to ask a friend to help us move.
19. He has a small van and he will probably drive it.
20. On moving day, we will get up very early.
21. We are going to carry boxes for many hours.
22. The children will help with the small things.
23. The cat is going to hide somewhere — she always does.
24. I think we will find her behind the sofa.
25. In the evening we will be tired but happy.
26. We are going to order pizza for our first dinner.
27. We will eat it on the floor, with no table yet.
28. After dinner the children will fall asleep quickly.
29. My wife and I are going to sit by the window.
30. We will talk about the new life that is going to start tomorrow.
