# Text zu Lektion 4: My family

**Thema: Familie, Beziehungen, wer mit wem verwandt ist**

---

## Hörtext

1. This is my family.
2. My father is Ivan.
3. My mother is Maria.
4. They are my parents.
5. I have a brother and a sister.
6. My brother is Mike.
7. He is twenty years old.
8. My sister is Anna.
9. She is sixteen.
10. We are three children.
11. My grandfather is Peter.
12. His wife is my grandmother.
13. Her name is Olga.
14. They are old but happy.
15. My uncle is my father's brother.
16. His name is Boris.
17. His wife is my aunt.
18. Her name is Lena.
19. Their son is my cousin.
20. His name is Sasha.
21. We have a dog too.
22. Its name is Rex.
23. Rex is big and friendly.
24. My family is in Moscow.
25. Our house is small but warm.
26. My father's car is old.
27. My mother's car is new.
28. I love my family.
29. They love me too.
30. This is our story.
