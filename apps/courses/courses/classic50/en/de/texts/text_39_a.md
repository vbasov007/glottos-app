# Text zu Lektion 39: A typical day
**Thema: Feste Artikelwendungen — einfache Alltagserzählung**

---

## Text zum Hören und Wiederholen

1. I wake up at seven in the morning.
2. I stay in bed for a few minutes.
3. Then I go to the kitchen and make breakfast.
4. After breakfast, I go to work.
5. I usually go to work by bus.
6. Sometimes I walk to work on foot.
7. At work, I have many meetings.
8. We start meetings on time.
9. At noon, I have lunch with colleagues.
10. After lunch, I drink coffee.
11. In the afternoon, I focus on reports.
12. Time passes quickly when I'm busy.
13. I leave the office at six.
14. On my way home, I think about dinner.
15. I go home by bus too.
16. At home, I cook something simple.
17. In the evening, I play the guitar.
18. I love music. It helps me relax.
19. Sometimes I read a book in bed.
20. I usually fall asleep before midnight.
21. On Saturdays, I'm not at work.
22. I go to the park with friends.
23. We play football in the afternoon.
24. The young men in our group are very fast.
25. After the game, we sit on a bench.
26. We talk about life, work, and family.
27. Sometimes we go to the cinema together.
28. On Sundays, I stay at home and rest.
29. I love the quiet of Sunday morning.
30. By Monday, I'm ready for work again.
