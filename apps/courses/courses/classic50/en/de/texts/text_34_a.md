# Text zur Lektion 34: How chocolate is made
**Thema: Prozessbeschreibung — Passiv im Present Simple**

---

## Hörtext zum Wiederholen

1. Chocolate is made from cocoa beans.
2. The beans are grown in tropical countries.
3. First, the pods are picked by hand.
4. Then the beans are taken out of the pods.
5. The beans are left in the sun for several days.
6. After that, they are dried and packed.
7. The packed beans are sent to factories.
8. At the factory, the beans are roasted at high temperatures.
9. The shells are removed from the inside.
10. The inside part is called the nib.
11. The nibs are ground into a thick liquid.
12. This liquid is called cocoa mass.
13. Sugar and milk are added at this stage.
14. The mixture is mixed for many hours.
15. The chocolate is then heated and cooled in cycles.
16. This process is called tempering.
17. Tempering gives chocolate its shine.
18. After tempering, the chocolate is poured into moulds.
19. The moulds are cooled until the chocolate is solid.
20. The bars are then wrapped in paper.
21. Each bar is checked for quality.
22. Bad bars are removed from the line.
23. Good bars are packed into boxes.
24. The boxes are loaded onto trucks.
25. The trucks are sent to shops all over the world.
26. In the shops, the chocolate is sold to customers.
27. Around the holidays, more chocolate is bought than usual.
28. Special shapes are made for Easter and Christmas.
29. Every step is controlled by the factory.
30. This is how good chocolate is made.
