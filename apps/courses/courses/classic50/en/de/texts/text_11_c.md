# Text zu Lektion 11: Present Continuous
**Thema: Anruf bei einem Freund — was machst du gerade?**

---

## Hörtext zum Wiederholen

1. Hello, Mark, it's Anna.
2. Where are you at the moment?
3. I am calling from a café.
4. What are you doing right now?
5. I am working on my laptop.
6. I am writing a long email to my boss.
7. Are you alone, or are you with someone?
8. My sister is sitting next to me.
9. She is reading a magazine and drinking coffee.
10. Outside, it is raining a little.
11. People are walking past with umbrellas.
12. A bus is stopping near the window.
13. Some students are getting off the bus.
14. They are laughing and talking loudly.
15. The waiter is bringing me another tea.
16. I am not eating anything — I am not hungry.
17. What is happening at your office?
18. My colleagues are having a meeting.
19. They are talking about a new project.
20. Our manager is showing slides on a screen.
21. I am listening, but I am also looking at my phone.
22. My dog is staying at home today.
23. My neighbour is taking care of him.
24. She is probably feeding him right now.
25. I think she is also walking him in the garden.
26. So, are we still meeting this evening?
27. Yes, I am still planning to come.
28. I am leaving the café in five minutes.
29. I am taking the metro and going straight there.
30. See you soon — I am hanging up now.
