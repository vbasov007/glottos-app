# Text zu Lektion 38: A rainy weekend
**Thema: Konnektoren der Zeit, des Grundes und des Kontrasts — einfache Alltagserzählung**

---

## Text zum Hören und Wiederholen

1. Last weekend was supposed to be sunny.
2. However, it rained from Friday to Sunday.
3. Because of the rain, we changed all our plans.
4. We had wanted to go to the park.
5. But since it was raining, we stayed home.
6. Although we were disappointed, we tried to make the best of it.
7. While my brother watched a film, I read a book.
8. As I was reading, I heard thunder outside.
9. The thunder was so loud that the dog hid under the bed.
10. Despite the noise, my brother kept watching his film.
11. When the rain finally stopped, we went outside for a walk.
12. The streets were wet, so we wore our boots.
13. We walked until we reached the river.
14. By the time we got there, the sun had come out.
15. Because the weather was nicer, more people came out too.
16. We sat on a bench so that we could rest for a while.
17. As soon as the clouds returned, we decided to head back.
18. Even though we got a little wet, we enjoyed the walk.
19. After we came home, my mother made hot tea.
20. Since we were cold, the tea was perfect.
21. While we drank the tea, we talked about old memories.
22. Although the weekend was rainy, it was good.
23. Because we stayed inside, we had time to talk.
24. I'd been wanting to talk to my brother for weeks.
25. Despite our different schedules, we finally connected.
26. As the evening went on, the rain started again.
27. We didn't mind, because we were warm and dry.
28. Before we went to bed, we played a board game.
29. I lost every round, even though I tried my best.
30. By the time we slept, I had stopped caring about the score.
