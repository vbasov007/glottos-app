# Text zu Lektion 16: Modalverben — can, must, should
**Thema: Fähigkeiten und Regeln**

---

## Hörtext

1. I can swim very well.
2. My brother can play the guitar.
3. She can speak three languages.
4. We can't come to the party tonight.
5. Can you drive a car?
6. Yes, I can. I can drive a car and a motorcycle.
7. He can cook, but he can't bake a cake.
8. Children must go to school.
9. You must wear a seatbelt in the car.
10. Students must arrive on time.
11. We must be quiet in the library.
12. You mustn't smoke in this building.
13. I must finish my homework today.
14. She must catch the early train.
15. They must follow the rules.
16. You should drink more water.
17. He should call his mother every week.
18. We should leave now, or we will be late.
19. You shouldn't eat so much chocolate.
20. They shouldn't work so many hours.
21. Should I take an umbrella with me?
22. What should I do about my headache?
23. You should see a doctor.
24. Can I ask you a question?
25. Of course you can.
26. Must I answer this letter today?
27. No, you can answer it tomorrow.
28. He can speak English, but he can't write it well.
29. You must always tell the truth.
30. We should help our friends when they need us.
