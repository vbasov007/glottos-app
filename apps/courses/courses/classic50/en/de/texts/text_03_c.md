# Text zu Lektion 3: How are you today?

**Thema: Gespräch über Zustände und Gefühle**

---

## Hörtext

1. Hello, Anna.
2. Hello, Tom. How are you?
3. I am fine, thank you.
4. And you? How are you today?
5. I am very tired.
6. Why are you tired?
7. The work is hard.
8. I am busy all day.
9. Sorry to hear that.
10. Is Mike here today?
11. No, he is not here.
12. He is at home.
13. He is sick.
14. Is it serious?
15. No, it is not serious.
16. He is just cold.
17. Where is Maria?
18. She is in the kitchen.
19. She is hungry.
20. The food is ready.
21. The food is hot.
22. Are you hungry too?
23. Yes, I am very hungry.
24. The coffee is ready too.
25. Thank you. The coffee is good.
26. The day is long.
27. But we are happy.
28. We are all friends here.
29. That is good.
30. See you tomorrow.
