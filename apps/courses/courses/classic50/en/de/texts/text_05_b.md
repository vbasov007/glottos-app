# Text zu Lektion 5: My friend Tom

**Thema: Erzählung über die Gewohnheiten einer anderen Person (3. Person, Fokus auf -s)**

---

## Hörtext

1. My friend Tom lives in London.
2. He works in a small office.
3. He is an engineer.
4. He gets up at six every morning.
5. He drinks a cup of coffee.
6. He reads the news.
7. He goes to work by car.
8. He starts at eight.
9. He works until five.
10. He has lunch at twelve.
11. He eats a salad.
12. He drinks water.
13. After work he goes to the gym.
14. He runs for thirty minutes.
15. He lifts weights.
16. Then he goes home.
17. He cooks dinner.
18. He eats fish and rice.
19. He watches the news on TV.
20. He reads a book in the evening.
21. He calls his family on Sundays.
22. He speaks with his mother.
23. He plays football on Saturdays.
24. He likes his job.
25. He loves his life in London.
26. He has many friends there.
27. He visits his family twice a year.
28. He always sends them photos.
29. He goes to bed at eleven.
30. Tom is a happy man.
