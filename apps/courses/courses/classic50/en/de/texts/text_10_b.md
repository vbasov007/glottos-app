# Text zu Lektion 10: Adjektive
**Thema: Ein wunderschöner Tag**

---

## Hörtext

1. Today is a beautiful day.
2. The sky is blue and the sun is bright.
3. The weather is warm but not hot.
4. I have a free day, and I am very happy.
5. I open the big window in my bedroom.
6. The air is fresh and clean.
7. I make a hot cup of strong coffee.
8. The coffee is dark and very good.
9. I eat fresh bread with sweet butter.
10. The bread is soft and tasty.
11. After breakfast, I take a long walk.
12. I walk in a big quiet park near my house.
13. The park is beautiful in the morning.
14. There are tall green trees everywhere.
15. There are bright red and yellow flowers.
16. A small grey bird sits on a low branch.
17. The bird is small but loud.
18. I see a young couple with a happy little dog.
19. The dog is brown and very friendly.
20. I sit on an old wooden bench.
21. The bench is hard but very nice.
22. I read an interesting book.
23. The book is long but not difficult.
24. Time passes slowly. I feel calm.
25. In the afternoon, I meet a good friend.
26. We go to a small cosy cafe.
27. The cafe has soft music and bright lights.
28. We drink fresh juice and eat warm cake.
29. We talk about our busy lives and big dreams.
30. It is a simple but perfect day.
