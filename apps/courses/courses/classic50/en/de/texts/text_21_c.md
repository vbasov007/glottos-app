# Hörtext zu Lektion 21: Present Perfect — dialogue (job interview)
**Thema: Dialog im Vorstellungsgespräch — Erfahrung und Erfolge**

---

## Hörtext

1. Good morning. Please sit down.
2. Thank you for inviting me.
3. So, tell me about yourself.
4. I have studied marketing at university.
5. I have worked in three different companies.
6. I have lived in two countries.
7. Have you ever managed a team?
8. Yes, I have managed a team of five people.
9. We have launched two big projects together.
10. How long have you been in this field?
11. I have worked in marketing for seven years.
12. Have you ever travelled for business?
13. Yes, I have visited clients in five countries.
14. I have spoken at three international conferences.
15. That is impressive. Have you learned any new skills recently?
16. Yes, I have just finished a course in data analysis.
17. I have also improved my Spanish.
18. Have you ever worked with our products?
19. I haven't used them personally, but I have read about them.
20. I have followed your company for years.
21. Why have you left your last job?
22. The company has changed direction.
23. I have decided to look for new challenges.
24. Have you applied to other companies?
25. Yes, I have spoken to two other firms.
26. But your company has always been my first choice.
27. Excellent. Do you have any questions for us?
28. Yes, what training have you offered new employees?
29. We have built a strong training programme.
30. Thank you. I have really enjoyed this conversation.
