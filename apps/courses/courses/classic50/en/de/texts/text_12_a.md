# Text zu Lektion 12: Past Simple
**Thema: Mein gestriger Tag**

---

## Hörtext zum Wiederholen

1. Yesterday was a busy day for me.
2. I got up at seven o'clock.
3. I had a quick breakfast in the kitchen.
4. I drank a cup of black coffee.
5. I ate two pieces of toast with butter.
6. After breakfast I went to work.
7. I took the bus to the city centre.
8. The bus was full, but I found a seat.
9. I arrived at the office at half past eight.
10. I worked on a long report all morning.
11. At one o'clock I had lunch with a colleague.
12. We talked about our weekend plans.
13. After lunch I answered many emails.
14. I called two clients on the phone.
15. I finished work at six in the evening.
16. On the way home, I stopped at a small shop.
17. I bought bread, milk and some fruit.
18. I came home around seven o'clock.
19. My wife was already at home.
20. She made a simple dinner for us.
21. We ate pasta with vegetables.
22. After dinner I read a book for an hour.
23. My wife watched a film in the living room.
24. Our cat slept on the sofa next to her.
25. At ten o'clock I had a hot shower.
26. I felt tired but happy.
27. I went to bed at half past ten.
28. I read a few pages of my book in bed.
29. Then I turned off the light.
30. I fell asleep in a few minutes.
