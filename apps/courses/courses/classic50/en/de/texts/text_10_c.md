# Text zu Lektion 10: Adjektive
**Thema: Zwei verschiedene Häuser — Dialog**

---

## Hörtext

1. Hi Anna! How is your new house?
2. Hi Mark! My new house is very nice.
3. Is it big or small?
4. It is a big white house with a beautiful garden.
5. My old flat was small and dark.
6. The new house is bright and warm.
7. How many rooms are there?
8. There are four big rooms and a small bathroom.
9. The kitchen is large and very modern.
10. The living room has tall windows.
11. There is a soft brown sofa and an old wooden table.
12. Is the bedroom comfortable?
13. Yes, my bedroom is very comfortable.
14. The bed is big and soft.
15. There are warm yellow curtains on the windows.
16. The walls are light blue.
17. What about the garden?
18. The garden is small but beautiful.
19. There are tall green trees and some red flowers.
20. There is also a low wooden bench.
21. It is a quiet, peaceful place.
22. And how is your house, Mark?
23. My house is old and small, but I love it.
24. It is in a nice quiet street.
25. The walls are thick and the windows are narrow.
26. But the house is very warm in winter.
27. The garden is wild and free.
28. There are no flowers, only tall grass.
29. My old cat loves this garden.
30. Different houses, different lives — but both are good homes.
