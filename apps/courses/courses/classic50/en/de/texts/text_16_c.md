# Text zu Lektion 16: Modalverben — can, must, should
**Thema: Dialog beim Arzt und Gesundheitstipps**

---

## Hörtext

1. Doctor, I don't feel well.
2. What's the problem? Can you describe it?
3. I have a headache and I can't sleep.
4. How long have you had these problems?
5. For about two weeks. What should I do?
6. You should rest more and drink more water.
7. You shouldn't drink so much coffee.
8. Can I still go to work?
9. You can work, but you must take breaks.
10. Should I take any medicine?
11. You should take this medicine three times a day.
12. You mustn't drink alcohol with this medicine.
13. Can I exercise?
14. You can walk, but you shouldn't run.
15. I can't eat spicy food now.
16. That's good. You shouldn't eat spicy food.
17. You should eat more fruit and vegetables.
18. Must I come back next week?
19. Yes, you must come back on Tuesday.
20. Can my wife come with me?
21. Of course she can.
22. You should also do some light exercise.
23. I can't go to the gym.
24. You can walk in the park every morning.
25. You must sleep at least eight hours.
26. Should I stop drinking coffee completely?
27. You shouldn't stop, but you should drink less.
28. Healthy food can help you feel better.
29. You must take care of yourself.
30. Thank you, doctor. I will follow your advice.
