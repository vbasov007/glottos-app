# Text zu Lektion 11: Present Continuous
**Thema: Was gerade jetzt passiert**

---

## Hörtext zum Wiederholen

1. I am sitting at my desk.
2. I am reading an English book.
3. My sister is studying in her room.
4. She is writing in her notebook.
5. Our mother is cooking dinner.
6. She is making soup and bread.
7. Our father is watching the news.
8. The dog is sleeping on the floor.
9. Outside, it is raining a little.
10. The children next door are playing in the garden.
11. I can hear them. They are laughing.
12. The phone is ringing now.
13. My brother is answering the phone.
14. He is talking to his friend.
15. They are planning to meet later.
16. I am drinking a cup of tea.
17. My tea is hot, but I like it that way.
18. The cat is looking at me from the window.
19. It is watching the rain.
20. My laptop is open in front of me.
21. I am typing a short message.
22. My friend is waiting for my reply.
23. He is sitting in a café in town.
24. He is drinking coffee and reading a magazine.
25. Now my mother is calling me for dinner.
26. I am closing my book.
27. I am standing up from my chair.
28. I am walking to the kitchen.
29. We are all sitting around the table.
30. Right now, we are starting our family dinner.
