# Text A zu Lektion 46: Wortfamilien in Aktion
**Thema: Berufliche Geschichten und persönliche Überlegungen — Wortbildung im natürlichen Text**

---

## Text zum Hören und Wiederholen

1. The decision was difficult, but it was the right one.
2. Her achievement surprised everyone in the company.
3. The development of this project took almost three years.
4. His kindness made the difficult day much easier.
5. We need a clear improvement before the next review.
6. The disagreement between the two teams was serious.
7. The misunderstanding came from a poorly written email.
8. They completely underestimated the complexity of the work.
9. Her flexibility is the main reason for her success.
10. The implementation of the new policy starts next month.
11. His responsibility for the team is very clear.
12. Friendship between colleagues makes the office feel warmer.
13. The interviewer asked three difficult questions.
14. The interviewee answered every question with confidence.
15. Our cooperation with the partner company is very productive.
16. The recommendation came from a trusted source.
17. She has remarkable creativity for solving everyday problems.
18. His weakness is that he doesn't ask for help.
19. The complexity of the legal system surprised the new employees.
20. The reliability of this car is its strongest feature.
21. Unfortunately, the result was completely unpredictable.
22. They had to rewrite the report from the beginning.
23. The misuse of the equipment caused a serious problem.
24. He is the ex-director of the regional office.
25. We need a more sustainable approach to this issue.
26. Her willingness to help impressed everybody.
27. The leadership of the team is in good hands.
28. His statement was both meaningful and powerful.
29. The investigation revealed several useful facts.
30. Her happiness was visible the moment she heard the news.
