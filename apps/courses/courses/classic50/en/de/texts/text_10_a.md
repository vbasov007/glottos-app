# Text zu Lektion 10: Adjektive
**Thema: Meine Familie**

---

## Hörtext

1. I live in a big happy family.
2. My father is a tall and strong man.
3. He has short black hair and brown eyes.
4. He works in a big modern bank.
5. He is a kind and clever person.
6. My mother is a beautiful young woman.
7. She has long brown hair and blue eyes.
8. She is a busy but happy teacher.
9. She works in a small school near our house.
10. I have one brother and one sister.
11. My brother is tall and funny.
12. He has short red hair.
13. He is a good student at a big university.
14. My sister is small and sweet.
15. She has long blonde hair and green eyes.
16. She is only six years old.
17. She is a happy and friendly child.
18. We have a small white dog.
19. The dog is old but very kind.
20. We also have a big black cat.
21. The cat is lazy but very beautiful.
22. We live in a nice old house.
23. The house has a big garden.
24. There are many tall trees in the garden.
25. There are also some beautiful red flowers.
26. Our home is warm and quiet.
27. The kitchen is small but bright.
28. The living room is big and comfortable.
29. I love my family very much.
30. We are different but very close.
