# Hörtext zu Lektion 25: Past Perfect
**Thema: Ein seltsamer Gast — eine Detektivgeschichte mit Vorgeschichte**

---

## Hörtext

1. One evening, an old friend came to visit me.
2. We had not seen each other for ten years.
3. He had moved to another country after university.
4. I had heard from him only twice in all those years.
5. So I was surprised when he called.
6. He had arrived in town that morning.
7. He had read about me in the local newspaper.
8. He wanted to meet for dinner.
9. I prepared a small meal.
10. He came at seven, just as he had promised.
11. He looked different.
12. He had grown a beard.
13. He had also lost a lot of weight.
14. We sat at the table and started talking.
15. He told me he had become a writer.
16. He had published two books in three years.
17. I had not known anything about it.
18. After dinner, he opened his bag.
19. He had brought me a gift.
20. It was a copy of his first novel.
21. He had written my name on the first page.
22. I had not received such a thoughtful gift in years.
23. We talked until late at night.
24. He told me about places he had visited.
25. He told me about people he had met.
26. I told him what had happened in my life.
27. By midnight we had remembered our whole student years.
28. When he left, he promised to write.
29. After he had gone, I read the first chapter of his book.
30. It was about a friendship that had survived ten years.
