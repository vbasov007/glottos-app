# Text zu Lektion 44: A secret revealed — Idiome zu Kommunikation und Beziehungen
**Thema: Familienüberraschung, Täuschung, Versöhnung**

---

## Hörtext

1. My sister Anna was about to get married.
2. We had been planning the wedding for months.
3. The biggest thing was a surprise party for our parents.
4. Only the cousins knew the plan, and they had to keep it quiet.
5. We told them not to spill the beans.
6. Then little Tom let the cat out of the bag at dinner.
7. He told our mother everything, off the top of his head.
8. Anna gave him a look that could kill.
9. But we all knew he had just been pulling our leg before.
10. After all, he was as quiet as a mouse with real secrets.
11. Our mother pretended she had not heard a word.
12. She is as smart as a fox.
13. The next day, she gave Anna a big hug.
14. They talked about the wedding plans openly.
15. Anna decided to face the music about the secret.
16. She apologised, and Mum smiled gently.
17. They were on the same page after a long talk.
18. Then Anna and our cousin Mark had a fight.
19. Mark felt his work for the wedding had been ignored.
20. He said Anna was biting off more than she could chew.
21. Anna had second thoughts about asking him to help.
22. But she did not want to rain on her own parade.
23. The next morning, she called him to bury the hatchet.
24. They agreed to play it by ear from that point on.
25. By the wedding day, everyone was as happy as a clam.
26. The bride looked as pretty as a picture.
27. The groom was over the moon all evening.
28. Mark gave a short speech, and he hit the nail on the head.
29. Everyone agreed it had been a piece of cake in the end.
30. Time flies when family pulls together.
