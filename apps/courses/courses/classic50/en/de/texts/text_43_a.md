# Text zu Lektion 43: A normal day — make, do, take, have im Alltag
**Thema: Tagesablauf — Verbkollokationen in Aktion**

---

## Hörtext

1. I wake up early and take a deep breath.
2. I take a shower and have a quick breakfast.
3. I make a strong cup of coffee for myself.
4. Then I make a few phone calls before work.
5. I take the bus to the office every day.
6. At work, I make decisions all morning.
7. I try not to make any mistakes.
8. Around eleven, I take a short break.
9. I have a chat with my colleagues in the kitchen.
10. We do business with clients from many countries.
11. At lunchtime, I have a small meal.
12. After lunch, I do some research online.
13. I take a few notes for my next report.
14. We are making good progress on the project.
15. At three, we have a quick meeting.
16. The boss gives a short speech about the new plan.
17. He pays a compliment to the engineering team.
18. We make a few suggestions to improve the schedule.
19. We take a chance on a new approach.
20. By five, we have a clear list of next steps.
21. I shut down my laptop and leave the office.
22. On the way home, I do some shopping.
23. I make dinner for my family.
24. We have dinner together and have a long chat.
25. After dinner, I help the kids do their homework.
26. Then I do the dishes while my partner reads.
27. I take a hot shower to relax before bed.
28. I make a small list of tasks for tomorrow.
29. I take one more deep breath and turn off the light.
30. By eleven, I am in a deep sleep.
