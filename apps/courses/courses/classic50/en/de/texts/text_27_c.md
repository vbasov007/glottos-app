# Hörtext zu Lektion 27: The runner
**Thema: Sportlerbiografie — alle Zeiten und Aspekte**

---

## Text zum Hören und Nachsprechen

1. Sarah has always loved running.
2. She started running when she was eight years old.
3. As a child, she ran every morning before school.
4. Her parents thought it was just a phase.
5. But Sarah has been running every day for over thirty years.
6. She has run in more than two hundred races.
7. Last year she won her first marathon.
8. When the race began, she was not feeling well.
9. She had eaten something strange the night before.
10. She had been training hard for six months.
11. By the tenth kilometre, she felt much better.
12. By the twentieth, she was leading the group.
13. By the time she crossed the line, she had broken the local record.
14. Right now Sarah is preparing for the next big event.
15. The race takes place in three weeks.
16. She is training six days a week.
17. She has been running about a hundred kilometres every week.
18. Her coach is helping her with technique and diet.
19. He has trained champions for thirty years.
20. He believes Sarah will win again this year.
21. The race starts at eight in the morning.
22. At nine, Sarah will be passing the halfway point.
23. By eleven, she will have finished the race.
24. By next summer, she will have run in eight international races.
25. After the next race, she is going to take a short break.
26. She is travelling to Greece with her family.
27. They are spending two weeks by the sea.
28. By the end of the trip, she will have rested for the first time in years.
29. Then she will start training again.
30. Running is not just her sport — it has become her whole life.
