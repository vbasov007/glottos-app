# Hörtext zu Lektion 24: Present Perfect Continuous
**Thema: Leben in letzter Zeit — was du gerade machst, womit du beschäftigt bist**

---

## Hörtext

1. How have you been? I haven't seen you for a while.
2. I've been very busy lately.
3. I've been working on a big project at the office.
4. I've been getting home very late every evening.
5. I've been sleeping only six hours a night.
6. I've been drinking too much coffee.
7. My wife has been complaining about my hours.
8. The kids have been asking when I'll come home earlier.
9. But the project is almost finished.
10. I've been telling myself: just one more week.
11. What about you? How have you been spending your time?
12. I've been studying for an exam.
13. I've been reading the same books for a month.
14. I've been taking notes every evening.
15. My head has been hurting from all the studying.
16. But I've been feeling more confident lately.
17. I think I'm ready for the test.
18. Has the weather been good in your city?
19. No, it's been raining for two weeks.
20. I haven't been going out much.
21. I've been watching films at home.
22. I've been cooking more than usual.
23. I've been learning new recipes online.
24. The dog has been getting bored without long walks.
25. He's been sitting by the window for hours.
26. He's been watching the rain like a sad film.
27. We have been waiting for the sun to come back.
28. Tomorrow the forecast is good, finally.
29. I've been planning a long walk with the dog.
30. I've been looking forward to it all week.
