# Text zu Lektion 17: Komparativ und Superlativ
**Thema: Vergleiche im Alltag**

---

## Hörtext

1. My brother is taller than me.
2. My sister is younger than my brother.
3. Today is hotter than yesterday.
4. This book is more interesting than the film.
5. English is easier than Chinese for me.
6. Tea is cheaper than coffee at this cafe.
7. My new phone is faster than my old one.
8. This restaurant is more expensive than the one near my house.
9. The red car is nicer than the blue car.
10. She is the smartest student in our class.
11. He is the tallest player on the team.
12. This is the biggest room in our flat.
13. Mount Everest is the highest mountain in the world.
14. The Nile is one of the longest rivers in the world.
15. Russia is the biggest country in the world.
16. This is the best coffee in the city.
17. That was the worst film of the year.
18. She is the happiest person I know.
19. This is the most beautiful park in our town.
20. My grandfather is the oldest person in our family.
21. Anna is as tall as Maria.
22. My job is as difficult as yours.
23. He is not as fast as his brother.
24. This question is not as easy as it looks.
25. Run as fast as you can!
26. I have less free time than my friends.
27. He has more books than anyone I know.
28. This is the most important day of my life.
29. The further you go, the colder it gets.
30. Better late than never.
