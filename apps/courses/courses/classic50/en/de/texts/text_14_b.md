# Text zu Lektion 14: Artikel — zweiter Anlauf
**Thema: Reise um die Welt**

---

## Hörtext zum Wiederholen

1. Last year I had a long holiday.
2. I decided to travel around Europe.
3. First I went to France, to the capital, Paris.
4. Paris is a beautiful city full of history.
5. I climbed the Eiffel Tower and walked along the Seine.
6. The Seine is the most famous river in France.
7. After France I took a train to Italy.
8. I spent a week in Rome and Florence.
9. The food in Italy is wonderful.
10. I ate pizza, pasta and ice cream every day.
11. From Italy I flew to Greece.
12. Greece is a country of islands.
13. I visited three of them in the Aegean Sea.
14. The sea was the bluest water I have ever seen.
15. I love the sun, and the sun in Greece is strong.
16. Next I went north to Germany.
17. I stayed in Munich for a few days.
18. Beer in Germany is famous, but I prefer water.
19. The streets of Munich are clean and well organised.
20. After Germany I crossed into the Netherlands.
21. The Netherlands is a small country with many bicycles.
22. People here ride bikes more than they drive cars.
23. Amsterdam is a city of canals and museums.
24. I visited the museum of an old Dutch painter.
25. The paintings were beautiful and full of light.
26. My last stop was the UK.
27. London is a busy city, very different from Moscow.
28. I crossed the Thames many times.
29. I drank tea every afternoon and ate cake in a café.
30. In the end I came home with many photos and a happy heart.
