# Текст к уроку 41: A bad day at work — фразовый глагол как рассказ
**Тема: один день в офисе — звонки, встречи, дедлайны (расширенный сценарий)**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Yesterday started badly.
2. My alarm went off at six, as usual.
3. But I had stayed up late watching a film.
4. I almost didn't get up.
5. I dragged myself out of bed and put on the kettle.
6. I switched on the news while I drank my coffee.
7. Then I picked up my bag and went out.
8. I missed the bus, so I had to wait twenty minutes.
9. When I finally got on, all the seats were taken.
10. I got off near the office and ran the last block.
11. I walked into the meeting room ten minutes late.
12. My boss did not look happy.
13. He asked me to look into a problem with the new client.
14. The client had turned down our last proposal.
15. We needed to find out why.
16. I sat down at my desk and went through the emails.
17. Then I came across an old message I had missed.
18. The client had pointed out a serious error.
19. No one had picked up on it at the time.
20. I called him up to apologise.
21. We talked it over for almost an hour.
22. In the end, he agreed to carry on with the project.
23. But he wanted us to set up a weekly call.
24. I wrote it all down and sent a report to my boss.
25. By lunchtime, the situation was sorted out.
26. I went out for a sandwich and a long walk.
27. In the afternoon, I caught up on the rest of my work.
28. I left the office at seven, tired but relieved.
29. On the way home, I ran into a friend.
30. We went out for a quick drink before I went home to sleep.
