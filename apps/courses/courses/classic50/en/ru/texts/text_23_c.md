# Текст к уроку 23: Past Continuous
**Тема: Парк в воскресенье — сценическое описание**

---

## Listening text

1. It was a warm Sunday in May.
2. The sky was blue and the sun was shining brightly.
3. I was walking in the park near my house.
4. Many people were enjoying the good weather.
5. Children were running and laughing.
6. Their parents were watching them from the benches.
7. A young couple was sitting under a tree.
8. They were reading a book together.
9. An old man was feeding the pigeons.
10. The pigeons were jumping around his feet.
11. Two girls were riding their bikes on the path.
12. They were singing a song from a film.
13. A man was throwing a ball to his dog.
14. The dog was running and barking happily.
15. By the pond, some teenagers were taking photos.
16. They were trying funny poses for their pictures.
17. A street musician was playing the guitar.
18. A small group was listening and clapping.
19. I was walking slowly and looking around.
20. Then it started to get cloudy.
21. The wind was getting stronger.
22. People were quickly packing their bags.
23. Mothers were calling their children.
24. The musician was putting his guitar away.
25. The first drops of rain were falling.
26. Everyone was running towards the gates.
27. I was running too, towards a small cafe.
28. Inside, other visitors were already drinking tea.
29. We were all watching the storm through the window.
30. After half an hour, the sun was shining again.
