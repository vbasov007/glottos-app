# Текст к уроку 41: Friends and family — фразовые глаголы в отношениях
**Тема: семья и друзья — get along, look after, take after, give up, come over**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. My sister and I get along really well.
2. We grew up together in a small town.
3. She takes after our mother in many ways.
4. I take after our father, people say.
5. When we were kids, our grandma looked after us.
6. She lived next door and came over every day.
7. Our parents worked late, so she picked us up from school.
8. She never gave up on us, even when we were difficult.
9. Now we look after her, because she is older.
10. Last year she came down with the flu.
11. It took her weeks to get over it.
12. I drop by every weekend to help out.
13. My sister calls her up every evening.
14. Last month we threw her a big birthday party.
15. All her old friends showed up.
16. We came up with the idea of inviting her cousin too.
17. He flew in from Canada for the weekend.
18. We hadn't seen him in ten years.
19. We caught up on everything over dinner.
20. He told us he had set up a small business.
21. He had given up his old job last spring.
22. He said he gets along with his new partners.
23. After dinner, the kids put on a little show.
24. The grown-ups sat down and watched.
25. We took lots of photos and looked through them later.
26. We even ran into a neighbour we used to know.
27. She had moved away years ago.
28. The party went on until midnight.
29. Before bed, my grandma thanked us all.
30. She said it was the best night she could remember.
