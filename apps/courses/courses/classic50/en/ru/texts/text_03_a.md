# Текст к уроку 3: Hello, I am Tom

**Тема: знакомство, представление себя и других**

---

## Текст для аудирования

1. Hello. My name is Tom.
2. I am from Russia.
3. I am twenty years old.
4. I am a student.
5. I am happy today.
6. This is my friend.
7. Her name is Anna.
8. Anna is from England.
9. She is twenty-one.
10. She is a teacher.
11. She is very kind.
12. This is my brother.
13. His name is Mike.
14. He is from America.
15. He is a doctor.
16. He is tall and strong.
17. He is not married.
18. These are my parents.
19. They are from Russia too.
20. They are at home now.
21. My father is an engineer.
22. My mother is a writer.
23. They are happy.
24. We are a small family.
25. Are you a student too?
26. Where are you from?
27. Are you happy here?
28. It is a beautiful day.
29. The sun is warm.
30. Nice to meet you.
