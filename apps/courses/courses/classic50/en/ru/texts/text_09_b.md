# Текст к уроку 9: There is / there are; квантификаторы
**Тема: На кухне — есть или нет?**

---

## Текст для аудирования

1. I want to make a salad for dinner.
2. Let me see what is in the kitchen.
3. There is a fridge near the window.
4. I open it and look inside.
5. There is some milk and a bottle of water.
6. There are five eggs in a small box.
7. There is a piece of cheese on the top shelf.
8. There aren't any tomatoes in the fridge.
9. There aren't any vegetables at all!
10. Oh no, I need vegetables for the salad.
11. Let me look in the cupboard.
12. There is some rice and some sugar.
13. There is a packet of salt and a small bottle of oil.
14. But there aren't any vegetables there either.
15. How much bread do I have? Just a little.
16. There is only one slice of bread on the plate.
17. I need to go to the shop.
18. In the shop there is a lot of fresh fruit.
19. There are many tomatoes, carrots and onions.
20. I take three tomatoes and two carrots.
21. There is also some cheese in the shop.
22. But I have cheese at home, so I don't buy any.
23. Are there any apples in the shop?
24. Yes, there are a lot of red apples.
25. I take four apples for dessert.
26. Now my bag is full.
27. There is some bread, some fruit and many vegetables.
28. Back at home, I make a big salad.
29. There is enough food for the whole family.
30. Everyone is happy!
