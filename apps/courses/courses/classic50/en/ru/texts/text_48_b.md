# Текст B к уроку 48: Вопросы и интонация
**Тема: Wh-вопросы и yes/no-вопросы — мелодика речи**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Where are you going on holiday this summer?
2. Are you ready to leave in five minutes?
3. What time does the next train arrive?
4. Do you know where the post office is?
5. How long have you been studying English?
6. Have you ever been to New York before?
7. Why didn't you tell me about it earlier?
8. Could you give me a hand with these boxes?
9. When did you start working at this company?
10. Is there a cash machine somewhere near here?
11. Who left the door open last night?
12. Can you speak a little more slowly, please?
13. How much does this jacket cost?
14. Did you finish the homework on time?
15. What's the best way to get to the city centre?
16. Are you coming to the party on Saturday?
17. Why are you wearing such a heavy coat today?
18. Does anyone know what time the show starts?
19. Have you tried this restaurant before?
20. Where did you put my phone charger?
21. Is your sister still living in London?
22. What kind of music do you usually listen to?
23. Could you call me back in about ten minutes?
24. When was the last time you saw him?
25. Do you really think it's going to rain tonight?
26. How many languages can your father speak?
27. Is this seat free, by any chance?
28. What did the doctor say about the test results?
29. Are you sure you don't want to come along?
30. Why does everyone always ask me the same question?
