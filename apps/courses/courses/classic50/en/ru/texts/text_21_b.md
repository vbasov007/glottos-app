# Текст к уроку 21: Present Perfect — recent news and results
**Тема: Свежие новости, актуальные результаты, маркеры just / already / yet**

---

## Listening text

1. Listen! I have some news.
2. I have just received a letter.
3. My brother has won a scholarship.
4. He has worked very hard this year.
5. The university has accepted his application.
6. He has already booked his ticket.
7. He hasn't told our parents yet.
8. They will be so proud of him.
9. I have just come back from the office.
10. My boss has given me a new project.
11. I haven't started it yet.
12. But I have already read the brief.
13. It looks interesting and difficult.
14. Have you seen the news today?
15. Yes, I have read everything online.
16. The president has made an important speech.
17. The journalists have asked many questions.
18. I have learned a lot from the article.
19. Wait, I have just remembered something.
20. I have lost my umbrella again.
21. I have left it in the taxi.
22. The driver has already gone.
23. I haven't called the taxi company yet.
24. I will do it after dinner.
25. The kids have eaten all the bread.
26. They haven't washed their hands yet.
27. The dog has run into the garden.
28. He has dug a big hole in the grass.
29. Mum has just come home from work.
30. She hasn't seen the garden yet!
