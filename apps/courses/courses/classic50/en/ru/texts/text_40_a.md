# Текст к уроку 40: A complicated job interview
**Тема: Сложные предложения с относительными, адвербиальными и косвенной речью — нарратив**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Last week I had an interview for a position that I had wanted for months.
2. The company, which is based in the city centre, is one of the biggest in the industry.
3. When I arrived, the receptionist told me that the interviewer was running late.
4. Although I felt nervous, I tried to stay calm.
5. After about twenty minutes, a woman who introduced herself as the head of HR came out.
6. She apologised for the delay and said that she appreciated my patience.
7. The interview, which I had been preparing for since I had applied, lasted almost an hour.
8. She asked me what I knew about the company and why I wanted the job.
9. I said that I had been following their work for a long time and that I admired their values.
10. She seemed pleased with my answer, although she didn't say so directly.
11. Then she asked me a question that I hadn't expected at all.
12. She wanted to know what I would do if I disagreed with a manager's decision.
13. I told her that I would express my concerns honestly but respect the final decision.
14. She nodded and made a note, which I tried not to look at.
15. After more questions, she asked if I had any questions for her.
16. I asked about the team I would be joining and how new employees were supported.
17. She explained that the company had a mentorship programme that had been very successful.
18. By the time the interview ended, I felt much more confident than I had at the start.
19. As I was leaving, she said that I would hear from them within a week.
20. The next few days were difficult because I couldn't stop thinking about it.
21. I kept wondering whether I had said the right things or if I had missed something important.
22. Although I knew that worrying wouldn't help, I couldn't help it.
23. On the fifth day, while I was at work, my phone rang.
24. It was the company calling to tell me that I had got the job.
25. I was so happy that I almost dropped the phone.
26. They told me that I would start in two weeks, after my current notice period.
27. I called my parents immediately, and they were as excited as I was.
28. Looking back, I realise that the interview, despite being stressful, was one of the most useful experiences of my life.
29. If I hadn't prepared so carefully, I'm not sure I would have got the offer.
30. Now I'm starting a new chapter, which I hope will bring everything I've been hoping for.
