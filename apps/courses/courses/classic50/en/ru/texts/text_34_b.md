# Текст к уроку 34: A news bulletin
**Тема: Новостной язык — пассив в Past Simple и Present Perfect**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Two men have been arrested in connection with the robbery.
2. The bank was robbed at around three o'clock this afternoon.
3. No one was hurt during the incident.
4. A small amount of money was taken from the safe.
5. The suspects were caught nearby an hour later.
6. They have been charged with armed robbery.
7. The investigation is being led by the local police.
8. A new park has been opened in the city centre.
9. The park was designed by a famous architect.
10. Three thousand trees were planted last spring.
11. The project was funded by private donations.
12. Free events will be held there every weekend.
13. A new motorway has been built between the two cities.
14. The road was finished six months ahead of schedule.
15. Several bridges were constructed along the route.
16. The first cars were allowed on the motorway yesterday.
17. The mayor was elected last week with a clear majority.
18. He was supported by both major parties.
19. New laws on housing have been announced.
20. Higher taxes are expected next year.
21. The president was interviewed on national television.
22. Important questions were asked about the economy.
23. Several reforms have been proposed by the new government.
24. A new hospital is being built in the north of the city.
25. It will be opened to patients next summer.
26. New equipment has already been ordered.
27. The lost child has been found safe and well.
28. He was found by a passer-by in a nearby park.
29. His parents were informed immediately.
30. Stay with us for more news after the break.
