# Текст к уроку 42: A weekend trip — фразовые глаголы путешествий
**Тема: короткая поездка — аэропорт, отель, друзья**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Last Friday we decided to get away for the weekend.
2. We booked a flight late on Thursday night.
3. My partner packed our bags while I shut down the computer.
4. We set off for the airport at six in the morning.
5. Traffic was light, and we got there in good time.
6. We checked in our luggage and went through security.
7. Our flight took off at half past eight.
8. We had a short stopover in Amsterdam.
9. The next plane got in to Lisbon at one o'clock.
10. A friend picked us up at the airport.
11. He dropped us off at our hotel in the old town.
12. We checked in and went straight out for lunch.
13. In the afternoon, we walked along the river.
14. We ran into another friend by accident.
15. She had moved to Lisbon two years ago.
16. We agreed to meet up for dinner that evening.
17. We took loads of photos and tried local food.
18. After dinner, our friends saw us back to the hotel.
19. The next day, we set off early to see more of the city.
20. We climbed up to the castle in the morning.
21. We hung around in a tiny cafe at lunchtime.
22. We bought a few small gifts to take back home.
23. We slowed down in the afternoon and rested.
24. In the evening, we caught up on emails.
25. On Sunday, we checked out by ten.
26. Our friend came to see us off at the airport.
27. The flight back was on time.
28. We landed at home around nine in the evening.
29. We were tired but happy to be back.
30. We could not wait to plan another getaway.
