# Текст к уроку 1: A phone call

**Тема: телефонный разговор, диктовка по буквам**

---

## Текст для аудирования

1. Hello.
2. Hi, this is Tom.
3. Good afternoon, Tom.
4. How are you?
5. Fine, thank you.
6. What is your name, please?
7. My name is Maria.
8. Sorry, can you spell it?
9. Yes. M, A, R, I, A.
10. Thank you, Maria.
11. What is your number?
12. My number is two, one, three.
13. Four, five, six.
14. Seven, eight, nine, zero.
15. Sorry, again please.
16. Two, one, three.
17. Four, five, six.
18. Seven, eight, nine, zero.
19. Thank you very much.
20. You are welcome.
21. Goodbye, Maria.
22. Bye, Tom.
23. See you tomorrow.
24. Yes, see you.
25. Eleven, twelve, thirteen.
26. Fourteen, fifteen, sixteen.
27. Seventeen, eighteen.
28. Nineteen, twenty.
29. That is twenty words.
30. Good. Very good.
