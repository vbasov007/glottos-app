# Текст к уроку 6: Вопросы и отрицания через do/does
**Тема: Будний день и расписание**

---

## Текст для аудирования

1. Do you work every day?
2. Yes, I work from Monday to Friday.
3. I don't work on Saturday or Sunday.
4. What time do you start work?
5. I start work at nine in the morning.
6. Does your brother work in a bank?
7. No, he doesn't. He works in a school.
8. Where does your sister live?
9. She lives in a small town near the sea.
10. Does she like her job?
11. Yes, she does. She teaches English to children.
12. When do you have lessons?
13. I have lessons on Tuesday and Thursday.
14. Why do you study English?
15. I study English because I want to travel.
16. Do your parents speak English?
17. My mother speaks a little, but my father doesn't.
18. What do you do on Sunday?
19. I read books and watch films at home.
20. How many hours do you sleep?
21. I sleep about seven hours every night.
22. Does your friend live in this city?
23. No, he doesn't. He lives in another country.
24. How often do you see him?
25. I see him only on holidays.
26. Why doesn't he visit you?
27. Because he works very hard.
28. Do we have a lesson on Wednesday?
29. No, we don't. We have it on Thursday.
30. Thank you. See you on Thursday morning.
