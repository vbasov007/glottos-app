# Текст к уроку 7: Артикли — первый заход
**Тема: Завтрак в кафе — диалог**

---

## Текст для аудирования

1. Good morning! Welcome to our cafe.
2. Good morning. Can I have a table near the window?
3. Of course. Here is the menu.
4. Thank you. I would like a coffee, please.
5. A coffee with milk or without?
6. With milk, please. And a sandwich.
7. A sandwich with cheese or with ham?
8. With cheese, please.
9. Would you like an apple or an orange?
10. I love apples. Give me an apple, please.
11. Anything else?
12. Yes. A glass of water too, please.
13. Cold or warm?
14. Cold water, please.
15. Very well. Just a moment.
16. Here is the coffee and the sandwich.
17. The apple and the water are coming now.
18. Thank you. The coffee smells very good.
19. We use only good coffee here.
20. I love this place.
21. Do you come here often?
22. Yes, I work near here.
23. I am a teacher in a school.
24. Oh, my brother is a teacher too!
25. He works in a school near the park.
26. The world is small!
27. Can I have the bill, please?
28. Here you are.
29. Thank you. Have a nice day!
30. Have a nice day too. Goodbye!
