# Текст к уроку 13: did в вопросах и отрицаниях
**Тема: Что случилось на работе**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Did you hear about Mark from the office?
2. No, I didn't. What happened?
3. On Monday morning he came to work very late.
4. The boss saw him and asked why.
5. Mark didn't answer at first.
6. Then he said his car didn't start.
7. The boss didn't believe him.
8. Did he have a real reason?
9. Yes, but he didn't tell the truth right away.
10. In fact, he forgot to set his alarm.
11. He woke up an hour after his usual time.
12. He tried to call a taxi, but no taxi came quickly.
13. So he ran to the metro station.
14. The metro was slow that morning because of an accident.
15. Did the boss get angry?
16. At first, yes. He didn't say much.
17. Then Mark told the whole story.
18. He apologised and promised it would not happen again.
19. The boss listened carefully and finally smiled.
20. He told Mark to go to his desk and start work.
21. Did the day end well?
22. In a way, yes. Mark finished his report in the evening.
23. He didn't go to lunch with us — he ate at his desk.
24. He didn't take any breaks all day.
25. We all helped him a little when we had time.
26. In the end, he sent the report to the boss at six.
27. The boss read it and said it was good.
28. Did Mark feel better after that?
29. He looked very tired but happy.
30. He didn't forget his alarm again — at least not that week.
