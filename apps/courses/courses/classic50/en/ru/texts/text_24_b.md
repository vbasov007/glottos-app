# Текст к уроку 24: Present Perfect Continuous
**Тема: Тренировки и проекты — длительные усилия**

---

## Listening text

1. I have been training for a marathon since January.
2. I have been running five times a week.
3. I have been waking up at five in the morning.
4. I have been eating much healthier than before.
5. I haven't been drinking alcohol for three months.
6. My coach has been helping me a lot.
7. He has been giving me a new plan every week.
8. He has been recording all my times.
9. My family has been supporting me.
10. They have been cooking healthy dinners.
11. My wife has been running with me on weekends.
12. The kids have been making posters for the race.
13. We have been thinking about this for years.
14. But this year I have finally been doing it.
15. The race is next month.
16. I have been getting faster every week.
17. I have been feeling stronger than ever.
18. My legs have been hurting sometimes.
19. But my doctor has been checking everything.
20. He has been telling me I am healthy.
21. I have been reading books about runners.
22. I have been watching marathons online.
23. I have been learning a lot about my body.
24. I have been understanding myself better.
25. Friends have been asking how I do it.
26. I have been answering: one step at a time.
27. They have been getting interested too.
28. Two of them have been joining my morning runs.
29. We have been pushing each other.
30. We have been becoming a small running group.
