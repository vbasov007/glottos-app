# Текст к уроку 25: Past Perfect
**Тема: Опоздание в аэропорт — рассказ с предысторией**

---

## Listening text

1. Last summer I had a very bad travel day.
2. I needed to fly to Madrid for a wedding.
3. I had bought the ticket three months earlier.
4. I had packed my bag the night before.
5. I had set two alarms for early morning.
6. But somehow I had not heard either of them.
7. When I woke up, it was already eight o'clock.
8. My flight was at ten thirty.
9. I had three hours before take-off.
10. The airport was an hour away by taxi.
11. I called a taxi and it arrived quickly.
12. But the driver had taken the wrong road.
13. We got stuck in heavy traffic.
14. By the time we reached the airport, it was nine forty.
15. I ran inside but I had forgotten my passport.
16. I had left it on the kitchen table.
17. I called my neighbour.
18. She had a spare key to my flat.
19. She agreed to drive my passport to me.
20. But she had to come from across the city.
21. By the time she arrived, the gate had closed.
22. By the time I reached the gate, the plane had taken off.
23. I sat down in a chair and almost cried.
24. I had paid so much for that ticket.
25. The airline agent was kind.
26. She had seen people like me many times.
27. She found me a flight the next morning.
28. By the time I got home, I had been awake for fifteen hours.
29. I had learned an important lesson that day.
30. Now I always check my passport the night before.
