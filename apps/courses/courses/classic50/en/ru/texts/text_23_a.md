# Текст к уроку 23: Past Continuous
**Тема: Вечер дома — фон и события**

---

## Listening text

1. Yesterday evening I was sitting at home.
2. I was reading a good book.
3. My wife was working on her laptop.
4. The children were doing their homework.
5. The cat was sleeping on the sofa.
6. The dog was lying near the door.
7. It was raining hard outside.
8. The wind was blowing through the trees.
9. Suddenly, the lights went off.
10. The whole street was dark.
11. We were looking for candles.
12. The children were laughing in the dark.
13. My wife was searching in a drawer.
14. I was checking my phone for news.
15. After ten minutes, the lights came back.
16. We were all relieved.
17. I made some tea while my wife was cooking dinner.
18. The children were watching cartoons.
19. The cat was still sleeping in the same place.
20. The dog was waiting for his food.
21. While we were eating, the rain stopped.
22. The sky was clearing up.
23. A bright moon was rising.
24. We were finishing our dessert when a friend called.
25. He was driving home and saw the lights on.
26. We invited him in for coffee.
27. While we were drinking coffee, we were talking about the storm.
28. He was telling stories about other power cuts.
29. The children were listening with big eyes.
30. It was a quiet and happy evening.
