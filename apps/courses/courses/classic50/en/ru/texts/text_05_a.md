# Текст к уроку 5: A normal day

**Тема: обычный день, утренние и вечерние привычки**

---

## Текст для аудирования

1. I wake up at seven.
2. I drink a glass of water.
3. I wash my face.
4. I get dressed.
5. I make coffee.
6. I eat breakfast.
7. I read the news on my phone.
8. I leave the house at eight.
9. I walk to the bus stop.
10. I take the bus to work.
11. I work in an office.
12. I write emails.
13. I speak with my colleagues.
14. I have lunch at one.
15. I eat a sandwich.
16. I drink water.
17. After lunch I work again.
18. I finish at six.
19. I take the bus home.
20. At home I cook dinner.
21. I eat dinner with my family.
22. We talk about our day.
23. After dinner I watch TV.
24. I read a book.
25. I listen to music.
26. I call my mother.
27. We speak for ten minutes.
28. I go to bed at eleven.
29. I sleep for eight hours.
30. The next day starts again.
