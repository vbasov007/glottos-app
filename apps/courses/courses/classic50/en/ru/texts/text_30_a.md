# Текст к уроку 30: My city
**Тема: связный многоабзацный текст — описание города**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. I live in a small city in the south of the country.
2. The city, which is over four hundred years old, has about two hundred thousand people.
3. It is famous for its narrow streets and its quiet parks.
4. Many tourists come here every summer.
5. They come mainly to see the old cathedral, which was built in the seventeenth century.
6. The cathedral is the heart of the old town.
7. I have lived here for almost twelve years.
8. I moved here after I finished university.
9. At first, I didn't know anyone in the city.
10. However, the people I met at work soon became my close friends.
11. The colleague who showed me around in those first weeks is now one of my best friends.
12. We often meet in the small café where we had our first lunch together.
13. The café, which is run by an old Italian couple, makes the best coffee in town.
14. Their son, whose name is Marco, took over the kitchen two years ago.
15. The food he cooks is simple but wonderful.
16. Although the city is small, there is always something to do.
17. In summer, the parks are full of families and street musicians.
18. In winter, the small theatre near the river offers excellent plays.
19. The river, which crosses the city from east to west, is one of my favourite places.
20. I often walk along it on Sunday mornings.
21. Last year, the city built a new bridge over the river.
22. The bridge, which is made of dark steel and light wood, has quickly become a symbol of the modern city.
23. People who pass it every day rarely think about its design.
24. But tourists usually stop to take photographs.
25. By the end of this year, the city will have completed three more public projects.
26. Next month I am moving to a new flat near the river.
27. The flat, which has a small balcony, looks out onto a quiet courtyard.
28. I am going to plant flowers there in the spring.
29. I love this city, and I cannot imagine living anywhere else.
30. In short, it is the place where I feel truly at home.
