# Текст к уроку 8: Множественное число и исчисляемое vs неисчисляемое
**Тема: В магазине — диалог**

---

## Текст для аудирования

1. Hello! Can I help you?
2. Yes, please. I need some food for the week.
3. What do you need exactly?
4. I need bread, milk, eggs and cheese.
5. How much bread do you want?
6. Two loaves, please. One white and one brown.
7. And the milk?
8. Three bottles of milk, please.
9. We have fresh milk today, from the farm.
10. That sounds good. I take it.
11. How many eggs do you need?
12. Ten eggs, please. We eat a lot of eggs.
13. The eggs are fresh too.
14. And cheese — a big piece, please.
15. We have many kinds of cheese.
16. Give me the yellow one. Half a kilo.
17. Anything else?
18. Yes. Some fruit and vegetables.
19. We have nice apples and oranges today.
20. Two kilos of apples and one kilo of oranges, please.
21. What about vegetables?
22. Five tomatoes and three carrots.
23. Do you need potatoes?
24. Yes, a kilo of potatoes, please.
25. How much rice do you want?
26. Just a small packet of rice today.
27. Do you need sugar or salt?
28. A packet of sugar, please. We have salt at home.
29. Here is your bag. How much does it cost?
30. That is twenty-five dollars, please. Thank you and have a nice day!
