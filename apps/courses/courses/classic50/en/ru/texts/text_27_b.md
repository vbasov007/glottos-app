# Текст к уроку 27: Twenty years in the city
**Тема: смешение всех времён в биографическом рассказе**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. I moved to this city twenty years ago.
2. Back then I was just twenty-three.
3. I had finished my studies a few months earlier.
4. I had been looking for a job for almost a year.
5. Finally, a small company offered me a position.
6. I started work on the first of September.
7. While I was learning the job, I lived in a tiny flat.
8. The flat had only one room and no kitchen.
9. By the end of the first year, I had saved a little money.
10. I rented a better place near the park.
11. Now I own a small house on the same street.
12. I have lived in this neighbourhood for fifteen years.
13. I have been working at the same company for ten years.
14. They have promoted me three times.
15. At the moment I am leading a small team of five people.
16. We are working on an interesting project.
17. The team usually meets every Monday morning.
18. We have already finished the first phase.
19. By next summer we will have launched the new product.
20. By then I will have been working here for eleven years.
21. My wife and I met in this city.
22. She was studying at the university when we met.
23. We have been married for twelve years.
24. We have two children, a boy and a girl.
25. They go to a school just down the street.
26. Tomorrow we are taking them to the zoo.
27. The zoo opens at ten on Saturdays.
28. Next year we are going to travel to Italy together.
29. We have never been there before, and we are very excited.
30. By the time we come back, we will have seen Rome, Florence and Venice.
