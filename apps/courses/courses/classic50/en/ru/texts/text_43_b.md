# Текст к уроку 43: Weather and feelings — adj+noun коллокации
**Тема: погода, эмоции — сильный, тяжёлый, глубокий**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. The forecast said we would have heavy rain all weekend.
2. Strong winds were also expected in the afternoon.
3. People worried about heavy traffic on the motorways.
4. By Saturday morning, the heavy snow began to fall.
5. The temperature dropped, and a deep cold settled over the city.
6. My grandmother had severe pain in her knees that day.
7. She is a deep sleeper, but the storm woke her up.
8. The wind carried a strong smell of pine from the forest.
9. The river ran deep and dark under the bridge.
10. Old houses suffered heavy damage from the storm.
11. The mayor gave a short speech to the public.
12. He paid respect to the emergency workers.
13. He made a strong promise to help every family.
14. People felt bitter disappointment with the slow response.
15. The local paper printed strong criticism the next day.
16. Many readers had high hopes for change.
17. Some felt deep sorrow for the lost homes.
18. Others showed broad agreement with the new plan.
19. The school had high standards, and stayed open all week.
20. The students made great progress on their winter project.
21. They took deep breaths before each presentation.
22. They paid close attention to every detail.
23. The teacher gave warm praise after every speech.
24. By Sunday, the rain finally became light.
25. The streets dried out under bright sunshine.
26. People went out to take photos of the clean sky.
27. Cafes had heavy crowds for the first time in days.
28. Friends had a long chat over strong coffee.
29. The whole city felt deep relief that the storm was over.
30. By evening, everyone was in a good mood again.
