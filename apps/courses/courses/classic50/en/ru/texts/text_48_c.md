# Текст C к уроку 48: Беглый бытовой диалог
**Тема: Естественная разговорная речь — стяжения, заполнители, ритм**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Hey, what are you up to this weekend?
2. Not much, honestly. Just gonna stay home and rest.
3. Oh come on, that sounds boring. Wanna come out with us?
4. Where are you guys going?
5. Well, we were thinking about that new place on Main Street.
6. The one with the live music? I've heard it's pretty good.
7. Yeah, exactly. We've gotta book a table though.
8. What time were you planning to go?
9. Around eight, I think. Earlier if we can get in.
10. You know what, count me in. I could use a night out.
11. Awesome. I'll let the others know you're coming.
12. By the way, is anyone driving, or are we getting a cab?
13. I'd rather not drive, to be honest.
14. Yeah, me neither. Let's just split a cab.
15. Cool. So I'll meet you at the corner around seven thirty.
16. Sounds good. Should I bring anything?
17. Nah, just yourself. Maybe wear something nicer than usual.
18. Why, is there a dress code or something?
19. Kind of. They don't like trainers and stuff like that.
20. Got it. I'll wear my black shoes then.
21. Perfect. Oh, and bring a jacket. It's supposed to get chilly.
22. Thanks for the heads-up. I would've totally forgotten.
23. No worries. That's what friends are for, right?
24. Speaking of which, is Sarah coming too?
25. Yeah, she's bringing her new boyfriend, apparently.
26. Oh really? I haven't met him yet. What's he like?
27. I dunno, I've only seen him once. He seems nice enough.
28. Well, I guess we'll find out tonight.
29. Anyway, I've gotta run. See you at seven thirty.
30. Cool, see you then. Don't be late!
