# Текст к уроку 19: Наречия
**Тема: Хобби и привычки**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. My sister always goes to the gym on Mondays.
2. She often plays tennis with her friends.
3. She rarely watches television.
4. She is usually very busy.
5. She speaks Italian fluently because she lived in Rome.
6. She also plays the piano beautifully.
7. My brother is completely different.
8. He often stays at home and reads books.
9. He never plays sport.
10. He drinks coffee too often, in my opinion.
11. He speaks English quite well, but not fluently.
12. He works hard and sleeps very little.
13. My parents are always together at the weekend.
14. They sometimes go to the cinema in the evening.
15. They usually cook dinner at home.
16. My father plays chess well and patiently.
17. My mother sings happily in the kitchen.
18. She is really a wonderful cook.
19. I often visit them on Sundays.
20. We usually have a big lunch together.
21. The food is always delicious.
22. I sometimes bring a friend with me.
23. My parents are always very kind to my friends.
24. I rarely feel bored at their house.
25. After lunch, we sometimes go for a walk in the park.
26. The park is quite close to their house.
27. My father walks slowly because of his knee.
28. My mother walks fast and talks a lot.
29. We always come back happy and tired.
30. I love these quiet Sunday afternoons very much.
