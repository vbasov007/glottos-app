# Текст к уроку 22: Present Perfect vs Past Simple
**Тема: Разговор друзей — путешествия, работа, отношения**

---

## Listening text

1. Hi Mark, I haven't seen you for ages.
2. Hi Tom. We last met two years ago.
3. Yes, at Sarah's birthday in 2022.
4. So much has happened since then.
5. I have moved to a new city.
6. Really? When did you move?
7. I moved last September.
8. Have you found a new job?
9. Yes, I have worked at a startup since November.
10. I really enjoy the team.
11. We have already launched two products.
12. That sounds great. And how is your family?
13. We are all well. My wife has started her own business.
14. She left her old company in March.
15. She has been busy but very happy.
16. Have you travelled recently?
17. Yes, we went to Greece in July.
18. We spent two weeks on a small island.
19. I have never seen such clear water.
20. We have already booked another trip for next year.
21. Lucky you. I have not had a holiday in ages.
22. When did you last go somewhere?
23. I went to Portugal in 2021, but only for a weekend.
24. You really need a break.
25. Have you thought about Spain?
26. I have, actually. I have looked at flights twice this week.
27. Just book it. You won't regret it.
28. You are right. I have already taken too long.
29. Let's meet again soon. It has been so good to see you.
30. Yes, let's not wait two more years.
