# Текст к уроку 43: A speech at the conference — наречные коллокации
**Тема: профессиональная презентация — strongly, deeply, highly, fully**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Dr Lopez stood up to give her speech at the conference.
2. She was highly qualified and widely respected in her field.
3. The audience was fully aware of her recent research.
4. She began by paying her respects to the organisers.
5. She deeply thanked them for the invitation.
6. Then she made a strong case for change.
7. She strongly believed that the current system was failing.
8. She was bitterly disappointed by the lack of progress.
9. She was deeply concerned about the next generation.
10. The data, she said, was perfectly clear.
11. The trend was utterly worrying.
12. She gave many examples of recent failures.
13. The audience listened in deep silence.
14. They paid close attention to every slide.
15. She fully understood the difficulty of her message.
16. But she felt strongly that someone had to speak up.
17. She highly recommended three key changes.
18. First, fully integrate the new training programme.
19. Second, deeply review the budget choices of last year.
20. Third, strongly support young researchers across the country.
21. She finished with a sincere apology for being so direct.
22. The audience completely agreed with her main points.
23. They gave her a long, warm round of applause.
24. Several people deeply thanked her after the talk.
25. A young scientist shyly said he was deeply moved.
26. He felt fully prepared to take new risks.
27. Dr Lopez gently smiled and shook his hand.
28. She felt deeply hopeful about the future of the field.
29. She missed her own students terribly that day.
30. She quietly decided to call them as soon as she got home.
