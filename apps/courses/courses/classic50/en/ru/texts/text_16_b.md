# Текст к уроку 16: Модальные глаголы — can, must, should
**Тема: На работе и в путешествии**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Can you help me with this report?
2. Yes, I can. What do you need?
3. I must finish this project by Friday.
4. You should ask your boss for help.
5. We can't be late for the meeting.
6. You must turn off your phone in the meeting room.
7. Should we order coffee or tea?
8. I think we should order both.
9. He can write emails in three languages.
10. She must travel to Berlin next week.
11. You shouldn't open this file without permission.
12. Can I work from home tomorrow?
13. Yes, but you must come to the office on Friday.
14. We must check our passports before the flight.
15. You should book the hotel today.
16. I can't find my ticket.
17. You should look in your bag again.
18. Passengers must show their tickets at the gate.
19. You mustn't take liquids on the plane.
20. We should arrive at the airport two hours early.
21. Can you carry this suitcase?
22. It's heavy, but I can carry it.
23. He should learn some Spanish before the trip.
24. Tourists must respect local customs.
25. You can pay in cash or by card.
26. Should I leave a tip for the waiter?
27. Yes, you should leave about ten percent.
28. We must remember to send a postcard.
29. You can rent a car at the airport.
30. I can drive, but I shouldn't drive at night.
