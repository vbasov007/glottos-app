# Текст к уроку 24: Present Perfect Continuous
**Тема: Долгая стройка — ремонт, который никак не кончится**

---

## Listening text

1. We have been renovating our flat since March.
2. The workers have been coming every day except Sunday.
3. They have been making a lot of noise.
4. They have been drilling through the walls.
5. They have been carrying heavy materials up the stairs.
6. Our neighbours have been complaining politely.
7. We have been apologising every week.
8. The dust has been getting into everything.
9. I have been cleaning the same shelves three times a day.
10. My wife has been losing patience with the workers.
11. They have been promising to finish soon.
12. But they have been finding new problems.
13. They have been changing the plan twice already.
14. The bathroom has been waiting for new tiles.
15. The kitchen has been missing a sink for two weeks.
16. We have been washing dishes in the bathtub.
17. We have been cooking with one electric pan.
18. The kids have been eating pizza too often.
19. They have been enjoying it actually.
20. But I have been missing real home food.
21. The painters have been working in the living room.
22. They have been choosing colours with my wife.
23. They have been showing her samples every day.
24. She has been changing her mind constantly.
25. Now she has been wanting a calm green.
26. The painters have been smiling patiently.
27. Tomorrow they are starting with the walls.
28. By next week the room will be ready.
29. I have been counting the days until the end of the project.
30. We have been dreaming of a normal evening at home.
