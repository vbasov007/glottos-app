# Текст A к уроку 47: Эссе — мини-аргументация с маркерами
**Тема: Связный письменный текст — как живёт дискурс в эссе**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Working from home has changed the way many people live.
2. First of all, it offers a kind of flexibility that office life never had.
3. People can manage their own schedule and their own breaks.
4. Moreover, it saves several hours of commuting every week.
5. For many parents, this extra time is a real gift.
6. However, the picture is not entirely positive.
7. Many workers feel isolated when they sit at home all day.
8. In addition, the line between work and rest becomes blurred.
9. Some employees work longer hours than before.
10. As a result, stress and burnout have actually increased.
11. Furthermore, certain tasks require face-to-face meetings.
12. For example, a difficult negotiation rarely works well online.
13. Creative brainstorming also tends to suffer on a screen.
14. Therefore, many companies now use a hybrid model.
15. In other words, two or three days in the office, the rest at home.
16. This approach seems to balance the benefits and the drawbacks.
17. Of course, it is not perfect for everyone.
18. Junior staff in particular still need direct contact with mentors.
19. Likewise, new employees benefit from being physically present.
20. Nevertheless, the hybrid system is here to stay.
21. Indeed, most surveys show that workers prefer it.
22. Admittedly, managers sometimes struggle with the shift.
23. They need new skills to lead remote teams effectively.
24. Trust, in particular, becomes more important than control.
25. Communication also has to be clearer and more deliberate.
26. To sum up, the world of work has changed forever.
27. The traditional five-day office week is becoming the exception.
28. In conclusion, flexibility is no longer a privilege but an expectation.
29. The challenge now is to make it work for everyone.
30. Overall, the future of work looks both freer and more demanding.
