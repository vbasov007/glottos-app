# Текст к уроку 39: Education and life choices
**Тема: Абстрактные существительные, заведения, generic the — рассуждение**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Education is one of the most important things in life.
2. In most countries, children start school at the age of six.
3. They study mathematics, history, music, and many other subjects.
4. The young learn quickly when teachers are good.
5. After school, many young people go to university.
6. At university, students choose a specific field.
7. Knowledge is power, but practical skills matter too.
8. Some students prefer learning the piano or the violin.
9. Others enjoy sports like football or basketball.
10. Music can teach patience, while sport teaches teamwork.
11. After graduation, life changes quickly.
12. Most graduates start work as soon as they can.
13. The world of work is very different from school.
14. Time becomes more valuable in the working world.
15. People often complain about a lack of time.
16. The rich often have more options than the poor.
17. But happiness does not depend only on money.
18. Love, family, and health are equally important.
19. Many people fall in love at university.
20. Some couples meet by chance, others through friends.
21. Marriage is a big step that requires thought.
22. Couples in love should still discuss money openly.
23. Money problems destroy many marriages every year.
24. The elderly often say that life passes faster than expected.
25. They advise the young to enjoy each moment.
26. Travel teaches people things that books cannot.
27. The Spanish, the French, and the English each see life differently.
28. By visiting other countries, we understand our own better.
29. In the end, education never really stops.
30. Life itself is the longest school of all.
