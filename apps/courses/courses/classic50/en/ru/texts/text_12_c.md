# Текст к уроку 12: Past Simple
**Тема: История знакомства**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. I met my best friend ten years ago.
2. We were students at the same university.
3. On the first day of classes I came to the wrong room.
4. A young woman sat alone near the window.
5. She read a thick book about history.
6. I asked her if this was room number twelve.
7. She smiled and said no, this was room fourteen.
8. We laughed and started to talk.
9. Her name was Olga, and she came from a small town.
10. I told her my name and my story.
11. After class we walked together to the cafeteria.
12. We had coffee and a cheap student lunch.
13. We talked for two hours about books and films.
14. She loved Russian writers, and I loved old British movies.
15. The next week we went to the cinema together.
16. We watched a long French film with subtitles.
17. After the film we ate pizza in a small restaurant.
18. That evening, I knew we were going to be good friends.
19. During the next year we studied together for many exams.
20. We helped each other when things were difficult.
21. In the summer she travelled to Spain with her family.
22. She sent me funny postcards from every city.
23. I worked in a café and saved money for the next term.
24. When she came back, she brought me a small gift from Madrid.
25. After university, we both got jobs in different cities.
26. But we wrote long emails to each other every month.
27. Last year she came to visit me for a week.
28. We walked around the old town and remembered our student days.
29. We laughed a lot and drank too much coffee.
30. Ten years passed, but our friendship stayed the same.
