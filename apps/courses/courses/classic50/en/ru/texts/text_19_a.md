# Текст к уроку 19: Наречия
**Тема: Распорядок дня**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. I always wake up at seven o'clock.
2. I usually have coffee for breakfast.
3. I sometimes eat eggs in the morning.
4. I never skip breakfast.
5. After breakfast, I quickly check my email.
6. I often work from home on Fridays.
7. I rarely take the bus to the office.
8. I usually walk to work because it's not far.
9. I always arrive on time.
10. My colleagues are often late.
11. I work hard every day.
12. I speak English fluently at meetings.
13. My boss speaks slowly and clearly.
14. I usually have lunch at one o'clock.
15. I sometimes eat at my desk.
16. In the afternoon I drink tea, never coffee.
17. I always leave the office at six.
18. After work, I go to the gym twice a week.
19. I rarely watch TV in the evening.
20. I prefer to read a book quietly.
21. I always brush my teeth before bed.
22. I usually go to sleep around eleven.
23. I sometimes read in bed for ten minutes.
24. I am never tired in the morning.
25. My friend is always tired because he goes to bed late.
26. On Saturdays, I sometimes sleep until nine.
27. I often meet friends on Sunday afternoons.
28. We usually have a long lunch together.
29. I always feel happy on Sundays.
30. This routine works very well for me.
