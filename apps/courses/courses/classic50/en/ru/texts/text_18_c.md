# Текст к уроку 18: Предлоги времени и места
**Тема: Диалог — встреча в городе**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Hi, Anna. Where are you?
2. I'm at home now. Where are you?
3. I'm in the centre, near the metro.
4. Can we meet at the cafe on Pushkin Street?
5. Yes. What time?
6. Let's meet at three in the afternoon.
7. Okay. Is the cafe between the bank and the bookshop?
8. Yes, exactly. It's on the corner.
9. I'll come by bus. The bus stop is in front of my house.
10. I'll wait for you at the door.
11. I'll be there at three, maybe a little later.
12. Don't worry. I'll order tea.
13. The cafe is on the second floor.
14. There is a nice view of the park from the window.
15. I'll see you soon.
16. Hi, sorry I'm late.
17. No problem. I was reading a book at the table by the window.
18. Where shall we go after the cafe?
19. There's a film at six o'clock at the cinema on Tverskaya Street.
20. Good idea. The cinema is near the metro station.
21. We can walk there in fifteen minutes.
22. After the film, let's have dinner at a restaurant.
23. There's a nice Italian restaurant on the next street.
24. It's in a small building behind the church.
25. I'll book a table for eight o'clock.
26. Are you free on Sunday?
27. On Sunday I'm at my parents' in the countryside.
28. We could meet on Monday after work.
29. Perfect. Let's meet at the same cafe at seven.
30. See you on Monday.
