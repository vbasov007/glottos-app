# Текст к уроку 44: A rainy Monday — идиомы погоды и времени
**Тема: типичный понедельник, на котором всё идёт не так**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Monday started with the sky as grey as iron.
2. It was raining cats and dogs by seven o'clock.
3. I felt a bit under the weather, but I had to go to work.
4. My alarm went off, and time was already flying.
5. I missed the bus by a hair, in the nick of time, I caught the next one.
6. Once in a blue moon, the trains run perfectly on Mondays.
7. Today was not one of those days.
8. The train was late, but better late than never.
9. At the office, my boss was already as cool as a cucumber.
10. He smiled and said, "Don't let it rain on your parade."
11. I tried to break the ice with my new colleagues.
12. We hit it off quickly over hot coffee.
13. The project was a piece of cake, they said.
14. But the deadline was tight as a drum.
15. We had to think outside the box.
16. By noon, we were all on the same page.
17. My colleague Sara always goes the extra mile.
18. She had already done the heavy lifting.
19. I felt as light as a feather after her good news.
20. Time flew between meetings and emails.
21. By five, it was time to call it a day.
22. The rain had finally stopped outside.
23. Every cloud has a silver lining, my friend said.
24. We laughed and grabbed an early dinner.
25. The waiter pulled my leg about my huge appetite.
26. The food cost an arm and a leg, but it was worth it.
27. I made up my mind to make Mondays better.
28. I would go to bed earlier and read once in a while.
29. Tomorrow was a new day, a new chance.
30. I went home feeling as good as gold.
