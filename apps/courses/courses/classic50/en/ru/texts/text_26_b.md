# Текст к уроку 26: A trip to London
**Тема: будущее — деловая поездка с расписанием**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Next month I am going to London on a business trip.
2. I am flying on Monday morning.
3. The plane takes off at seven thirty.
4. I have to be at the airport by five thirty in the morning.
5. The flight arrives at Heathrow at eight forty-five local time.
6. A driver is meeting me at the airport.
7. He is taking me straight to my hotel near Hyde Park.
8. I am checking in at about ten o'clock.
9. After a quick shower, I am having my first meeting at noon.
10. We are discussing a new contract with our British partners.
11. The negotiations will probably last two days.
12. By Wednesday afternoon, we will have signed the agreement.
13. On Wednesday evening, I am going to a small reception.
14. The reception begins at seven in the hotel restaurant.
15. I will meet several important people there.
16. On Thursday I am giving a presentation to the team.
17. The presentation starts at ten in the morning.
18. After lunch, I will be answering questions for about an hour.
19. By Thursday evening, I will have done all my work.
20. On Friday I am taking the day off.
21. I am going to visit the British Museum in the morning.
22. The museum opens at ten and closes at five thirty.
23. In the afternoon I am meeting an old friend for coffee.
24. We will probably talk for hours.
25. My return flight leaves at nine in the evening.
26. By midnight, I will be back at home.
27. I think I will sleep for ten hours on Saturday.
28. The whole trip is going to be exhausting but useful.
29. I am not planning another trip until next quarter.
30. I will definitely need some quiet weekends after this one.
