# Текст к уроку 7: Артикли — первый заход
**Тема: Дом и предметы вокруг**

---

## Текст для аудирования

1. This is a house.
2. The house is big and white.
3. There is a door and a window.
4. The door is open.
5. The window is closed.
6. In the room there is a table.
7. On the table there is a book.
8. The book is interesting.
9. Near the table there is a chair.
10. The chair is brown.
11. There is a bag on the chair.
12. In the bag there is a phone and a key.
13. The phone is black, the key is small.
14. There is also an apple in the bag.
15. The apple is red and sweet.
16. I love apples.
17. I want an apple now.
18. There is a cat in the room.
19. The cat is white.
20. Cats are nice animals.
21. The cat sleeps on a soft chair.
22. There is a glass of water on the table too.
23. Water is good for you.
24. I drink water every day.
25. My mother is a teacher.
26. My father is an engineer.
27. They love coffee in the morning.
28. The coffee in this cup is hot.
29. We live in a small town.
30. The town is near the sea.
