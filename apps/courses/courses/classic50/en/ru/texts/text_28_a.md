# Текст к уроку 28: My neighbours
**Тема: defining relative clauses в описаниях людей**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. I live in a small building that has only six flats.
2. The people who live here are very different from each other.
3. The man who lives in flat one is a retired teacher.
4. He has a small dog that barks at everyone.
5. The woman who lives in flat two is a doctor.
6. She works at the hospital that is just down the road.
7. In flat three there is a young couple who got married last summer.
8. The husband, whose name is Tom, works from home.
9. His wife, who is a journalist, travels a lot.
10. The students who live in flat four are from Spain.
11. They study at the university which is near the river.
12. The flat above mine belongs to an old lady whose son visits her every Sunday.
13. The son, who is a lawyer, brings flowers every time.
14. The flowers that he chooses are always beautiful.
15. The man whose flat is next to mine is a musician.
16. He plays the piano that he keeps in his living room.
17. The music that he plays in the evenings is very pleasant.
18. I have a balcony that looks onto a small park.
19. The park where the children play is full of trees.
20. The cat that I see every morning belongs to nobody.
21. The neighbours who feed it have given it a name.
22. The name that they chose is simply Tiger.
23. The lady who runs the small shop downstairs knows everyone.
24. The shop, which has been here for thirty years, sells almost everything.
25. The bread that she sells is the best in the area.
26. There is also a cafe where I have my coffee every morning.
27. The owner, who comes from Italy, makes wonderful pastries.
28. The pastries that he bakes attract people from all over the city.
29. I love the street where I live.
30. It is full of people who make every day interesting.
