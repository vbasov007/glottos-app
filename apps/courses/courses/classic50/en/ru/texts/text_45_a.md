# Текст к уроку 45: A formal letter — registers in business correspondence
**Тема: образец делового письма клиенту**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. Dear Mr Hopkins, I am writing to inform you of an important update.
2. Our team has reviewed the latest proposal in detail.
3. We anticipate several minor adjustments to the timeline.
4. The original launch date will be postponed by approximately two weeks.
5. This decision was made after careful consideration of the technical risks.
6. We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.
7. Please rest assured that we remain fully committed to the project.
8. We will conduct a comprehensive review of the schedule next week.
9. A revised plan will be submitted to your office on Friday.
10. We kindly request your input on the proposed changes.
11. Should you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact me.
12. I would be most grateful for your prompt response.
13. We highly value the partnership we have established with your company.
14. We are confident that the project will deliver the expected results.
15. The engineering team is investigating the cause of the recent delays.
16. We have implemented additional measures to prevent similar issues.
17. Furthermore, we have allocated extra resources to the project.
18. The quality of the final product remains our highest priority.
19. We assure you that no further postponements are expected.
20. Our weekly progress reports will continue as scheduled.
21. The next milestone meeting is set for the fifteenth of this month.
22. Please confirm your availability at your earliest convenience.
23. We look forward to discussing these matters in person.
24. I would also like to thank you for your patience throughout this process.
25. Your understanding has been greatly appreciated by our entire team.
26. I trust this update finds you well.
27. Please convey our regards to your colleagues at headquarters.
28. We remain at your disposal for any further clarifications.
29. Yours sincerely, Anna Brown, Project Manager.
30. Telephone and email details are provided below.
