# Текст к уроку 22: Present Perfect vs Past Simple
**Тема: Жизнь и работа — что было и что есть сейчас**

---

## Listening text

1. My name is Anna and I am thirty years old.
2. I have lived in this city for ten years.
3. I came here in 2015 to study at the university.
4. I studied economics for four years.
5. After that, I started working in a bank.
6. I have worked in three different banks since then.
7. My current job is the best one.
8. I have been there for two years.
9. I really enjoy my work.
10. Last year I bought a small apartment.
11. I have lived alone since I bought it.
12. Before that, I shared a flat with two friends.
13. We lived together for five years.
14. We still meet for coffee every weekend.
15. I have known them since university.
16. Last month I went to Italy for a holiday.
17. I have been to Italy three times before.
18. But this time I visited new cities.
19. I spent a week in Florence and Siena.
20. I have never seen such beautiful art.
21. I took hundreds of photos.
22. I have already shown them to my parents.
23. They came to visit me last weekend.
24. They have not seen my new apartment yet.
25. Wait — they saw it last weekend, actually.
26. I love showing visitors around.
27. Have you ever been to my city?
28. If not, you should come.
29. I have lived here long enough to be a good guide.
30. I will show you everything.
