# Текст к уроку 1: At the language school

**Тема: первый день в языковой школе**

---

## Текст для аудирования

1. Hello!
2. Good morning!
3. My name is Anna.
4. What is your name?
5. I am a student.
6. This is my teacher.
7. This is a book.
8. This is a pen.
9. This is a chair.
10. Please sit down.
11. Thank you.
12. You are welcome.
13. Open your book.
14. Read the word.
15. The word is hello.
16. The word is thank you.
17. Three. Four. Five.
18. Six. Seven. Eight.
19. Nine. Ten. Eleven.
20. Twelve. Thirteen.
21. Excuse me.
22. Yes?
23. What is this word?
24. This is a question.
25. Sorry, I do not know.
26. That is OK.
27. Good. Very good.
28. See you tomorrow.
29. Goodbye!
30. Bye!
