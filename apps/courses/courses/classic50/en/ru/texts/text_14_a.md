# Текст к уроку 14: Артикли — второй заход
**Тема: Дом и город**

---

## Текст для аудирования и повторения

1. I live in a small town in the south of Russia.
2. The town is old and quiet.
3. My house is on a long street near the river.
4. The street is called Cherry Street.
5. The river is not very wide, but it is clean.
6. Every morning I walk to the bakery on the corner.
7. The bakery is the best one in our town.
8. I buy fresh bread and sometimes a small cake.
9. After the bakery I go to a café next to the post office.
10. The café is owned by an old friend of my father.
11. He makes excellent coffee and serves a simple breakfast.
12. Coffee is my favourite drink in the morning.
13. From the café I can see the church on the hill.
14. The church is the oldest building in the town.
15. Children play in the square in front of it.
16. They are happy children, always laughing.
17. My neighbours are kind people.
18. One is a teacher, another is a doctor.
19. The teacher has a small white dog.
20. The dog often comes to my garden.
21. In the evening I cook dinner at home.
22. I love simple food: soup, bread and salad.
23. Sometimes a friend comes for dinner.
24. The friend brings wine or a small gift.
25. After dinner we sit on the balcony.
26. The sky is full of stars.
27. The moon shines over the river.
28. Life in a small town is slow but rich.
29. I would not trade it for a big city.
30. This is my home, and I love it.
