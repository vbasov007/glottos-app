# Текст к уроку 6: Вопросы и отрицания через do/does
**Тема: Знакомство — вопросы о семье и интересах**

---

## Текст для аудирования

1. Where do you come from?
2. I come from a small town in Russia.
3. Do you live there now?
4. No, I don't. I live in Moscow now.
5. Why do you live in Moscow?
6. Because I work there.
7. What does your father do?
8. He works in a hospital. He is a doctor.
9. Does your mother work too?
10. Yes, she does. She teaches at a school.
11. How many brothers and sisters do you have?
12. I have one brother and two sisters.
13. Where do your sisters live?
14. They live with our parents.
15. Does your brother study at the university?
16. No, he doesn't. He works in a small shop.
17. What does he sell?
18. He sells books and newspapers.
19. Do you visit your family often?
20. I visit them on the weekend.
21. When do you usually call your mother?
22. I call her every Sunday evening.
23. Does she like flowers?
24. Yes, she does. She loves yellow roses.
25. Why do you study English?
26. I study English because I want a better job.
27. How many languages do you speak?
28. I speak Russian and a little English.
29. Do you have a dog or a cat at home?
30. I don't have a dog, but I have two cats.
