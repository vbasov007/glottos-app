# Урок 25: Past Perfect. Прошлое до прошлого
**Словарный запас: лексика последовательности и фоновых событий**

---

## Как работать с этим уроком

1. **Прочитай Часть 1** — это про **порядок двух прошедших событий**. Концепция простая, важно её увидеть.
2. **Запомни форму**: одна и та же для всех лиц — **had + past participle**. Это самое простое в формальном плане время английского.
3. **Прогони матрицу с *after*, *before*, *by the time*, *when***. Эти союзы — главные «места жительства» Past Perfect.
4. **Отдельно поработай над «когда не нужен Past Perfect»** — частая ошибка: ставить его без нужды.

> Past Perfect — это «мета-время рассказчика». Им маркируют: «это было РАНЬШЕ, чем основной рассказ». Без него рассказ всё ещё понятен — но с ним он становится точным.

## Часть 1: Что такое Past Perfect

Past Perfect — **прошедшее до прошлого**. Используется, когда в рассказе о прошлом нужно показать, что **одно событие случилось раньше другого**.

> **Past Perfect** маркирует **более раннее** из двух прошедших событий.

Сравни:

> <say>When I arrived at the station, the train **left**.</say>
> — Когда я пришёл на вокзал, поезд **уехал**.
> *Спорно*: либо «уехал в момент моего прихода», либо «к тому моменту уже ушёл». Двусмысленно.

> <say>When I arrived at the station, the train **had left**.</say>
> — Когда я пришёл на вокзал, поезд **уже уехал**.
> *Точно*: поезд ушёл **раньше**, я пришёл позже, застал пустую платформу.

Past Perfect — это «**раньше, чем то прошлое, о котором речь**».

### Временная шкала:

```
прошлое (раньше) ────► прошлое (позже) ────► настоящее
HAD LEFT                   arrived              now
(Past Perfect)         (Past Simple)
```

## Часть 2: Форма — had + past participle

> **had + past participle** (та же третья форма, что в Present Perfect — L21)

| Лицо | had | + причастие |
|------|-----|-------------|
| I | **had** | seen |
| you | **had** | seen |
| he / she / it | **had** | seen |
| we | **had** | seen |
| they | **had** | seen |

> **Хорошая новость:** **had — для всех лиц**, никакого согласования.

Сокращения: <say>I'd seen, she'd left, they'd gone</say>.

> **Ловушка с *'d*:** это и **had**, и **would**. Различаешь по следующей форме:
> <say>I'd seen</say> = I **had** seen (Past Perfect, причастие после)
> <say>I'd see</say> = I **would** see (Conditional, base form после)

Отрицание: <say>hadn't + причастие</say>.
<say>I hadn't seen him before.</say> — Я раньше его не видел.

Вопрос: had выходит вперёд.
<say>Had you finished by 5?</say> — Ты закончил к пяти?

## Часть 3: Где живёт Past Perfect — четыре союза

### 1. After — «после того как»

<say>**After** I had finished my work, I went home.</say> — После того как я закончил работу, я пошёл домой.
<say>**After** she had read the letter, she started crying.</say> — Прочитав письмо, она расплакалась.

Логика: «finished» было **раньше**, потом «went home».

### 2. Before — «до того как»

<say>I went home **before** she had arrived.</say> — Я ушёл домой до того, как она пришла.
<say>He had eaten dinner **before** he came to the party.</say> — Он поужинал до того, как пришёл на вечеринку.

### 3. By the time — «к моменту, когда…»

<say>**By the time** we got there, the film had started.</say> — К моменту, когда мы приехали, фильм уже начался.
<say>**By the time** she called, I had already left.</say> — К моменту, когда она позвонила, я уже ушёл.

> **Самый частый «магнит» для Past Perfect.** *By the time + Past Simple* почти всегда зовёт второй глагол в Past Perfect.

### 4. When — «когда» (если нужна последовательность)

<say>**When** I got home, my wife had cooked dinner.</say> — Когда я пришёл домой, жена уже приготовила ужин.
<say>**When** the police arrived, the thieves had escaped.</say> — Когда приехала полиция, воры уже сбежали.

> С *when* — выбор по смыслу: если хочешь сказать «уже сделал к моменту», бери Past Perfect; если хочешь «и тогда сделал» — Past Simple.

## Часть 4: Сигналы и контекст — «уже / никогда / только что» в прошлом

Маркеры из Present Perfect (L21) — *already, just, never, ever, yet* — **переезжают** в Past Perfect, когда речь о прошлом контексте.

| Present Perfect (сейчас) | Past Perfect (прошлое) |
|--------------------------|------------------------|
| <say>I **have already** finished.</say> | <say>I **had already** finished when she arrived.</say> |
| <say>She **has just** left.</say> | <say>She **had just** left when I came.</say> |
| <say>I **have never** been to Paris.</say> | <say>Before that trip, I **had never** been to Paris.</say> |
| <say>**Have** you **ever** seen this film?</say> | <say>I asked if he **had ever** seen this film.</say> |
| <say>**Have** you finished **yet**?</say> | <say>I asked whether he **had** finished **yet**.</say> |

> **Хак:** перенося историю в прошлое (нарратив, косвенная речь, L35), Present Perfect автоматически становится Past Perfect. Это называется *backshift*.

## Часть 5: Типичные ситуации

### Ситуация 1: «Когда я пришёл — он уже…»

<say>When I came in, she had already eaten.</say>
<say>When the bus arrived, we had been waiting for an hour.</say>
<say>When the doctor came, the patient had recovered.</say>

### Ситуация 2: Воспоминание о причине / предыстории

<say>He was tired because he had worked all night.</say> — Он устал, потому что работал всю ночь.
<say>I knew the answer because I had read the book.</say> — Я знал ответ, потому что прочитал книгу.
<say>She couldn't enter — she had forgotten her keys.</say> — Она не могла войти — забыла ключи.

> **Логика:** **причина** была раньше **следствия**. Past Perfect для причины, Past Simple для следствия.

### Ситуация 3: Первый раз в жизни

<say>It was the first time I had seen the ocean.</say> — Это был первый раз, когда я увидел океан.
<say>That was the worst film he had ever watched.</say> — Это был худший фильм, который он когда-либо смотрел.

> **Паттерн:** *the first / second / best / worst… that someone had ever done* → Past Perfect.

### Ситуация 4: Восстановление событий

<say>When I came home, I realised that someone had been in my flat.</say>
— Когда я пришёл домой, я понял, что у меня кто-то был.
<say>She looked sad. Something had happened.</say>
— Она выглядела грустной. Что-то случилось.

## Часть 6: Когда Past Perfect НЕ нужен

Это важно: русскоязычные часто **переусердствуют** с Past Perfect. Он нужен только тогда, когда **порядок неочевиден**.

### Не нужен, если порядок и так ясен из союзов

<say>First I ate, then I went out.</say> — Сначала поел, потом вышел. (порядок чёткий — Past Simple)
<say>I had breakfast and went to work.</say> — Позавтракал и пошёл на работу. (последовательность, не контекст)

### Не нужен, если события одновременные

<say>When I saw him, I smiled.</say> — Когда увидел, улыбнулся. (одновременно)
<say>While she was cooking, I read the news.</say> — Пока она готовила, я читал новости. (одновременно)

### Не нужен, если нет «второго прошлого момента»

<say>I lived in Paris in 2010.</say> — Я жил в Париже в 2010 году. (нет второго события — просто факт)
✗ *I had lived in Paris in 2010* — без контекста звучит обрывисто.

> **Правило большого пальца:** Past Perfect нужен, **только когда есть ДВА прошедших момента**, и нужно показать, что одно случилось **раньше** другого. Если в рассказе один прошедший момент — Past Simple, и не выдумывай.

## Language scales

### Scale 1 — had finished

> <say>I had finished my work by 6 p.m.</say>
> <say>You had finished your work by 6 p.m.</say>
> <say>He had finished his work by 6 p.m.</say>
> <say>She had finished her work by 6 p.m.</say>
> <say>We had finished our work by 6 p.m.</say>
> <say>They had finished their work by 6 p.m.</say>

### Scale 2 — when I arrived, … had already left

> <say>When I arrived, the bus had already left.</say>
> <say>When she arrived, the bus had already left.</say>
> <say>When we arrived, the bus had already left.</say>
> <say>When they arrived, the bus had already left.</say>

### Scale 3 — отрицание + ever (опыт в прошлом)

> <say>I had never seen the ocean before that trip.</say>
> <say>You had never seen the ocean before that trip.</say>
> <say>He had never seen the ocean before that trip.</say>
> <say>She had never seen the ocean before that trip.</say>
> <say>We had never seen the ocean before that trip.</say>
> <say>They had never seen the ocean before that trip.</say>

### Scale 4 — by the time… had

> <say>By the time I got home, my wife had cooked dinner.</say>
> <say>By the time he got home, his wife had cooked dinner.</say>
> <say>By the time we got home, our wife had cooked dinner.</say> *(шуточный пример)*
> <say>By the time they got home, their wife had cooked dinner.</say>

## Упражнения

### Упражнение 1. Past Simple или Past Perfect?

Поставь глагол в правильную форму:

1. When I (arrive), the party (already / start).
2. She (be) tired because she (work) all day.
3. After he (finish) his homework, he (go) out.
4. By the time we (get) to the cinema, the film (begin).
5. I (recognise) her immediately because I (see) her photo.
6. He (eat) breakfast and (leave) for work.
7. It was the first time she (try) sushi.
8. The thieves (escape) before the police (arrive).
9. When I (call) him, he (already / leave) the office.
10. We (live) in that house for ten years before we (sell) it.

<details><summary>Ключи</summary>

1. *When I arrived, the party had already started.*
2. *She was tired because she had worked all day.*
3. *After he had finished his homework, he went out.* (или: *After he finished…* — *after* допускает Past Simple, если порядок ясен)
4. *By the time we got to the cinema, the film had begun.*
5. *I recognised her immediately because I had seen her photo.*
6. *He ate breakfast and left for work.* (последовательность, Past Perfect не нужен)
7. *It was the first time she had tried sushi.*
8. *The thieves had escaped before the police arrived.*
9. *When I called him, he had already left the office.*
10. *We had lived in that house for ten years before we sold it.*

</details>

### Упражнение 2. Найди и исправь лишний Past Perfect

В каждом предложении Past Perfect лишний. Поставь Past Simple:

1. <say>I had got up at 7 and had had breakfast.</say>
2. <say>Last summer we had gone to Spain.</say>
3. <say>I had met John in 2015.</say>
4. <say>When I had seen her, I had said hello.</say>

<details><summary>Ключи</summary>

1. *I got up at 7 and had breakfast.* — простая последовательность.
2. *Last summer we went to Spain.* — просто факт прошлого.
3. *I met John in 2015.* — факт, конкретное время.
4. *When I saw her, I said hello.* — одновременные / последовательные действия.

</details>

### Упражнение 3. Соедини два предложения с Past Perfect

Соедини, используя союз в скобках. Раннее событие → Past Perfect, позднее → Past Simple.

1. He ate dinner. He came to the party. (before)
2. The film started. We arrived at the cinema. (by the time)
3. She finished her work. She left the office. (after)
4. I never saw snow. I went to Russia. (before / first time)
5. We waited for an hour. The bus came. (by the time)

<details><summary>Ключи</summary>

1. *He had eaten dinner before he came to the party.* (или: *He had had dinner before he came to the party.*)
2. *By the time we arrived at the cinema, the film had started.*
3. *After she had finished her work, she left the office.* (или: *After she finished her work, she left the office.*)
4. *Before I went to Russia, I had never seen snow.* (или: *It was the first time I had seen snow when I went to Russia.*)
5. *By the time the bus came, we had waited for an hour.* (или: *…we had been waiting for an hour.*)

</details>

### Упражнение 4. Переведи на английский

1. Когда я пришёл, она уже ушла.
2. К моменту, когда мы приехали, фильм уже начался.
3. Он устал, потому что работал всю ночь.
4. Это был первый раз, когда я ел суши.
5. Я не мог войти, потому что забыл ключи.
6. Воры сбежали до того, как приехала полиция.
7. Когда я понял ошибку, было уже поздно.
8. Она никогда раньше не видела океан.
9. К концу дня он сделал всю работу.
10. Это был лучший фильм, который я когда-либо видел.

<details><summary>Ключи</summary>

1. *When I came, she had already left.* (или: *When I arrived, she had already gone.*)
2. *By the time we arrived, the film had already started.*
3. *He was tired because he had worked all night.* (или: *He was tired because he had been working all night.*)
4. *It was the first time I had eaten sushi.* (или: *It was the first time I had tried sushi.*)
5. *I couldn't enter because I had forgotten my keys.* (или: *I couldn't get in because I had forgotten my keys.*)
6. *The thieves had escaped before the police arrived.* (или: *The robbers had run away before the police came.*)
7. *When I realised the mistake, it was already too late.* (или: *By the time I realised the mistake, it was too late.*)
8. *She had never seen the ocean before.*
9. *By the end of the day, he had done all his work.* (или: *By the end of the day, he had finished all the work.*)
10. *It was the best film I had ever seen.* (или: *It was the best movie I had ever watched.*)

</details>

### Упражнение 5. Восстанови историю

Поставь глаголы в Past Simple или Past Perfect:

> When Tom (come) home from work, he (notice) immediately that something (be) wrong. The front door (be) open. Someone (be) in his flat. He carefully (walk) in. In the living room, the drawers (be) open. The thieves (take) his laptop and his watch. They (leave) just before he (arrive). Tom (realise) that he (forget) to lock the door that morning. He (call) the police and (wait) for them. By the time they (come), an hour (pass).

<details><summary>Ключи</summary>

> When Tom **came** home from work, he **noticed** immediately that something **was** wrong. The front door **was** open. Someone **had been** in his flat. He carefully **walked** in. In the living room, the drawers **were** open. The thieves **had taken** his laptop and his watch. They **had left** just before he **arrived**. Tom **realised** that he **had forgotten** to lock the door that morning. He **called** the police and **waited** for them. By the time they **came**, an hour **had passed**.

</details>

### Упражнение 6. Read aloud — нарратив с Past Perfect

Прочитай вслух, отчётливо выделяя **had + причастие**:

> <say>By the time I got to the airport, my flight had already left. I had spent two hours in traffic. I had called the airline three times. They had told me they couldn't help. I had never missed a flight before. I sat down in the waiting area. A woman next to me was crying — she had missed her flight too. We started talking. By the end of the day, we had become friends.</say>

## Памятка урока

```
ФОРМА:
  had + past participle  (одна форма для всех лиц)
  I had seen, she had gone, they had finished
  I'd seen, she'd gone (сокращение)
  Hadn't you finished?

ВРЕМЕННАЯ ШКАЛА:
  ──── HAD DONE ────► did ────► НАСТОЯЩЕЕ
       (раньше)     (позже)
  Past Perfect → Past Simple → Now

КОГДА ИСПОЛЬЗОВАТЬ — ДВА ПРОШЕДШИХ СОБЫТИЯ:
  Раннее → Past Perfect
  Позднее → Past Simple

ЧЕТЫРЕ ГЛАВНЫХ СОЮЗА:
  after I had finished, I went out
  I had eaten before she arrived
  By the time he called, I had left
  When I came, she had already gone

ПРИЧИНА И СЛЕДСТВИЕ:
  Past Simple — следствие
  Past Perfect — причина (раньше во времени)
  He was tired because he had worked all night.

ПЕРВЫЙ РАЗ / САМЫЙ…:
  It was the first time I had seen…
  It was the best film he had ever watched…

МАРКЕРЫ:
  already, just, never (before), ever, yet
  by the time, by then, by 5 p.m.
  before, after, until
  earlier, previously

КОГДА PAST PERFECT НЕ НУЖЕН:
  ✗ Простая последовательность:
    I had eaten and had gone out  → I ate and went out
  ✗ Одиночное событие без второго момента:
    I had lived in Paris in 2010  → I lived in Paris in 2010
  ✗ Одновременные действия:
    While she was cooking, I read.
    
ПРАВИЛО: PAST PERFECT — это «РАНЬШЕ, ЧЕМ ТО ПРОШЛОЕ, О КОТОРОМ РЕЧЬ».
  Если нет «того прошлого, о котором речь» — Past Perfect не нужен.

BACKSHIFT (предвестник L35):
  Present Perfect (сейчас)  → Past Perfect (в нарративе прошлого)
  I have just left         → I had just left
  She has never been here  → She had never been here
```
