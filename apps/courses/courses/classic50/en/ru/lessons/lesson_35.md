# Урок 35: Косвенная речь
**Словарный запас: глаголы передачи речи и коммуникации**

---

## Как работать с этим уроком

1. **Прочитай** правило сдвига времён назад при передаче чужих слов (5 минут).
2. **Прогоняй превращения прямой речи в косвенную вслух** — пары «прямая → косвенная» по 20 раз.
3. **Тренируй вопросы и команды** — у них своя логика, которую важно зафиксировать отдельно.

> Это финал «backshift»-логики. Ты уже видел сдвиг времён назад в L32 (условные) и L33 (*wish*). Здесь — ровно тот же принцип, но для передачи чужих слов. Хорошая новость: схема одна, и она универсальная.

---

## Часть 1: Прямая речь и косвенная — главное противопоставление

**Прямая речь** — слова, которые человек сказал, в кавычках, как есть:

<say>He said, "I am tired."</say>

**Косвенная речь** — пересказ этих слов от лица говорящего сейчас:

<say>He said he was tired.</say>

При переходе **трое** меняются:

1. **Время** глагола — сдвигается на шаг назад (если ведущий глагол в прошлом).
2. **Местоимения** — *I* → *he/she*, *my* → *his/her* и т.д.
3. **Слова времени и места** — *now* → *then*, *today* → *that day*, *here* → *there*.

Плюс — пропадают кавычки и слово *that* (опционально) появляется как связка.

---

## Часть 2: Сдвиг времён — главное правило backshift

Если ведущий глагол (*said, told, asked*) в **прошедшем** времени, время прямой речи сдвигается **на шаг назад**:

| Прямая речь (что было сказано) | Косвенная речь (как мы пересказываем) |
|---------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| **Present Simple** → | **Past Simple** |
| <say>"I work here."</say> | <say>He said he worked there.</say> |
| **Present Continuous** → | **Past Continuous** |
| <say>"I am working."</say> | <say>He said he was working.</say> |
| **Present Perfect** → | **Past Perfect** |
| <say>"I have finished."</say> | <say>He said he had finished.</say> |
| **Past Simple** → | **Past Perfect** |
| <say>"I went home."</say> | <say>He said he had gone home.</say> |
| **Past Continuous** → | **Past Perfect Continuous** |
| <say>"I was working."</say> | <say>He said he had been working.</say> |
| **will** → | **would** |
| <say>"I will help you."</say> | <say>He said he would help me.</say> |
| **can** → | **could** |
| <say>"I can do it."</say> | <say>He said he could do it.</say> |
| **may** → | **might** |
| <say>"I may come."</say> | <say>He said he might come.</say> |
| **must** → | **had to** |
| <say>"I must go."</say> | <say>He said he had to go.</say> |

> **Главная мысль:** один шаг назад во времени. Точно так же, как мы делали в условных и в *wish*.

### Когда сдвига времён **не** происходит

Бывают три случая:

1. **Ведущий глагол сам в настоящем:** <say>He says he is tired.</say> — сдвига нет, оба в настоящем.
2. **Универсальная истина:** <say>The teacher said that water boils at 100°C.</say> — закон природы сдвигать не надо.
3. **Past Perfect и would/could/might/should/ought to** — они **уже** в «прошедшей» зоне, дальше сдвигать некуда.

## Часть 3: Что меняется кроме времени — местоимения и слова времени/места

### Местоимения

Перевод «с точки зрения говорящего сейчас»:

| Прямая | Косвенная |
|---------|-----------|
| I, me, my, mine | he/she, him/her, his/her, his/hers |
| we, us, our, ours | they, them, their, theirs |
| you, your, yours | (зависит от того, кому говорил — me, him, her, them, etc.) |

| Прямая | Косвенная |
|---------|-----------|
| <say>Anna said, "I am tired."</say> | <say>Anna said she was tired.</say> |
| <say>Tom said to me, "I will help you."</say> | <say>Tom said he would help me.</say> |
| <say>They said, "We don't know."</say> | <say>They said they didn't know.</say> |

### Слова времени и места — тоже сдвигаются назад

| Прямая | Косвенная |
|---------|-----------|
| **now** → | **then** |
| **today** → | **that day** |
| **tomorrow** → | **the next day / the following day** |
| **yesterday** → | **the day before / the previous day** |
| **this week** → | **that week** |
| **last week** → | **the week before** |
| **next week** → | **the following week** |
| **here** → | **there** |
| **this** → | **that** |
| **these** → | **those** |
| **ago** → | **before** |

Примеры:

| Прямая | Косвенная |
|---------|-----------|
| <say>"I'll see you tomorrow."</say> | <say>He said he would see me the next day.</say> |
| <say>"I arrived yesterday."</say> | <say>She said she had arrived the day before.</say> |
| <say>"I'm here now."</say> | <say>He said he was there then.</say> |
| <say>"This book is mine."</say> | <say>She said that book was hers.</say> |

## Часть 4: Say и Tell — главное различие

| Глагол | Кому говорит — нужно указать? | Пример |
|--------|--------------------------------|--------|
| **say** | нет (или с *to* + кому) | <say>He said he was tired.</say> / <say>He said to me he was tired.</say> |
| **tell** | да, без *to* | <say>He told me he was tired.</say> |

| ✅ | ❌ |
|---|---|
| <say>He said he was tired.</say> | <say>He told he was tired.</say> |
| <say>He told me he was tired.</say> | <say>He said me he was tired.</say> |

> **Простое правило:** *tell* требует адресата (*tell **me, him, her, us, them***), *say* — нет. Если адресат с *say* всё-таки нужен — добавляй *to*: *He said **to me**…*

---

## Часть 5: Косвенные вопросы

Когда мы пересказываем вопрос, в косвенной форме он **превращается в утверждение**: прямой порядок слов, без знака вопроса.

### Вопросы со словом *wh-*

Сохраняется вопросительное слово, дальше — прямой порядок:

| Прямой вопрос | Косвенный |
|----------------|------------|
| <say>"Where do you live?"</say> | <say>He asked where I lived.</say> |
| <say>"What are you doing?"</say> | <say>She asked what I was doing.</say> |
| <say>"When did you arrive?"</say> | <say>He asked when I had arrived.</say> |
| <say>"Why is she sad?"</say> | <say>He asked why she was sad.</say> |

> **Главное:** в косвенной форме **нет** *do/does/did*. *Where do you live?* → *where I lived* (а не *where did I live*).

### Yes/No вопросы — через *if* или *whether*

| Прямой вопрос | Косвенный |
|----------------|------------|
| <say>"Do you speak English?"</say> | <say>He asked if I spoke English.</say> |
| <say>"Are you happy?"</say> | <say>She asked whether I was happy.</say> |
| <say>"Have you seen him?"</say> | <say>He asked if I had seen him.</say> |
| <say>"Will you come?"</say> | <say>He asked whether I would come.</say> |

> **Whether** и **if** в этой роли взаимозаменяемы. *Whether* чуть формальнее.

## Часть 6: Косвенные команды и просьбы

Когда пересказываем приказ, просьбу или совет, используем **глагол + объект + to + V**:

| Прямая речь | Косвенная |
|--------------|------------|
| <say>"Open the window."</say> | <say>He told me to open the window.</say> |
| <say>"Don't be late."</say> | <say>She told us not to be late.</say> |
| <say>"Please help me."</say> | <say>He asked me to help him.</say> |
| <say>"You should see a doctor."</say> | <say>She advised me to see a doctor.</say> |

| Тип | Глагол | Шаблон |
|-----|--------|---------|
| **Команда** | tell | <say>tell someone to + V</say> |
| **Просьба** | ask | <say>ask someone to + V</say> |
| **Совет** | advise | <say>advise someone to + V</say> |
| **Запрет** | tell / order | <say>tell someone not to + V</say> |
| **Призыв** | invite, encourage | <say>invite someone to + V</say> |

> **Отрицание:** *not* ставится **перед** *to*: *He told me **not to** be late*.

## Часть 7: Repository verbs — много способов «сказать»

Английский богат глаголами передачи речи. Каждый несёт оттенок. Знать их — значит точно передавать содержание чужой речи.

| Глагол | Перевод | Шаблон |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **say** | сказать | say (to sb) that… |
| **tell** | сказать (кому-то) | tell sb that…; tell sb to + V |
| **ask** | спросить, попросить | ask if/wh-…; ask sb to + V |
| **answer / reply** | ответить | answer/reply that… |
| **explain** | объяснить | explain (to sb) that… |
| **suggest** | предложить | suggest + V-ing; suggest (that) sb (should) + V |
| **advise** | посоветовать | advise sb to + V |
| **recommend** | рекомендовать | recommend + V-ing; recommend that sb… |
| **warn** | предупредить | warn sb (not) to + V |
| **promise** | обещать | promise (sb) to + V; promise that… |
| **agree** | согласиться | agree to + V; agree that… |
| **refuse** | отказаться | refuse to + V |
| **insist** | настаивать | insist on + V-ing; insist that… |
| **complain** | жаловаться | complain (that)…; complain about + V-ing |
| **admit** | признать | admit + V-ing; admit (that)… |
| **deny** | отрицать | deny + V-ing |
| **announce** | объявить | announce that… |

> **Заметь:** *suggest, recommend* требуют **герундия** (*-ing*) или конструкции *that … should*. Не «*suggest to do*» — это типичная ошибка.

| ✅ | <say>He suggested going to the cinema.</say> |
| ❌ | <say>He suggested to go to the cinema.</say> |

## Часть 9: Языковые гаммы

```

```


### Упражнение 1. Передай в косвенной речи

1. <say>Anna said, "I am very busy."</say>
2. <say>Tom said, "I have just finished."</say>

<details>
<summary>Ключ</summary>

1. *Anna said (that) she was very busy.*
2. *Tom said (that) he had just finished.*
3. *Mary said (that) she had gone home early.*
4. *Jack said (that) he would call me the next day.*
5. *She said (that) she couldn't speak Spanish.*
6. *He said (that) he was working from home that day.*
7. *They said (that) they had lived there for ten years.*

</details>

### Упражнение 2. Передай вопросы в косвенной форме

1. <say>"Where do you work?" she asked.</say>
2. <say>"What time is it?" he asked.</say>
3. <say>"Do you speak English?" she asked me.</say>
4. <say>"Have you seen my keys?" he asked.</say>
5. <say>"Why are you crying?" she asked the child.</say>
6. <say>"Will you come to the party?" they asked me.</say>

<details>
<summary>Ключ</summary>

1. *She asked where I worked.*
2. *He asked what time it was.*
3. *She asked me if/whether I spoke English.*
4. *He asked if/whether I had seen his keys.*
5. *She asked the child why he/she was crying.*
6. *They asked me if/whether I would come to the party.*

</details>

### Упражнение 3. Передай команды и просьбы

1. <say>"Sit down," she said to me.</say>
2. <say>"Don't be late," he told us.</say>
3. <say>"Please help me," she said to him.</say>
4. <say>"You should see a doctor," he said to her.</say>
5. <say>"Don't touch that," the teacher said to the children.</say>
6. <say>"Could you open the window?" she asked him.</say>

<details>
<summary>Ключ</summary>

1. *She told me to sit down.*
2. *He told us not to be late.*
3. *She asked him to help her.*
4. *He advised her to see a doctor.*
5. *The teacher told the children not to touch that.*
6. *She asked him to open the window.*

</details>

### Упражнение 4. Найди ошибку

1. <say>He told that he was tired.</say>
2. <say>She said me she was happy.</say>
3. <say>He asked where do I live.</say>
4. <say>She told to me not to come.</say>
5. <say>He suggested to go to the cinema.</say>
6. <say>She asked if I will be there.</say>

<details>
<summary>Ключ</summary>

1. ❌ *He **said** that he was tired.* (или: *He told **me** that he was tired*)
2. ❌ *She **told me** she was happy.* (или: *She **said to me*** she was happy*)
3. ❌ *He asked where **I lived**.* (без *do*, прямой порядок)
4. ❌ *She told **me** not to come.* (после *tell* — без *to*)
5. ❌ *He suggested **going** to the cinema.* (*suggest* + герундий)
6. ❌ *She asked if I **would** be there.* (will → would при сдвиге)

</details>

### Упражнение 5. Переведи на английский (косвенная речь)

1. Анна сказала, что она устала.
2. Том сказал, что он позвонит мне завтра.
3. Мэри сказала, что она уже видела этот фильм.
4. Он спросил, где я живу.
5. Она спросила, говорю ли я по-английски.
6. Учитель сказал детям не шуметь.
7. Он попросил меня открыть окно.
8. Врач посоветовал ей отдохнуть.
9. Она объяснила, что у неё нет времени.
10. Он пообещал, что вернётся вовремя.

<details>
<summary>Ключ</summary>

1. *Anna said (that) she was tired.*
2. *Tom said (that) he would call me the next day.* (or: *Tom said he would call me tomorrow*; *Tom told me he would call me the next day*)
3. *Mary said (that) she had already seen the film.* (or: *…the movie*)
4. *He asked where I lived.*
5. *She asked if I spoke English.* (or: *whether I spoke English*)
6. *The teacher told the children not to make noise.* (or: *…not to be noisy*)
7. *He asked me to open the window.*
8. *The doctor advised her to rest.* (or: *…to take a rest*)
9. *She explained that she didn't have time.* (or: *…she had no time*)
10. *He promised (that) he would come back on time.* (or: *He promised to come back on time*; *He promised he would return on time*)

</details>

### Упражнение 6. Прогон вслух — пересказ диалога

Прямая речь:
> — <say>Anna: "I'm going to Paris next week."</say>
> — <say>Tom: "Have you booked the hotel?"</say>
> — <say>Anna: "Not yet, but I will book it tomorrow."</say>
> — <say>Tom: "Don't forget to take your passport!"</say>
> — <say>Anna: "Thanks for the reminder."</say>

Пересказ:
> <say>Anna said she was going to Paris the following week. Tom asked if she had booked the hotel. Anna said she hadn't yet, but she would book it the next day. Tom told her not to forget to take her passport. Anna thanked him for the reminder.</say>

## Памятка урока

```
КОСВЕННАЯ РЕЧЬ — ТРИ ИЗМЕНЕНИЯ:

  1. ВРЕМЯ: один шаг назад (если ведущий глагол в прошлом)
     Present Simple   → Past Simple
     Present Cont.    → Past Continuous
     Present Perfect  → Past Perfect
     Past Simple      → Past Perfect
     Past Continuous  → Past Perfect Continuous
     will             → would
     can              → could
     may              → might
     must             → had to

  2. МЕСТОИМЕНИЯ:
     I / me / my      → he/she, him/her, his/her
     we / us / our    → they / them / their
     you / your       → (по контексту)

  3. ВРЕМЕННЫЕ И МЕСТНЫЕ МАРКЕРЫ:
     now      → then
     today    → that day
     tomorrow → the next day
     yesterday → the day before
     here     → there
     this/these → that/those
     ago      → before

КОГДА СДВИГА НЕТ:
  - Ведущий глагол сам в настоящем: He says he is tired.
  - Универсальная истина: She said water boils at 100°C.
  - Уже past perfect / would / could — дальше некуда.

SAY vs TELL:
  say (to sb) что-то      He said (to me) he was busy.
  tell sb что-то           He told me he was busy.
  Tell всегда с адресатом, say — нет.

КОСВЕННЫЕ ВОПРОСЫ:
  Wh-:  He asked WHERE I LIVED. (прямой порядок, без do)
  Yes/No: He asked IF / WHETHER I LIVED THERE.
  НИКОГДА в косвенном вопросе:
    - do/does/did
    - инверсия (was I → if I was)
    - знак вопроса

КОСВЕННЫЕ КОМАНДЫ И ПРОСЬБЫ:
  tell sb to + V         He told me to open the door.
  ask sb to + V          He asked me to help.
  advise sb to + V       She advised him to rest.
  Отрицание: tell sb NOT TO + V
    He told me not to be late.

REPORTING VERBS — расширенный список:
  say, tell, ask, answer, reply, explain,
  suggest (+ -ing), recommend (+ -ing),
  advise/warn sb to + V,
  promise / agree / refuse to + V,
  insist on / complain about / admit + V-ing,
  deny + V-ing, announce that…

ЛОВУШКИ ДЛЯ РУССКОГОВОРЯЩЕГО:
  - Не путай say и tell. Tell — с адресатом.
  - В косвенном вопросе НЕТ do/does/did.
  - В косвенном вопросе НЕТ инверсии: "where I lived", не "where lived I".
  - Suggest + герундий, не to-инфинитив.
  - Will → would, can → could, must → had to.
```
