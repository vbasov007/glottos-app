# Test for Lesson 3: Places, objects, fluent reading with nikkud

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson. Translate with nikkud, drawing on the lesson's vocabulary: places around town, everyday objects, the *yesh li / ein li* constructions, the prepositions *ba- / la- / ʿal*. Remember the gender (ʿir — f., dira — f., chanut — f., delet — f., mita — f.).
