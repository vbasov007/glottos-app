# Test for Lesson 25: All 7 Binyanim in the Full Table. Choosing a Binyan for the Right Shade

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew without vowels. One root (ר-א-ה "to see," כ-ת-ב "to write," ש-מ-ר "to keep") can work through different binyanim — choose the binyan by the shade of meaning. All verbs in 3rd-pers. sg. (unless stated otherwise).

---

1. He saw the sea. (ר-א-ה, Pa'al)
2. He showed me a book. (ר-א-ה, Hif'il = "let see")
3. The house was visible from afar. (ר-א-ה, Nif'al)
4. They met (saw each other) in a café. (ר-א-ה, Hitpa'el, pl.)
5. The film was shown yesterday. (ר-א-ה, Huf'al)
6. She looks tired. (ר-א-ה, Nif'al, f.)
7. The teacher will show the students a map. (ר-א-ה, Hif'il, fut.)
8. See you tomorrow! (ר-א-ה, Hitpa'el, 1st-pers. pl., fut.)
9. I saw your brother yesterday. (ר-א-ה, Pa'al, 1st-pers.)
10. The painting was shown in the museum. (ר-א-ה, Huf'al, f.)
11. He wrote a letter. (כ-ת-ב, Pa'al)
12. The letter was written yesterday. (כ-ת-ב, Nif'al)
13. The teacher dictated a sentence to the students. (כ-ת-ב, Hif'il = "made write")
14. They corresponded for years. (כ-ת-ב, Hitpa'el, pl.)
15. The book was written in Jerusalem. (כ-ת-ב, Nif'al, f.)
16. I will write a new letter. (כ-ת-ב, Pa'al, 1st-pers., fut.)
17. Dad will dictate to the secretary. (כ-ת-ב, Hif'il, fut.)
18. They will correspond. (כ-ת-ב, Hitpa'el, pl., fut.)
19. He guarded the house. (ש-מ-ר, Pa'al)
20. The document was saved. (ש-מ-ר, Nif'al)
21. The custom preserved itself. (ש-מ-ר, Hitpa'el with metathesis)
22. The soldiers guarded the border. (ש-מ-ר, Pa'al, pl.)
23. The children are studying at school. (ל-מ-ד, Pa'al, pl.)
24. The teacher teaches the children. (ל-מ-ד, Pi'el, f.)
25. The window broke by itself. (ש-ב-ר, Nif'al)
26. He broke the window. (ש-ב-ר, Pa'al)
27. Which binyan would you pick: "it was made" (from ע-ש-ה)? Name the binyan.
28. Which binyan: "they met" (reciprocity)? Name the binyan.
29. Which binyan: "he made someone study"? Name the binyan.
30. Which binyan: "the door opened by itself"? Name the binyan.

<summary>Keys</summary>

1. *הוא ראה את הים.* (hu ra'ah et ha-yam) — Pa'al, simple active.
2. *הוא הראה לי את הספר.* (hu her'ah li et ha-sefer) — Hif'il, causative "let see."
3. *הבית נראה מרחוק.* (ha-bayit nir'ah me-rachok) — Nif'al, "was visible."
4. *הם התראו בבית הקפה.* (hem hitra'u be-vet ha-kafe) — Hitpa'el, reciprocity.
5. *הסרט הוראה אתמול.* (ha-seret hor'ah etmol) — Huf'al, passive of Hif'il.
6. *היא נראית עייפה.* (hi nir'et ayefa) — Nif'al, present, f.
7. *המורה יראה לתלמידים את המפה.* (ha-more yar'eh la-talmidim et ha-mapa) — Hif'il, fut.
8. *נתראה מחר!* (nitra'eh machar) — Hitpa'el, 1st-pers. pl., fut.
9. *ראיתי את אחיך אתמול.* (ra'iti et achikha etmol) — Pa'al, 1st-pers.
10. *התמונה הוראתה במוזיאון.* (ha-temuna hor'ata ba-muze'on) — Huf'al, f.
11. *הוא כתב מכתב.* (hu katav mikhtav) — Pa'al.
12. *המכתב נכתב אתמול.* (ha-mikhtav nikhtav etmol) — Nif'al, passive.
13. *המורה הכתיב לתלמידים משפט.* (ha-more hikhtiv la-talmidim mishpat) — Hif'il, causative "made write."
14. *הם התכתבו במשך שנים.* (hem hitkatvu be-meshekh shanim) — Hitpa'el, reciprocity.
15. *הספר נכתב בירושלים.* (ha-sefer nikhtav bi-Yerushalayim) — Nif'al. (If the book were f., *הספרה* — no, sefer is m., the form is correct.)
16. *אכתוב מכתב חדש.* (ekhtov mikhtav chadash) — Pa'al, fut.
17. *אבא יכתיב למזכירה.* (aba yakhtiv la-mazkira) — Hif'il, fut.
18. *הם יתכתבו.* (hem yitkatvu) — Hitpa'el, fut.
19. *הוא שמר על הבית.* (hu shamar al ha-bayit) — Pa'al.
20. *המסמך נשמר.* (ha-mismakh nishmar) — Nif'al, passive.
21. *המנהג השתמר.* (ha-minhag hishtamer) — Hitpa'el with metathesis (ת + ש → שת).
22. *החיילים שמרו על הגבול.* (ha-chayalim shamru al ha-gvul) — Pa'al, pl.
23. *הילדים לומדים בבית הספר.* (ha-yeladim lomdim be-vet ha-sefer) — Pa'al, pl., present.
24. *המורה מלמדת את הילדים.* (ha-mora melamedet et ha-yeladim) — Pi'el, f.
25. *החלון נשבר.* (ha-chalon nishbar) — Nif'al, "broke by itself."
26. *הוא שבר את החלון.* (hu shavar et ha-chalon) — Pa'al, active.
27. **Huf'al** — internal passive of Hif'il (no causer). If simple passive — **Nif'al**. From ע-ש-ה the living passive is Nif'al: *na'asah*.
28. **Hitpa'el** — reciprocity.
29. **Hif'il** — causative "make do."
30. **Nif'al** — passive/middle "happened by itself."
