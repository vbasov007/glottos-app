# Test for Lesson 32: Infinitive in every binyan. Modal constructions

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson.

Use modal words (tsarikh, yakhol, kheday, efshar, asur, mutar, chayav) and the right binyan infinitive. Remember: tsarikh/yakhol/chayav agree in gender and number, while efshar/asur/mutar/kheday are usually impersonal. Hebrew — no nikkud.

---

1. I (m.) have to go.
2. I (f.) have to study.
3. He needs to speak with the doctor.
4. She needs to stay at home.
5. We (m.) have to work every day.
6. They (f.) have to get up early.
7. Smoking is forbidden here.
8. May one (in general) enter?
9. You (m.) may sit here.
10. You (f.) should read this book.
11. I (m.) can come tomorrow.
12. She can explain the lesson.
13. We (m.) cannot understand.
14. Can I see the menu, please?
15. Silence must be kept. (impersonal)
16. It's forbidden to enter with food.
17. They (m.) are allowed to use the computer.
18. If you (m.) want to see the film, you have to come earlier.
19. I (m.) am obligated to pay now.
20. She should call him.
21. We (f.) need to get dressed quickly.
22. May I (m.) call you later?
23. Talking during the lesson is forbidden.
24. I (m.) want to study Hebrew.
25. You (m.) can decide for yourself.
26. Sitting on the grass is allowed here.
27. We (m.) need to meet tomorrow.
28. Sometimes it's worth sitting down and thinking.
29. You (f.) mustn't eat this.
30. If you want to speak with the manager, you (m.) need to come early.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *אני צריך ללכת.* (ani tsarikh lalekhet) — tsarikh m. + lalekhet (Pa'al, weak root)
2. *אני צריכה ללמוד.* (ani tsricha lilmod) — tsricha f. + lilmod (Pa'al)
3. *הוא צריך לדבר עם הרופא.* (hu tsarikh ledaber im ha-rofe) — ledaber (Pi'el)
4. *היא צריכה להישאר בבית.* (hi tsricha lehisha'er ba-bayit) — lehisha'er (Nif'al)
5. *אנחנו צריכים לעבוד כל יום.* (anachnu tsrikhim la'avod kol yom) — tsrikhim m. pl.
6. *הן צריכות לקום מוקדם.* (hen tsrikhot lakum mukdam) — tsrikhot f. pl. + lakum (Pa'al)
7. *אסור לעשן כאן.* (asur le'ashen kan) — impersonal asur + le'ashen (Pi'el)
8. *אפשר להיכנס?* (efshar lehikanes?) — efshar + lehikanes (Nif'al)
9. *מותר לך לשבת פה.* (mutar lekha lashevet po) — mutar + lekha + lashevet (Pa'al, weak)
10. *כדאי לך לקרוא את הספר הזה.* (kheday lakh likro et ha-sefer ha-ze) — kheday + lakh + likro (Pa'al)
11. *אני יכול לבוא מחר.* (ani yakhol lavo machar) — yakhol m. + lavo (Pa'al)
12. *היא יכולה להסביר את השיעור.* (hi yekhola lehasbir et ha-shi'ur) — yekhola f. + lehasbir (Hif'il)
13. *אנחנו לא יכולים להבין.* (anachnu lo yekholim lehavin) — yekholim m. pl. + lehavin (Hif'il)
14. *אפשר לראות את התפריט, בבקשה?* (efshar lir'ot et ha-tafrit, bevakasha?) — lir'ot (Pa'al)
15. *צריך לשמור על השקט.* (tsarikh lishmor al ha-sheket) — impersonal tsarikh + lishmor (Pa'al)
16. *אסור להיכנס עם אוכל.* (asur lehikanes im okhel) — asur + lehikanes (Nif'al)
17. *מותר להם להשתמש במחשב.* (mutar lahem lehishtamesh ba-machshev) — mutar + lahem + lehishtamesh (Hitpa'el with metathesis)
18. *אם אתה רוצה לראות את הסרט, אתה צריך לבוא מוקדם.* (im ata rotse lir'ot et ha-seret, ata tsarikh lavo mukdam)
19. *אני חייב לשלם עכשיו.* (ani chayav leshalem achshav) — chayav m. (stronger than tsarikh) + leshalem (Pi'el)
20. *כדאי לה להתקשר אליו.* (kheday la lehitkasher elav) — kheday + la = "to her" + lehitkasher (Hitpa'el)
21. *אנחנו צריכות להתלבש מהר.* (anachnu tsrikhot lehit'lavesh maher) — tsrikhot f. pl. + lehit'lavesh (Hitpa'el)
22. *אפשר לי להתקשר אליך מאוחר יותר?* (efshar li lehitkasher elekha me'uchar yoter?) — efshar + li + lehitkasher
23. *אסור לדבר בזמן השיעור.* (asur ledaber bi-zman ha-shi'ur) — impersonal asur + ledaber (Pi'el)
24. *אני רוצה ללמוד עברית.* (ani rotse lilmod ivrit) — rotse m. + lilmod
25. *אתה יכול להחליט בעצמך.* (ata yakhol lehachlit be-atsmekha) — yakhol + lehachlit (Hif'il)
26. *מותר לשבת פה על הדשא.* (mutar lashevet po al ha-deshe) — impersonal mutar + lashevet
27. *אנחנו צריכים להיפגש מחר.* (anachnu tsrikhim lehipagesh machar) — tsrikhim + lehipagesh (Nif'al)
28. *לפעמים כדאי לשבת ולחשוב.* (lefamim kheday lashevet ve-lachshov) — impersonal kheday + lashevet + lachshov (Pa'al)
29. *אסור לך לאכול את זה.* (asur lakh le'ekhol et ze) — asur + lakh = "to you f." + le'ekhol (Pa'al)
30. *אם אתה רוצה לדבר עם המנהל, אתה צריך לבוא מוקדם.* (im ata rotse ledaber im ha-menahel, ata tsarikh lavo mukdam)

> **Principle:** first pick the modal by meaning (obligation? ability? permission? prohibition? advice?), then agree it in gender/number with the subject (if it changes), then put the infinitive according to the binyan of the root.
