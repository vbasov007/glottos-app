# Block-Test 4 (Lessons 31–40): Sar / Sara — complex syntax and heavy morphology

**How do you say it in Hebrew (without nikud)?**
This is a cumulative test for the fourth block. Translate into Hebrew. Use: indirect speech with **she-** / **ki** (L31), infinitive + modals **tzarikh / efshar / rotze** (L32), real / hypothetical / counterfactual conditionals with **im** and **ilu** (L33), verbal nouns shem pe'ula (L34), conjunctions **ksheh- / kdei she- / mipnei she-** (L35), passive and impersonal constructions (L36), advanced smikhut — chains, smikhut + adjective (L37), word-order shifts for emphasis (L38), essay connectors **ulam / lakhen / lamrot ze** (L39), long compound sentences with nested clauses (L40).

---

1. I think Hebrew is a difficult language.
2. He said that he would arrive on Sunday. (indirect speech, ki)
3. The teacher (f.) explained that the exam would be postponed.
4. I didn't know that you were sick.
5. You need to study a lot to reach B2. (tzarikh + inf. + kdei + inf.)
6. May I come in? — Of course you may. (efshar)
7. I'd like to talk with you. (rotze + inf., polite hayiti rotze)
8. To study in Israel is a dream. (inf. as subject)
9. If I have time, I'll come. (im — real condition)
10. If I had known, I would have told you. (ilu — counterfactual)
11. If she were here, we'd talk with her. (ilu)
12. If you come early, we'll have dinner together. (im + fut.)
13. Reading books develops thinking. (shem pe'ula from Pa'al kri'a)
14. Entry into the building is permitted only to employees. (shem pe'ula knisa)
15. Studying Hebrew requires patience. (shem pe'ula limud)
16. The development of technology has changed the world. (shem pe'ula from Hitpa'el hitpatchut)
17. When I got home, everyone was already asleep. (ksheh-)
18. I'm learning Hebrew in order to talk with my grandmother. (kdei she- or kdei + inf.)
19. I didn't come because I was sick. (mipnei she-)
20. Before he started working, he studied at the university. (lifney she-)
21. Although it was raining, we went for a walk. (lamrot she-)
22. The report was published yesterday. (Pu'al or Hif'il-Huf'al)
23. The decision was taken by the government. (Hif'il-Huf'al + al yedey)
24. People read a lot in this country. (impersonal: "they read" = kor'im)
25. Three languages are spoken here. (medabrim)
26. The mayor's car was stolen. (smikhut + Nif'al past)
27. The head of our country's Ministry of Internal Security will speak tomorrow. (long smikhut)
28. The new building of Tel Aviv University was built in the city centre. (smikhut + adj.)
29. It is exactly this student who solved the problem. (emphasis: davka)
30. This is the book I was looking for. (it-cleft via ze + she-)
31. As for the exam — it will be easy. (topic: lega'bey)
32. Tel Aviv is a big city; however, Jerusalem is older. (ulam)
33. Prices are going up, therefore we must save. (lakhen)
34. Despite this, we keep on working. (lamrot ze)
35. In addition, we have other plans too. (mi-lvad ze or nosaf le-khakh)
36. Since the situation demands an immediate solution, it is important to act quickly.
37. Although the experts warned of the danger, the government did not react.
38. After we have dinner, I'll call my parents.
39. The teacher (f.) who taught us for three years said that the exam would be postponed.
40. In order to understand the phenomenon, one must analyse the data that the scholars collected.
41. I think that you remember the house we grew up in.
42. If I had more time, I would learn five more languages.
43. The minister announced a new project that will be presented next week.
44. The journalist who wrote the article claimed that the facts he brought proved his hypothesis.
45. It is important to understand that the development of the economy depends on the investments the state makes.
46. Despite the difficulties the team faced, the research was completed on time.
47. After the publication of the report by the ministry, the public demanded explanations.
48. The journalist claims that if the government does not react, the crisis will deepen.
49. The scientist announced the results of the research, which lasted ten years.
50. Since the data the team collected show a clear trend, it is important to publish the conclusions as soon as possible.

1. *אני חושב שעברית היא שפה קשה.* (Ani choshev she-ivrit hi safa kasha.) — L31, indirect speech via she-.
2. *הוא אמר כי הוא יגיע ביום ראשון.* (Hu amar ki hu yagia be-yom rishon.) — L31, formal ki.
3. *המורה הסבירה שהמבחן יידחה.* (Ha-mora hisbira she-ha-mivchan yidache.) — L31/L14/L36, Hif'il + Nif'al fut. (passive).
4. *לא ידעתי שאתה חולה.* (Lo yadati she-ata chole.) — L31, she- after a verb of knowing.
5. *צריך ללמוד הרבה כדי להגיע ל-B2.* (Tzarikh lilmod harbe kdei lehagia le-B2.) — L32/L35, tzarikh + inf. + kdei + inf.
6. *אפשר להיכנס? — בוודאי, אפשר.* (Efshar lehikanes? — Bevaday, efshar.) — L32, efshar + Nif'al inf.
7. *הייתי רוצה לדבר איתך.* (Hayiti rotze ledaber itkha.) — L32, polite "would like".
8. *ללמוד בישראל זה חלום.* (Lilmod be-Israel ze chalom.) — L32, inf. as subject.
9. *אם יהיה לי זמן, אבוא.* (Im yihye li zman, avo.) — L33, real condition: both verbs in fut.
10. *אילו ידעתי, הייתי אומר לך.* (Ilu yadati, hayiti omer lekha.) — L33, counterfactual: ilu + past, haya + present.
11. *אילו היא הייתה פה, היינו מדברים איתה.* (Ilu hi hayta po, hayinu medabrim ita.) — L33, counterfactual via hayta.
12. *אם תבוא מוקדם, נאכל ארוחת ערב יחד.* (Im tavo mukdam, nokhal aruchat erev yachad.) — L33/L20, real condition + smikhut.
13. *קריאת ספרים מפתחת חשיבה.* (Kri'at sfarim mefatachat chashiva.) — L34, shem pe'ula kri'a (from Pa'al k-r-a).
14. *הכניסה לבניין מותרת רק לעובדים.* (Ha-knisa la-binyan muteret rak la-ovdim.) — L34, shem pe'ula knisa (from Nif'al).
15. *לימוד עברית דורש סבלנות.* (Limud ivrit doresh savlanut.) — L34, shem pe'ula limud (from Pi'el).
16. *התפתחות הטכנולוגיה שינתה את העולם.* (Hitpatchut ha-technologia shinta et ha-olam.) — L34, shem pe'ula from Hitpa'el.
17. *כשהגעתי הביתה, כולם כבר ישנו.* (Ksheh-higati ha-bayta, kulam kvar yashnu.) — L35, ksheh-.
18. *אני לומד עברית כדי לדבר עם סבתא.* (Ani lomed ivrit kdei ledaber im savta.) — L35, kdei + inf. (one subject).
19. *לא באתי מפני שהייתי חולה.* (Lo bati mipnei she-hayiti chole.) — L35, mipnei she-.
20. *לפני שהוא התחיל לעבוד, הוא למד באוניברסיטה.* (Lifney she-hu hitchil la'avod, hu lamad ba-universita.) — L35, lifney she- + Hif'il past.
21. *למרות שירד גשם, יצאנו לטייל.* (Lamrot she-yarad geshem, yatzanu letayel.) — L35, lamrot she-.
22. *הדוח פורסם אתמול.* (Ha-doch pursam etmol.) — L36/L24, Pu'al past 3 m.
23. *ההחלטה התקבלה על ידי הממשלה.* (Ha-hachlata hitkabla al yedey ha-memshala.) — L36, "was taken" + al yedey-agent.
24. *במדינה הזאת קוראים הרבה.* (Ba-medina ha-zot kor'im harbe.) — L36, impersonal "kor'im" = "(they) read".
25. *כאן מדברים בשלוש שפות.* (Kan medabrim be-shalosh safot.) — L36, impersonal medabrim.
26. *המכונית של ראש העיר נגנבה.* (Ha-mekhonit shel rosh ha-ir nignva.) — L37/L20, smikhut rosh ha-ir + Nif'al past.
27. *ראש המשרד לביטחון פנים של ארצנו ינאם מחר.* (Rosh ha-misrad le-vitachon pnim shel artzenu yin'am machar.) — L37, long smikhut + shel.
28. *בניין אוניברסיטת תל אביב החדש נבנה במרכז העיר.* (Binyan universitat Tel Aviv he-chadash nivna be-merkaz ha-ir.) — L37, smikhut "binyan universita(T) Tel Aviv" + the adjective at the end of the chain.
29. *דווקא התלמיד הזה פתר את התרגיל.* (Davka ha-talmid ha-ze patar et ha-targil.) — L38, emphasis via davka.
30. *זה הספר שחיפשתי.* (Ze ha-sefer she-chipasti.) — L38, it-cleft.
31. *לגבי המבחן — הוא יהיה קל.* (Le-gabey ha-mivchan — hu yihye kal.) — L38, topicalisation.
32. *תל אביב היא עיר גדולה, אולם ירושלים עתיקה יותר.* (Tel Aviv hi ir gdola, ulam Yerushalayim atika yoter.) — L39, ulam "however".
33. *המחירים עולים, לכן צריך לחסוך.* (Ha-mechirim olim, lakhen tzarikh lachasoch.) — L39, lakhen "therefore".
34. *למרות זאת, אנחנו ממשיכים לעבוד.* (Lamrot zot, anachnu mamshikhim la'avod.) — L39, lamrot zot "despite this".

36. *מאחר שהמצב דורש פתרון מיידי, חשוב לפעול במהירות.* (Me'achar she-ha-matsav doresh pitaron miyadi, chashuv lif'ol bi-mehirut.) — L35/L40, me'achar she-.
37. *למרות אזהרות המומחים מפני הסכנה, הממשלה לא הגיבה.* (Lamrot azharot ha-mumchim mi-pney ha-sakana, ha-memshala lo hegiva.) — L35/L34, lamrot + shem pe'ula azharot.
38. *אחרי שנאכל ארוחת ערב, אתקשר להורים.* (Acharei she-nokhal aruchat erev, etkasher la-horim.) — L35, acharei she- + fut.
39. *המורה, שלימדה אותנו שלוש שנים, אמרה שהמבחן יידחה.* (Ha-mora, she-limda otanu shalosh shanim, amra she-ha-mivchan yidache.) — L29/L31/L36, nested clauses.
40. *כדי להבין את התופעה, יש לנתח את הנתונים שאספו החוקרים.* (Kdei lehavin et ha-tofa'a, yesh lenate'ach et ha-netunim she-asfu ha-chokrim.) — L35/L32, kdei + inf. + yesh + inf. (impersonal "one must").
41. *אני חושב שאתה זוכר את הבית שגדלנו בו.* (Ani choshev she-ata zokher et ha-bayit she-gadalnu bo.) — L31/L29, she- + resumptive bo.
42. *אילו היה לי יותר זמן, הייתי לומד עוד חמש שפות.* (Ilu haya li yoter zman, hayiti lomed od chamesh safot.) — L33, counterfactual.
43. *השר הודיע על פרויקט חדש שיוצג בשבוע הבא.* (Ha-sar hodi'a al project chadash she-yutzag ba-shavua ha-ba.) — L36, Hif'il + Huf'al fut. (passive).
44. *העיתונאי, שכתב את הכתבה, טען שהעובדות שהביא מוכיחות את ההשערה.* (Ha-itona'i, she-katav et ha-katava, ta'an she-ha-uvdot she-hevi mochichot et ha-hashara.) — L29/L31/L40, triple embedding.
45. *חשוב להבין שהתפתחות הכלכלה תלויה בהשקעות שעושה המדינה.* (Chashuv lehavin she-hitpatchut ha-kalkala tluya ba-hashka'ot she-osa ha-medina.) — L34/L31, shem pe'ula hitpatchut + she-.
46. *למרות הקשיים שאיתם התמודד הצוות, המחקר הסתיים בזמן.* (Lamrot ha-kshayim she-itam hitmoded ha-tsevet, ha-mechkar histayem ba-zman.) — L35/L29, lamrot + relative with resumptive itam.
47. *לאחר פרסום הדוח על ידי המשרד, הציבור דרש הסברים.* (Le'achar pirsum ha-doch al yedey ha-misrad, ha-tsibur darash hesberim.) — L34/L36, shem pe'ula pirsum + al yedey.
48. *העיתונאי טוען שאם הממשלה לא תגיב, המשבר יחריף.* (Ha-itona'i to'en she-im ha-memshala lo tagiv, ha-mashber yachrif.) — L31/L33, indirect speech + real condition.
49. *המדען הודיע על תוצאות המחקר, שנמשך עשר שנים.* (Ha-mada'an hodi'a al totsa'ot ha-mechkar, she-nimshach eser shanim.) — L29/L37, smikhut totsa'ot ha-mechkar + Nif'al past.
50. *מאחר שהנתונים שאסף הצוות מראים מגמה ברורה, חשוב לפרסם את המסקנות בהקדם האפשרי.* (Me'achar she-ha-netunim she-asaf ha-tsevet mar'im megama brura, chashuv lefarsem et ha-maskanot ba-hekdem ha-efshari.) — L35/L40, me'achar she- + zero-object resumptive "she-asaf".

> **Block 4 takeaway:** by L40 a Hebrew sentence can nest 2–3 clauses inside another. The key technique is **compression**: a long English chain like "which was published by the ministry" is often replaced with shem pe'ula + al yedey: *pirsum ha-doch al yedey ha-misrad*. The counterfactual **ilu** (not im!) requires **haya + participle** in both halves. Essay connectors (ulam, lakhen, lamrot zot, nosaf le-khakh) are the backbone of formal text — learn them in pairs or triples, not as a list.
