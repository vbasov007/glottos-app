# Test for Lesson 34: Verbal nouns (shem pe'ula) by binyan

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson.

Translate into Hebrew (without nikkud). Use verbal nouns from the five productive binyanim's models: Pa'al → CCiCa (ktiva), Pi'el → CiCuC (sipur), Hif'il → haCCaCa (haknasa), Nif'al → hiCaCCut (hikansut), Hitpa'el → hitCaCCut (hit'orerut).

---

1. Writing is an art. (Pa'al → ktiva)
2. Reading books is important. (Pa'al → kri'a)
3. Learning (as a process) is a long road. (Pa'al → lemida)
4. The choice was hard. (Pa'al → bchira)
5. The opening of the store was yesterday. (Pa'al → ptikha)
6. The story is interesting. (Pi'el → sipur)
7. Education in Israel is important. (Pi'el → chinukh)
8. The management of the company is complex. (Pi'el → nihul)
9. The search for work continues. (Pi'el → chipus)
10. The change is fast. (Pi'el → shinui)
11. The receipt of the passport takes a month. (Pi'el → kabala)
12. The state's income has dropped. (Hif'il → haknasa)
13. The company's expenses have grown. (Hif'il → hotsa'a)
14. The teacher's decision was correct. (Hif'il → hachlata)
15. Registration for the course is open. (Hif'il → harshama)
16. The invitation (order) is ready. (Hif'il → hazmana)
17. Success in studies is important. (Hif'il → hatslacha)
18. The investment is large. (Hif'il → hashka'a)
19. The announcement was on the news. (Hif'il → hakhraza)
20. The child's development is fast. (Hitpa'el → hitpatchut)
21. The awakening was early. (Hitpa'el → hit'orerut)
22. Her excitement was great. (Hitpa'el → hitragshut)
23. Interest in the topic is growing. (Hitpa'el → hit'anyenut)
24. The correspondence with the friend has lasted years. (Hitpa'el → hitkatvut)
25. The progress is noticeable. (Hitpa'el → hitkadmut)
26. His attitude to work is good. (Hitpa'el → hityachasut)
27. The entry into the hall was quiet. (Nif'al → hikansut)
28. Caution is necessary. (Nif'al → hizaharut)
29. The teacher's absence was noted. (Nif'al → he'adrut)
30. The decision to close the company was accepted. (hachlata + sgira — two shem pe'ula)

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *הכתיבה היא אומנות.* — **ha-ktiva hi omanut** (ktiva, Pa'al shem pe'ula from likhtov)
2. *קריאת ספרים חשובה.* — **kri'at sfarim chashuva** (kri'a in smikhut → kri'at, Pa'al from likro)
3. *הלמידה היא דרך ארוכה.* — **ha-lemida hi derekh aruka** (lemida, Pa'al from lilmod)
4. *הבחירה הייתה קשה.* — **ha-bchira hayta kasha** (bchira, Pa'al from livchor)
5. *פתיחת החנות הייתה אתמול.* — **ptichat ha-chanut hayta etmol** (ptikha → ptichat in smikhut, Pa'al from liftoach)
6. *הסיפור מעניין.* — **ha-sipur me'anyen** (sipur, Pi'el from lesaper)
7. *החינוך בישראל חשוב.* — **ha-chinukh be-Yisra'el chashuv** (chinukh, Pi'el from lechanekh)
8. *ניהול החברה מסובך.* — **nihul ha-chevra mesubakh** (nihul, Pi'el from lenahel; smikhut)
9. *החיפוש אחר עבודה נמשך.* — **ha-chipus achar avoda nimshakh** (chipus, Pi'el from lechapes)
10. *השינוי מהיר.* — **ha-shinui mahir** (shinui, Pi'el from leshanot)
11. *קבלת הדרכון אורכת חודש.* — **kabalat ha-darkon orekhet chodesh** (kabala → kabalat in smikhut, Pi'el from lekabel)
12. *הכנסות המדינה ירדו.* — **haknasot ha-medina yardu** (haknasa, pl. haknasot, Hif'il from lehakhnis)
13. *הוצאות החברה גדלו.* — **hotsa'ot ha-chevra gadlu** (hotsa'a, pl., Hif'il from lehotsi)
14. *ההחלטה של המורה הייתה נכונה.* — **ha-hachlata shel ha-more hayta nekhona** (hachlata, Hif'il from lehachlit)
15. *ההרשמה לקורס פתוחה.* — **ha-harshama la-kurs ptucha** (harshama, Hif'il from leharshim)
16. *ההזמנה מוכנה.* — **ha-hazmana mukhana** (hazmana, Hif'il from lehazmin)
17. *ההצלחה בלימודים חשובה.* — **ha-hatslacha ba-limudim chashuva** (hatslacha, Hif'il from lehatsliach)
18. *ההשקעה גדולה.* — **ha-hashka'a gdola** (hashka'a, Hif'il from lehashki'a)
19. *ההכרזה הייתה בחדשות.* — **ha-hakhraza hayta ba-chadashot** (hakhraza, Hif'il from lehakhriz)
20. *ההתפתחות של הילד מהירה.* — **ha-hitpatchut shel ha-yeled mehira** (hitpatchut, Hitpa'el from lehitpate'ach)
21. *ההתעוררות הייתה מוקדמת.* — **ha-hit'orerut hayta mukdemet** (hit'orerut, Hitpa'el from lehit'orer)
22. *ההתרגשות שלה הייתה גדולה.* — **ha-hitragshut shela hayta gdola** (hitragshut, Hitpa'el from lehitragesh)
23. *ההתעניינות בנושא גדלה.* — **ha-hit'anyenut ba-nose gdela** (hit'anyenut, Hitpa'el from lehit'anyen)
24. *ההתכתבות עם החבר נמשכת שנים.* — **ha-hitkatvut im ha-chaver nimshekhet shanim** (hitkatvut, Hitpa'el from lehitkatev)
25. *ההתקדמות ניכרת.* — **ha-hitkadmut nikeret** (hitkadmut, Hitpa'el from lehitkadem)
26. *ההתייחסות שלו לעבודה טובה.* — **ha-hityachasut shelo la-avoda tova** (hityachasut, Hitpa'el from lehityaches)
27. *הכניסה לאולם הייתה שקטה.* — **ha-knisa la-ulam hayta sheketa** (knisa — colloquial Pa'al form; Nif'al variant: ha-hikansut)
28. *ההיזהרות הכרחית.* — **ha-hizaharut hekhrechit** (hizaharut, Nif'al from lehizaher)
29. *ההיעדרות של המורה נרשמה.* — **ha-he'adrut shel ha-more nirshema** (he'adrut, Nif'al from lehe'ader)
30. *ההחלטה על סגירת החברה התקבלה.* — **ha-hachlata al sgirat ha-chevra hitkabla** (two shem pe'ula: hachlata from Hif'il + sgira → sgirat in smikhut, Pa'al from lisgor)
