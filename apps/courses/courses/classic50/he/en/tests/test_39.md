# Test for Lesson 39: Full inventory of connectors

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson.
Translate without vowel points. Use the full connector set: gam, aval, akh, ela, lakhen, im zot, mi-kol mekom, larot zot, be-khol zot, rishonit, shenit, lebsof. Pay close attention to register: aval — neutral, akh — formal, ela — only after lo.

---

1. I wanted to come, but I couldn't. (neutral)
2. He is not a teacher, but a doctor.
3. It started to rain, therefore we stayed at home.
4. The study lasted a year, however the results were not unambiguous. (formal)
5. I too think so.
6. First, we have no money. Second, we have no time. Finally, we have no desire.
7. The book is interesting, but very long. (neutral)
8. This is not the book, but another one.
9. There was no evidence, from which it follows that the judge acquitted her. (formal)
10. It was cold, in any case we went out for a walk.
11. He was late, nevertheless we started without him. (formal)
12. I had no strength, all the same I went to work.
13. He speaks both Hebrew and English.
14. I'm tired, but I finished the work.
15. The house is not red, but blue.
16. Despite this, we enjoyed ourselves. (formal)
17. First, the study was expensive. Second, we don't have enough participants.
18. The family didn't agree, all the same he married her.
19. The cake is tasty, but very expensive.
20. This article is short, however very profound. (formal)
21. He didn't come, so we started without him. (colloquial)
22. The road is blocked, so we'll go a different way. (colloquial)
23. He promised to come, despite this, he didn't show up. (formal)
24. I didn't want to go, but I went anyway.
25. The country has many merits, however zero growth. (formal)
26. The hearings continued, along with this, the plaintiff agreed to wait.
27. Finally, it's worth waiting another year.
28. I love coffee and tea.
29. He is not an engineer, but an architect.
30. First, the weather is bad. Second, there are no participants. Finally, let's postpone the meeting.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *רציתי לבוא, אבל לא יכולתי.* (Ratziti lavo, aval lo yakholti.) — neutral.
2. *הוא לא מורה, אלא רופא.* (Hu lo more, ela rofé.) — after lo
3. *ירד גשם, לכן נשארנו בבית.* (Yarad geshem, lakhen nisharnu ba-bait.)
4. *המחקר נמשך שנה, אך התוצאות לא היו חד-משמעיות.* (Ha-mehkar nimshakh shanah, akh ha-totza'ot lo hayu chad-mashma'iyot.) — formal
5. *גם אני חושב כך.* (Gam ani choshev kakh.) — gam before ani
6. *ראשית, אין לנו כסף. שנית, אין לנו זמן. לבסוף, אין לנו חפץ.* (Rishonit, ein lanu kesef. Shenit, ein lanu zman. Lebsof, ein lanu chefetz.)
7. *הספר מעניין, אבל ארוך מאוד.* (Ha-sefer me'anyen, aval aroch me'od.)
8. *זה לא הספר, אלא אחר.* (Ze lo ha-sefer, ela acher.) — after lo
9. *לא היו עדויות, מכך ש-השופט זיכה אותה.* (Lo hayu eduyot, mi-kakh she-ha-shofet zikah otah.) — formal
10. *היה קר, מכל מקום יצאנו לטיול.* (Hayah kar, mi-kol mekom yatsanu letiyul.)
11. *הוא איחר, לראות זאת, התחלנו בלעדיו.* (Hu i'cher, larot zot, hithalnu bli'ado.) — formal
12. *לא היה לי כוח, בכל זאת הלכתי לעבודה.* (Lo hayah li koach, be-khol zot halakhti la-avodah.)
13. *הוא מדבר גם עברית וגם אנגלית.* (Hu medaber gam ivrit ve-gam anglit.) — paired gam… ve-gam…
14. *אני עייף, אבל גמרתי את העבודה.* (Ani ayef, aval gamarti et ha-avodah.)
15. *הבית לא אדום, אלא כחול.* (Ha-bayit lo adom, ela kachol.) — after lo
16. *לראות זאת, נהנינו.* (Larot zot, neheneinu.) — formal
17. *ראשית, המחקר היה יקר. שנית, אין לנו מספיק משתתפים.* (Rishonit, ha-mehkar haya yakar. Shenit, ein lanu maspik mishtatfim.)
18. *המשפחה לא הסכימה, בכל זאת הוא התחתן איתה.* (Ha-mishpacha lo hiskima, be-khol zot hu hithaten itah.)
19. *העוגה טעימה, אבל יקרה מאוד.* (Ha-uga te'ima, aval yekara me'od.)
20. *המאמר הזה קצר, אך עמוק מאוד.* (Ha-ma'amar ha-ze katzar, akh amok me'od.) — formal
21. *הוא לא בא, ככה ש-התחלנו בלעדיו.* (Hu lo bah, kakh-she-hithalnu bli'ado.) — colloquial
22. *הכביש חסום, אז ניסע בדרך אחרת.* (Ha-kvish chasum, az nis'a be-derekh acheret.) — colloquial az
23. *הוא הבטיח לבוא, לראות זאת, לא הופיע.* (Hu hivtiach lavo, larot zot, lo hofi'a.) — formal
24. *לא רציתי ללכת, אלא הלכתי בכל זאת.* (Lo ratziti lalekhet, ela halakhti be-khol zot.) — lo is present → ela
25. *יש למדינה תרומות רבות, אך אפס בצמיחה.* (Yesh la-medinah trumot rabot, akh efes ba-tsmichah.) — formal
26. *המשפט שער נמשך, עם זאת, התובע הסכים לחכות.* (Ha-mishpat sha'ar nimshakh, im zot, ha-tovel hiskim lechakot.)
27. *לבסוף, כדאי לחכות עוד שנה.* (Lebsof, kdai lechakot od shanah.)
28. *אני אוהב קפה ותה.* (Ani ohev kafe ve-tey.) — prefix ve-
29. *הוא לא מהנדס, אלא אדריכל.* (Hu lo mehandes, ela adrikhal.) — after lo
30. *ראשית, מזג האוויר רע. שנית, אין משתתפים. לבסוף, נדחה את הפגישה.* (Rishonit, mezeg ha-avir ra. Shenit, ein mishtatfim. Lebsof, nidcheh et ha-pgisha.)

> Check yourself: aval — neutral (1, 7, 14, 19); akh — formal (4, 20, 25); ela — only after lo (2, 8, 15, 24, 29); lakhen — neutral consequence (3); mi-kakh she- — formal (9); az / kakh-she- — colloquial (21, 22); mi-kol mekom / larot zot / be-khol zot / im zot — concession (10, 11, 12, 16, 18, 23, 26); rishonit / shenit / lebsof — sequence (6, 17, 27, 30); gam / gam… ve-gam… — addition (5, 13, 28).
