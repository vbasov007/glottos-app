> Notice: "more than a thousand" — yoter **me-** elef (mi- merges into **me-** before a vowel). Same with "less than ten" — pachot **me-** asara.
> Fractions 1/2, 1/3, 1/4 — unique words (chetzi, shlish, reva). Fractions from 1/5 — by the -it pattern (chamishit, shishit...).
> ahuz after a large number often stays singular: chamishim ahuz, me'a ahuz. After 2–10 — plural: shnei ahuzim, chamisha ahuzim.
