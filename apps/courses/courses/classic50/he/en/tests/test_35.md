# Test for Lesson 35: Adverbial subordinate clauses — time, purpose, cause, concession

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson.

Translate the phrases into Hebrew without nikkud. Use *kshe-*, *kedei (she-)*, *mipnei she-* / *ki* / *biglal (she-)*, *lamrot (she-)* / *af al pi she-*. Remember: *kedei she-* always requires the **future**; if the subject is the same — *kedei* + infinitive.

---

1. When I get up in the morning, I drink coffee.
2. When he came, we had already eaten.
3. When you come, we'll talk.
4. Before I ate, I took a shower.
5. After we finished, we went home.
6. Since I moved here, I'm happy.
7. We'll wait until he comes.
8. I'm studying to succeed.
9. I came to see you.
10. He's saving to buy an apartment.
11. I'm teaching him so that he passes the exam.
12. I called so that you would know.
13. So that the children understand, I'll explain slowly.
14. So that you come tomorrow, you have to buy a ticket.
15. I'm keeping quiet so they can sleep.
16. I didn't come because I was sick. (colloquial)
17. I didn't come because I was sick. (neutral)
18. Because of the rain we stayed home.
19. Because of the fact that it rained, we stayed home.
20. Since I have no time, I'll be brief.
21. Since he is a manager, he decides. (bookish)
22. I'm studying Hebrew because I live in Israel.
23. Although it was raining, we went for a walk.
24. Despite the rain, we went for a walk.
25. Despite the fact that he is young, he is smart. (formal)
26. I'll come, although I'm tired.
27. Although there's no time, I'm coming.
28. It was raining. We went out anyway.
29. When I was a child, I lived in Haifa.
30. I'm explaining slowly so that everyone understands.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *כשאני קם בבוקר, אני שותה קפה.* (kshe-ani kam ba-boker, ani shote kafe)
2. *כשהוא בא, כבר אכלנו.* (kshe-hu ba, kvar achalnu)
3. *כשתבוא, נדבר.* (kshe-tavo, nedaber)
4. *לפני שאכלתי, התקלחתי.* (lifnei she-achalti, hitkalachti)
5. *אחרי שגמרנו, הלכנו הביתה.* (acharei she-gamarnu, halachnu ha-bayta)
6. *מאז שעברתי לכאן, אני שמח.* (me'az she-avarti le-kan, ani sameach)
7. *נחכה עד שיגיע.* (nechake ad she-yagia)
8. *אני לומד כדי להצליח.* (ani lomed kedei lehatzliach) — same subject, infinitive
9. *באתי כדי לראות אותך.* (bati kedei lir'ot otkha) — same subject, infinitive
10. *הוא חוסך כדי לקנות דירה.* (hu chosech kedei liknot dira)
11. *אני מלמד אותו כדי שיצליח במבחן.* (ani melamed oto kedei she-yatzliach ba-mivchan) — different subjects, fut.
12. *התקשרתי כדי שתדעי.* (hitkasharti kedei she-ted'i) — different subjects, fut., f.
13. *כדי שהילדים יבינו, אסביר לאט.* (kedei she-ha-yeladim yavinu, asbir le'at)
14. *כדי שתבוא מחר, צריך לקנות כרטיס.* (kedei she-tavo machar, tsarich liknot kartis)
15. *אני שותק כדי שיוכלו לישון.* (ani shotek kedei she-yuchlu lishon)
16. *לא באתי כי הייתי חולה.* (lo bati ki hayiti chole)
17. *לא באתי מפני שהייתי חולה.* (lo bati mipnei she-hayiti chole)
18. *בגלל הגשם נשארנו בבית.* (biglal ha-geshem nish'arnu ba-bayit) — *biglal* + noun
19. *בגלל שירד גשם, נשארנו בבית.* (biglal she-yarad geshem, nish'arnu ba-bayit) — *biglal she-* + clause
20. *מכיוון שאין לי זמן, אדבר קצר.* (mikeyvan she-ein li zman, adaber katsar)
21. *היות שהוא מנהל, הוא מחליט.* (heyot she-hu menahel, hu machlit)
22. *אני לומד עברית כי אני גר בישראל.* (ani lomed ivrit ki ani gar be-Yisrael)
23. *למרות שהיה גשם, יצאנו לטייל.* (lamrot she-haya geshem, yatzanu letayel)
24. *למרות הגשם, יצאנו לטייל.* (lamrot ha-geshem, yatzanu letayel) — *lamrot* + noun
25. *אף על פי שהוא צעיר, הוא חכם.* (af al pi she-hu tsa'ir, hu chacham)
26. *אני אבוא, למרות שאני עייף.* (ani avo, lamrot she-ani ayef)
27. *למרות שאין זמן, אני בא.* (lamrot she-ein zman, ani ba)
28. *ירד גשם. בכל זאת יצאנו.* (yarad geshem. be-chol zot yatzanu) — or *אף על פי כן יצאנו* (af al pi chen yatzanu)
29. *כשהייתי ילד, גרתי בחיפה.* (kshe-hayiti yeled, garti be-Haifa)
30. *אני מסביר לאט כדי שכולם יבינו.* (ani masbir le'at kedei she-kulam yavinu)
