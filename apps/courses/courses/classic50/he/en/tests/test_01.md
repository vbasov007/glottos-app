# Test for Lesson 1: Alphabet, greetings, numbers 1–10

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson. Remember: the forms of "how are you", "what's your name", and the numbers all change with gender.

---

1. Hello!
2. Good morning!
3. Good evening!
4. Good night.
5. Goodbye!
6. Bye! (informal, borrowed)
7. Thank you.
8. Thanks a lot.
9. Please.
10. Sorry.
11. Yes.
12. No.
13. How are you? (to a man)
14. How are you? (to a woman)
15. Good, thanks.
16. What's your name? (to a man)
17. What's your name? (to a woman)
18. My name is Dana.
19. Very nice to meet you.
20. What? Who?
21. Book.
22. Page.
23. Teacher (m.) and teacher (f.).
24. Pupil (m.) and pupil (f.).
25. Write the number: 3 (for books, m.)
26. Write the number: 5 (for female teachers, f.)
27. Write the number: 7 (for male pupils, m.)
28. Write the number: 10 (for pages, m.)
29. Hello! What's your name? — My name is Yossi.
30. Good morning! How are you? — Good, thanks.
