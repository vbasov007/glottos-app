# Test for Lesson 36: Passive voice, impersonal constructions, valency

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson.
Use the three passive paths: formal binyanim (Nif'al, Pu'al, Huf'al), impersonal 3rd person plural (omrim, kotvim), and the passive infinitive (lehi-). Remember valency: if the verb takes a preposition, there is no passive — use 3rd person plural.

---

1. The book was written by a famous writer.
2. They say on the radio that it will rain tomorrow.
3. This document must be sent today.
4. The news was published in the newspaper yesterday.
5. My wallet was stolen.
6. They sell fresh bread here.
7. The house was built last year.
8. They write in the newspaper that prices are rising.
9. The decision must be taken by the end of the week.
10. The bank called me.
11. The plan was approved by the director.
12. They opened a new restaurant on Allenby Street.
13. The letter was sent to the minister.
14. He is considered to be right.
15. Smoking is not allowed here.
16. The minister was invited to the ceremony.
17. You were offered a job.
18. The price was set by the authorities.
19. The book must be read slowly.
20. Smoking is not permitted.
21. It was decided to set up a special committee.
22. People talk about him in town.
23. This street must be crossed carefully.
24. The committee was established last month.
25. His friends help him.
26. It is reported that the conclusions will be published next week.
27. The meeting was held yesterday in the ministry.
28. May I come in?
29. The public is invited to send comments.
30. Following the discussion the decision was conveyed to the press.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *הספר נכתב על ידי סופר מפורסם.* (ha-sefer nikhtav al yedei sofer mefursam) — Nif'al from כ-ת-ב.
2. *אומרים ברדיו שמחר ירד גשם.* (omrim ba-radio she-machar yered geshem) — 3rd person plural of omer.
3. *המסמך הזה צריך להישלח היום.* (ha-mismakh ha-ze tsarikh lehishalach ha-yom) — passive infinitive of Nif'al from ש-ל-ח.
4. *הידיעה פורסמה בעיתון אתמול.* (ha-yedi'a pursema ba-iton etmol) — Pu'al from פ-ר-ס, f. (yedi'a).
5. *גנבו לי את הארנק.* (ganvu li et ha-arnak) — 3rd person plural of ganav, a typical colloquial formula.
6. *מוכרים פה לחם טרי.* (mokhrim po lechem tari) — 3rd person plural of mokher.
7. *הבית נבנה בשנה שעברה.* (ha-bayit nivna ba-shana she-avra) — Nif'al from ב-נ-ה.
8. *כותבים בעיתון שהמחירים עולים.* (kotvim ba-iton she-ha-mechirim olim) — 3rd person plural of kotev.
9. *ההחלטה צריכה להתקבל עד סוף השבוע.* (ha-hachlata tsrikha lehitkabel ad sof ha-shavua) — passive infinitive of Hitpa'el with a passive nuance.
10. *התקשרו אליי מהבנק.* (hitkashru elai me-ha-bank) — 3rd person plural of hitkasher.
11. *התוכנית אושרה על ידי המנהל.* (ha-tochnit ushra al yedei ha-menahel) — Pu'al from אישר, f. (tochnit).
12. *ברחוב אלנבי פתחו מסעדה חדשה.* (ba-rechov Alenbi patchu mis'ada chadasha) — 3rd person plural of patach.
13. *המכתב נשלח לשר.* (ha-michtav nishlach la-sar) — Nif'al from ש-ל-ח.
14. *חושבים שהוא צודק.* (choshvim she-hu tsodek) — 3rd person plural of choshev.
15. *אסור לעשן כאן.* (asur le'ashen kan) — impersonal modal.
16. *השר הוזמן לטקס.* (ha-sar huzman la-tekes) — Huf'al from ז-מ-נ.
17. *הציעו לך עבודה.* (hitsi'u lekha avoda) — 3rd person plural of hitsia.
18. *המחיר נקבע על ידי השלטונות.* (ha-mechir nikba al yedei ha-shiltonot) — Nif'al from ק-ב-ע.
19. *הספר צריך להיקרא לאט.* (ha-sefer tsarikh lehikare le'at) — passive infinitive of Nif'al from ק-ר-א.
20. *לא מרשים לעשן.* (lo marshim le'ashen) — 3rd person plural of marshe.
21. *הוחלט להקים ועדה מיוחדת.* (huchlat lehakim va'ada meyuchedet) — Huf'al from ח-ל-ט, the standard newspaper formula.
22. *מדברים עליו בעיר.* (medavrim alav ba-ir) — 3rd person plural (workaround: dibber requires the preposition al, no direct passive).
23. *צריך לחצות את הרחוב הזה בזהירות.* (tsarikh lachatsot et ha-rechov ha-ze bi-zhirut) OR *הרחוב הזה צריך להיחצות בזהירות* (ha-rechov ha-ze tsarikh lehichatsot bi-zhirut).
24. *הוועדה הוקמה בחודש שעבר.* (ha-va'ada hukma ba-chodesh she-avar) — Huf'al from ק-ו-ם, f. (va'ada).
25. *עוזרים לו חברים.* (ozrim lo chaverim) — 3rd person plural (la'azor takes le-, no direct passive).
26. *נמסר כי המסקנות יפורסמו בשבוע הבא.* (nimsar ki ha-maskanot yefursemu ba-shavua ha-ba) — impersonal Nif'al + Pu'al future plural.
27. *הישיבה נערכה אתמול במשרד.* (ha-yeshiva ne'erkha etmol ba-misrad) — Nif'al from ע-ר-כ.
28. *אפשר להיכנס?* (efshar lehikanes?) — passive infinitive of Nif'al from כ-נ-ס.
29. *הציבור מוזמן לשלוח הערות.* (ha-tsibur muzman lishloach he'arot) — Huf'al from ז-מ-נ.
30. *בעקבות הדיון, ההחלטה נמסרה לעיתונות.* (be-ikvot ha-diyun, ha-hachlata nimsera la-itonut) — Nif'al from מ-ס-ר, f. (hachlata).
