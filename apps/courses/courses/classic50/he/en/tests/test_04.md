# Test for Lesson 4: Nouns — gender, number, endings. Family, professions.

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson. Translate with nikkud. Remember: every noun is either m. or f., and the form of the adjective/number depends on gender.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *אַבָּא וְאִמָּא* (aba ve-ima)
2. *בֵּן וּבַת* (ben u-vat)
3. *אָח וְאָחוֹת* (ach ve-achot)
4. *סָבָא וְסָבְתָא* (saba ve-savta)
5. *דּוֹד וְדוֹדָה* (dod ve-doda)
6. *בַּעַל וְאִשָּׁה* (ba'al ve-isha)
7. *אִישׁ וְאִשָּׁה* (ish ve-isha)
8. *יֶלֶד וְיַלְדָּה* (yeled ve-yalda)
9. *בָּחוּר וּבַחוּרָה* (bachur u-vachura)
10. *חָבֵר וַחֲבֵרָה* (chaver va-chavera)
11. *מוֹרֶה וּמוֹרָה* (more u-mora)
12. *תַּלְמִיד וְתַלְמִידָה* (talmid ve-talmida)
13. *רוֹפֵא וְרוֹפְאָה* (rofe ve-rof'a)
14. *סְטוּדֶנְט וּסְטוּדֶנְטִית* (student u-studentit) — f. on **-it**!
15. *מְהַנְדֵּס וּמְהַנְדֶּסֶת* (mehandes u-mehandeset) — f. on **-et**!
16. *שׁוֹפֵט וְשׁוֹפֶטֶת* (shofet ve-shofetet) — f. on **-et**.
17. *מֶלֶךְ וּמַלְכָּה* (melech u-malka)
18. *מִשְׁפָּחָה* (mishpacha) — f.
19. *מִשְׁפָּחוֹת* (mishpachot) — f. pl. on **-ot**.
20. *יְלָדִים* (yeladim) — m. pl. on **-im**.
21. *יְלָדוֹת* (yeladot) — f. pl. on **-ot**.
22. *אַחִים* (achim)
23. *אֲחָיוֹת* (achayot) — don't confuse with achot (sister, sg.)!
24. *מוֹרִים* (morim)
25. *מוֹרוֹת* (morot)
26. *אָח אֶחָד וְאָחוֹת אַחַת* (ach echad ve-achot achat) — the number agrees in gender.
27. *שְׁלוֹשָׁה תַּלְמִידִים וְחָמֵשׁ תַּלְמִידוֹת* (shlosha talmidim ve-chamesh talmidot) — long shlosha for m., short chamesh for f.
28. *יֵשׁ לִי מִשְׁפָּחָה גְּדוֹלָה.* (yesh li mishpacha gdola) — gdola (f.), because mishpacha is f.
29. *אִמָּא שֶׁלִּי רוֹפְאָה וְאַבָּא שֶׁלִּי מוֹרֶה.* (ima sheli rof'a ve-aba sheli more) — the profession agrees in gender with its bearer.
30. *בַּמִּשְׁפָּחָה שֶׁלִּי אַרְבַּע אֲחָיוֹת וּשְׁנֵי אַחִים.* (ba-mishpacha sheli arba achayot u-shnei achim) — arba (f. short) for achayot, shnei (m. construct) for achim.
