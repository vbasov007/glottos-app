# Test for Lesson 9: The article ha-, adjective agreement, preposition-prefixes ב/ל/מ

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew with vowel points. Watch: the article ה- is glued, it flows onto the adjective; the prefixes ב-, ל-, מ- are written as one word; *be- + ha- = ba-*, *le- + ha- = la-*, *mi- + ha- = me-ha-*.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *סֵפֶר גָּדוֹל* — **sefer gadol** (both without ha-)
2. *הַסֵּפֶר הַגָּדוֹל* — **ha-sefer ha-gadol** (article on both)
3. *יַלְדָּה קְטַנָּה* — **yalda ktana** (f.s. + f.s.)
4. *הַיַּלְדָּה הַקְּטַנָּה* — **ha-yalda ha-ktana**
5. *בַּיִת חָדָשׁ* — **bayit chadash** (m.s. + m.s.)
6. *הַבַּיִת הֶחָדָשׁ* — **ha-bayit he-chadash** (*he-* before ח — a typical variant)
7. *יְלָדִים טוֹבִים* — **yeladim tovim** (m.pl. + m.pl.)
8. *הַיְלָדִים הַטּוֹבִים* — **ha-yeladim ha-tovim**
9. *נָשִׁים חֲכָמוֹת* — **nashim chakhamot** (f.pl. + f.pl.)
10. *הַנָּשִׁים הַחֲכָמוֹת* — **ha-nashim ha-chakhamot**
11. *מְכוֹנִית אֲדֻמָּה* — **mekhonit aduma** (f.s. + f.s.; *mekhonit* — f.)
12. *הַמְּכוֹנִית הָאֲדֻמָּה* — **ha-mekhonit ha-aduma**
13. *דֶּלֶת לְבָנָה* — **delet levana** (f.s. + f.s.; *delet* — f.)
14. *הַדֶּלֶת הַלְּבָנָה* — **ha-delet ha-levana**
15. *אִישׁ גָּבוֹהַּ* — **ish gavoha** (m.s. + m.s.)
16. *הָאִישׁ הַגָּבוֹהַּ* — **ha-ish ha-gavoha** (before א the article lengthens to *ha-* with kamatz)
17. *הַבַּיִת גָּדוֹל* — **ha-bayit gadol** (article only on noun → sentence "the house is big")
18. *הַמּוֹרָה טוֹבָה* — **ha-mora tova** ("the teacher is good")
19. *הַסֵּפֶר חָדָשׁ* — **ha-sefer chadash** ("the book is new")
20. *הַיְלָדִים שְׂמֵחִים* — **ha-yeladim smechim** ("the children are happy")
21. *בְּבַיִת* — **be-vayit** (ב- + bayit, no article)
22. *בַּבַּיִת* — **ba-bayit** (ב- + ha-bayit; fusion *be- + ha- = ba-*)
23. *לְמוֹרֶה* — **le-more**
24. *לַמּוֹרֶה* — **la-more** (fusion *le- + ha- = la-*)
25. *מֵעִיר* — **me-ir** (מ- before guttural ע = *me-*)
26. *מֵהָעִיר* — **me-ha-ir** (מ- does NOT fuse with the article; the ה- stays; before ע — *ha-* with kamatz)
27. *לְבֵית הַסֵּפֶר* — **le-vet ha-sefer** (literally "to the house-of-book"; "school" — *bet-sefer*)
28. *מֵהַיְלָדִים* — **me-ha-yeladim** (from the children — definite)
29. *יַלְדָּה קְטַנָּה בַּבַּיִת הַגָּדוֹל* — **Yalda ktana ba-bayit ha-gadol.** (girl not specified → without ha-; house specified → *ba-* + ha-gadol)
30. *הָאֲנָשִׁים הָעֲיֵפִים הָאֵלֶּה מֵהָעִיר* — **Ha-anashim ha-ayefim ha-ele me-ha-ir.** (article on noun + two adjectives; *me-ha-* — from the definite city)
