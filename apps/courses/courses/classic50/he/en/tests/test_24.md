# Test for Lesson 24: Pu'al and Huf'al — Internal Passives

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. In parentheses the desired register is given: **B** — bookish (Pu'al/Huf'al), **C** — conversational (3rd-pers. pl. of the active verb). If no register is given — both versions are acceptable. Hebrew without vowels.

---

1. The letter was received yesterday. (B)
2. They received the letter yesterday. (C)
3. The room is in order.
4. The meal is ready.
5. That's not customary with us.
6. The children are ready to go.
7. All the papers are in order.
8. The story was told in the newspaper. (B)
9. The war is talked about in the news. (C)
10. The law was explained by the teacher. (B)
11. The teacher explained the law. (C)
12. The guests were invited for seven o'clock. (B)
13. They invited the guests for seven o'clock. (C)
14. The books were brought from Jerusalem. (B)
15. They brought the books from Jerusalem. (C)
16. The matter was put in order. (B)
17. It is customary to say "thank you."
18. It is brought to the public's attention that the store is closed. (B)
19. It is about an important decision. (B)
20. It was decided to open a new school. (B)
21. They decided to open a new school. (C)
22. War was declared. (B)
23. They announced the war on the radio. (C)
24. A new ministry was founded last year. (B)
25. They founded a new ministry last year. (C)
26. The table is ready, and the chairs are ready too.
27. I am invited to the wedding. (B: "invited" as a participle)
28. They invited me to the wedding. (C)
29. There's a lot of talk about him in the papers. (B)
30. They talk a lot about him in the papers. (C)

<summary>Keys</summary>

1. *המכתב קובל אתמול.* (Ha-mikhtav kubal etmol.) — Pu'al, past.
2. *קיבלו את המכתב אתמול.* (Kiblu et ha-mikhtav etmol.) — Pi'el, 3rd-pers. pl.
3. *החדר מסודר.* (Ha-cheder mesudar.) — Pu'al participle as adjective.
4. *הארוחה מוכנה.* (Ha-arucha mukhana.) — Huf'al participle as adjective.
5. *זה לא מקובל אצלנו.* (Ze lo mekubal etslenu.) — Pu'al participle as adjective.
6. *הילדים מוכנים ללכת.* (Ha-yeladim mukhanim lalekhet.) — Huf'al participle, m.pl.
7. *כל הניירות מסודרים.* (Kol ha-niyarot mesudarim.) — Pu'al participle, m.pl.
8. *הסיפור סופר בעיתון.* (Ha-sipur supar ba-iton.) — Pu'al, past.
9. *מספרים בחדשות על המלחמה.* (Mesaprim ba-chadashot al ha-milchama.) — Pi'el, 3rd-pers. pl.
10. *החוק הוסבר על-ידי המורה.* (Ha-chok husbar al-yedei ha-more.) — Huf'al, past.
11. *המורה הסביר את החוק.* (Ha-more hisbir et ha-chok.) — Hif'il, active (in fact active is better than 3rd-pers. pl.).
12. *האורחים הוזמנו לשבע.* (Ha-orchim huzmenu le-sheva.) — Huf'al, past.
13. *הזמינו את האורחים לשבע.* (Hizminu et ha-orchim le-sheva.) — Hif'il, 3rd-pers. pl.
14. *הספרים הובאו מירושלים.* (Ha-sfarim huv'u mi-Yerushalayim.) — Huf'al, past.
15. *הביאו את הספרים מירושלים.* (Hevi'u et ha-sfarim mi-Yerushalayim.) — Hif'il, 3rd-pers. pl.
16. *התיק סודר.* (Ha-tik sudar.) — Pu'al, past.
17. *מקובל להגיד «תודה».* (Mekubal lehagid "toda".) — Pu'al participle, impersonal.
18. *מובא לידיעת הציבור שהחנות סגורה.* (Muva li-yedi'at ha-tsibur she-ha-chanut sgura.) — Huf'al participle, formula.
19. *מדובר בהחלטה חשובה.* (Meduber be-hachlata chashuva.) — Pu'al participle, formula.
20. *הוחלט לפתוח בית ספר חדש.* (Huchlat liftoach beit sefer chadash.) — Huf'al, past.
21. *החליטו לפתוח בית ספר חדש.* (Hechlitu liftoach beit sefer chadash.) — Hif'il, 3rd-pers. pl.
22. *הוכרז על המלחמה.* (Hukhraz al ha-milchama.) — Huf'al, past.
23. *הכריזו על המלחמה ברדיו.* (Hikhrizu al ha-milchama ba-radio.) — Hif'il, 3rd-pers. pl.
24. *המשרד החדש הוקם בשנה שעברה.* (Ha-misrad ha-chadash hukam ba-shana she-avra.) — Huf'al, past, root k-w-m.
25. *הקימו משרד חדש בשנה שעברה.* (Hekimu misrad chadash ba-shana she-avra.) — Hif'il, 3rd-pers. pl.
26. *השולחן מוכן וגם הכיסאות מוכנים.* (Ha-shulchan mukhan ve-gam ha-kis'ot mukhanim.) — Huf'al participle.
27. *אני מוזמן לחתונה.* (Ani muzman la-chatuna.) — Huf'al participle.
28. *הזמינו אותי לחתונה.* (Hizminu oti la-chatuna.) — Hif'il, 3rd-pers. pl.
29. *מדובר עליו הרבה בעיתונים.* (Meduber alav harbe ba-itonim.) — Pu'al participle, formula.
30. *מדברים עליו הרבה בעיתונים.* (Medabrim alav harbe ba-itonim.) — Pi'el, 3rd-pers. pl.
