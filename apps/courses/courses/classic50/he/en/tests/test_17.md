# Test for Lesson 17: Binyan Hitpa'el — reflexive, reciprocity, metathesis with sibilants

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson.

---

1. I (m.) get dressed quickly.
2. She gets dressed slowly.
3. The children (m.) get dressed by themselves.
4. Yesterday I (f.) dressed warmly.
5. He got dressed and went out.
6. I (m.) am nervous before the exam. ("before" = *lifney*)
7. Mom worries about the children. ("about" = *mi-*)
8. We were very excited yesterday.
9. Don't worry (to f.)! ("don't" in commands = *al*)
10. The students (m.) are excited.
11. I (m.) correspond with a friend from Haifa.
12. They (m.) correspond by email. ("by email" = *be-do'ar elektroni*)
13. We corresponded all summer.
14. She corresponds with her grandmother.
15. I (m.) wake up early every day.
16. The children wake up at seven o'clock.
17. Yesterday I (f.) woke up late.
18. He woke up from the noise. ("noise" = *ra'ash*, "from" = *mi-*)
19. We woke up and went to work.
20. I (m.) am getting used to the new apartment.
21. Have you (f.) gotten used to the climate? ("climate" = *aklim*)
22. We got used to the noise of the city.
23. I (m.) use a dictionary. ("dictionary" = *milon*)
24. She uses a new phone.
25. What do you (m.) use?
26. We use the bus every day. ("bus" = *otobus*)
27. Life changes quickly.
28. The weather has changed. ("weather" = *mezeg ha-avir*)
29. I (f.) have changed this year.
30. Everything changes, nothing stays. ("to stay" = *nish'ar*, Nif'al)

<summary>Keys</summary>

1. *אֲנִי מִתְלַבֵּשׁ מַהֵר.* (Ani mitlabesh maher.)
2. *הִיא מִתְלַבֶּשֶׁת לְאַט.* (Hi mitlabeshet le'at.)
3. *הַיְלָדִים מִתְלַבְּשִׁים לְבַד.* (Ha-yeladim mitlabshim levad.)
4. *אֶתְמוֹל הִתְלַבַּשְׁתִּי בְּחֹם.* (Etmol hitlabashti be-chom.)
5. *הוּא הִתְלַבֵּשׁ וְיָצָא.* (Hu hitlabesh ve-yatsa.)
6. *אֲנִי מִתְרַגֵּשׁ לִפְנֵי הַמִּבְחָן.* (Ani mitragesh lifney ha-mivchan.)
7. *אִמָּא מִתְרַגֶּשֶׁת מֵהַיְלָדִים.* (Ima mitrageshet me-ha-yeladim.)
8. *הִתְרַגַּשְׁנוּ מְאוֹד אֶתְמוֹל.* (Hitragashnu me'od etmol.)
9. *אַל תִּתְרַגְּשִׁי!* (Al titragshi!)
10. *הַסְּטוּדֶנְטִים מִתְרַגְּשִׁים.* (Ha-studentim mitragshim.)
11. *אֲנִי מִתְכַּתֵּב עִם חָבֵר מֵחֵיפָה.* (Ani mitkatev im chaver me-Cheyfa.)
12. *הֵם מִתְכַּתְּבִים בְּדֹאַר אֶלֶקְטְרוֹנִי.* (Hem mitkatvim be-do'ar elektroni.)
13. *הִתְכַּתַּבְנוּ כָּל הַקַּיִץ.* (Hitkatavnu kol ha-kayits.)
14. *הִיא מִתְכַּתֶּבֶת עִם הַסַּבְתָּא.* (Hi mitkatevet im ha-savta.)
15. *אֲנִי מִתְעוֹרֵר מֻקְדָּם כָּל יוֹם.* (Ani mit'orer mukdam kol yom.)
16. *הַיְלָדִים מִתְעוֹרְרִים בְּשֶׁבַע.* (Ha-yeladim mit'orerim be-sheva.)
17. *אֶתְמוֹל הִתְעוֹרַרְתִּי מְאֻחָר.* (Etmol hit'orarti me'uchar.)
18. *הוּא הִתְעוֹרֵר מֵהָרַעַשׁ.* (Hu hit'orer me-ha-ra'ash.)
19. *הִתְעוֹרַרְנוּ וְהָלַכְנוּ לָעֲבוֹדָה.* (Hit'orarnu ve-halakhnu la-avoda.)
20. *אֲנִי מִתְרַגֵּל לַדִּירָה הַחֲדָשָׁה.* (Ani mitragel la-dira ha-chadasha.)
21. *הִתְרַגַּלְתְּ לָאַקְלִים?* (Hitragalt la-aklim?)
22. *הִתְרַגַּלְנוּ לָרַעַשׁ שֶׁל הָעִיר.* (Hitragalnu la-ra'ash shel ha-ir.)
23. *אֲנִי מִשְׁתַּמֵּשׁ בַּמִּלּוֹן.* (Ani mishtamesh ba-milon.) — **metathesis ש!**
24. *הִיא מִשְׁתַּמֶּשֶׁת בַּטֵּלֵפוֹן הֶחָדָשׁ.* (Hi mishtameshet ba-telefon he-chadash.)
25. *בַּמָּה אַתָּה מִשְׁתַּמֵּשׁ?* (Ba-ma ata mishtamesh?)
26. *אֲנַחְנוּ מִשְׁתַּמְּשִׁים בָּאוֹטוֹבּוּס כָּל יוֹם.* (Anachnu mishtamshim ba-otobus kol yom.)
27. *הַחַיִּים מִשְׁתַּנִּים מַהֵר.* (Ha-chayim mishtanim maher.) — **metathesis ש!**
28. *מֶזֶג הָאֲוִיר הִשְׁתַּנָּה.* (Mezeg ha-avir hishtana.)
29. *הִשְׁתַּנֵּיתִי בַּשָּׁנָה הַזֹּאת.* (Hishtaneti ba-shana ha-zot.)
30. *הַכֹּל מִשְׁתַּנֶּה, כְּלוּם לֹא נִשְׁאָר.* (Ha-kol mishtaneh, klum lo nish'ar.)
