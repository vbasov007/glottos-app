# Test for Lesson 37: Advanced smikhut — chains and smikhut with an adjective

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson.
Translate without vowel points. Use smikhut chains of 3-4 elements and adjective agreement. Remember: one article — on the very last noun of the chain; the adjective comes **after** the chain and agrees in gender/number with the word it describes.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *משרד הבריאות* (misrad ha-bri'ut) — article on the second (last) noun.
2. *משרד החינוך* (misrad ha-chinukh)
3. *משרד הביטחון* (misrad ha-bitachon)
4. *משרד החוץ* (misrad ha-chuts)
5. *משרד הפנים* (misrad ha-pnim)
6. *משרד האוצר* (misrad ha-otsar)
7. *ראש הממשלה* (rosh ha-memshala)
8. *שר הביטחון* (sar ha-bitachon) — *sar* (person-minister) ≠ *misrad* (institution).
9. *שר החוץ* (sar ha-chuts)
10. *ראש העיר* (rosh ha-ir) — article ha- + ʻayin → ha-ir.
11. *תעודת הזהות* (te'udat ha-zehut) — *te'uda → te'udat* in the construct.
12. *כרטיס האשראי* (kartis ha-ashrai)
13. *חשבון הבנק* (cheshbon ha-bank)
14. *שירות הביטוח הלאומי* (sheirut ha-bituach ha-le'umi) — 3-element chain + adjective *ha-le'umi* (m., agreed with *bituach*). Article on *bituach* (the last noun of the chain), the adjective repeats the article.
15. *בית הספר היסודי* (bet ha-sefer ha-yesodi) — smikhut *bet sefer* + adj. *ha-yesodi* (m., like *sefer*).
16. *בית הספר התיכון* (bet ha-sefer ha-tichon)
17. *תוכנית הלימודים החדשה* (tochnit ha-limudim ha-chadasha) — attention! The adj. describes *tochnit* (f., "curriculum"), not *limudim* (m. pl.). So *ha-chadasha* — f., agreed with the **first** element of the smikhut.
18. *מנהל בית הספר* (menahel bet ha-sefer) — 3-element chain: menahel + bet + ha-sefer. Article only on ha-sefer.
19. *מנהל מחלקת המכירות* (menahel machleket ha-mechirot) — 3-element chain (machlaka → machleket in the construct).
20. *בית המשפט העליון* (bet ha-mishpat ha-elyon) — smikhut + adj. *ha-elyon* (m., like *mishpat*).
21. *משרד הבריאות הישראלי* (misrad ha-bri'ut ha-yisre'eli) — adj. *ha-yisre'eli* (m., like *misrad*, not like *bri'ut*) — refers to the ministry as a whole.
22. *דמי אבטלה* (dmei avtala) — *damim → dmei* in the construct; "money-unemployment" = benefit.
23. *תעודת הזהות של החייל* (te'udat ha-zehut shel ha-chayal) — hybrid: smikhut + shel + a definite common noun.
24. *בית הספר של הבת שלי* (bet ha-sefer shel ha-bat sheli) — hybrid: smikhut "school" + shel + ha-bat sheli (my daughter).
25. *משרד ראש הממשלה* (misrad rosh ha-memshala) — pure 3-element chain: misrad + rosh + ha-memshala. Article only on ha-memshala.
26. *משרד הבריאות פרסם נתונים חדשים.* (misrad ha-bri'ut pirsem netunim chadashim) — *netunim chadashim* without article = "some new data".
27. *הבת של ראש הממשלה לומדת בבית הספר התיכון.* (Ha-bat shel rosh ha-memshala lomedet be-vet ha-sefer ha-tichon.) — three smikhut blocks joined via shel and the preposition *be-*.
28. *תוכנית הלימודים של מערכת החינוך הישראלית משתנה.* (Tochnit ha-limudim shel ma'arekhet ha-chinukh ha-yisre'elit mishtana.) — two smikhut blocks (*tochnit ha-limudim* + *ma'arekhet ha-chinukh*) + adj. *ha-yisre'elit* (f., agreed with *ma'arekhet*, f.) + shel.
29. *איבדתי את חשבון הבנק שלי.* (Ibadeti et cheshbon ha-bank sheli.) — hybrid *cheshbon ha-bank* (smikhut, bank account) + *sheli* (possession).
30. *מורת בית הספר החדשה עובדת במשרד המנהל.* (Morat bet ha-sefer ha-chadasha ovedet be-misrad ha-menahel.) — *ha-chadasha* f. → agreed with *morat* (teacher). If it were *ha-chadash* (m.), it would refer to *sefer* and mean "the teacher of the new school".

> ❌ Categorically not allowed: *ha-misrad ha-bri'ut*, *ha-bet ha-sefer ha-yesodi*, *ha-tochnit ha-limudim*. Article on the first element of the smikhut is a gross mistake. The article goes **only** on the last noun of the chain, and the adjective at the end repeats it.

> **Notice №14, 15, 16, 20:** four chains of the same model "smikhut (2 nouns) + defining adjective". All four are institutional terms. The adjective is agreed with the **second** noun of the smikhut and repeats its article.

> **Notice №17, 21:** the adjective may agree with the **first** element of the smikhut if it refers to it by meaning. The gender of the adjective is an arrow: *ha-chadasha* (f.) points to *tochnit*; *ha-yisre'eli* (m.) — to *misrad*.
