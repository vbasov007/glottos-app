# Test for Lesson 12: Past tense in Pa'al — suffixal conjugation

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson.

---

1. Yesterday I wrote a letter. *(1 sg., m.)*
2. Yesterday I wrote a letter. *(1 sg., f. — same form!)*
3. You (m.) studied Hebrew last week.
4. You (f.) studied at university.
5. He worked in Tel Aviv a year ago.
6. She worked here two days ago.
7. We closed the shop yesterday.
8. You (m.) wrote the article a week ago.
9. You (f.) studied together.
10. They (m.) have already closed the door.
11. They (f.) wrote a book last year.
12. I went to school in Moscow. *(1 sg.)*
13. Anna wrote a letter to Mom.
14. Dani worked in the garden.
15. We learned all the words the day before yesterday.
16. You (m.) haven't closed the windows yet.
17. The pupils learned the lesson.
18. The teacher (f.) wrote an answer.
19. An hour ago I closed the door.
20. A month ago we moved to Jerusalem. *(avarnu)*
21. She hasn't written the article yet.
22. They closed all the windows yesterday evening.
23. Have you (m.) already written an answer?
24. I never worked there. *(af pa'am lo)*
25. We once lived in Haifa. *(garnu — from lagur)*
26. Have you (f.) already learned the letters?
27. Last week he closed the shop.
28. Last year I finished university. *(gamarti)*
29. They went to school together.
30. Did you (m.) work at the office yesterday?

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *אֶתְמוֹל כָּתַבְתִּי מִכְתָּב.* (Etmol katavti michtav.) — 1 sg., katavti.
2. *אֶתְמוֹל כָּתַבְתִּי מִכְתָּב.* (Etmol katavti michtav.) — same form, katavti (one form for m. and f.!).
3. *לָמַדְתָּ עִבְרִית הַשָּׁבוּעַ שֶׁעָבַר.* (Lamadta ivrit ha-shavua sheavar.) — 2 sg. m., lamadta.
4. *לָמַדְתְּ בָּאוּנִיבֶרְסִיטָה.* (Lamadt ba-universita.) — 2 sg. f., lamadt.
5. *הוּא עָבַד בְּתֵל אָבִיב לִפְנֵי שָׁנָה.* (Hu avad be-Tel Aviv lifnei shana.) — 3 sg. m., "naked" form avad.
6. *הִיא עָבְדָה פֹּה לִפְנֵי יוֹמַיִם.* (Hi avda po lifnei yomayim.) — 3 sg. f., avda (with vowel drop).
7. *אֲנַחְנוּ סָגַרְנוּ אֶת הַחֲנוּת אֶתְמוֹל.* (Anachnu sagarnu et ha-chanut etmol.) — 1 pl., sagarnu.
8. *אַתֶּם כְּתַבְתֶּם אֶת הַמַּאֲמָר לִפְנֵי שָׁבוּעַ.* (Atem ktavtem et ha-ma'amar lifnei shavua.) — 2 pl. m., ktavtem (stress shift).
9. *אַתֶּן לְמַדְתֶּן בְּיַחַד.* (Aten lmadten be-yachad.) — 2 pl. f., lmadten (stress shift).
10. *הֵם כְּבָר סָגְרוּ אֶת הַדֶּלֶת.* (Hem kvar sagru et ha-delet.) — 3 pl., sagru (with vowel drop).
11. *הֵן כָּתְבוּ סֵפֶר בַּשָּׁנָה שֶׁעָבְרָה.* (Hen katvu sefer ba-shana sheavra.) — 3 pl., katvu (one form for hem/hen).
12. *לָמַדְתִּי בְּבֵית סֵפֶר בְּמוֹסְקְבָה.* (Lamadti be-veit sefer be-Moskva.) — 1 sg., lamadti.
13. *אַנָּה כָּתְבָה מִכְתָּב לְאִמָּא.* (Anna katva michtav le-ima.) — 3 sg. f., katva.
14. *דָּנִי עָבַד בַּגַּן.* (Dani avad ba-gan.) — 3 sg. m., avad.
15. *לָמַדְנוּ אֶת כָּל הַמִּלִּים שִׁלְשׁוֹם.* (Lamadnu et kol ha-milim shilshom.) — 1 pl., lamadnu.
16. *עֲדַיִן לֹא סְגַרְתֶּם אֶת הַחַלּוֹנוֹת.* (Adayin lo sgartem et ha-chalonot.) — 2 pl. m., sgartem.
17. *הַתַּלְמִידִים לָמְדוּ אֶת הַשִּׁעוּר.* (Ha-talmidim lamdu et ha-shi'ur.) — 3 pl., lamdu.
18. *הַמּוֹרָה כָּתְבָה תְּשׁוּבָה.* (Ha-mora katva teshuva.) — 3 sg. f., katva.
19. *לִפְנֵי שָׁעָה סָגַרְתִּי אֶת הַדֶּלֶת.* (Lifnei sha'a sagarti et ha-delet.) — 1 sg., sagarti.
20. *לִפְנֵי חֹדֶשׁ עָבַרְנוּ לִירוּשָׁלַיִם.* (Lifnei chodesh avarnu li-Yerushalayim.) — 1 pl., avarnu (root ע-ב-ר).
21. *הִיא עֲדַיִן לֹא כָּתְבָה אֶת הַמַּאֲמָר.* (Hi adayin lo katva et ha-ma'amar.) — 3 sg. f., katva.
22. *הֵם סָגְרוּ אֶת כָּל הַחַלּוֹנוֹת אֶתְמוֹל בָּעֶרֶב.* (Hem sagru et kol ha-chalonot etmol ba-erev.) — 3 pl., sagru.
23. *כְּבָר כָּתַבְתָּ תְּשׁוּבָה?* (Kvar katavta teshuva?) — 2 sg. m., katavta.
24. *אַף פַּעַם לֹא עָבַדְתִּי שָׁם.* (Af pa'am lo avadti sham.) — 1 sg., avadti.
25. *פַּעַם גַּרְנוּ בְּחֵיפָה.* (Pa'am garnu be-Cheifa.) — 1 pl., garnu (weak root, see L26; form by analogy).
26. *כְּבָר לְמַדְתֶּן אֶת הָאוֹתִיּוֹת?* (Kvar lmadten et ha-otiyot?) — 2 pl. f., lmadten.
27. *הַשָּׁבוּעַ שֶׁעָבַר הוּא סָגַר אֶת הַחֲנוּת.* (Ha-shavua sheavar hu sagar et ha-chanut.) — 3 sg. m., sagar.
28. *בַּשָּׁנָה שֶׁעָבְרָה גָּמַרְתִּי אוּנִיבֶרְסִיטָה.* (Ba-shana sheavra gamarti universita.) — 1 sg., gamarti.
29. *הֵם לָמְדוּ בְּבֵית סֵפֶר בְּיַחַד.* (Hem lamdu be-veit sefer be-yachad.) — 3 pl., lamdu.
30. *אֶתְמוֹל עֲבַדְתֶּם בַּמִּשְׂרָד?* (Etmol avadtem ba-misrad?) — 2 pl. m., avadtem.
