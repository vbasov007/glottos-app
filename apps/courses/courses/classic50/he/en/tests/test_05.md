# Test for Lesson 5: Personal pronouns. Verbless present. The pronoun-copula

**How do you say it in Hebrew?**
Translate the phrases into Hebrew. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson. Translate with nikkud. Remember: 10 pronouns (gender!), no verb "to be" in the present, the copula `hu / hi / hem / hen` is placed with a 3rd-person subject or with two definite elements.

---

1. I am an engineer. *(a man speaking)*
2. I am an engineer. *(a woman speaking)*
3. You are a teacher. *(to a man)*
4. You are a teacher. *(to a woman)*
5. He is a student.
6. She is a student.
7. We are pupils. *(mixed group)*
8. We are pupils. *(only women)*
9. You are engineers. *(to men)*
10. You are teachers. *(to women)*
11. They are doctors. *(men)*
12. They are doctors. *(women)*
13. I am from Russia. *(a man)*
14. Where are you from? *(to a woman)*
15. I am an Israeli.
16. She is Russian.
17. We are Americans.
18. They (m.) are French.
19. David is an engineer. *(with copula)*
20. Sara is a doctor. *(with copula)*
21. The brothers are students. *(with copula)*
22. The sisters are teachers. *(with copula)*
23. David is (that very) teacher. *(both definite — copula required)*
24. I live in Tel Aviv. *(a man)*
25. She lives in Haifa.
26. I speak Hebrew. *(a woman)*
27. We speak Russian.
28. My name is Dana. I am a student.
29. Where are you from? — I am from Ukraine. *(to a man)*
30. He is Israeli, and she is Russian.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *אֲנִי מְהַנְדֵּס.* (ani mehandes)
2. *אֲנִי מְהַנְדֶּסֶת.* (ani mehandeset)
3. *אַתָּה מוֹרֶה.* (ata more)
4. *אַתְּ מוֹרָה.* (at mora)
5. *הוּא סְטוּדֶנְט.* (hu student)
6. *הִיא סְטוּדֶנְטִית.* (hi studentit)
7. *אֲנַחְנוּ תַּלְמִידִים.* (anachnu talmidim) — mixed = m. pl.
8. *אֲנַחְנוּ תַּלְמִידוֹת.* (anachnu talmidot)
9. *אַתֶּם מְהַנְדְּסִים.* (atem mehandesim)
10. *אַתֵּן מוֹרוֹת.* (aten morot)
11. *הֵם רוֹפְאִים.* (hem rof'im)
12. *הֵן רוֹפְאוֹת.* (hen rof'ot)
13. *אֲנִי מֵרוּסְיָה.* (ani me-Rusiya)
14. *מֵאַיִן אַתְּ?* (me-ayin at?)
15. *אֲנִי יִשְׂרְאֵלִי.* (ani yisre'eli) (f.: *יִשְׂרְאֵלִית* yisre'elit)
16. *הִיא רוּסִיָּה.* (hi rusiya)
17. *אֲנַחְנוּ אָמֵרִיקָאִים.* (anachnu amerika'im)
18. *הֵם צָרְפָתִים.* (hem tsarfatim)
19. *דָּוִד הוּא מְהַנְדֵּס.* (David hu mehandes)
20. *שָׂרָה הִיא רוֹפְאָה.* (Sara hi rof'a)
21. *הָאַחִים הֵם סְטוּדֶנְטִים.* (Ha-achim hem studentim)
22. *הָאֲחָיוֹת הֵן מוֹרוֹת.* (Ha-achayot hen morot)
23. *דָּוִד הוּא הַמּוֹרֶה.* (David hu ha-more) — both definite, `hu` required
24. *אֲנִי גָּר בְּתֵל אָבִיב.* (ani gar be-Tel Aviv)
25. *הִיא גָּרָה בְּחֵיפָה.* (hi gara be-Cheifa)
26. *אֲנִי מְדַבֶּרֶת עִבְרִית.* (ani medaberet ivrit)
27. *אֲנַחְנוּ מְדַבְּרִים רוּסִית.* (anachnu medabrim rusit)
28. *קוֹרְאִים לִי דָּנָה. אֲנִי סְטוּדֶנְטִית.* (Kor'im li Dana. Ani studentit.)
29. *מֵאַיִן אַתָּה? — אֲנִי מֵאוּקְרָאִינָה.* (Me-ayin ata? — Ani me-Ukra'ina.)
30. *הוּא יִשְׂרְאֵלִי, וְהִיא רוּסִיָּה.* (Hu yisre'eli, ve-hi rusiya.)
