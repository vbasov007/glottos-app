# Text 31a for Lesson 31: Reported speech — what was said and what was announced
**Theme: Verbs of speaking (amar, sipur, hisbir, hodia, sha'al) + subordinate clause with she-/ki**

> Text without nikkud. Thirty reporting sentences: "he said that…", "she explained that…", "the teacher announced that…", "mom asked where…". We drill the conversion from direct to reported speech and the non-shifting of tense backward.

---

> **Notice #7, 8, 26, 27:** in reported speech the pronouns shift ("I" → "he/she"), but **the tense does not shift back** — Hebrew, like Russian, not like English. "She said that **she will call**" = hi amra **she-titkasher** (future, because the call is after the moment of her words).

> **Notice #19–23:** reported questions — **no** she-. With a question word (eifo, mi, lama) — right after the verb of asking. A yes/no question ("whether") — through **im**.

> **Notice #13, 14, 17:** **ki** after verbs of speaking and explaining = "that". Bookish-neutral register.
