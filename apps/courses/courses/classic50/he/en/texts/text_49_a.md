# Text A for Lesson 49: Fast-speech samples — dialogues from life
**Theme: Natural reduction in action — café, street, phone**

> Each line appears in two columns after the dialogues: textbook form and reduced (fast) form. Read both aloud. Without nikkud.

---

## Dialogue 1: On the street — a meeting

1. שלום אחי, מה נשמע?
2. סבבה, מה קורה אצלך?
3. הכל בסדר, באמת.
4. איפה אתה עכשיו?
5. אני כבר בדרך הביתה.
6. אז יאללה, נדבר אחר כך.
7. בסדר, ביי.

## Dialogue 2: In a café

8. מה אתה רוצה לשתות?
9. תן לי קפה הפוך, בבקשה.
10. גם לי, אותו דבר.
11. ממש חם היום, לא?
12. כן, איזה כיף שיש מזגן.
13. סליחה, אפשר עוד מים?
14. בטח, רגע אחד.

## Dialogue 3: On the phone

15. הלו, מי זה?
16. זה אני, דני. מה אתה עושה?
17. כלום, סתם יושב בבית.
18. בא לך לצאת?
19. דווקא עכשיו לא, אני עייף.
20. אז מחר אולי?
21. יש מצב, נראה.

## Dialogue 4: Running late

22. סליחה שאיחרתי, היה פקק.
23. לא נורא, גם אני רק הגעתי.
24. באמת? איזה מזל.
25. נו, יאללה, נכנסים?
26. בוא, אני כבר רעב.

## Dialogue 5: Saying goodbye

27. טוב, אני חייב לזוז.
28. כבר? מה פתאום?
29. יש לי פגישה בעוד חצי שעה.
30. אוקיי, נדבר. כל הכבוד שבאת.

## Textbook form vs. fast form

| Textbook form | Fast (reduced) | English |
|---------------|---------------------------|---------|
| shalom achi, ma nishma? | shal'achi, mnishma? | hi bro, how's it going? |
| sababa, ma kore etzlekha? | sababa, m'kore etzl'cha? | okay, what's up with you? |
| hakol beseder, be'emet | akol b'seder, 'emet | all fine, really |
| eyfo ata achshav? | 'fo 'ta 'shav? | where are you now? |
| ani kvar baderech habayta | 'ni kvar b'derech abayta | I'm already on the way home |
| az yalla, nedaber achar kakh | az yalla, n'daber 'char'kakh | so c'mon, we'll talk later |
| ma ata rotseh lishtot? | mata rotse l'shtot? | what do you want to drink? |
| ten li kafe hafukh | tn'li kafe hafuch | give me a hafuch (kind of latte) |
| oto davar | oto'davar | same here |
| mamash cham hayom | mamash cham hayom | really hot today |
| eyze keif she-yesh mazgan | eyz'keif sh'yesh mazgan | what a treat that there's AC |
| slicha, efshar od mayim? | slich, 'fshar od mayim? | sorry, can I get more water? |
| betach, rega echad | betach, rega'echad | sure, one moment |
| halo, mi zeh? | halo, mi'ze? | hello, who is this? |
| ze ani, Dani | z'ani, Dani | it's me, Dani |
| ma ata oseh? | mata'ose? | what are you doing? |
| klum, stam yoshev babayit | klum, stam yoshev b'bait | nothing, just sitting at home |
| ba lekha latset? | balcha latset? | feel like going out? |
| davka achshav lo, ani ayef | davka 'shav lo, 'ni ayef | not just now, I'm tired |
| az machar ulai? | az machar'ulai? | so tomorrow maybe? |
| yesh matzav, nir'eh | yesh matsav, nir'e | maybe, we'll see |
| slicha she-icharti, haya pkak | slich sh'icharti, haya pkak | sorry I'm late, there was traffic |
| lo nora, gam ani rak higati | lonora, gam'ani rak higati | no big deal, I just arrived too |
| eyze mazal | eyz'mazal | what luck |
| nu, yalla, nikhnasim? | nu, yalla, nichnasim? | well c'mon, going in? |
| bo, ani kvar ra'ev | bo, 'ni kvar ra'ev | c'mon, I'm already hungry |
| ani chayav lazuz | 'ni chayav lazuz | I gotta go |
| kvar? ma pitom? | kvar? m'pitom? | already? why so soon? |
| yesh li pgisha be'od chatzi sha'a | yesh li pgisha b'od chatzi sha'a | I have a meeting in half an hour |
| okay, nedaber. kol hakavod she-bata | okay, n'daber. kol'akavod sh'bata | okay, we'll talk. well done for coming |
