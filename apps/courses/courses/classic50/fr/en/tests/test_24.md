# Test for Lesson 24: Participle agreement

**How do you say it in French?**
Translate each phrase into French. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson.

---

1. She arrived yesterday.
2. They (m.) came late.
3. They (f.) left for Paris.
4. We (two women) were born in Russia.
5. I saw her.
6. I saw them (m.).
7. I saw them (f.).
8. The books that I read.
9. The letters that she wrote.
10. She washed her hands.
11. She washed (herself).
12. They (f.) met yesterday.
13. We (f.) talked to each other.
14. Which song did you listen to?
15. How many books have you read?
16. She bought them (f.).
17. We (m.) left at eight o'clock.
18. The girls went down.
19. I washed my hands.
20. She put on a dress.
21. The dress that she put on.
22. They (f.) called each other.
23. Marie was born in Lyon.
24. The boys fell.
25. I heard her.
26. The houses that we built.
27. She woke up early.
28. They (f.) sat down.
29. I met her yesterday.
30. All the women left.

9. *Les lettres qu'elle a écrites.*

11. *Elle s'est lavée.*
12. *Elles se sont rencontrées hier.*
13. *Nous nous sommes parlé.*

15. *Combien de livres as-tu lus ?* (or: *Combien de livres tu as lus ?*)

17. *Nous sommes partis à huit heures.*
18. *Les filles sont descendues.*
19. *Je me suis lavé les mains.*
20. *Elle a mis une robe.* (or: *Elle a mis sa robe.*)
21. *La robe qu'elle a mise.*
22. *Elles se sont téléphoné.*
23. *Marie est née à Lyon.*
24. *Les garçons sont tombés.*
25. *Je l'ai entendue.*
26. *Les maisons que nous avons construites.*
27. *Elle s'est réveillée tôt.*

29. *Je l'ai rencontrée hier.*
