# Test for Lesson 18: Pronoun order in a sentence

**How do you say it in French?**
Translate each phrase into French. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson.

---

1. I'm giving it to you (as a gift).
2. He gives them to me.
3. She tells it to us.
4. I'm explaining it to you (pl.).
5. We're sending them to you.
6. You're showing it to me.
7. I'm giving it to him.
8. I'm giving them to her.
9. We explain it to them.
10. He sends them to him.
11. She gives it to him (as a gift).
12. I'm sending them to them.
13. He's taking us there.
14. He gives me some. (about money)
15. He gives him some. (about advice)
16. I give you some. (about apples)
17. There are three of them. (apples)
18. Give it to me, please. (tu)
19. Give them to me, please. (vous)
20. Don't give it to me!
21. Don't tell it to them!
22. I don't want to tell it to him.
23. She can explain it to you.
24. We must tell it to them.
25. I'm not giving it to you (as a gift).
26. He doesn't send them to us.
27. Are you going to tell me about it? (use futur proche)
28. Give them to her! (tu) (as a gift)
29. I just told it to him.
30. Thanks for the gift! — You're welcome, I'm giving it to you from the bottom of my heart.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *Je te l'offre.* (or: *Je te le donne.*)
2. *Il me les donne.*
3. *Elle nous le dit.*
4. *Je vous l'explique.*
5. *Nous te les envoyons.* (or: *On te les envoie.*)
6. *Tu me le montres.*
7. *Je le lui donne.*
8. *Je les lui donne.*
9. *Nous le leur expliquons.* (or: *On le leur explique.*)
10. *Il les lui envoie.*
11. *Elle la lui offre.* (or: *Elle la lui donne.*)
12. *Je les leur envoie.*
13. *Il nous y emmène.*
14. *Il m'en donne.*
15. *Il lui en donne.*
16. *Je t'en donne.*
17. *Il y en a trois.*
18. *Donne-le-moi, s'il te plaît.*
19. *Donnez-les-moi, s'il vous plaît.*
20. *Ne me le donne pas !*
21. *Ne le leur dis pas !*
22. *Je ne veux pas le lui dire.*
23. *Elle peut te l'expliquer.*
24. *Nous devons le leur dire.* (or: *On doit le leur dire.*)
25. *Je ne te l'offre pas.* (or: *Je ne te le donne pas.*)
26. *Il ne nous les envoie pas.*
27. *Tu vas m'en parler ?* (or: *Est-ce que tu vas m'en parler ?*)
28. *Offre-les-lui !* (or: *Donne-les-lui !*)
29. *Je viens de le lui dire.*
30. *Merci pour le cadeau ! — De rien, je te l'offre de tout cœur.*
