# Test for Lesson 19: Imperative

**How do you say it in French?**
Translate each phrase into French. Use the grammar and vocabulary from this lesson.

---

1. Speak!
2. Speak! (formal/plural)
3. Let's speak!
4. Eat your soup!
5. Don't eat that!
6. Listen to me!
7. Don't listen to him!
8. Let's go to the cinema!
9. Go straight ahead.
10. Turn right.
11. Turn left. (formal/plural)
12. Be good! (to a boy)
13. Be careful! (formal)
14. Have patience!
15. Don't be afraid! (literally: don't have fear)
16. Give me the book!
17. Don't give me the book!
18. Tell me the truth!
19. Call him! (on the phone)
20. Don't call her!
21. Give them to me!
22. Go there!
23. Don't go there!
24. Take some! (about apples)
25. Don't take any.
26. Add salt and mix. (formal/plural)
27. Cross the street and keep going straight.
28. Please repeat. (literally: be kind, repeat — formal)
29. Wash your hands before eating!
30. Don't forget to call her tomorrow. (formal/plural)

1. *Parle !*

7. *Ne l'écoute pas !*
8. *Allons au cinéma !*
9. *Va tout droit.* (or: *Continue tout droit.*)
10. *Tourne à droite.*
11. *Tournez à gauche.*
12. *Sois sage !*
13. *Soyez prudent !* (or: *Soyez attentif !*; *Faites attention !*)
14. *Aie de la patience !* (or: *Sois patient !*)
15. *N'aie pas peur !*
16. *Donne-moi le livre !*
17. *Ne me donne pas le livre !*
18. *Dis-moi la vérité !*
19. *Appelle-le !* (or: *Téléphone-lui !*)
20. *Ne l'appelle pas !* (or: *Ne lui téléphone pas !*)
21. *Donne-les-moi !*
22. *Vas-y !*
23. *N'y va pas !*
24. *Prends-en !*
25. *N'en prends pas.*
26. *Ajoutez du sel et mélangez.* (or: *Ajoutez le sel et mélangez.*)
27. *Traverse la rue et continue tout droit.*
28. *Soyez gentil, répétez.* (or: *Ayez la gentillesse de répéter.*; *Veuillez répéter.*)
29. *Lave-toi les mains avant de manger !*
30. *N'oubliez pas de l'appeler demain.* (or: *N'oubliez pas de lui téléphoner demain.*)
