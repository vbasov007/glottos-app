# Test for Lesson 21: Pretérito indefinido — regular verbs

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson: regular *pretérito indefinido*, past-time markers, and the spelling changes in the 1st person of -car/-gar/-zar verbs.

---

1. Yesterday I worked.
2. You ate paella.
3. She lived in Madrid.
4. We spoke with the teacher.
5. They bought bread.
6. I arrived at eight.
7. I started the course two weeks ago.
8. I looked for my phone.
9. I paid for the coffee.
10. I was born in 1990.
11. Last week we visited the museum.
12. Last year they traveled around Spain.
13. Last night I had dinner at a restaurant.
14. I met her in Barcelona.
15. She wrote me a long letter.
16. The children received presents.
17. We came home late.
18. What did you buy at the market?
19. Where did you (vosotros) live as children?
20. When did you start working?
21. I didn't understand the question.
22. They sold the car in March.
23. I liked this book.
24. Did you like the songs?
25. I told her that yesterday.
26. We opened the door.
27. An hour ago he called me.
28. That day I didn't eat anything.
29. They learned the lesson in an hour.
30. Hi! What did you do on the weekend? — I visited my parents and we had lunch together.
