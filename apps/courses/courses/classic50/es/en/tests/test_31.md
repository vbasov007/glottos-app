# Test for Lesson 31: Presente de subjuntivo — the forms

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson. Focus on the correct subjunctive form after the frames *quiero que, espero que, ojalá*.

---

1. I want you to speak Spanish.
2. I want you (ustedes) to eat well.
3. Hopefully he arrives on time.
4. I hope she sleeps well.
5. I want us to go together.
6. Hopefully I can help you.
7. I hope they tell the truth.
8. I want you to know the truth.
9. Hopefully it's true.
10. I want him to do his homework.
11. I hope you (vosotros) are here tomorrow.
12. Hopefully the kids sleep quickly.
13. I want her to be happy.
14. I hope you get the job.
15. Hopefully you have luck.
16. I want you to ask for help.
17. I hope he knows the answer.
18. Hopefully they come soon.
19. I want us to think the same.
20. I hope she's at home.
21. Hopefully we can go to Madrid.
22. I want you (ustedes) to put this here.
23. I hope he says "yes".
24. Hopefully I have more time.
25. I want you to be attentive (atento).
26. I hope they work well.
27. Hopefully it doesn't rain.
28. I want us to leave early.
29. I hope you understand me.
30. Hopefully we have a good day.
