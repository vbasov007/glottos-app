# Test for Lesson 22: Pretérito indefinido — irregular verbs

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I had a lot of work yesterday.
2. Where were you last night?
3. My friends came at eight.
4. We did the homework together.
5. She told the truth.
6. The children slept for eight hours.
7. What did you order at the restaurant?
8. Pedro couldn't come.
9. The waiter served us coffee.
10. On Saturday I went to the cinema.
11. My mother set the table.
12. The teachers brought the books.
13. Yesterday was a hard day.
14. I saw Carmen on the street.
15. Who drove the car?
16. I had to work all night.
17. I found out about it yesterday.
18. They refused to go.
19. I tried to open the door.
20. What did you do yesterday?
21. We went to Madrid last week.
22. He gave me a present.
23. Ana put the book on the table.
24. The children asked for ice cream.
25. Did you sleep well?
26. I didn't say anything.
27. Last year we were in Spain.
28. She translated the letter.
29. They brought wine.
30. Hi! How was the weekend? — Very good, I went to the mountains.
