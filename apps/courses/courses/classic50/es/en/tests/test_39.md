# Test for Lesson 39: Perfect subjunctive. Type-3 si-clause. Regret

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson: *presente perfecto del subjuntivo* (haya + part.), *pluscuamperfecto del subjuntivo* (hubiera + part.), and the third type of conditional sentences.

---

1. I'm glad you've come.
2. It's a shame you (pl.) didn't call.
3. Hope you slept well.
4. I don't think he's arrived yet.
5. They've probably already left.
6. I doubt they've finished the project.
7. It's good that you told me the truth.
8. It's a pity I couldn't make it.
9. It's strange she hasn't answered yet.
10. I didn't believe you'd come back so soon.
11. If I had known, I would have told you.
12. If I had studied more, I would have passed the exam.
13. If you had listened to me, you wouldn't have been late.
14. If you had called me, I would have helped you.
15. If it hadn't rained, we would have gone out.
16. If she had told me, I would have come earlier.
17. If we had left earlier, we wouldn't have missed the train.
18. If you had asked me, I would have answered you.
19. If they had studied, they would have understood.
20. If I had seen you, I would have said hello.
21. I should have studied more.
22. I could have helped you.
23. I shouldn't have said that.
24. I ought to have called him.
25. If only I had known that earlier!
26. If only you had arrived on time!
27. I regret not calling my grandmother.
28. I would have liked to see it in person.
29. Just as well (thank goodness) you came back on time.
30. If I had known then what I know now, I would have done everything differently.

1. *Me alegro de que hayas venido.*

9. *Es extraño que todavía no haya respondido.* (or: *…no haya contestado.*)
10. *No creía que hubieras vuelto tan pronto.* (or: *…tan temprano.*)
11. *Si lo hubiera sabido, te lo habría dicho.* (colloq.: *…te lo hubiera dicho.*)
12. *Si hubiera estudiado más, habría aprobado el examen.*

14. *Si me hubieras llamado, te habría ayudado.*
15. *Si no hubiera llovido, habríamos salido.*

17. *Si hubiéramos salido antes, no habríamos perdido el tren.*

19. *Si hubieran estudiado, habrían entendido.*
20. *Si te hubiera visto, te habría saludado.*
