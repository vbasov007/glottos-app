# Test for Lesson 18: Reflexive verbs. Daily routine

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I get up at seven in the morning.
2. You go to bed late.
3. He takes a shower every day.
4. We wake up at six.
5. They get dressed quickly.
6. I wash my hands.
7. She combs her hair in front of the mirror.
8. My dad shaves in the morning.
9. My sister puts on her make-up in the bathroom.
10. The children sit down at the table.
11. I put on my jacket.
12. He takes off his shoes.
13. My name is Anna.
14. What's your name?
15. I'm leaving, bye!
16. The child falls asleep in the car.
17. I want to get up early.
18. We're going to take a shower.
19. I can't fall asleep.
20. She's getting dressed right now.
21. Get up!
22. Don't go to bed late!
23. Wash your hands!
24. First I take a shower, then I have breakfast.
25. Before going to bed, I read a book.
26. After I wake up, I drink a coffee.
27. What time do you go to bed?
28. I'm staying home.
29. We're saying goodbye to our friends.
30. Every day I get up at seven, take a shower, get dressed and leave for work.
