# Test for Lesson 38: Conditional sentences with *si* — the three types

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson. Most phrases are Type 2 (*si + imperfecto de subjuntivo → condicional*); a few are real conditions and a few are counterfactual past.

---

1. If I have time, I call you.
2. If I have time tomorrow, I'll call you.
3. If it's raining, we stay home.
4. If I had time, I would go to the cinema.
5. If I had money, I would travel the world.
6. If I were you, I wouldn't do it.
7. If I knew the answer, I would tell it to you.
8. If I lived in Spain, I would speak better.
9. If I could, I would quit my job.
10. What would you do if you won the lottery?
11. If I won the lottery, I would buy a house by the sea.
12. Where would you go if you could travel for free?
13. If I could teleport, I would go to Japan.
14. What would you change about your life if you could?
15. If I were president, I would improve education.
16. If you were me, what would you do?
17. If I were you, I would accept.
18. If only I had more time!
19. It's a pity I can't help you.
20. I'd love to travel more.
21. I'd like to live by the sea.
22. If I had more money, I would help my parents.
23. If he had come earlier, he would have seen Ana. *(type 3)*
24. If I had studied, I would have passed the exam. *(type 3)*
25. If we had known the truth, we wouldn't have come. *(type 3)*
26. If you're hungry, eat something.
27. If you call me, I answer.
28. Then, in that case, I'm not going.
29. If I had a second chance, I would do it differently.
30. If I didn't work so much, I would have more time for family.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *Si tengo tiempo, te llamo.*
2. *Si tengo tiempo mañana, te llamaré.*
3. *Si llueve, nos quedamos en casa.*
4. *Si tuviera tiempo, iría al cine.*
5. *Si tuviera dinero, viajaría por el mundo.*
6. *Si yo fuera tú, no lo haría.* (or: *Yo que tú, no lo haría.*)
7. *Si supiera la respuesta, te la diría.*
8. *Si viviera en España, hablaría mejor.*
9. *Si pudiera, dejaría el trabajo.*
10. *¿Qué harías si ganaras la lotería?* (or: *…si te tocara la lotería?*)
11. *Si ganara la lotería, compraría una casa cerca del mar.*
12. *¿Adónde irías si pudieras viajar gratis?*
13. *Si pudiera teletransportarme, iría a Japón.*
14. *¿Qué cambiarías de tu vida si pudieras?*
15. *Si fuera presidente, mejoraría la educación.*
16. *Si tú fueras yo, ¿qué harías?*
17. *Si yo fuera tú, aceptaría.* (or: *En tu lugar, aceptaría.*)
18. *¡Ojalá tuviera más tiempo!*
19. *Lástima que no pueda ayudarte.* (or: *Ojalá pudiera ayudarte.*)
20. *Quisiera viajar más.* (or: *Me gustaría viajar más.*)
21. *Me gustaría vivir cerca del mar.*
22. *Si tuviera más dinero, ayudaría a mis padres.*
23. *Si hubiera venido antes, habría visto a Ana.*
24. *Si hubiera estudiado, habría aprobado el examen.*
25. *Si hubiéramos sabido la verdad, no habríamos venido.*
26. *Si tienes hambre, come algo.*
27. *Si me llamas, te contesto.* (or: *…te contestaré.*)
28. *Entonces, en ese caso, no voy.*
29. *Si tuviera una segunda oportunidad, lo haría de otra manera.*
30. *Si no trabajara tanto, tendría más tiempo para la familia.*
