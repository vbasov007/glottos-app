# Test for Lesson 23: Pretérito imperfecto. Habits, descriptions, background

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson: imperfecto (-aba / -ía), the three exceptions (ser, ir, ver), markers of childhood and regularity.

---

1. I lived in Madrid.
2. You spoke Spanish very well.
3. She worked at a school.
4. We ate together every day.
5. They played soccer on Sundays.
6. As a kid I walked to school.
7. When I was little, I had a dog.
8. My grandma cooked really well.
9. Every summer we used to go to the seaside.
10. My parents worked a lot.
11. I watched TV every evening.
12. It was raining when I arrived.
13. It was cold and cloudy.
14. It was eight in the morning.
15. I was ten years old.
16. We were friends.
17. They were very kind.
18. I didn't want to go to school.
19. She knew the answer.
20. I often read books.
21. We almost never watched TV.
22. On Saturdays we went to the countryside.
23. I usually got up at seven.
24. Sometimes we went to the movies.
25. From time to time grandma would call us.
26. I used to smoke a lot.
27. The house was big and had a garden.
28. When I was young, I traveled a lot.
29. We were sleeping when the phone rang.
30. As a kid I liked ice cream.
