# Test for Lesson 34: Subjuntivo after doubt, denial, and uncertainty

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson. Pay special attention to the *creo que / no creo que* flip and the difference between *puede que / quizás / a lo mejor*.

---

1. I think he's coming.
2. I don't think he's coming.
3. I doubt it's true.
4. It may rain. *(puede que)*
5. Maybe it'll rain. *(quizá)*
6. Maybe it'll rain. *(a lo mejor)*
7. I think you have time.
8. I don't think you have time.
9. I'm sure they're at home.
10. I'm not sure they're at home.
11. It seems to me she's right.
12. It doesn't seem to me that she's right.
13. You may be right.
14. There will probably be a traffic jam.
15. It's impossible that he knows it.
16. It's unlikely they'll arrive on time.
17. I suppose they already know.
18. I don't think they already know.
19. Maybe they're running late. *(es posible que)*
20. It's not true that he lives here.
21. He denies that it's his fault.
22. I doubt he speaks Russian.
23. It's not that I don't want to, it's that I can't.
24. Maybe they don't know the way.
25. Do you think he's coming? — I don't know, maybe he is.
26. I don't think they want to go.
27. We'll probably have to wait.
28. Maybe we have a problem. *(tal vez)*
29. I think she calls every day; I don't think she forgets.
30. You may be right, but I doubt it'll work.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *Creo que viene.*
2. *No creo que venga.*
3. *Dudo que sea verdad.*
4. *Puede que llueva.*
5. *Quizá llueva.* (or: *Quizás llueva.*)
6. *A lo mejor llueve.* *(note: ind.!)*
7. *Creo que tienes tiempo.*
8. *No creo que tengas tiempo.*
9. *Estoy seguro de que están en casa.*
10. *No estoy seguro de que estén en casa.*
11. *Me parece que tiene razón.*
12. *No me parece que tenga razón.*
13. *Puede que tengas razón.*
14. *Probablemente haya tráfico.* (or: *Es probable que haya tráfico.*)
15. *Es imposible que lo sepa.*
16. *Es improbable que lleguen a tiempo.*
17. *Supongo que ya lo saben.*
18. *No creo que ya lo sepan.*
19. *Es posible que lleguen tarde.*
20. *No es verdad que viva aquí.*
21. *Niega que sea culpa suya.*
22. *Dudo que hable ruso.*
23. *No es que no quiera, es que no puedo.*
24. *Puede que no sepan el camino.*
25. *¿Crees que viene? — No sé, puede que venga.*
26. *No creo que quieran ir.*
27. *Probablemente tengamos que esperar.* (or: *Es probable que tengamos que esperar.*)
28. *Tal vez tengamos un problema.*
29. *Creo que llama todos los días; no creo que se olvide.*
30. *Puede que tengas razón, pero dudo que funcione.*
