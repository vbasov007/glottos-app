# Test for Lesson 19: The verb *gustar* and its family. Hobbies, tastes, aches

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I like coffee.
2. I like dogs.
3. Do you like music?
4. He likes soccer.
5. She likes books.
6. We like the movies.
7. Do you (Spain, pl.) like video games?
8. They like to dance.
9. I love chocolate.
10. María loves to travel.
11. I don't like tea.
12. I, on the other hand, like tea.
13. He, however, likes coffee, not tea.
14. My head hurts.
15. Does your stomach hurt?
16. His feet hurt.
17. Her throat hurts.
18. Our eyes hurt.
19. I'm short on money.
20. History interests me.
21. I don't care about that.
22. That seems fine to me.
23. I have two euros left.
24. The noise bothers me.
25. I like to read and cook.
26. We like to play soccer.
27. Do you like movies or TV series?
28. What do you like to do on weekends?
29. I like coffee, and he likes tea.
30. My parents' backs hurt.

1. *Me gusta el café.*

10. *A María le encanta viajar.* (or: *Le encanta viajar a María.*)
11. *No me gusta el té.*
12. *A mí sí me gusta el té.* (or: *A mí me gusta el té.*)
13. *A él le gusta el café, no el té.*
14. *Me duele la cabeza.*
15. *¿Te duele el estómago?*
16. *Le duelen los pies.* (or: *A él le duelen los pies.*)

22. *Me parece bien.*

24. *Me molesta el ruido.*

28. *¿Qué te gusta hacer los fines de semana?*
