# Test for Lesson 17: Combining direct and indirect object pronouns

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson: pronoun pairs in the order "to whom → what", the *se* rule when both pronouns are third person, and attachment to infinitives, gerunds and affirmative imperatives.

---

1. I give it to you.
2. She tells it to me (the truth).
3. We bring them to you (the coffees, pl.; for Spain).
4. They send them to us (the letters).
5. You lend it to me (the car).
6. I give it to him (the book).
7. I tell it to her (the truth).
8. We sell them to them (the documents).
9. They give them to her as a gift (the flowers).
10. I explain it to you (formal).
11. Give it to me!
12. Tell it to him!
13. Bring them to us (the coffees)!
14. Show it to her (the photo)!
15. Lend it to me (the phone)!
16. Don't tell it to me!
17. Don't give it to him!
18. Don't send it to them (the message)!
19. I want to show it to you (the photo).
20. We're going to send it to them (the gift).
21. I'm telling it to her right now.
22. They're explaining it to us right now (the grammar).
23. I give it to him as a gift (the book).
24. Tell me the truth! — I'm telling it to you.
25. Can you lend it to me? (the dictionary — *el diccionario*)
26. I'll bring it to you tomorrow (formal).
27. I don't want to tell it to them (the secret).
28. Do me a favor!
29. Have you already told it to her? — Yes, I told it to her.
30. María gives the book to Juan. — María gives it to him.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *Te lo doy.* (или: *Te la doy.* — если предмет ж.р.)
2. *Me la dice.* (или: *Me la cuenta.*)
3. *Os los traemos.* (Исп.) (или: *Se los traemos.* — Лат. Ам.)
4. *Nos las envían.* (или: *Nos las mandan.*)
5. *Me lo prestas.*
6. *Se lo doy (a él).*
7. *Se la digo (a ella).*
8. *Se los vendemos (a ellos).*
9. *Se las regalan (a ella).*
10. *Se lo explico (a usted).*
11. *¡Dámelo!*
12. *¡Díselo!*
13. *¡Tráenoslos!*
14. *¡Muéstrasela!* (или: *¡Enséñasela!*)
15. *¡Préstamelo!*
16. *¡No me lo digas!*
17. *¡No se lo des!*
18. *¡No se lo envíes!* (или: *¡No se lo mandes!*)
19. *Quiero mostrártela.* (или: *Te la quiero mostrar.*; *Quiero enseñártela.*)
20. *Vamos a enviárselo.* (или: *Se lo vamos a enviar.*; *Vamos a mandárselo.*)
21. *Se lo estoy contando (a ella).* (или: *Estoy contándoselo.*; *Se lo cuento ahora.*)
22. *Nos la están explicando.* (или: *Están explicándonosla.*)
23. *Se lo regalo (a él).*
24. *¡Dime la verdad! — Te la digo.*
25. *¿Me lo puedes prestar?* (или: *¿Puedes prestármelo?*)
26. *Se lo traigo mañana.* (или: *Se lo llevo mañana.*; *Se lo voy a traer mañana.*)
27. *No quiero decírselo.* (или: *No se lo quiero decir.*)
28. *¡Hazme un favor!*
29. *¿Ya se lo dijiste? — Sí, se lo dije.* (или: *¿Ya se lo has dicho? — Sí, se lo he dicho.*)
30. *María le da el libro a Juan. — María se lo da.*
