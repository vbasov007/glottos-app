# Test for Lesson 47: Conjunctions and discourse markers

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson. Try to match the marker to the right register and category (cause / consequence / contrast / concession / addition / sequence / reformulation / example / conclusion).

---

1. I didn't come because I was sick.
2. Since you insist, I'll tell you.
3. Given that there are no objections, we'll continue.
4. Considering that the climate has changed, the harvest is at risk.
5. The delay was caused by traffic. (formal, + noun)
6. Since it was raining, we stayed home. (sentence-initial)
7. I had no money, so I didn't buy anything.
8. It was raining, so we didn't go out.
9. The budget is limited; therefore, we must save. (formal)
10. Resources are lacking; as a result, the project is postponed. (academic)
11. He didn't study; hence the failure. (+ subjuntivo)
12. It's expensive, but it's worth it. (neutral)
13. It's expensive; nevertheless, it's worth it. (formal)
14. There were difficulties; nonetheless, we finished. (very formal)
15. My brother is tall; I, on the other hand, am short.
16. He works, whereas she studies.
17. Although it's raining (I can see it), I'm going out.
18. Even if it rains, I'm going out.
19. Despite the cold, we went to the park. (+ noun, formal)
20. Although it's true, it isn't relevant. (academic writing)
21. I study Spanish, and also Italian.
22. It's expensive and, on top of that, it doesn't work well.
23. They approved the plan; they also allocated funds. (formal)
24. On the other hand, we have to consider the cost. (formal)
25. First, we need to define the problem. Second, analyze the causes. Finally, draw a conclusion.
26. He arrived late, that is, after ten.
27. There is fruit, for example, apples and pears.
28. I like music, especially jazz.
29. In conclusion, the proposal deserves support. (formal, essay)
30. Ultimately, there is no alternative.

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *No vine **porque** estaba enfermo.*
2. ***Ya que** insistes, te lo cuento.*
3. ***Puesto que** no hay objeciones, continuamos.*
4. ***Dado que** el clima ha cambiado, las cosechas peligran.*
5. *El retraso fue **debido al** tráfico.* (или: *a causa del tráfico*)
6. ***Como** llovía, nos quedamos en casa.*
7. *No tenía dinero, **por eso** no compré nada.*
8. *Llovía, **así que** no salimos.*
9. *El presupuesto es limitado; **por lo tanto**, hay que ahorrar.* (или: *por tanto*)
10. *Faltan recursos; **en consecuencia**, el proyecto se aplaza.*
11. *No estudió; **de ahí que suspendiera**.* (*de ahí que* + subj.)
12. *Es caro, **pero** vale la pena.*
13. *Es caro; **sin embargo**, vale la pena.*
14. *Hubo dificultades; **no obstante**, terminamos.*
15. *Mi hermano es alto; yo, **en cambio**, soy bajo.*
16. *Él trabaja, **mientras que** ella estudia.*
17. ***Aunque** llueve, voy a salir.* (ind. — известный факт)
18. ***Aunque** llueva, voy a salir.* (subj. — гипотеза)
19. ***A pesar del** frío, fuimos al parque.* (или: *pese al frío*)
20. ***Si bien** es cierto, no es relevante.*
21. *Estudio español y **también** italiano.*
22. *Es caro y, **además**, no funciona bien.*
23. *Aprobaron el plan; **asimismo**, asignaron fondos.*
24. ***Por otra parte**, hay que considerar el coste.*
25. ***En primer lugar**, hay que definir el problema. **En segundo lugar**, analizar las causas. **Por último**, sacar una conclusión.* (или: *finalmente*)
26. *Llegó tarde, **es decir**, después de las diez.*
27. *Hay frutas, **por ejemplo**, manzanas y peras.* (или: *tales como*)
28. *Me gusta la música, **en particular**, el jazz.*
29. ***En conclusión**, la propuesta merece apoyo.* (или: *para concluir*)
30. ***En definitiva**, no hay alternativa.*
