# Test for Lesson 28: Conditional simple — politeness, hypothesis, future-in-the-past

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson.

---

1. I'd like a coffee, please.
2. Could you (formal) help me?
3. Could you (informal) speak more slowly?
4. Would it be possible to pay by card?
5. In your place, I'd tell the truth.
6. He said he would come tomorrow.
7. She promised she would call.
8. You (informal) should rest.
9. You (informal) could write to him.
10. Would you (formal) happen to have a pen?
11. I would go to Spain.
12. We would speak with the manager.
13. They wouldn't say that.
14. I wouldn't do it that way.
15. It would be better to wait.
16. It would be a good idea.
17. How can I help you? — I'd like a table for two.
18. Would you (formal) mind opening the window?
19. I thought we'd have more time.
20. We knew they wouldn't come.
21. In your place, I wouldn't go.
22. In that case, I would talk to him.
23. You'd need to study more.
24. I'd like two tickets to Madrid.
25. Could you (formal) repeat that?
26. If I were you, I would wait.
27. He said he would do it tomorrow.
28. I'd like to know what time the train leaves.
29. Good afternoon. — I'd like to book a table for eight. — Of course, gladly.
30. Excuse me, could you tell me where the station is? — Of course, go straight ahead, it's five minutes away.

8. *Deberías descansar.*

10. *¿Tendría usted un bolígrafo?*
11. *Iría a España.*
12. *Hablaríamos con el gerente.*
13. *No dirían eso.*
14. *Yo no haría así.* (or: *Yo no lo haría así.*)
15. *Sería mejor esperar.*
16. *Sería una buena idea.*

18. *¿Le importaría abrir la ventana?*
19. *Pensé que tendríamos más tiempo.*
20. *Sabíamos que no vendrían.*

22. *En ese caso hablaría con él.*
23. *Tendrías que estudiar más.*
24. *Me gustaría dos billetes para Madrid.* (or: *Querría dos billetes para Madrid.*)
25. *¿Podría usted repetir?*

27. *Dijo que lo haría mañana.*
28. *Me gustaría saber a qué hora sale el tren.*
29. *Buenas tardes. — Me gustaría reservar una mesa para las ocho. — Por supuesto, con mucho gusto.*
