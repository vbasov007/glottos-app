# Block-Test 2 (Lessons 11–20): "Escudero / Escudera" — A2 expansion

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. This test covers the whole block: stem-changing verbs, irregular yo forms (*hago, pongo, salgo, traigo*), possessives and demonstratives, direct and indirect pronouns and their combination, reflexive verbs, *gustar* and similar verbs, *estar + gerundio*.
