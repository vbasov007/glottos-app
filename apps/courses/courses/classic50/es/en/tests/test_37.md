# Test for Lesson 37: Imperfecto de subjuntivo — forms

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson. Both forms — -ra and -se — are acceptable (the keys give -ra as the main one).

---

1. I wanted you to come.
2. I wanted you to call me.
3. Mom wanted us to eat more.
4. The teacher wanted us to speak Spanish.
5. I asked him to help me.
6. I asked them to wait.
7. She told me to tell the truth.
8. I expected you to have time.
9. We were hoping he'd arrive on time.
10. I was hoping you'd say "yes".
11. I was glad you were here.
12. It surprised me that he knew so much.
13. It was important that we studied.
14. It was necessary that they worked.
15. I was afraid he wouldn't come.
16. We were afraid something would happen.
17. I didn't think it would be easy.
18. I didn't believe he'd done it.
19. I doubted she had any money.
20. It wasn't true that he knew the answer.
21. I was looking for someone who spoke Russian.
22. I needed someone who knew how to cook.
23. There was nobody who would understand me.
24. He told me to come early.
25. She asked me to give her the book.
26. I wanted you to be happy.
27. We wanted you to stay.
28. I didn't think it was true.
29. I wanted him to tell me where he'd been.
30. Dad told us to sleep early.

1. *Quería que vinieras.*

6. *Pedí que esperaran.*
7. *Ella dijo que yo dijera la verdad.* (or: *Me dijo que dijera la verdad.*)

10. *Esperaba que dijeras que sí.*
11. *Me alegré de que estuvieras aquí.*
12. *Me sorprendió que supiera tanto.*
13. *Era importante que estudiáramos.*
14. *Era necesario que trabajaran.*
15. *Tenía miedo de que no viniera.*
16. *Teníamos miedo de que pasara algo.*
17. *No creía que fuera fácil.*
18. *No creía que lo hubiera hecho.* (or: *No creía que lo hiciera.*)
19. *Dudaba que tuviera dinero.*
20. *No era cierto que supiera la respuesta.*
21. *Buscaba a alguien que hablara ruso.*
22. *Necesitaba a alguien que supiera cocinar.*

24. *Me dijo que viniera temprano.*
25. *Me pidió que le diera el libro.*
26. *Quería que fueras feliz.*
27. *Queríamos que os quedarais.* (or: *que se quedaran.*)
28. *No creía que fuera verdad.*
29. *Quería que me dijera dónde estaba.* (or: *…dónde estuvo.*)
30. *Papá dijo que durmiéramos temprano.*
