# Test for Lesson 36: Subjunctive in adverbial clauses. Conjunctions and planning

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson — in particular, the correct mood after each conjunction.

---

1. I'm telling you this so that you know the truth.
2. I won't go unless they invite me.
3. Call me before you leave.
4. He came in without anyone noticing.
5. I'll help you provided that you listen to me.
6. Take an umbrella in case it rains.
7. I work so that my children live better.
8. Explain it so that the children understand. *(purpose)*
9. He explained it so well that the children understood everything. *(result)*
10. When you get home, call me.
11. When I get home, I take a shower. *(habit)*
12. I'll wait for you until you come back.
13. I waited for him until he came back. *(past, fact)*
14. As soon as I know something, I'll let you know.
15. Leave as soon as you can.
16. After you have dinner, we'll go to the cinema.
17. While you're here, rest.
18. Even if it rains, we'll go out. *(hypothesis)*
19. Although it's raining, we're going out. *(fact)*
20. Even if she's rich, I don't want her. *(hypothesis)*
21. When I have money, I'll buy a car.
22. We'll have dinner when Dad gets back.
23. I won't leave until I finish.
24. I'll tell you everything so that you don't worry.
25. Agreed, provided that you arrive on time.
26. I'll let you know as soon as I find out the date.
27. We'll postpone the meeting unless you prefer another date.
28. I'll bring plan B just in case. *(in case something happens)*
29. Every time he comes, I receive him. *(habit)*
30. Whenever you come, I'll receive you. *(always, hypothesis)*

<summary>Key</summary>

1. *Te lo digo para que sepas la verdad.*
2. *No iré a menos que me inviten.*
3. *Llámame antes de que te vayas.* (or: *antes de que salgas*)
4. *Entró sin que nadie (lo) notara.* (or: *sin que nadie se diera cuenta*)
5. *Te ayudo con tal de que me escuches.*
6. *Lleva el paraguas en caso de que llueva.* (or: *Toma…*)
7. *Trabajo a fin de que mis hijos vivan mejor.*
8. *Explícalo de modo que los niños entiendan.* (or: *de manera que…*)
9. *Lo explicó de modo que los niños lo entendieron todo.*
10. *Cuando llegues a casa, llámame.*
11. *Cuando llego a casa, me ducho.*
12. *Te esperaré hasta que vuelvas.*
13. *Lo esperé hasta que volvió.*
14. *En cuanto sepa algo, te aviso.* (or: *tan pronto como sepa…*)
15. *Vete tan pronto como puedas.* (or: *en cuanto puedas*)
16. *Después de que cenes, iremos al cine.*
17. *Mientras estés aquí, descansa.*
18. *Aunque llueva, saldremos.*
19. *Aunque llueve, salimos.*
20. *Aunque sea rica, no la quiero.*
21. *Cuando tenga dinero, compraré un coche.*
22. *Cenaremos cuando papá vuelva.*
23. *No me iré hasta que termine.*
24. *Te lo diré todo para que no te preocupes.*
25. *De acuerdo, con tal de que llegues a tiempo.*
26. *Te avisaré en cuanto sepa la fecha.*
27. *Pospondremos la reunión a menos que prefieras otra fecha.*
28. *Llevaré un plan B por si acaso.* (or: *…en caso de que pase algo.*)
29. *Siempre que viene, lo recibo.*
30. *Siempre que vengas, te recibo.*
