# Test for Lesson 41: Three ways to express the passive voice. Pasiva con ser, estar and se

**How do you say it in Spanish?**
Translate the phrases into Spanish. Use the grammar and vocabulary from the lesson. Choose the right type of passive (ser / estar / se) according to the meaning of the sentence.

---

1. The novel was written by García Márquez.
2. The law was signed by the president yesterday.
3. This house was built in 1900.
4. America was discovered in 1492.
5. These books were published in Madrid.
6. The museum was inaugurated by the mayor on Saturday.
7. The contract is already signed.
8. The door is closed.
9. Dinner is ready.
10. The shops are open until ten.
11. These houses are already built.
12. The window is open.
13. Spanish is spoken here.
14. Car for sale.
15. Rooms for rent.
16. How is it spelled?
17. Life is good here.
18. In Spain people have dinner late.
19. Smoking is forbidden.
20. People work a lot here.
21. These newspapers are published every day.
22. This painting was painted by Picasso.
23. The city was founded by the Romans.
24. The agreement will be signed tomorrow.
25. The museum is closed on Mondays.
26. The building is being restored at the moment.
27. The new director was appointed in March.
28. The doors open at nine.
29. The law was approved by parliament.
30. This building is already restored, and it was opened last year.

28. *Las puertas se abren a las nueve.*
29. *La ley fue aprobada por el parlamento.*
